## What to Look for in an Automatic Cat Feeder

**Portion accuracy:** Look for feeders with independent reviews confirming consistent dispensing — some motors drift over time or jam with small kibble.

**Power backup:** All quality feeders include battery backup. Never buy a feeder that relies solely on AC power.

**Capacity:** For a single cat eating 60g/day, 5L lasts approximately 30 days.

**Wet vs dry:** Most feeders are dry-food only. For wet food, choose Cat Mate or similar tray-style feeders.

**App quality:** WiFi feeders are only as good as their apps. PETLIBRO and SureFlap have the most reliable app ecosystems.
