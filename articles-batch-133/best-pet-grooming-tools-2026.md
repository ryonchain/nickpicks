## Building a Home Grooming Kit

For most pet owners, three tools cover 90% of grooming needs:

1. **Daily brush** — Hertzko Slicker or equivalent
2. **Deshedding tool** — FURminator (use weekly during shedding seasons)
3. **Nail care** — Dremel grinder or safety nail clippers

Add clippers only if you intend to do haircuts at home. The upfront cost ($100–$150 for a quality clipper kit) pays back in 1–2 professional groom sessions saved.
