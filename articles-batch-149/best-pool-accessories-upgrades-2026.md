## Frequently Asked Questions

**How often should I test my pool water?**
Test chlorine and pH 2–3 times per week during swim season, daily during heat waves or heavy use. Test alkalinity and calcium hardness monthly. Adjust chemical levels immediately when readings are out of range.

**What's the best way to prevent pool algae?**
Maintain free chlorine between 1–3 ppm, pH between 7.2–7.6, and brush pool walls weekly. Algae thrives in low-chlorine conditions — shock the pool (raise chlorine to 10+ ppm) if you see green tinting on the walls.

**Do I need a cover reel?**
Not required, but strongly recommended. A 20'x40' solar cover weighs 30–40 lbs wet. A reel makes daily deployment and removal possible without effort — which means you'll actually use the cover consistently.
