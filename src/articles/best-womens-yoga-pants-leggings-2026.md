## How to Know If Leggings Are Squat-Proof

**The bend test:** Squat in-store or take a photo in the dressing room. If you can see the pattern of your underwear through the fabric, the legging isn't squat-proof. Most quality leggings include a gusset (triangular panel) at the crotch — this is the clearest sign of thoughtful construction.

**White and light colors:** Squat-proof doesn't necessarily mean opaque in light colors. Test specifically in the color you're buying.

**Seam placement:** Center seams on the back of leggings create visible panty lines. Look for flat-stitched seams placed to the sides.

## Sizing and Inseam Guide

|--------|-------------------|
| Under 5'3" | 23" or 25" |
| 5'3"–5'7" | 25" or 28" |
| Over 5'7" | 28" or 30" |

Many brands now offer petite and tall sizing — check before defaulting to a length that's shortened or bunched.

## How Long Should Good Leggings Last?

Quality leggings (Lululemon, Alo, Colorfulkoala) should maintain shape and opacity for 2–4 years with proper care:

- Wash cold, inside out
- Skip the dryer — hang or lay flat
- No fabric softener (degrades moisture-wicking)
- Wash with similar colors and fabrics (zippers destroy Lycra)
