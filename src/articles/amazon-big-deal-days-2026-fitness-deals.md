## Big Deal Days Fitness Shopping Strategy

**1. Equipment ships slow.** Large cardio equipment takes 1–2 weeks to ship and may require scheduling delivery. Ordering in October means having it set up by November rather than waiting until late December or January.

**2. Assembly services are available.** Amazon partners (HelloTech, TaskRabbit) offer treadmill and bike assembly services. If you're not comfortable assembling a treadmill, budget $100–$150 for professional setup.

**3. iFIT/Echelon subscriptions compound.** If you buy NordicTrack or Echelon equipment, factor in the $44/month subscription cost. The first 1–3 months are usually included free. After that, annual pre-pay is typically cheaper than monthly.

**4. Test the warranty return window.** Most large fitness equipment has a 30-day return window. Use the trial period — there's no substitute for actually using a treadmill at home to know if you like it.
