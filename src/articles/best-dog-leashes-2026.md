## What to Avoid

**Cheap retractable leashes:** Plastic lock mechanisms fail. Cord can snap under 50+ lbs of force. Cord burns when grabbed.

**Thin nylon with plastic snaps:** Plastic clips fail with consistent pulling. Go metal for dogs over 30 lbs.

**Traffic handles at the end:** Leashes with handles only at the far end give no close-control option when you need to shorten the leash fast.
