## What to Look for in a Standing Desk

**Height Range:** Your sitting height = elbow level when forearms parallel to floor. Your standing height = same measurement while standing. Most desks cover both, but verify at your specific height before buying.

**Load Capacity:** A 27" monitor weighs ~15 lbs. A 32" ultrawide weighs ~25 lbs. Add your PC (4–8 lbs), monitor arm (4 lbs), and accessories. A two-monitor setup needs at least 80 lbs of rated capacity; budget 2× the calculated load for stability.

**Motor Type:** Single motor handles desktops up to 60" wide under standard load. Dual motor is recommended for 70"+ desktops, heavy equipment, or frequent height adjustments throughout the day.

**Desktop Size:** 48"×30" is the minimum for a comfortable single-monitor setup with keyboard and accessories. 60"×30" is the sweet spot for most home office users. 72"×30" fits dual 27" monitors side-by-side with room for a notebook.
