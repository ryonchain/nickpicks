## What Can You Clean with an Electric Spin Scrubber?

**Bathroom:** Tile grout, tub surrounds, shower doors (with appropriate brush), toilet exterior, sink basins

**Kitchen:** Stovetop grates, grout between backsplash tiles, oven interior walls, sink basin

**Outdoors:** Patio furniture, grill exteriors, garden pots

**Vehicle:** Wheels, interior carpet spots, door jambs

**Caution:** Avoid using stiff brushes on acrylic tubs, fiberglass showers, or polished chrome — these surfaces scratch easily. Use soft dome brushes on delicate materials.

## How to Choose the Right Brush Head

| Surface | Recommended Head |
|---------|-----------------|
| Tile grout | Stiff corner or pointed brush |
| Flat tile | Flat disc brush |
| Tub/shower surround | Medium dome brush |
| Toilet exterior | Corner brush |
| Stovetop | Stiff flat disc |
| Acrylic surfaces | Soft dome brush |
