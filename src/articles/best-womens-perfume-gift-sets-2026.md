## What Makes a Great Women's Perfume Gift Set

### The Concentration Hierarchy
- **Eau de Cologne (EDC):** Lightest, best for sensitive skin or summer
- **Eau de Toilette (EDT):** 5–15% concentration; most versatile, ideal for daytime
- **Eau de Parfum (EDP):** 15–20%; richer projection, longer wear, best for fall/winter
- **Parfum/Extrait:** 20%+; most intense, a few drops last all day

Premium gift sets almost always lead with EDP formulas. If a set uses "EDT" to justify a lower price on a luxury brand, check whether the flanker formula is weaker than the original.

### Fragrance Family Guide for Gifting
- **Floral:** Rose, jasmine, peony. The safest bet—universally appreciated
- **Gourmand:** Vanilla, caramel, praline. Warm, comfort-forward; polarizing but loved by devotees
- **Fresh/Citrus:** Bergamot, grapefruit, green tea. Clean, office-appropriate, universally wearable
- **Oriental/Amber:** Amber, musk, sandalwood. Rich, sensual, evening-forward
- **Chypre:** Moss, labdanum, citrus. Sophisticated, complex, for confident fragrance wearers

### Gift Set Composition to Look For
- Lead fragrance in full-size (50ml minimum)
- Matching body lotion or shower gel
- Travel spray or rollerbottle for touch-ups
- Premium outer packaging (gift-ready without rewrapping)
