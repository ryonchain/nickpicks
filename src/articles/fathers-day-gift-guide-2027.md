## Experience Gifts for Dad

- **Golf Lesson or Round at a New Course** — A 9-hole round at a bucket-list course he hasn't played. $60–$200.
- **Fishing Charter** — Half-day guided charter fishing trip. Most coastal areas: $100–$150/person.
- **Distillery or Brewery Tour** — VIP tasting and behind-the-scenes tour. $50–$100.
- **Racing Experience** — Drive a sports car on a real track. Many cities have events. $100–$500.
- **Concert or Sporting Event** — His team, his band, his call. Use StubHub or Ticketmaster.
