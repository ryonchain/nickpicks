# Best Spirulina Supplements of 2026: Superfood Green Powder and Capsule Picks

Three grams of spirulina contains more iron than 100 grams of spinach. The clinical evidence supports benefits for cholesterol improvement, blood pressure reduction, anti-inflammatory effects via phycocyanin, and anemia prevention. The main quality concern is heavy metal contamination from poorly controlled growing environments — making third-party testing essential for this category.
