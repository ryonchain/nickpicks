## The Golden Rule for Last-Minute Amazon Gifts

**Always check the delivery estimate before adding to cart.** Amazon's same-day and next-day estimates change by ZIP code, time of day, and stock level. What says "arrives today" at 9am may say "arrives in 3 days" at noon.

Look for the **"FREE Same-Day Delivery"** or **"FREE One-Day Delivery"** banner on the product page. If you see "2-day delivery," that's not fast enough — keep looking.

## How to Guarantee Same-Day Delivery

1. **Order before noon local time.** Most Amazon same-day windows close around 12pm.
2. **Check the delivery estimate before adding to cart.** The product detail page shows your estimated delivery date after you enter your address.
3. **Look for "Sold by Amazon.com"** — third-party sellers rarely offer same-day.
4. **Have your Prime membership active.** Same-day delivery requires Prime.
5. **Urban vs. suburban:** Same-day is almost always available in major US metros. Suburban and rural addresses typically show 1–2 day delivery.
