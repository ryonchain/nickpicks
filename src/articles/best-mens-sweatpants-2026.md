## Men's Sweatpants Buying Guide

**Fit options:**
- **Tapered/jogger:** Narrow ankle cuff. The modern standard — looks intentional as an outfit.
- **Slim tapered:** More aggressive taper. Best for lean builds.
- **Relaxed:** Loose throughout. Best for pure comfort at home.
- **Straight leg:** No taper — old-school sweatpant look. Coming back in streetwear.

**Fabric options:**
- **Standard fleece:** Warmth and softness. Most common. Best for home/casual.
- **French terry:** Lighter than fleece, slightly textured. Good for mild weather.
- **Tech/moisture-wicking:** Best for gym and outdoor activity.
- **Heavyweight cotton:** Most durable. Takes time to break in.

**Fit and style:**
- Tapered sweatpants look best with fitted tops (tee, fitted hoodie) and clean sneakers
- Relaxed fit works with oversized tops as a streetwear aesthetic
- Dark sweatpants (black, charcoal) are more versatile for outings vs. grey for home
