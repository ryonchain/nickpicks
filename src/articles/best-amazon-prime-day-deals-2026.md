## Prime Day Shopping Strategy

**What to do:**
1. Check prices before Prime Day using CamelCamelCamel.com — this shows price history so you know if the "deal" is real
2. Add items to your Cart or Wish List before Prime Day — easier to track and compare
3. Check Lightning Deals every 2 hours — they change throughout the day
4. Use the Amazon Shopping app for Deal Alert notifications

**What to avoid:**
- Products without verifiable price history discounts
- Lightning Deals on no-name brands with recent price inflation
- "Amazon's Choice" labels — these aren't editorial endorsements
- Buying things you don't need just because they're discounted

**Best categories (deepest genuine discounts):**
- Amazon branded devices (Echo, Fire, Kindle, Ring, Eero)
- Robot vacuums
- Kitchen appliances from name brands
- True wireless earbuds (Sony, Bose, Beats, Samsung)
- Smart home accessories
