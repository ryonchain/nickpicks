### #2 Spikeball 3-Ball Kit — Best Competitive Lawn Game

**[Check price on Amazon →](https://www.amazon.com/s?k=spikeball+3+ball+kit&tag=nickniche-20){rel="nofollow sponsored" target="_blank"}**

Spikeball is the fastest-growing competitive lawn game in North America. Four players form two teams, rally a ball off a circular net in the middle, and try to make it impossible for the other team to return. It's played on any surface (grass, beach, gym floor), requires genuine athleticism, and is the best "we need to actually move" option on this list.

The 3-ball kit includes the net, 3 balls (one of which you'll inevitably lose), and a carrying bag. Works equally well for casual summer play and competitive league play (yes, competitive Spikeball leagues exist in most major cities).

### #3 Kan Jam Original Set — Best Fast Setup

**[Check price on Amazon →](https://www.amazon.com/s?k=kan+jam+original+disc+slam&tag=nickniche-20){rel="nofollow sponsored" target="_blank"}**

Kan Jam requires zero setup beyond placing two cans 50 feet apart. Four players (two teams of two) throw a disc — one teammate deflects it into a can for points. Landing it directly in the can slot is an instant win. High energy, no downtime, 30-minute games.

Best game for beach groups where setup simplicity matters. The can disassembles and fits in a bag smaller than a cornhole board.

### #5 Bocce Ball Set — Best for All Ages

**[Check price on Amazon →](https://www.amazon.com/s?k=bocce+ball+set+resin&tag=nickniche-20){rel="nofollow sponsored" target="_blank"}**

Bocce is played by 2–8 players, requires almost no physical ability, works on any surface, and keeps retirees, kids, and anyone in between equally engaged. Roll balls as close to the pallino (small white target ball) as possible. Simple scoring, strategic play, deeply satisfying.

A resin bocce set from Baden or GoSports (~$40–60) lasts years and works better on uneven terrain than cheap plastic sets.
