## Building Your RGB Desk Setup

**Layer your lighting.** The most effective setups combine three layers: bias lighting behind the monitor (strips), desk/under-shelf accent (rope or strips), and room fill (smart bulbs or floor lamp). Each layer adds depth without competing.

**Match your ecosystem.** If you plan to expand, pick one app ecosystem and stick with it: Govee, Philips Hue, or Nanoleaf. Cross-brand control is possible through Alexa or Google Home but loses advanced features like screen sync.

**Start with bias lighting.** If you only buy one RGB product, make it a backlight strip for your monitor. The eye strain reduction benefit is real and immediate, and the visual effect looks professional on stream.

**Color temperature for streaming:** Keep main room illumination at 4000–5500K (neutral to daylight) when streaming on camera — it reads accurately on webcam. Use warmer RGB colors (2700–3000K) for ambient accents that don't wash out your face lighting.
