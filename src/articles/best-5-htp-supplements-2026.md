## Safety and Drug Interactions — Critical Read

**Absolute contraindications:**
- SSRIs (sertraline, fluoxetine, escitalopram, etc.) — risk of serotonin syndrome
- SNRIs (venlafaxine, duloxetine) — same risk
- MAOIs (phenelzine, selegiline) — serious interaction
- Tramadol, meperidine, or other opioids with serotonergic activity
- Dextromethorphan (in many OTC cold medicines)

**Do not combine with:** St. John's Wort, SAMe, or any other serotonin-supporting supplements without medical supervision.

**Symptoms of serotonin syndrome:** Rapid heartbeat, high blood pressure, dilated pupils, muscle twitching, agitation, confusion, diarrhea. Seek immediate medical attention.

**For mood disorders:** 5-HTP is a supplement, not a replacement for prescription antidepressants. If you're managing clinical depression or anxiety disorder, consult your prescriber before starting.

**Who can benefit safely:**
- People with mild-to-moderate sleep difficulties not on serotonergic medications
- Those seeking appetite support for weight management
- Individuals with mild mood fluctuations not currently on psychiatric medications
