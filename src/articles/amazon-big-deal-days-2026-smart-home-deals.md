## Smart Home Big Deal Days Strategy

**1. Echo devices are always the best deal.** Amazon prices Echo hardware at a loss during its own events. If you're building an Alexa home, Big Deal Days is better than Black Friday for Echo devices — same or better pricing.

**2. Multi-room lighting pays off.** The Philips Hue starter kit on Big Deal Days is a genuine investment — the ecosystem grows over time and the bulbs last 25,000 hours. Buy the bridge + 3 bulbs now, add to it gradually.

**3. Ring subscriptions compound.** Ring cameras are nearly free (in the $10–$15 range on deep sale) but require Protect to see video history. Factor $5/month ($60/year) into your Ring camera decision.

**4. Smart plugs are the best entry point.** A 4-pack of Kasa smart plugs at $20 converts any lamp or fan into a smart device. The lowest-cost way to start a smart home.
