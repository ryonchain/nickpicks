## Humidifier Types Explained

**Ultrasonic:** Uses high-frequency vibrations to create a cool mist. Very quiet and energy-efficient. Risk: if not cleaned regularly, can spread bacteria and minerals (white dust) into the air.

**Evaporative:** Fan blows through a wet wick. Self-regulating, no white dust, but requires regular filter replacement. The healthiest type for daily use.

**Warm Mist (Steam Vaporizer):** Boils water to create steam. Sterile output (heat kills bacteria) but uses more energy and the heating element requires cleaning.

**Humidistat:** Always get a humidifier with a built-in humidistat (automatic shut-off when target humidity is reached). Without it, you risk over-humidification — which encourages mold growth.
