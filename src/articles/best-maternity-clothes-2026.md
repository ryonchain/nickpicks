## What to Look For in Maternity Clothes

- **Over-bump vs. under-bump panels** — Over-bump stays in place better in the third trimester; under-bump works better for early pregnancy
- **Stretchy waistbands** — Avoid rigid waistbands entirely. Look for wide ruched panels
- **Fabric**: Jersey and modal stretch comfortably; avoid tight synthetics
- **Postpartum wearability**: The best maternity pieces work for 6–12 months postpartum too

## Sizing Guide for Maternity Clothes

| Pre-Pregnancy Size | Maternity Size |
|-------------------|----------------|
| XS–S | Small |
| M | Medium |
| L | Large |
| XL | XL or 1X |

Most maternity brands size to pre-pregnancy weight. Buy your normal size — the panels accommodate the bump.

**When to start shopping:** Around week 12–14. By week 16, most women find regular pants uncomfortable. Having leggings and a few loose tops ready before that point makes the transition easier.

## What to Skip in Maternity Clothing

- **Maternity-specific swimwear** — Regular swimwear in a size up works better and lasts postpartum
- **Maternity formal gowns** — Unless you have a formal event, a wrap dress is more versatile
- **Trendy statement pieces** — You'll wear maternity clothes for ~6 months; basics outlast trends
