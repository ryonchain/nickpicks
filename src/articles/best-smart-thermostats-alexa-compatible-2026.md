## Alexa Thermostat Buying Guide

### Setting Up Alexa with Your Thermostat

**Amazon Smart Thermostat:** Setup happens entirely in the Alexa app. No additional account or skill needed — discover devices and it appears.

**Ecobee:** Create an Ecobee account, then enable the Ecobee skill in Alexa. Discovery finds your thermostat automatically.

**Nest:** Create a Google/Nest account, enable the Nest skill in Alexa, authorize your Google account. This is the most steps of any thermostat but works reliably once done.

**Honeywell:** Create a Honeywell Home account, enable the Honeywell Home skill in Alexa.

### Alexa Routines with Thermostats

All of these thermostats can be included in Alexa routines. Useful routine ideas:

- **"Alexa, good morning"** → set temperature to 72°F, turn on lights
- **"Alexa, I'm leaving"** → eco mode, lock doors
- **"Alexa, bedtime"** → set temperature to 68°F, turn off lights
- **Auto-geofence** → Alexa detects your phone left home and switches thermostat to away mode (Amazon Smart Thermostat only for deepest integration)

### Does Alexa Work When Internet Is Down?

If your home internet goes down, Alexa voice commands to your thermostat stop working. The thermostat continues to operate on its last-set schedule. Direct thermostat controls (touching the display) always work regardless of connectivity.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Which thermostat has the best Alexa integration?**
The Amazon Smart Thermostat has the deepest native Alexa integration. The Ecobee SmartThermostat Premium has Alexa built-in as a speaker and is the best overall premium Alexa thermostat.

**Can I use a smart thermostat with Alexa without paying for Prime?**
Yes. Alexa device control does not require Amazon Prime. The Amazon Smart Thermostat and Alexa app are free to use for thermostat control.

**Do Alexa thermostat commands work when I'm away from home?**
Yes, as long as your thermostat and Alexa device have internet connectivity. You can set your home temperature from anywhere using the Alexa app on your phone.

**Can Alexa control my thermostat automatically based on presence?**
The Amazon Smart Thermostat supports Alexa Hunches and Alexa's built-in routines based on your presence (phone location). Other thermostats use their own geofencing apps.

**Will my Alexa thermostat work if I switch to Google Home later?**
All of the thermostats listed here (except the Amazon Smart Thermostat) work with both Alexa and Google Home. If you switch ecosystems, Ecobee, Nest, and Honeywell all support both.
