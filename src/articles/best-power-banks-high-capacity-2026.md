## Buyer's Guide: Choosing the Right Power Bank

### How Much Capacity Do You Need?

**Quick reference (real-world charges, accounting for 20% efficiency loss):**
- 10,000 mAh → ~2× iPhone 15 / ~1.5× Samsung S24
- 20,000 mAh → ~4× iPhone 15 / ~3× Samsung S24
- 25,000 mAh → ~5× iPhone 15 / ~4× Samsung S24

**For day trips:** 10,000 mAh is usually enough.
**For multi-day trips/camping:** 20,000–25,000 mAh.
**For laptop charging:** 26,800+ mAh with 65W+ output.

### Airline Rules

The FAA limits power banks to 100Wh for checked and carry-on luggage without approval, and 160Wh with airline approval. Most 20,000–30,000mAh lithium power banks fall in the 72–108Wh range — verify your specific model's Wh rating before flying.

### Fast Charging: How Much Do You Need?

- **15W output:** Adequate for overnight or slow charging. Fine for phones that don't support fast charge.
- **30W output:** Standard fast charging for most modern phones.
- **65W output:** Fast charges phones + thin laptops (MacBook Air, Dell XPS 13).
- **100W+ output:** Charges gaming laptops and MacBook Pro at meaningful speed.

### PD (Power Delivery) vs. QC (Quick Charge)

- **USB Power Delivery (PD):** Universal standard. Works with iPhone, Android, MacBook, iPad, Switch.
- **Qualcomm Quick Charge (QC):** Android-specific. Works on Qualcomm-chipped phones.
- Most modern power banks support both — check for "PD" in specs if you have an iPhone or MacBook.

## FAQ

**How long does a 20,000mAh power bank take to recharge itself?**
At 30W input: ~3.5–4 hours. At 15W input: ~7–8 hours. Power banks recharge faster when you buy one with high-wattage input (look for "30W+ input" in specs).

**Are off-brand power banks safe?**
Quality varies widely. Stick to reputable brands (Anker, Baseus, INIU, Mophie). Cheap generic power banks often overstate capacity by 20–40% and skip thermal protection that prevents overheating.

**Can you bring a 20,000mAh power bank on a plane?**
Most 20,000mAh banks are under 100Wh and are allowed in carry-on (not checked) bags. Never put lithium batteries in checked luggage.
