- USB-C data port
- SD and MicroSD card readers
- 100W pass-through charging (plug your charger into the hub, not the laptop)

The single most useful laptop purchase after the machine itself, for any MacBook Air or Windows ultrabook with USB-C only.

## What You Don't Need to Buy

- **Expensive laptop case:** A $12 neoprene sleeve does the same thing as a $60 leather sleeve
- **Wireless mouse:** The trackpad on most laptops is fine; buy a mouse only if you genuinely prefer it
- **Keyboard:** Only relevant if you're at a desk all day — your laptop keyboard is fine for class use
- **Multiple monitors for college:** Helpful for productivity but not essential for most students; upgrade if you find yourself wanting it, don't buy speculatively
- **Smart notebook (Rocketbook):** Useful if you handwrite and scan notes obsessively — not needed if you type everything or just take photos of handwritten notes with your phone
