## Buying Guide: Easter Gift Tips

**Skip the dollar store filler:** It breaks by Easter afternoon and teaches kids that Easter is about quantity, not quality.

**Buy one meaningful thing:** A Polaroid camera, a science kit, or a Magna-Tiles set makes a bigger impression than 12 small things that lose novelty in an hour.

**Spring-theme when possible:** Garden kits, outdoor water toys, sidewalk chalk, and nature exploration sets tie the gift to the season.

**Candy is still fine:** Just don't let it be the whole basket. One good chocolate egg + one real gift is better than candy soup.
