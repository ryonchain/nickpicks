## Smart Pet Door Buying Guide

**Microchip vs RFID collar:** Microchip doors (SureFlap) work with your pet's existing implant — no collar needed. RFID collar doors (PetSafe SmartDoor) require a special collar tag but support larger door sizes for big dogs.

**Installation type:**
| Installation | Best For |
|-------------|---------|
| Wall mount | Permanent, most common |
| Sliding panel | Renters, no cutting |
| Door insert | Hollow-core door, easier cut |
| Screen insert | Summer screen door use |

**Sizing:** Measure your pet from the top of their back to the floor, and from shoulder to shoulder. Add 2 inches to each dimension for the minimum door opening size.

**Security note:** Smart pet doors prevent wildlife from entering but are not burglary-deterrent. A determined human can remove most pet door panels.
