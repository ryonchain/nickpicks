The electric lawn equipment market has grown dramatically — and EGO and GreenWorks are the two brands homeowners compare most. Both eliminate gas, oil changes, and pull-cord frustration. But they're priced and positioned very differently.

## The Core Difference

**EGO** = premium cordless lawn tools. 56V ARC Lithium batteries, commercial-grade build quality, top-tier power output. Higher price, longer-lasting tools, broader product ecosystem.

**GreenWorks** = value cordless lawn tools. 40V, 60V, and 80V platforms. Lower price point, good performance for typical residential yards, more accessible for budget-conscious buyers.

**For large yards (1+ acre) or heavy use:** EGO.
**For small to medium yards (under 1/2 acre) or light seasonal use:** GreenWorks.

## Head-to-Head by Tool Category

### Lawn Mowers

| | EGO LM2150SP | GreenWorks 40V 21" |
|---|---|---|
| **Voltage** | 56V | 40V |
| **Battery** | 7.5Ah (included) | 6Ah (included) |
| **Runtime** | ~60 min | ~45 min |
| **Deck** | Steel 21" | Steel 21" |
| **Self-propelled** | Yes | Yes |
| **Price** | ~$550 | ~$380 |

**Winner: EGO** (more runtime, higher voltage), but GreenWorks is excellent value for smaller lawns.

### Leaf Blowers

EGO LB6004 (600 CFM) vs GreenWorks GBL80300 (500 CFM). EGO wins on air volume; GreenWorks wins on price for comparable performance. For light to moderate leaf clearing, GreenWorks is sufficient. For heavy leaf loads or wet leaves, EGO.

**Winner: EGO** (power), GreenWorks (value)

### String Trimmers

Both brands offer capable trimmers. EGO ST1511T has a 15-inch head with carbon fiber shaft for durability. GreenWorks 40V trimmer at 13-inch is lighter. Casual users won't notice the difference; heavy users will appreciate EGO's build.

**Winner: EGO** (durability), GreenWorks (weight and cost)

### Battery Ecosystem

EGO 56V batteries are interchangeable across all EGO tools. GreenWorks splits across 40V and 80V platforms — you need different batteries for different tools. EGO's unified ecosystem is more convenient.

**Winner: EGO**

**Buy EGO if:**
