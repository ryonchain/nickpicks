## Kids' Toys — What to Expect by Category

### LEGO — Rare Discounts, Worth Any Deal

LEGO is notorious for not going on sale. They protect their pricing more aggressively than almost any other toy brand. Any BF LEGO discount is genuine value.

**When LEGO does discount at BF:**
- Retiring sets (sets being discontinued) see price protection lifted — check brickset.com for retiring sets before BF
- Amazon runs occasional LEGO deals during their BF sale, sometimes 15–20% off select sets
- Target Circle offers 20% off LEGO purchases as an occasional member-exclusive deal — watch for this

**Best LEGO BF targets:**
- Large Creator Expert or Architecture sets (higher MSRP = larger absolute savings)
- Technic sets (these occasionally get meaningful discounts)
- Retiring Harry Potter or Star Wars sets

## Black Friday Baby & Kids Shopping Strategy

### For Baby Gear (High Priority)

1. **Buy early in BF week** — car seats and strollers at BF prices sell out before Black Friday itself
2. **Check Buy Buy Baby** (now available at Bed Bath & Beyond) alongside Amazon — they run competing BF sales
3. **Register or add to registry** — retailers sometimes give registry completion discounts that stack with BF pricing
4. **Car seat safety note:** Never buy a used car seat (BF or otherwise). Unknown crash history, potentially expired. Only buy new.

### For Toys

1. **Toy deals sell out** — popular toys at deep BF discounts can sell out within hours on Amazon. Have alternatives ready.
2. **Price compare across retailers** — the same toy at Target might be $5–10 cheaper than Amazon on BF
3. **LEGO watch list:** Set up CamelCamelCamel alerts on specific LEGO sets — any hit to your target price is worth acting on, BF or not
4. **Age-appropriate, not impressive-looking:** The best toy gift is one matched to the child's actual interests, not the one with the most features
