## Frequently Asked Questions

**Is KitchenAid worth the price?**
For most home bakers, yes. The build quality, attachment ecosystem, and resale value make it a worthwhile long-term investment.

**What's the difference between tilt-head and bowl-lift?**
Tilt-head (Artisan) tilts the motor head up to remove the bowl. Bowl-lift (Professional 600) raises the bowl up to the head. Bowl-lift is more stable for heavy mixing.

**Can I use a stand mixer for bread?**
Yes, with the dough hook. KitchenAid handles average bread doughs; the Bosch handles heavy, high-hydration doughs better.
