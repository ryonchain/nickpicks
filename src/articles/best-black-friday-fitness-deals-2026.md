## Black Friday Fitness Shopping Strategy

**1. Equipment ships slow.** Home gym equipment takes 1–2 weeks to ship. Order as early as possible — if you want it for New Year's workouts, buy by December 1.

**2. Floor model alerts.** Local fitness retailers (Dick's, Academy Sports) offer floor model discounts on Black Friday that are 40–60% off. Worth checking if you're willing to transport.

**3. Verify warranty transfers.** NordicTrack and Bowflex include warranty — confirm terms before purchase.

**4. Bundle the accessories.** Buy resistance bands, yoga mat, and foam roller together — shipping is often combined and saves.

Start your home gym Black Friday list now. Equipment at these prices won't last past December 1.
