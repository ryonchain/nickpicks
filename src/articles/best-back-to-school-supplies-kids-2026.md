## What to Skip or Buy Cheap

**Items not worth spending more on:**
- **Lined paper** — any brand, any price. Paper is paper.
- **Regular pencils (bulk)** — generic pencils work fine for classroom pool supplies
- **Basic folders** — the cheap plastic ones survive fine
- **Tape** — Scotch tape is worth the name; generic adhesive is indistinguishable

**Items worth paying more for:**
- **Pencil case** — a flimsy pencil case means lost supplies all year
- **Backpack** — see our [kids' backpack guide](https://amazon.com/s?k=kids+backpack+elementary+school+2026&tag=nickpicks-20)
- **Scientific calculator** — the TI-30X IIS lasts through middle school and beyond; don't buy a $5 calculator
- **Colored pencils** — if your child does any art or visual projects, quality pencils matter
