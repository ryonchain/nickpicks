## Collar Fit Guide

- **Measure:** Use a soft tape measure around your dog's neck where they'd wear the collar — not too tight, not too loose.
- **Two-finger rule:** You should be able to slip two fingers between the collar and the neck easily.
- **Buckle type:** Metal buckles for large/strong dogs. Plastic quick-release acceptable for small dogs.
- **Replace when:** Fraying, cracking, or the buckle fails to click solidly.
