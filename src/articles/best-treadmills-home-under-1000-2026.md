## What to Look For

**Motor (CHP):** For walkers, 2.0 CHP is enough. Joggers need 2.5 CHP+. Runners should look for 3.0 CHP to avoid burnout.

**Deck length:** 50 inches for walkers, 55+ inches for runners with longer strides.

**Noise:** DC motors are quieter than AC. Cushioned decks also dampen impact sound.

**Weight capacity:** Always add 20–30 lbs buffer above your actual weight.

**Fold mechanism:** Hydraulic-assist fold is worth it — freefall folds are a safety risk.
