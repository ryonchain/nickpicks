## What Makes a Great Women's Athletic Short

Workout shorts are where fabric technology and fit engineering matter in tangible, sweat-session ways. A poorly designed pair rides up during lunges, chafes in the inner thigh during runs, or loses its shape after three washes. The best pairs disappear into your workout — you stop thinking about them, which is the entire goal.

**Fabric** is the most important variable. Polyester-spandex blends dominate because they wick sweat efficiently, stretch in all directions, and hold their shape. The spandex percentage matters: 15–20% means recovery stretch; below 10% means the shorts may bag during movement. Nylon-spandex blends (as in Lululemon's Luon and Nulu fabrics) tend to be softer but less common at lower price points.

**Liner vs. no liner.** Running shorts typically include a built-in brief liner for support. Training and HIIT shorts may or may not. If you run without leggings, a liner is important. For lifting, yoga, or studio classes, unlined shorts are often preferred.

**Inseam length** varies from 2.5" (the Hotty Hot's most popular length) to 5" (more modest for box jumps and HIIT). Longer inseams reduce ride-up during dynamic movements.

**What to look for:**
- Minimum 80% polyester or nylon for moisture wicking
- 15%+ spandex for four-way stretch
- Flat-lock seams to prevent chafing
- Secure waistband (drawstring or wide elastic band with grip)
- Appropriate inseam for your workout type

## FAQ

**Do workout shorts shrink in the wash?**
Polyester-spandex blends are dimensionally stable and rarely shrink. Wash on cold or warm (not hot) and avoid high-heat drying to preserve elasticity over time.

**How do I prevent inner-thigh chafing?**
Choose shorts with flat-lock inner seams (Nike's Dri-FIT One has these). Anti-chafe balm applied to the inner thigh before long runs is an additional preventive measure.

**Should I size up or down in athletic shorts?**
Start with your normal size. Nike runs slightly large — size down if between sizes. Lululemon's fit is true to size but the Hotty Hot is cut close; try on or order two sizes to compare. Under Armour runs true to size with an athletic cut (slimmer through the hips).

**What's the best way to wash athletic shorts?**
Cold water wash, delicate or activewear cycle, no fabric softener (it damages moisture-wicking technology), air dry or tumble dry on low. Fabric softener is the single biggest enemy of wicking performance — avoid it entirely for workout clothes.

**Can I wear athletic shorts for activities beyond the gym?**
Absolutely. The Lululemon Hotty Hot and Nike Dri-FIT are popular for casual summer wear, beach days, and brunch — not just workouts. Athletic shorts have fully crossed over into everyday lifestyle wear.
