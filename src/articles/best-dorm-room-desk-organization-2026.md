**Q: What dorm desk organization products have the most impact?**
Cable management first — hiding the power strip eliminates 80% of visible clutter. A monitor riser second, for ergonomics and hidden storage underneath.

**Q: Can I use adhesive products in a dorm room?**
Most dorms allow removable adhesive products like Command Strips and cable clips. Check your dorm's policy. Nailing or drilling is almost universally prohibited.

**Q: Is a second monitor worth it in a dorm room?**
Yes for CS, engineering, or research-heavy majors. Dual screens meaningfully reduce switching between apps and accelerate writing, coding, and research.

**Q: What is the best way to manage cables on a small desk?**
Three steps: (1) Cable management box for the power strip. (2) Adhesive cable clips routing cables along desk edges. (3) Velcro ties bundling cables that travel together.
