## What to Look For

**CADR rating:** Higher is better. Match CADR to your room size (CADR × 1.5 = recommended room area in sq ft).

**True HEPA vs. HEPA-type:** Only "True HEPA" or "H13 HEPA" guarantees 99.97% filtration. "HEPA-type" is unregulated marketing.

**Noise level (dB):** Under 30 dB is bedroom-appropriate. Under 20 dB is nearly silent.

**Replacement filter cost:** Factor in annual cost. Some purifiers look cheap but filters cost $60+/year.

**Ionizers:** Optional. They help eliminate bacteria and odors but generate trace ozone. Turn off if you have respiratory sensitivity.
