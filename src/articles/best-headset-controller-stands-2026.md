## Gaming Desk Organization Tips

**Position stands at monitor edges.** Headset stands sit naturally at the right or left side of your monitor — out of the main workspace but within easy reach. Controller stands belong near the desk edge for console grab-and-go access.

**Use cable ties at the stand base.** Most combo stands have a cable slot — run controller charging cables through it and tie-wrap excess length. Your desk surface stays clear.

**Buy matching finishes.** Mixing matte black, brushed aluminum, and glossy plastic stands creates visual clutter. Stick to one material across your desk accessories for a cohesive setup look.

**Store headsets stored, not worn.** Hanging your headset on a stand when not in use protects the headband from stretching and keeps it off the desk surface where it's more likely to be knocked or stepped on.
