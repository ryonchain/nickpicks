## What 2-Year-Olds Actually Need From Gifts

At age 2, the best gifts support:
- **Pretend play** — kitchens, tool sets, doctor kits
- **Gross motor development** — ride-ons, push toys, outdoor equipment
- **Fine motor development** — puzzles, building blocks, art supplies
- **Sensory exploration** — sand, water, paint, playdough

Skip: electronic toys with screens, anything requiring complex instructions, small parts.
