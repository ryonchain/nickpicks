## What to Avoid

- **Bags with vague leather descriptions** — "faux leather," "PU leather," or no leather mention at all signal fast-fashion materials that peel and crack within a year.
- **Lightweight hardware** — if the zippers and clasps feel hollow or plastic-adjacent, the bag will look cheap fast.
- **Unbranded "designer-inspired" bags** — at this price point, you can buy actual Kate Spade, Coach, or Fossil. There's no reason to settle for a lookalike.
- **Single-color interior lining** — quality bags have contrast or patterned linings that hide wear; plain dark interiors can mask poor construction.
