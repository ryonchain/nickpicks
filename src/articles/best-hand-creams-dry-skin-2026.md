## What Dermatologists Say to Look For

The best hand creams for dry skin contain a blend of: **humectants** (draw moisture in — look for glycerin, hyaluronic acid), **emollients** (fill cracks and smooth texture — shea butter, jojoba oil), and **occlusives** (seal moisture in — petrolatum, dimethicone). Fragrance-free formulas are strongly preferred by dermatologists for sensitive skin.
