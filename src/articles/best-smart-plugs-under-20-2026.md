## The Easiest Smart Home Upgrade You'll Make

Smart plugs turn any standard outlet into a Wi-Fi-controlled outlet in about 5 minutes. Schedule your coffee maker to start at 6:45 AM, have your floor lamp turn off automatically at bedtime, or check whether you left the iron on from an airport lounge. All for under $20.

The market is crowded with options. These five represent the best per-dollar picks in 2026 based on reliability, app quality, and ecosystem compatibility.

---

## What to Look for in a Smart Plug

**Ecosystem compatibility:** The plug needs to work with your voice assistant (Alexa, Google Assistant, or Siri/HomeKit). Check before buying — not all plugs support all platforms.

**Energy monitoring:** Worth having if you want to track device electricity usage. Not included in every budget plug.

**Multi-pack value:** Single smart plugs often cost $12–$15 each. Multi-packs frequently drop the per-plug price to $6–$10, which is significant when outfitting multiple rooms.

**App quality:** A buggy app undermines the whole experience. Kasa and Eve have the best-rated apps in this category consistently.
