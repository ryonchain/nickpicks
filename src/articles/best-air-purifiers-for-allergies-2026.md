## Buying Guide: What Actually Matters

### CADR (Clean Air Delivery Rate)
CADR is the industry standard measurement for how quickly an air purifier cleans a room. Match CADR to your room size:
- **100–200 CFM:** Bedrooms up to 500 sq ft
- **200–300 CFM:** Living rooms 500–800 sq ft
- **300+ CFM:** Open-plan spaces 800+ sq ft

Rule of thumb: CADR should equal at least ⅔ of the room's square footage.

### True HEPA vs. "HEPA-type"
Only **True HEPA** filters meet the HEPA standard of removing 99.97% of particles 0.3 microns. "HEPA-type," "HEPA-like," and "99% HEPA" filters are marketing terms that fall short. Every model we recommend uses True HEPA.

### Activated Carbon Filter
Essential for smoke, pet odors, and VOCs. If you have pets, cook frequently, or live near a highway, make sure your purifier includes an activated carbon layer.

### Room Coverage Claims
Manufacturers often use single-air-change-per-hour (1× ACH) to calculate room coverage. For allergy relief, you want 4–5× ACH. Divide the manufacturer's room size claim by 4 to get the real effective coverage area.

### Noise Level
- **Below 25 dB:** Nearly silent, suitable for sleeping
- **25–40 dB:** Library-quiet, fine for offices and bedrooms
- **40–55 dB:** Noticeable but manageable
- **55+ dB:** Loud, best for daytime only

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Do air purifiers help with pet allergies?**
Yes. True HEPA filters capture pet dander particles (2.5–10 microns) with 99.97% efficiency. The Coway Airmega 400S and iRobot Roomba j9+ combo (purifier + robot vacuum) is our recommended one-two punch for pet-allergy households.

**Can one air purifier work for a whole house?**
One unit works well for a single open-plan space. For multi-room coverage, you need a unit in each major living area and bedroom. Budget one mid-size unit per 600–800 sq ft of frequently occupied space.

**How often do filters need replacing?**
True HEPA filters typically last 12 months at 8–12 hours/day use. Carbon filters may need replacement every 6 months if odor control is heavy. All our recommended models have filter life indicators.

**Do air purifiers help with COVID and viruses?**
True HEPA filters capture viral particles carried on respiratory aerosols (0.5–5 microns). In a well-sealed room, a high-CADR purifier running continuously can reduce viral load. The IQAir HealthPro Plus's HyperHEPA is the most effective for viral particles.
