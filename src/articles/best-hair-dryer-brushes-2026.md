## What Is a Hair Dryer Brush?

A hot air brush (also called a blow dry brush or volumizing brush) combines a paddle brush with a built-in blow dryer. The rotating barrel and airflow simultaneously dry and style your hair — no separate tools needed. Most users cut their morning routine by 10–20 minutes.

### Barrel Size Guide

- **Small barrel (1–1.5"):** Tight waves, defined curls, fine hair
- **Medium barrel (1.5–2"):** Natural waves, medium thickness
- **Large barrel (2"+):** Volume, loose waves, blowout look, thick hair

### Heat Damage: What to Know

Hot air brushes produce 250–400°F heat. Apply heat protectant spray before use. Don't leave brush stationary on hair — keep moving. The Dyson Airwrap's lower heat is genuinely better for damaged hair; all other options run similar temperatures.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Does a hair dryer brush work on natural/coily hair?**
Only the Shark FlexStyle was specifically designed for 4A–4C hair. Other brushes work best on straight to wavy patterns.

**Can I use it on wet hair?**
Most are designed for damp (not dripping wet) hair. Towel dry first, then brush style from damp to dry.

**How long does a blowout last?**
2–5 days depending on hair type, humidity, and sleep position. Sleeping on a silk pillowcase extends results significantly.
