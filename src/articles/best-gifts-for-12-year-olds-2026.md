## Buying Guide: Gifting a 12-Year-Old

**What matters at 12:**
- Social validity (what do their friends have?)
- Independence (gifts that enable their own creativity or exploration)
- Identity (music, style, gaming, sports — pick based on their passion)

**Gaming:**
- Nintendo: family-friendly, excellent games
- PlayStation/Xbox: more teen-oriented, check for parent approval
- PC gaming: if they have a computer, a gaming mouse or headset upgrade is appreciated

**What to skip:**
- Children's board games designed for younger kids
- Anything baby-facing or toy-store juvenile
- Toys without social/identity elements (solo play fades fast at 12)

**What works every time:**
- AirPods or wireless earbuds (status symbol + genuine utility)
- Quality water bottle (Hydro Flask is the brand)
- A gift card to somewhere they actually shop
