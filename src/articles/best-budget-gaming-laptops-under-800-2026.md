For 2026, RTX 4060 remains the recommended choice for DLSS 3 Frame Generation, which can effectively double framerate in supported titles.

### RAM: 16GB vs 32GB

At under $800, most laptops ship with 16GB DDR5. This is sufficient for most games, but upgrading to 32GB is worthwhile if you:
- Stream while gaming
- Run Chrome + Discord + game simultaneously
- Use the laptop for video editing

Most laptops here have user-accessible RAM slots.

## FAQs

**Can you upgrade a gaming laptop under $800?**
Most laptops in this guide offer user-accessible RAM and SSD slots (bottom panel screws, no soldering). GPU is not upgradeable. Adding RAM is the highest-value upgrade.

**Is 144Hz enough or should I get 165Hz?**
For competitive games (Valorant, CS2, Fortnite): aim for 165Hz+. For story games (RPGs, single-player): 144Hz is perfectly smooth.

**Do these laptops work for school/work too?**
Yes. RTX 4060 laptops handle video editing, CAD, and coding well. The Victus 15 and TUF A15 are particularly versatile.
