## What to Look For in a Dorm Folding Chair

**Weight limit:** 225 lbs covers most users but not all. If you're near or above that range, look at the HERCULES (500 lbs) or Timber Ridge (300 lbs). Never exceed a chair's rated capacity.

**Folded size:** Measure where you plan to store it before ordering. A chair that folds to 4 inches stores behind a door easily. One that doesn't fold flat (like a moon chair) needs closet floor space.

**Seat material:** Vinyl wipes clean — best for dorm rooms where spills happen. Fabric is more comfortable for long sessions. Mesh breathes best in warm rooms.

**How you'll use it:** Desk seating (upright posture) → standard flat-fold chairs. Casual hangouts and gaming → moon/lounge chairs. Move frequently or outdoor events → lightweight options with carry bags.
