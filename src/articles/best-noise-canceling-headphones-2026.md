## FAQs

**Is AirPods Max worth the premium over Sony XM5?**
Only if you're in the Apple ecosystem. For iPhone + Mac users, the seamless switching, Personalized Spatial Audio, and Transparency Mode create a combined experience Sony cannot match. For Android users or mixed-ecosystem setups, the Sony XM5 is the better value by a wide margin.

**How long do noise-canceling headphones last?**
Premium headphones typically last 3–5 years with normal use. Battery degradation is the limiting factor — most rechargeable batteries retain ~80% capacity after 500 charge cycles. All the models on this list use rechargeable integrated batteries; replacement programs vary by brand.

**Can I use these on phone calls while commuting?**
Yes. All five headphones support Bluetooth phone calls. ANC remains active during calls on your end (you hear less background noise). Your caller's experience depends on the microphone quality — Jabra leads, Sony is a solid second.

**Do I need a DAC or amplifier with these?**
No. These are wireless Bluetooth headphones designed for direct device connection. The amplifier is built into the headphone. LDAC (on Sony and Sennheiser) provides the highest wireless bitrate for Android users with LDAC-capable phones.
