## What to Look for in a Budget Smart Doorbell

**Subscription model:** This is the most important factor under $100. Ring charges $5-10/month to access stored footage. Wyze has a low-cost Cam Plus plan. Eufy stores locally for free.

**Wired vs battery:** Wired doorbells use your existing chime wiring and never need charging. Battery-powered doorbells install in 15 minutes with a screwdriver.

**Video resolution:** 1080p is the minimum worth buying. Look for HDR or color night vision for better low-light performance.

**Motion zones:** Customizable zones reduce false alerts from passing cars or trees.

**Two-way audio:** All good doorbells have this. Look for noise cancellation if you're on a busy street.

## FAQ

**Can I use a smart doorbell without an existing doorbell wiring?**
Yes — battery-powered models like the Ring Video Doorbell and Blink need no wiring at all.

**Does Eufy really have no monthly fees?**
Correct. Eufy's HomeBase stores footage locally. There's an optional Eufy Security Plus cloud plan, but it's never required.

**Which is best for renters?**
Battery-powered options (Ring, Blink) with no wiring or hub are easiest for renters — minimal installation, easy removal.
