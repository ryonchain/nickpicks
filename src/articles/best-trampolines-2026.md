## Size Guide

- **8–10 ft:** 1–2 kids, smaller yards. Adequate bounce area for kids under 12.
- **12–14 ft:** 2–3 kids, standard backyard. Most popular size for families.
- **15 ft:** Maximum residential size. Requires ~20 ft of clear space (table plus safety perimeter).

---

## Safety Checklist Before Buying

1. Enclosure net included (not sold separately)
2. Net attaches at mat level, not spring level
3. Spring padding covers all springs completely
4. Frame rated for your intended user weight + 25% buffer
5. TÜV, ASTM, or JPMA certification (avoid brands with none)
