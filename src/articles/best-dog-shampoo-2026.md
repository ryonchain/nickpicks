## How Often Should You Bathe Your Dog?

- **Active/outdoorsy dogs:** Every 4–6 weeks, or after muddy activities
- **Indoor dogs with oily coats:** Every 2–4 weeks
- **Dogs with skin conditions:** Follow vet instructions — often weekly with medicated shampoo
- **Over-bathing:** Strips natural oils, causes dry itchy skin. More isn't better.
