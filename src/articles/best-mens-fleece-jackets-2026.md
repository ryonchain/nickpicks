## Men's Fleece Jacket Buying Guide

**Fleece types:**
- **Standard polar fleece:** Most affordable, decent warmth. Gets fuzzy with wear.
- **Anti-pill fleece:** Better longevity, maintains appearance longer.
- **Polartec Thermal Pro:** Performance version. More breathable and moisture-wicking.
- **Sweater-face fleece:** Exterior mimics knitwear look. Best for lifestyle use.

**Full-zip vs. quarter-zip vs. pullover:**
- **Full-zip:** Most versatile. Easy on/off, full ventilation control. Works as a light outer layer.
- **Quarter-zip:** Clean look, less ventilation. Works as a casual top substitute.
- **Pullover:** Warmest (no zipper draft). Most casual look.

**When to wear fleece:**
- Solo in mild fall (40–55°F)
- Mid-layer under a shell or down jacket in cold conditions
- Layered over a thermal base layer for cold outdoor work

**Fleece care:**
- Machine wash cold, tumble dry low
- Avoid fabric softener — reduces moisture-wicking properties
- Zip up zippers before washing to prevent snagging
- Store in a breathable bag to prevent compression of fibers
