## Safety Notes

- **Supervise first sessions** with any new toy to see how aggressively your dog chews.
- **Replace when:** Sharp edges form, small pieces break off, or the safety indicator activates (Goughnuts).
- **Antler chews:** Natural antlers are hard enough to crack teeth — not recommended for power chewers. Deer antler "splits" (hollow) are safer.
- **Rawhide:** Digestive issues if consumed — avoid.
