## How to Choose the Right Mattress

### By Sleep Position

**Back sleepers:** Need medium-firm to firm support to keep spine aligned. Best picks: Saatva Classic (Luxury Firm), Casper Wave Hybrid.

**Side sleepers:** Need pressure relief at shoulders and hips. Best picks: Purple Hybrid, Nectar Premier Copper.

**Stomach sleepers:** Need firm support to prevent lower back sag. Best picks: Saatva Classic (Firm), Leesa Original.

**Combination sleepers:** Need responsive foam or hybrid that adjusts quickly. Best picks: Purple Hybrid, Casper Wave Hybrid.

### By Body Weight

**Under 130 lbs:** Softer mattress recommended — standard firmness will feel too hard. Look for Plush Soft options.

**130–230 lbs:** Most mattresses are designed for this range. Any option on our list will work.

**Over 230 lbs:** Firm hybrid recommended for adequate support. Saatva Classic (Firm) or a dedicated heavyweight mattress.

### By Budget

- **Under $1,000:** Leesa Original, Nectar Original
- **$1,000–$1,500:** Nectar Premier Copper, Purple Hybrid
- **$1,500–$2,000:** Saatva Classic
- **$2,000+:** Casper Wave Hybrid
