## Buying Guide: What to Know About 7 Year Olds

**Reading level:** Most 7-year-olds are early chapter book readers. Buy books slightly above their current level — they'll grow into them.

**Screen preferences:** Roblox and Minecraft dominate. A Roblox or Minecraft gift card ($25) is always right if they play these games.

**Social development:** 7-year-olds are increasingly peer-oriented. Games they can play with friends (Nerf, card games, sports toys) get more use than solo toys.

**Attention span:** 45-90 minutes for engaged independent play on a complex toy (LEGO, perler beads). Plan gifts accordingly — if it takes 3+ hours to build, it may not get finished in one sitting.

**Avoid:** Very young-looking toys (they're too old for Fisher-Price), toys with too many small parts without storage, or anything with a character they've outgrown.
