## The 72-Hour Window: What Still Runs After Prime Day

Amazon Prime Day runs for 48 hours, but deal activity extends beyond that:

**Products that often hold their Prime Day price:**
1. **Amazon's own devices** (Echo, Ring, Fire TV, Kindle) — these often maintain the sale price for days or weeks after Prime Day ends. Check the current page price before assuming the deal is gone.
2. **Amazon Warehouse Deals** — these aren't tied to Prime Day's timeline and fluctuate independently
3. **Subscribe & Save deals** — the Prime Day S&S promotion often extends a few days

**What is definitely over:**
- Lightning Deals (timed, quantity-limited)
- Prime Day-exclusive deep discounts
- Products that were discounted specifically as a 48-hour promotion

## Competing Retailer Sales: Still Running

Major retailers launched competing sales during Prime Day week. Some are still running:

### Walmart (Walmart Plus Week)

Walmart runs their Walmart Plus equivalent of Prime Day — called "Walmart Plus Week" — concurrently with Amazon's event. After the Amazon event ends, Walmart occasionally extends specific deals.

**What to check at Walmart.com:**
- TVs (Walmart often matches or beats Amazon on 4K TV pricing)
- Grocery and household essentials (Walmart's strength)
- Electronics clearance after the competitive sale window

### Target Circle Week

Target Circle Week runs during Prime Day and overlaps afterward. Target Circle members (free to join) get access to:
- 30–40% off selected items
- Circle Bonus Deals (extra 5–10% on specific categories)

**Check Target.com for:** Kitchenware, baby and kids items, home goods, clothing, and beauty.

### Best Buy Flash Sale / "Black Friday in July"

Best Buy runs a competing tech sale during Prime Day. After the Amazon event:
- Open Box deals at Best Buy often remain discounted (Best Buy Open Box is essentially their clearance)
- Tech deals on TVs, laptops, and headphones may continue at the extended sale

### Wayfair Way Day Aftermath

Wayfair's Way Day coincides with Prime Day and sometimes runs slightly longer. If you missed Way Day deals on furniture and home goods, check the Wayfair sale page for remaining stock.

### Chewy (Pet Supplies)

Chewy runs a competing sale called "Chewy Prime Day rival" with pet food, supplies, and medications. Pet supplies often remain discounted a day or two longer.

## How to Find Deals That "Stuck" After Prime Day

A key Prime Day strategy that many shoppers miss: **some products that were discounted during Prime Day get repriced at a new lower regular price.**

This happens because:
1. Amazon reprices dynamically — the "sale" price becomes the new baseline
2. Sellers lower their regular price after seeing volume at Prime Day pricing
3. Amazon Warehouse Deals prices reset based on new sell-through rates

## Protect Yourself From Post-Prime Day Fakes

After major sales events, Amazon listings with inflated "original" prices and fake "deal" markdowns often appear in search results. Always:

1. **Check CamelCamelCamel** before buying anything "on sale" — verify the price was actually higher before
2. **Avoid unknown brands with 5-star reviews posted all on the same date** — review bombing pattern
3. **Check the seller**: Buy from Amazon directly or brand-authorized sellers for electronics and baby products
4. **Ignore "% off" without knowing the starting price** — inflated MSRPs make 40% off sound good on items that were never worth the full price
