# Best Water Shoes for Adults and Kids in 2026

Water shoes protect feet from sharp rocks, hot sand, slippery pool decks, and seashells while draining water instantly and drying within minutes. They're essential for rocky shorelines, river walking, kayaking, and any activity where feet go in and out of water repeatedly. Unlike regular sneakers, they're designed to be waterlogged and still functional.

---

## What Separates Good Water Shoes from Bad Ones

**Drainage:** Holes in the sole and upper that let water exit. No drainage = feet stay submerged in standing water inside the shoe.

**Grip:** Rubber outsole with lug pattern. Critical on wet rocks, boat decks, and algae-covered surfaces.

**Quick-dry material:** Mesh upper that dries in 15–30 minutes out of water. Avoid fabric that retains water.

**Foot security:** Lace-up or bungee-close systems keep the shoe on during swimming or current. Slip-on designs lose easily in moving water.

## How to Choose Size

Water shoes typically run true to size or one half-size small due to the snug upper construction. If between sizes, size up. Wet neoprene and mesh materials feel tighter when wet.

---

## Care and Storage

- Rinse with fresh water after saltwater use to prevent salt crystal buildup in the drainage holes
- Air dry in a ventilated area — never in a closed bag or car trunk
- Avoid machine washing — it can damage the adhesive bonding the sole
