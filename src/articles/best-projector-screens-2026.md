- Best with: Ultra short throw projectors (UST)

**Important:** CLR screens are optimized for projectors placed low (near the floor) or UST projectors. Standard ceiling-mount projectors may not benefit from CLR material.

## Gain Explained

- **Under 1.0 (gray material):** Absorbs some light, improves contrast and black levels. Best for dark rooms with high-lumen projectors.
- **1.0 (white neutral):** Reflects all light evenly in all directions. Most versatile.
- **1.3–1.5 (high gain white):** Reflects more light toward the viewer. Brighter from center, but "hot spot" visible from severe angles. Best for narrow rooms.
- **CLR:** Reflects low-angle projector light; rejects overhead ambient light. Best for UST projectors in bright rooms.
