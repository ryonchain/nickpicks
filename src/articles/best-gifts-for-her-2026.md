## 6. Silk'n BellaFlash Home IPL Hair Removal

At-home IPL (Intense Pulsed Light) that delivers permanent hair reduction for fair-to-medium skin tones. Professional laser hair removal runs $1,000–3,000 per treatment area. The Silk'n BellaFlash achieves comparable results over 6–8 sessions at home.

**Price:** $150–250  
**Best for:** Women interested in long-term hair removal, beauty-focused recipients

Note: IPL works on skin tones Fitzpatrick I–IV. Not recommended for very dark skin tones.
