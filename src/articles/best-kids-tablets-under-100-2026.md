### Amazon Kids+ vs. Google Play

**Amazon Kids+** ($5.99/mo after trial) provides curated, age-appropriate content in a walled garden — no ads, no upsells. Best for children under 8 who need maximum content protection.

**Google Play** enables full Android app access — including Google Classroom, which is increasingly used in schools. Better for older children (10+) and families in the Google ecosystem.

### Should I Buy a Warranty or Kids Edition?

The Amazon Fire HD 8 Kids' 2-year worry-free guarantee is uniquely valuable. Amazon's replacement policy covers actual accidental damage — drops, spills, cracks. If you buy the standard edition, check whether your home insurance covers electronics.
