## How to Choose a Standing Desk Under $500

- **Weight capacity:** Add up your monitor(s), arms, laptop, and accessories. Stay at least 50 lbs under the desk's rated limit.
- **Height range:** Verify minimum and maximum heights work for your body — tall users (6'2"+) should confirm max exceeds 49".
- **Desktop surface size:** A 48"×24" top is the minimum for a single monitor. Dual monitors need 60"×24" or larger.
- **Motor warranty:** Budget desks carry 1–2 year warranties. FlexiSpot E7 offers 5 years; Uplift's commercial frame is lifetime.
- **Memory presets:** Four presets is the practical minimum. Two presets becomes limiting if multiple people share the desk.
