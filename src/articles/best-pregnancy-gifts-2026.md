## What to Avoid Giving a Pregnant Woman

- **Clothing in her current size** — she'll outgrow it in weeks
- **Unsolicited parenting books** — often comes across as preachy
- **Baby clothes in newborn size** — babies grow out of them in 2 weeks; size 3-month is better
- **Anything with strong fragrance** — sensitivity is often heightened during pregnancy
