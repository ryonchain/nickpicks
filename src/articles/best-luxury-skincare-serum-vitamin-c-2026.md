## How to Use Vitamin C Serum

1. **AM application:** Vitamin C is an antioxidant. Apply in the morning for maximum UV protection benefit, before SPF.
2. **Apply to clean skin:** Use after cleansing and toning, before moisturizer.
3. **Let it absorb:** Wait 1–2 minutes before applying the next product.
4. **Storage:** Keep in a dark, cool place. Refrigeration extends shelf life for L-ascorbic acid formulas.
5. **Expiration:** Discard if the serum has turned dark orange or brown — it's oxidized and ineffective.
