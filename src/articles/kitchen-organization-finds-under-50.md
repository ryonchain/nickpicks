## Kitchen Organization: The Sequence That Works

The mistake most people make is buying organization products before sorting and purging. Here's the sequence that works:

1. **Purge first:** Remove everything from the target zone. Eliminate duplicates, expired items, and things that belong elsewhere.
2. **Categorize:** Group items by type and frequency of use. Most-used items get prime real estate (front of drawer, eye-level shelf, countertop).
3. **Measure:** Cabinet depth, drawer dimensions, door clearance. Most Amazon listings include dimensions — measure first, buy once.
4. **Buy targeted:** One zone at a time (start with the drawer that bothers you most).
5. **Label (optional but helpful):** For spice drawers and pantry shelves, label before filling so zones stay consistent.
