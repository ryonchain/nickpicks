## What to Look For

**CADR Rating:** Clean Air Delivery Rate measures how quickly the purifier cleans a room. Higher is better. Match CADR to your room size.

**True HEPA vs. HEPA-type:** Only True HEPA (H11+) captures 99.97% of 0.3-micron particles. "HEPA-type" is a marketing term — avoid it.

**Filter replacement costs:** Budget for annual filter replacement ($20–$80 depending on model). This is often the hidden cost of "cheap" purifiers.

**Room coverage:** Always size up by 20–30% for rooms with high ceilings or open floor plans.
