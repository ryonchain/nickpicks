## Why Ice Quality Matters in Cocktails

Ice is the most used ingredient in cocktail-making, yet it's the most overlooked. The problems with standard ice:

- **Fast melting:** Small cubes dilute cocktails quickly — a Negroni on regular ice is watery in 10 minutes
- **Off flavors:** Freezer odors absorbed into ice transfer directly to your drink
- **Aesthetic:** Cloudy, crescent-shaped convenience store ice looks out of place in a proper rocks glass

The upgrade path: large cube trays → sphere molds → clear ice systems → dedicated countertop machine.
