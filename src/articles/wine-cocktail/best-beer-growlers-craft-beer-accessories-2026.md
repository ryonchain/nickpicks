## Why Growler Material Matters

Beer is light-sensitive, temperature-sensitive, and oxidation-sensitive. The right growler addresses all three:

- **Insulation:** Double-wall stainless steel maintains serving temperature for hours
- **Light protection:** Opaque stainless blocks UV light that creates skunky off-flavors in hops
- **Seal quality:** A tight, CO2-compatible seal prevents oxidation from killing freshness within hours
- **Capacity:** Match to your drinking habits — 32 oz (2 pints), 64 oz (4 pints), 128 oz (8 pints)
