## Why the Right Opener Actually Matters

A bad corkscrew pushes cork fragments into your wine, snaps old corks in half, or takes three frustrating minutes for a task that should take ten seconds. The good news: quality wine openers are inexpensive and last years.

Key factors to evaluate:

- **Mechanism:** Electric, lever, winged, waiter's, or ah-so style each suit different use cases
- **Worm quality:** A teflon-coated, open-spiral helix grips cork cleanly without tearing
- **Build material:** Stainless steel or zinc alloy bodies outlast plastic handles significantly
- **Foil cutter:** Built-in foil cutting saves time; essential for lever and electric models
