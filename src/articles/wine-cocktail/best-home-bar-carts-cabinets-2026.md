## Why a Dedicated Bar Setup Pays Off

A bar cart or cabinet does two things: it organizes your spirits and tools so you can make drinks quickly, and it signals to guests that drinks are welcome. Both matter for entertaining.

What to evaluate:

- **Shelf capacity:** Enough room for 6–10 bottles standing upright, plus glasses and tools
- **Stability:** Heavy wheels or a solid base — a bar cart loaded with glass should not wobble
- **Wine storage:** Built-in wine rack slots save counter space and look intentional
- **Mobility:** Lockable casters allow repositioning for parties and cleaning
- **Style:** Matches your existing home aesthetic — industrial, mid-century, farmhouse, or contemporary
