## Decanter vs. Original Bottle

A whiskey decanter isn't for aging or improving spirit quality the way a wine decanter improves tannins. Whiskey doesn't change meaningfully from a few hours of air exposure. What a decanter does:

- Elevates a bar cart from functional to impressive
- Allows you to display and access whiskey without reaching for labels
- Makes for a beautiful, giftable presentation
- Works as a centerpiece for home bars and dining sideboards

For serious long-term storage, keep the original bottle sealed. Fill the decanter fresh for use within 2–3 months.
