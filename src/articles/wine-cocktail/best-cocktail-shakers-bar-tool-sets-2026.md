## How to Choose a Cocktail Shaker

The cocktail shaker is the most used tool in home bartending. The right one shakes without leaking, opens without a fight, and holds enough volume for two cocktails at once.

Key considerations:

- **Style:** Cobbler (3-piece, beginner-friendly) vs. Boston (2-piece, professional)
- **Volume:** 18–24 oz for single-cocktail shaking; 28–32 oz for two cocktails at once
- **Material:** Stainless steel is standard — 18/8 food-grade steel is the minimum
- **Strainer:** Cobbler shakers have built-in strainers; Boston shakers need a separate Hawthorne strainer
- **Seal quality:** Look for a leak-proof seal — cheap cobbler shakers dribble
