## CADR — Actual Cleaning Performance

CADR (Clean Air Delivery Rate) is the most important spec for air purifiers. It measures cubic feet of air filtered per minute at each fan setting. Higher CADR = faster room cleaning.

Both flagship models have high CADR ratings around 400+ CFM. This makes both effective for large living rooms, open-plan spaces, and office environments up to 1,500–1,600 sq ft. Neither has a meaningful CADR advantage over the other at the flagship level.

For smaller rooms (bedroom, office, 200–400 sq ft), both brands have optimized models:

**Coway Airmega 200M** (~$109): CADR of 246 CFM, 360 sq ft coverage  
**Levoit Core 300** (~$89): CADR of 145 CFM, 219 sq ft coverage

For bedroom use, Coway's 200M has notably higher CADR than Levoit's Core 300. The Core 300 is a better value for small rooms, but the 200M cleans air faster.

## Filter Quality and Cost

Both use true HEPA H13 filters that capture 99.97% of particles 0.3 microns and larger. Both combine HEPA with activated carbon for odor and VOC reduction.

**The key long-term difference is filter replacement cost:**
- Coway replacement filters: ~$50–70 per set (every 12 months)
- Levoit replacement filters: ~$30–40 per set (every 12 months)

Over 5 years of ownership:
- Coway: $250–350 in filters
- Levoit: $150–200 in filters

This ~$100–150 difference over 5 years is meaningful when the upfront price gap is already $120 (Coway $339 vs Levoit $219 flagship). Levoit wins on total cost of ownership.

---

## Smart Features and App

Both flagship models connect to a companion app (VeSync for Levoit, Airmega app for Coway) with air quality display, scheduling, and voice control via Alexa and Google Assistant.

Coway's app is polished but Levoit's VeSync app handles multiple Levoit products (humidifiers, smart plugs) in one place — useful if you're building a smart home ecosystem. Neither app is significantly better for core air purifier control.

Both have real-time AQI sensors that automatically increase fan speed when air quality drops. This auto mode is one of the most useful features in both brands.

---

## Build Quality and Aesthetics

Coway has a premium, more distinctive build quality. The Airmega 400S has a design award (Red Dot Design Award 2022) and is objectively more attractive in a living room. The HEPA filter access panel is easier to use on Coway models.

Levoit models are attractive and modern but feel more mass-market. The cylindrical Core models are compact and work well in bedrooms. Build quality is solid for the price but not on Coway's level.
