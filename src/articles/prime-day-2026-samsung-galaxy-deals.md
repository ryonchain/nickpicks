## Samsung TVs

### QLED vs. Neo QLED vs. OLED

**QLED (Q-series):** Quantum dot LCD. Great brightness, good colors. Entry-level Samsung TV.

**Neo QLED (QN-series):** Mini-LED backlight. Much better local dimming. Better HDR. The step worth paying for.

**OLED (S-series):** True black levels, per-pixel control. Better motion. Higher price.
