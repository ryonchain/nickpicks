## What to Avoid on Prime Day

**No-name brands at suspiciously low prices.** Budget brands from unknown manufacturers flood Amazon's search results around Prime Day. Suction ratings are self-reported, filtration claims are unreliable, and replacement parts rarely exist. Stick with Dyson, Shark, Bissell, Miele, Kenmore, and Hoover.

**Previous-generation models without obvious price drops.** Some retailers list older models without meaningfully discounting them, relying on the "Prime Day deal" label to move inventory. Check price history before buying any vacuum older than 2 years.
