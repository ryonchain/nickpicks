## Prime Day Shopping Tips for Home Buyers

1. **Check if it's a bundle deal.** Many "Prime Day" home deals include extra attachments or accessories that inflate the apparent value. Compare the base unit price to buying it standalone.
2. **Compare with Target and Walmart.** Both run competing sales. For appliances, Walmart Deals sometimes beats Amazon.
3. **Read recent reviews.** Prime Day drives high volume to older model numbers. Check the date on reviews to confirm you're getting the current generation.
