## Ergonomics for Students

**Chair height:** When seated, feet should be flat on the floor, thighs parallel to the ground. Dangling feet cause back strain in children.

**Monitor height:** Top of screen at or slightly below eye level. Stacked books under a laptop work as a temporary riser.

**Lighting:** Desk lamp from the left for right-handed writers (reduces hand shadow), from the right for lefties. Natural light behind the student (not in their eyes).

**Chair back support:** A small lumbar pillow ($10–$15) helps students who slouch in standard chairs.
