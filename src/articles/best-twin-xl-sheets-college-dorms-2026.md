## Buying Guide: What to Know Before You Buy

### Twin XL vs Standard Twin
Twin XL measures **39" × 80"**. Standard Twin is **39" × 75"**. That 5-inch difference matters — standard twin sheets will slip off at the foot every night.

### Deep Pocket vs Standard
Standard pocket depth is 9". Dorm mattresses with a topper can be 10–15" thick. Look for **14–16" deep pockets** with all-around elastic — not just corner elastic.

### Cotton vs Microfiber
- **Microfiber:** Softer out of package, wrinkle-resistant, cheaper, stays warm
- **Cotton:** Breathable, cooler for hot sleepers, gets better with washing, more expensive

### How Many Sets to Buy
Buy **2 sets minimum**. Dorm laundry logistics mean your sheets will sit in a basket for days before you wash them. Having a backup set means you're never sleeping on a bare mattress.
