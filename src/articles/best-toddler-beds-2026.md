# Best Toddler Beds in 2026

Toddler beds are the transition point between cribs and full-size beds. They use the standard crib mattress (27" x 52") — if you already own a crib mattress, a toddler bed is a low-cost transition. Side rails prevent falls; the low height (about 6 inches off the floor) allows toddlers to climb in and out independently.

## What to Buy with a Toddler Bed

**Mattress:** If transitioning from a crib, your existing crib mattress fits (27" x 52"). If buying new, look for a firm mattress — soft mattresses are a suffocation risk for toddlers.

**Waterproof mattress protector:** Essential. Accidents during toilet training are inevitable. A waterproof cover protects the mattress.

**Rail guards:** Foam rail bumpers add another layer of fall protection if your child is a very active sleeper.

---

## When to Transition from Crib

Signs a toddler is ready:
- Climbing out of the crib (safety risk)
- 18+ months old and showing interest in a "big kid" bed
- New sibling arriving who needs the crib

Most children transition between 18 months and 3 years. There's no rush — if the crib is safe and the child is content, wait.
