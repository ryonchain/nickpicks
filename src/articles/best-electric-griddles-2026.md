## Electric Griddle Buying Guide

### Size: How Much Cooking Surface Do You Need?

- **1–2 people:** 100–150 sq in (Presto Tilt 'N Drain, Dash Mini)
- **3–4 people:** 150–200 sq in (Hamilton Beach 38518)
- **5+ people:** 200+ sq in (Blackstone 22", Cuisinart GR-6S)

For pancake breakfasts, the standard recipe makes 8–12 pancakes per batch. A 200 sq in griddle fits 4–6 pancakes at once; a 280 sq in griddle fits 8–10.

### Temperature: Higher Isn't Always Better

Most electric griddles max at 400°F — adequate for pancakes, eggs, bacon, grilled cheese, and sandwiches. For genuine searing (burgers, steaks), you need 450–500°F. The Blackstone 22" is the only countertop electric griddle we found that achieves true searing temperatures.

### Non-Stick Coating: PFOA-Free vs Steel

**PFOA-free non-stick:** Easy cleanup, low-fat cooking, gentle with delicate foods. Avoid metal utensils that scratch the coating. Replace when coating shows damage.

**Pre-seasoned steel (Blackstone):** Like cast iron — improves with use, lasts indefinitely. Requires seasoning maintenance. Can use metal utensils. Takes a few uses to become properly non-stick.

### Grease Management

If you regularly cook bacon, sausage, or fatty meats, grease management matters. Look for:
- Built-in grease channel with drip tray
- Tilting mechanism (Presto)
- Easy-to-remove and clean drip cups

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Is an electric griddle worth it?**
If you regularly cook breakfast for multiple people or want a flat cooking surface for sandwiches, burgers, and eggs, yes. Electric griddles offer temperature consistency and surface area that stovetop pans can't match.

**Can you cook steak on an electric griddle?**
Only on high-heat models. The Blackstone 22" (500°F) can achieve a proper sear. Most other electric griddles max at 400°F, which is too low for searing — they'll cook steak through but without the crust.

**Are electric griddles easy to clean?**
Models with removable, dishwasher-safe plates (Cuisinart GR-6S) or fully submersible surfaces (Hamilton Beach) are the easiest. Steel surface griddles require more maintenance but last indefinitely.

**How long do electric griddles last?**
With proper care, 5–10 years. Non-stick coatings eventually wear and should be replaced rather than used with damaged coatings. Steel surface griddles (Blackstone) last indefinitely with proper seasoning.
