## Should You Buy a MacBook or a Windows Laptop?

**Get a MacBook if:**
- You're in a creative field (design, video, music, architecture)
- You already have an iPhone/iPad and want seamless continuity
- Battery life and build quality matter more than gaming
- Your school's software is Mac-compatible (check before buying)

**Get a Windows laptop if:**
- You need specific Windows-only software for your major (SolidWorks, some engineering tools)
- You want to game on your laptop
- Budget is under $700

**Reality check:** Most software that matters — Office, Zoom, Canvas, Adobe Suite, Spotify, browsers — runs on both platforms equally well.
