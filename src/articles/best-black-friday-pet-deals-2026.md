## Black Friday Pet Shopping Strategy

**1. Buy in bulk where sensible.** Dog food and cat litter have the best cost-per-unit in large quantities. Black Friday is the time to stock up — not individual bags.

**2. Subscribe-and-save stacks.** Amazon Subscribe & Save (typically 5–15% off) can sometimes be applied on top of Black Friday deals. Check before ordering at the deal price.

**3. Chewy vs. Amazon.** Chewy runs its own aggressive promotions separate from Amazon. Compare both before buying food — Chewy's autoship discount sometimes beats Amazon's BF price.

**4. Smart pet tech is the deepest discount.** GPS trackers, cameras, and automatic feeders see bigger percentage discounts than food. If you've been eyeing smart pet tech, Black Friday is when to move.
