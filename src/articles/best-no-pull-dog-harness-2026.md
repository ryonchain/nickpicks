## Tips for Using a No-Pull Harness

1. **Start on low-distraction walks** — let the dog adjust before heading to busy areas.
2. **Change direction when they pull** — the harness redirects them, but you need to reinforce it by turning around.
3. **Reward loose-leash walking** immediately with treats.
4. **Check fit weekly** — especially with puppies who are growing.
