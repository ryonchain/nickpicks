## What to Avoid

- **Bonded leather wallets** — these are leather scraps glued to fabric backing and will peel apart within a year of daily use. Look for "genuine leather" or "full-grain leather" in the description.
- **Wallets with no RFID protection in high-travel areas** — if you use contactless payment cards regularly, an unprotected wallet leaves you vulnerable to skimming.
- **Overly decorative closures** — fancy magnetic clasps and decorative snaps often fail before the leather does. Stick to simple zip or snap mechanisms.
- **Accordion-style wallets with too many slots** — they look organized but become uncomfortable and misshapen quickly when loaded to capacity.
