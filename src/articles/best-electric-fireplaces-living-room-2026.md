## Flame Technology Explained

### Standard LED Flame
Entry-level electric fireplaces use LEDs to project light onto a silver screen with a rotating motor below. Convincing at 10+ feet; obvious at arm's length.

### 3D Flame Technology
Multiple LED arrays project from different angles to create depth. Visually convincing at 4–6 feet. Available in mid-range ($200–$600) models.

### Water Vapor Flame (Steam Mist)
Cold water heated to create actual steam, lit with LED. Creates true 3D floating flames with genuine depth and translucency. Indistinguishable from real fire at any distance. Premium pricing ($600+). Dimplex's Revillusion technology and RealFlame models use this approach.

### Holographic Flame
Latest technology—a flat panel projector creates high-definition flame visuals. Extremely photorealistic. Finding its way into high-end models in 2026.
