## Lock Technique Matters

The best lock is useless with bad technique:

1. **Lock through the frame + rear wheel + to the rack** — not just through the wheel
2. **Position the lock shackle down** — keeps the keyhole off the ground and reduces picking/shimming attacks
3. **Fill the space inside the U-lock** — less wiggle room = harder to use a jack to break it
4. **Use two locks** — a U-lock AND a cable or chain is standard for high-risk areas
