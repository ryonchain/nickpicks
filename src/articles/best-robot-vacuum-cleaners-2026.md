Robot vacuums have matured dramatically. The best 2026 models self-empty, mop, map your home in 3D, and avoid obstacles with laser precision. Here's how the top robots compare — and which one belongs in your home.

---

### Key Features to Compare

- **Navigation**: LiDAR (laser mapping) is more accurate than camera or gyroscope-only navigation
- **Self-empty base**: Eliminates manual emptying for 30–60 days
- **Obstacle avoidance**: Avoids cables, socks, and pet waste
- **Mopping**: Combo units save a separate purchase; check if the mop lifts automatically on carpet
- **Suction power**: Measured in Pa — 2,000 Pa+ handles pet hair and carpet well
