## How to Choose the Right Mattress Topper

**Too firm mattress:** Add a 2–4" memory foam or latex topper. Thicker = more softening.

**Too soft mattress:** A topper won't fix a collapsed mattress. A latex topper is firmer and will help somewhat, but a new mattress is the real solution.

**Back pain:** A 2–3" medium-firm latex or gel memory foam topper often helps by distributing weight more evenly.

**Hot sleeping:** Latex > graphite-infused foam > gel foam > standard memory foam. Down alternative sleeps cool but provides less support.

**Material longevity:** TEMPUR and natural latex outlast standard memory foam significantly (7–10 years vs. 2–4 years for budget foam).
