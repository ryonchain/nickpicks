### What to Look for in a Standing Desk Converter

Before you buy, consider:

- **Height range**: You need at least 14–16 inches of lift for the average adult.
- **Weight capacity**: Make sure it holds your monitor(s), keyboard, and any extras.
- **Surface area**: Single-monitor users can go compact; dual-monitor setups need 28+ inches of width.
- **Adjustment mechanism**: Spring-assisted risers are easier to operate than purely manual lifts.
- **Stability**: A wobbly surface undermines everything.
