## Backpack Sizing Guide by Grade

Choosing the right capacity prevents the "too heavy to carry" problem:

| Grade | Recommended Capacity | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| PreK–K | 8–12L | Small body, minimal supplies |
| Grades 1–2 | 12–16L | Folders, lunchbox, water bottle |
| Grades 3–5 | 16–20L | Textbooks, binders, standard supplies |
| Grades 6+ | 20–26L | Middle school supply load |

**Fit checklist:**
- Top of pack sits 2 inches below shoulder line — not above the neck
- Bottom of pack rests at waist level, not below the hips
- Both straps worn — single-strap wearing causes asymmetric strain
- Pack when loaded weighs less than 15% of child's body weight
