## Automatic Dog Feeder Buying Guide

**Portion accuracy matters more than capacity.** Test your feeder's portion consistency with a kitchen scale before trusting it long-term — some motors drift by 10–15% per meal.

**Kibble size compatibility:** Wide dispensing chutes (PETLIBRO Air, PetSafe Smart Feed) handle large breed kibble. Standard chutes may jam with pieces larger than 1/2 inch.

**How long will the tank last?**
| Dog Size | Daily Food | 6L Tank Life |
|----------|-----------|-------------|
| Small (< 20 lbs) | 1 cup/day | 60+ days |
| Medium (20–50 lbs) | 2–3 cups/day | 20–30 days |
| Large (50–90 lbs) | 4–5 cups/day | 10–15 days |

**Slow feed for bloat-prone breeds:** Large, deep-chested breeds (Great Danes, German Shepherds, Weimaraners) are at risk for bloat. PetSafe Smart Feed's slow dispense mode is the only feeder with a built-in solution.
