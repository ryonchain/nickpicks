# Best Black Friday Mattress Deals 2026 — Casper, Saatva, Purple, Nectar, and More

## Black Friday Mattress Shopping Strategy

**1. Trial periods matter more than price.** A 365-night trial beats a 90-night trial when you're buying blind. Prioritize brands with longer trials (Nectar and DreamCloud both offer 365 nights).

**2. Factor in free accessories.** A mattress deal that includes $150 worth of pillows and sheets at the same price as a competing deal without them is legitimately better. Calculate total value.

**3. Black Friday prices are the real price.** Like NordicTrack in fitness, mattress brand MSRP is inflated. The Black Friday price is closer to actual market value. If you're not buying at Black Friday prices, wait — they'll come around again by Presidents' Day in February.

**4. Check firmness return policies.** Some brands offer firmness exchanges; others don't. If you're not sure whether you want plush vs. firm, choose a brand that allows firmness swap returns.
