## How to Choose a Gaming Headset Under $200

**Wireless vs wired:** If budget allows, go wireless — the best 2.4GHz headsets have no perceptible latency disadvantage and dramatically improve desk comfort. If you're mostly at a fixed PC setup, wired is fine and typically sounds better per dollar.

**By use case:**
- **Competitive shooter (VALORANT, CS2):** Prioritize positional audio accuracy — EPOS H3 or Arctis Nova Pro
- **Streaming/content creation:** Prioritize mic quality — Logitech G Pro X 2 with Blue VO!CE
- **PS5 gaming:** Sony PULSE 3D or PULSE Explore for native Tempest integration
- **Music + gaming dual use:** EPOS H3 or Arctis Nova Pro for balanced tuning
- **Never want to charge:** HyperX Cloud Alpha Wireless
