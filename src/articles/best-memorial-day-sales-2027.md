Memorial Day 2027 falls on **Monday, May 31**. The long weekend (May 29–31) is one of the best deal windows of the year for mattresses, appliances, furniture, grills, and outdoor gear. If you missed Presidents' Day and can't wait for Labor Day, Memorial Day is your window.

---

## Why Memorial Day Is a Strong Sales Event

- **Mattress season peak:** May–June is peak mattress-buying season. Brands offer their biggest spring deals at Memorial Day.
- **Appliance clearance:** Retailers clear spring inventory before June model refreshes.
- **Outdoor gear is fresh stock:** Grills and patio furniture are at full inventory but retailers want volume, so they compete on price.
- **Long weekend = more shopping time:** Three days of retail competition across Home Depot, Lowe's, Best Buy, Amazon, and Wayfair.

## Memorial Day Shopping Strategy

1. **Mattresses:** Buy during Memorial Day weekend, not early — deals often improve through Sunday and Monday.
2. **Appliances:** Check Best Buy, Home Depot, and Lowe's simultaneously — they price-match but the initial promotions differ.
3. **Grills:** If you see a Weber or Traeger at a genuine discount, buy it. They don't go on sale often.
4. **Patio furniture:** Wayfair Memorial Day event is the best patio furniture sale outside Black Friday.
5. **TVs:** Memorial Day is secondary. Wait for Labor Day or Black Friday unless the deal is genuinely strong.
