## Benefits Backed by Research

**Skin and Coat:** A 2016 Veterinary Dermatology study found significant improvement in coat quality, pruritus (itching), and skin barrier function in dogs with atopic dermatitis given EPA/DHA supplementation at therapeutic doses.

**Joint Health:** A 2010 Journal of the American Veterinary Medical Association study showed dogs with osteoarthritis given omega-3 supplements had significantly better scores for pain, weight bearing, and mobility.

**Kidney Health:** Omega-3 fatty acids slow progression of chronic kidney disease in dogs by reducing inflammatory cytokines. The University of Minnesota veterinary research team has published extensively on this application.

**Cognitive Function:** DHA specifically supports brain development and may slow cognitive decline in aging dogs (canine cognitive dysfunction syndrome).
