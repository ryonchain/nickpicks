## How to Use Packing Cubes Effectively

**Category system (recommended):**
- Large cube: shirts, pants
- Medium cube: underwear, socks
- Small cube: accessories, cables
- Shoe bag: shoes (keeps dirt contained)
- Toiletry bag: bathroom items

**Color coding:** Many travelers use one color per person or one color per category. Eagle Creek and VEKEN both offer multi-color options.

**Don't overstuff:** Cubes that are jammed shut are harder to close and wear out faster. Fill to 80% for best results.
