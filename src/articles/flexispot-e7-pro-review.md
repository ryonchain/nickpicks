# FlexiSpot E7 Pro Review: The Best Standing Desk Under $600?

FlexiSpot launched the E7 in 2020 and it quickly became the most-recommended standing desk under $600. In 2022, they released the E7 Pro — a heavier-duty version with an upgraded motor and wider height range. 

I have been using it for two months. Here is whether it is worth the extra $100 over the standard E7.

## What the E7 Pro Does Well

**1. Height range is exceptional.** 22.8"–48.4" covers users from 4'9" to 6'7" standing. Standard E7 tops out at 48.1" — almost identical, but the lower floor (22.8") means shorter users can actually sit comfortably. If you are under 5'4", the lower minimum height matters.

**2. Lifting capacity is overkill (in a good way).** 355 lbs means you can put two monitors, a laptop, a speaker, and a desktop computer on the desk without worrying about the motor straining. The standard E7 handles 275 lbs — fine for most setups but the Pro's extra headroom means slower motor wear over years.

**3. Motor is genuinely quiet.** At 45 dB, the E7 Pro raises silently on video calls. This is not a given — budget standing desks run at 60–70 dB and are audible to meeting participants.

**4. Memory preset buttons.** Program your seated and standing heights, press once to transition. Takes ~5 seconds to move from sitting (29") to standing (44"). The button panel also tracks standing time, which is a useful nudge.

|---|---|---|
| Height range | 24.4" – 50" | 22.8" – 48.4" |
| Lifting capacity | 275 lbs | 355 lbs |
| Motor | Dual | Dual (upgraded) |
| Anti-collision | Yes | Yes (upgraded sensitivity) |
| Warranty (frame) | 15 years | 15 years |

**Verdict on upgrade:** The E7 Pro is worth the $100 if: (1) you are shorter than 5'4" (need the lower floor), or (2) you have a heavy multi-monitor setup over 250 lbs. For most users, the standard E7 is sufficient and saves $100.

## Setup Notes

Assembly takes 45–60 minutes solo. A second person is useful for attaching the desktop. The instructions are clear with picture-based steps. One watch-out: the cable management tray is sold separately (~$25 on Amazon) and is worth adding.

---

## The Verdict

The FlexiSpot E7 Pro is the best standing desk under $600 for most home office users. The combination of quiet dual motor, 355 lb capacity, 15-year warranty, and a 22.8" minimum height makes it the most capable desk in its price tier.

If you have a standard-height desk setup and a mid-weight monitor arrangement, the standard E7 at $449 saves $100 with nearly identical performance.
