## What to Look for in Carry-On Travel Accessories

**Weight.** Every accessory adds to your carry-on's weight. Choose lightweight options and skip anything that doesn't solve a real recurring problem you've actually experienced.

**TSA rules.** Power banks under 100Wh are carry-on legal. Liquids must be under 3.4 oz in a clear quart bag. No sharp objects in carry-on (pack your travel scissors in checked luggage).

**Durability.** Travel accessories get thrown in overhead bins, crammed into seat pockets, and sprayed with TSA screening chemicals. Invest in quality zippers and fabrics.
