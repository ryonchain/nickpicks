Standard strollers are not safe above a walking pace.

## Safety Rules for Jogging with a Stroller

1. **Never jog with infants under 6 months** — their necks cannot handle road vibration. Use car seat adapter for walking only.
2. **Always lock the front wheel before running** — a swiveling wheel at jogging speed is dangerous.
3. **Use the wrist safety leash** — it prevents runaway strollers on hills.
4. **Check tire pressure before every run** — soft tires reduce stability.
5. **Maximum recommended running speed** with most jogging strollers is 12 mph (comfortable jog pace).
