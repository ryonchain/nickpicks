## FAQ

**How do smart water bottles track hydration?**
Most use one of three methods: (1) weight sensors that detect sips, (2) accelerometers that detect tilting, or (3) manual logging via app. Weight sensors (HidrateSpark) are the most accurate.

**Do smart water bottles work with iOS and Android?**
All five on this list work with both iOS and Android. Integration depth varies — HidrateSpark has the most extensive third-party connections (Apple Health, Fitbit, MyFitnessPal).

**How long does a smart water bottle battery last?**
LED/sensor systems typically last 1–2 weeks on a charge (HidrateSpark, Rebo). LCD display systems using coin cell batteries can last 30+ days (Thermos Connected).

**Are smart water bottles dishwasher safe?**
The bottle body typically is, but the lid with electronics usually isn't. Always hand-wash the smart lid and check manufacturer guidelines before putting any electronic component in the dishwasher.
