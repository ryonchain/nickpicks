## How to Choose a Gaming Mouse

**Match shape to grip style.** Palm grip = full-contact rounded mice (DeathAdder). Claw grip = mid-size with a defined hump. Fingertip = compact, lightweight (under 65g). Test shapes if possible before committing.

**Wireless is the right choice for most players.** At 2.4GHz, the latency is imperceptible. The freedom from cable drag improves tracking consistency, especially during large arm swipes.

**DPI isn't performance.** A higher-DPI number doesn't mean more accurate tracking. It means faster cursor movement. Most competitive players use 400–1600 DPI. High DPI is useful only for small, high-resolution displays where you need to cover more screen with less physical movement.
