## Setting Up Your Streaming Mic

**Position matters most.** Place the mic 6–8 inches from your mouth, slightly off to the side to reduce plosives (P and B sounds). Pointing a cardioid mic slightly off-axis (rather than directly at your mouth) reduces harshness while maintaining clarity.

**Use a boom arm.** Most included stands position the mic too low and too far away. A $20 Amazon boom arm solves this and keeps desk clutter minimal.

**Set gain correctly.** Too high = noise floor and distortion. In OBS, watch your audio meter during normal speech — peaks should hit -12 to -6 dB consistently, never redlining.
