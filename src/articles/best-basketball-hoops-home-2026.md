## Types of Basketball Hoops

### Portable Hoops

Portable hoops sit on a wheeled base filled with water or sand. Easy to move, no installation, works on any driveway surface. The base weight is what separates stable from wobbly.

**Best for:** Renters, people who move, shared driveways, kids.

### In-Ground Hoops

Concrete-anchored in the driveway or yard. Zero flex, no base tip-over risk, and the most realistic feel for serious players. Requires permanent installation — plan on 4–6 hours with proper tools.

**Best for:** Serious players, homes with kids who play frequently, families planning to stay 5+ years.

### Wall-Mounted Hoops

Bolted directly to the garage. Ultra-stable, takes up no floor space. Adjustment range is fixed (or limited). Best for small driveways where you don't want to walk around a base.

**Best for:** Tight driveways, garage players, homes with no yard.

### Indoor / Mini Hoops

Over-the-door hoops for bedrooms and offices. Small foam or rubber balls included. Safe for indoor use, no installation needed.

**Best for:** Kids, office break rooms, dorm rooms, small apartments.
