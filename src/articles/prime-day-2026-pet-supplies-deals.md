## What to Skip

**Exotic brands with sudden "Prime Day" discounts** — stick to brands your pet already tolerates. Switching food to save $10 can cause digestive issues.

**Perishable items with short expiration dates** — check dates on food deals, especially canned food.

**Cheap no-brand cat toys with plastic parts** — cats can ingest small pieces.
