If past patterns hold, the iPhone 17 will share dimensions with the iPhone 16 — but a slightly repositioned camera module means not all iPhone 16 cases will fit perfectly. This guide covers cases from brands that pre-release "iPhone 17 compatible" products ahead of launch, with real picks updated post-announcement.

## What to Expect from the iPhone 17 Case Market

Based on Apple's history and leaked CAD files as of mid-2026:

- **iPhone 17** (6.1") — similar to iPhone 16, repositioned rear camera
- **iPhone 17 Plus** (6.7") — same footprint as 16 Plus
- **iPhone 17 Pro** (6.3") — upgraded from 6.1", new titanium finish
- **iPhone 17 Pro Max** (6.9") — same size range as 16 Pro Max

**MagSafe remains** — Apple has committed to MagSafe as its standard wireless ecosystem. Any MagSafe case for iPhone 15/16 that fits the physical body will work with MagSafe on iPhone 17.

## What to Avoid

- **Generic "iPhone 17 Ready" cases before specs drop** — Some sellers list fake dimensions. Buy from established brands only.
- **Tempered glass without case** — Screen-only protection leaves back and frame exposed.
- **Cheap TPU-only cases** — They yellow within 3–6 months and don't protect corners.
