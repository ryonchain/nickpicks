## Choosing the Right Gaming Headset

**Competitive vs immersive gaming:** For shooters and battle royales, prioritize precise spatial positioning — DTS:X, Dolby Atmos, or Tempest 3D. For RPGs and story games, a wide natural soundstage (open-back) may feel more immersive than processed surround.

**Wireless:** 2.4GHz USB dongles are the only wireless tech with low enough latency for competitive play. Bluetooth introduces 50–200ms delay — avoid for gaming. Only use Bluetooth mode for music or calls.

**Microphone quality:** For casual play, a flip-to-mute boom is fine. For streaming or voice-heavy play, look for noise cancellation and cardioid pickup. Blue VO!CE (Logitech) and ClearCast (Arctis) are the best on-headset mic systems.
