Resistance bands are one of the most versatile pieces of home gym equipment available. They're inexpensive, portable, easy to store, and effective for everything from mobility work to serious strength training. Here are the best resistance band sets in 2026, broken down by training style.

---

### Types of Resistance Bands

- **Loop bands (flat)**: Great for glute work, lateral walks, mobility
- **Tube bands with handles**: Mimic cable machines; best for upper body
- **Fabric/cloth bands**: Durable, don't roll up, ideal for lower body
- **Figure-8 bands**: Compact, for ankle and wrist work
- **Therapy bands (flat latex)**: Rehab and mobility
