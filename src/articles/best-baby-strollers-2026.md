## How to Choose a Baby Stroller

### By Lifestyle

**Urban (city life, public transit):** Chicco Bravo, Baby Jogger City Mini GT2, Bugaboo Butterfly
**Suburban (car-dependent, parks):** UPPAbaby VISTA V2, Baby Jogger City Mini GT2
**Active/Outdoor (jogging, trails):** BOB Revolution Flex 3.0
**Travel-focused:** Bugaboo Butterfly, Baby Jogger City Tour Lux

**One child:** Any stroller works
**Planning a second child:** UPPAbaby VISTA V2 (converts to double)
**Twins:** UPPAbaby VISTA V2 with Rumble Seat, or Baby Jogger City Mini GT2 Double

### By Budget

- **Under $300:** Chicco Bravo ($249–$299)

- **$500–$700:** Bugaboo Butterfly ($599)
- **$900+:** UPPAbaby VISTA V2 ($969–$1,099)

**Newborn–6 months:** Need flat recline or bassinet. UPPAbaby VISTA V2 (includes bassinet), Bugaboo Butterfly (flat recline), Baby Jogger City Mini GT2 (near-flat recline).

**6 months+:** Most strollers work once baby has head control.

**Toddler:** Look for seat weight limits of 50+ lbs and longevity.
