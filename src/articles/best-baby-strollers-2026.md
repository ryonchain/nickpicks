## Stroller Buying Guide

### Stroller Types Explained

**Travel systems:** Car seat + stroller sold together. Best for newborns through 12 months. Simplifies the "car to stroller" transfer.

**Full-size strollers:** Large, feature-rich, most comfortable for long outings. Heaviest and bulkiest fold.

**Lightweight/umbrella strollers:** Under 15 lbs, compact fold. Best for travel, secondary strollers, or parents who use public transit.

**Jogging strollers:** Fixed or lockable front wheel, air-filled tires, hand brake. Built for running with a child.

**Double strollers:** Side-by-side or tandem seating for two children. Wider and heavier but essential for twins or close-in-age siblings.

### Key Features to Prioritize

1. **Fold mechanism:** One-hand folds are worth extra cost. You'll fold it while holding a child 1,000+ times.
2. **Storage basket:** Look for "under-seat access from the back" — basket-from-front-only designs fill poorly.
3. **Car seat compatibility:** If you have a specific car seat, verify the stroller accepts it (with or without an adapter).
4. **Canopy coverage:** Larger canopies keep sun off the child's face without requiring separate covers.
5. **Handlebar height:** Taller parents (6'+) often find adjustable handlebars essential to prevent back strain.

## FAQ

**What age can a baby use a stroller?**
Most full-size strollers and travel systems accept infants from birth (with a flat-recline bassinet or compatible infant seat). Umbrella/lightweight strollers typically require the child to hold their head independently (around 6 months).

**How long will we use a stroller?**
Most children use strollers until age 3–4. Some continue occasionally until 5. Budget accordingly — a stroller used for 3 years works out to $60–$270/year even at higher price points.

**Can I use any car seat with any stroller?**
No. Car seat + stroller compatibility requires either: (1) the same brand ecosystem (e.g., Graco car seat + Graco stroller), or (2) a brand-specific adapter sold separately. Always verify compatibility before purchasing.
