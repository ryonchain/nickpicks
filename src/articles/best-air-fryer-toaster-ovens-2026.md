## Air Fryer vs. Air Fryer Toaster Oven: What's the Difference?

**Standalone air fryer:**
- More efficient for small batches (1–2 portions)
- Faster preheat time
- Takes up less counter space
- No toast/bake capability

**Air fryer toaster oven:**
- Multi-function (toast, bake, broil + air fry)
- Handles larger portions and full pans
- Replaces 2–3 appliances
- Slightly slower air fry cycle due to larger chamber

**Our recommendation:** If you already have a toaster oven and want to add air frying, get a combo. If you air fry small batches only (1–2 portions), a compact standalone air fryer is faster.

## What to Look for in an Air Fryer Toaster Oven

**Interior size:** Measure your counter first. Mid-size units fit 12" pizzas; large units fit 13" pizzas or 9x13" pans.

**Wattage:** 1,800W+ gives you faster preheat and better crisping. Units under 1,500W often underperform.

**Number of functions:** More functions = more versatility, but also more complexity. Pick the functions you'll actually use.

**Accessories included:** Look for an air fryer basket, baking pan, and wire rack at minimum.

**Ease of cleaning:** Non-stick interiors and dishwasher-safe crumb trays save time.
