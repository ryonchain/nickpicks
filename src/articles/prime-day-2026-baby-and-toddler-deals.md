## What NOT to Buy on Prime Day for Baby

Some baby items are better purchased elsewhere or outside Prime Day:

- **Crib mattresses**: Safety certifications matter more than price. Stick to brands you've researched year-round (Newton, Naturepedic, Sealy). Don't let a sale push you to an unfamiliar brand.
- **Formula**: Not sold on Amazon (regulatory reasons). Skip.
- **Car seat adapters**: Make sure the adapter specifically lists your stroller + car seat combination. Sales create impulse buys with incompatible combos.
- **White noise machines**: Good ones ($30–$60) are available year-round. Not worth waiting.
