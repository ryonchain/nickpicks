# Best Quercetin Supplements of 2026: Anti-Inflammatory and Immune Support Picks

Quercetin is one of the most abundant dietary flavonoids — a polyphenol that provides the anti-inflammatory signaling behind the "eat more colorful vegetables" dietary advice. The research covers anti-inflammatory, antihistamine, cardiovascular, and antiviral mechanisms — making it one of the most versatile natural anti-inflammatory compounds available. The key purchase consideration is bioavailability — plain quercetin is poorly absorbed, and the right formulation makes a substantial difference.
