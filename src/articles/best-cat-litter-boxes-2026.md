## Litter Box Setup Rules

- **Placement:** Quiet, accessible, away from food/water
- **Rule of thumb:** 1 box per cat + 1 extra
- **Scoop frequency:** At minimum daily — odor comes from decomposing waste, not the box itself
- **Replace litter:** Fully empty and clean with unscented soap monthly
- **Depth:** 2–3 inches of litter minimum
