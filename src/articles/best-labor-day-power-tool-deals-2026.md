## Why Labor Day Is a Real Power Tool Sale Event

Unlike mattress "sales" that run year-round, Labor Day power tool discounts are tied to quarterly inventory cycles at Home Depot, Lowe's, and Amazon. Retailers receive new SKUs in September and October for the holiday gifting season, so summer-season models need to clear. Combo kits (drill + driver + batteries) are especially discounted because they carry high per-unit cost and take up warehouse space.

## What to Skip at Labor Day Tool Sales

- **Cordless tool kits without batteries listed** — "bare tool" kits save money but require batteries you may not have. Read listings carefully.
- **Off-brand tools (Avid, Teccpo, Artigiano)** — These routinely use inferior motors that burn out quickly. Pay for a brand name.
- **240V/corded tools** — Labor Day doesn't typically discount corded tools meaningfully; those are a Black Friday category.
- **Air compressors above $200** — Unless you're using it daily, the portability vs. capacity tradeoff usually isn't worth it.
