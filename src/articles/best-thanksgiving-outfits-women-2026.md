## Colors That Work for Thanksgiving

**Fail-safe fall palette:** Burgundy, rust, camel, forest green, chocolate brown, mustard, plum, navy

**What to avoid:** White and cream (gravy happens), hot pink, tropical prints, anything that reads "summer"

**The power move:** Match one element to the season (burgundy dress, rust blouse, olive blazer) and let the rest be neutral.

---

## What NOT to Wear

**Tight waistbands:** Avoid fitted pencil skirts, stiff high-rise jeans with no stretch, or structured bodices without give. You will regret it.

**Very light colors:** White, cream, and pale gray are magnet colors for gravy, red wine, and cranberry sauce.

**New shoes:** Never debut new shoes at Thanksgiving. You'll be on your feet for hours.

**Completely unrestricted waistbands (elastic-only):** The opposite extreme — full-elastic sweats read casual even if that's what you want. The knit set solves this.
