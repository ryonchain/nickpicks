## When Is Amazon Big Deal Days 2026?

Amazon has held its fall Prime Day event in **mid-October** for the past three years. Based on that pattern, expect **October 14–15, 2026** (Tuesday–Wednesday). Deals typically go live a week early with "early access" pricing and peak on the event days themselves.

**Key dates to watch:**

## Should You Wait for Black Friday Instead?

**Short answer: Big Deal Days often beats Black Friday on TVs.**

Historically, TV deals in October are just as deep as November — sometimes deeper because manufacturers are clearing pre-holiday inventory. Black Friday adds more competition from doorbuster deals, but specific models often see their lowest prices earlier.

**Rule of thumb:**
- If a TV you want drops 20%+ on Big Deal Days — buy it
- If it drops less than 15% — wait and see what Black Friday brings
- If it's out of stock on Big Deal Days — the Black Friday price will likely be similar but units will be tight

## What to Avoid

- **8K TVs** — Content ecosystem is still thin; not worth the premium
- **Last year's models at "50% off"** — Verify the original price; some retailers inflate MSRP
- **TVs without HDMI 2.1** — Required for PS5/Xbox 120Hz gaming; avoid budget TVs with HDMI 2.0 only
- **Smart TVs with obscure OS** — Stick to Google TV, Fire TV, or webOS for app availability
