## Filtration Technologies Compared

**Activated carbon:** Removes chlorine, taste, odor, VOCs. Common in pitcher filters. Does NOT remove lead, fluoride, PFAS, or bacteria reliably.

**Reverse osmosis (RO):** Forces water through a semi-permeable membrane; removes 99%+ of most contaminants including PFAS, lead, nitrates, arsenic, fluoride. Produces wastewater (3:1 to 1:1 ratio depending on system).

**Ultrafiltration (UF):** Removes bacteria, particles, some heavy metals but NOT dissolved solids, fluoride, or nitrates. No wastewater; maintains minerals.

## What Actually Matters

Test your water first. Your local utility's Consumer Confidence Report is published annually and shows contaminant levels. Target your filter choice at what's actually in your water — not just the worst-case contaminant list on the packaging.
