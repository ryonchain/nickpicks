## Water Bottle Cleaning Tips for School Year Sanity

The leading cause of parent complaints about kids' water bottles isn't leaking — it's smell. A water bottle used daily without complete cleaning develops a biofilm in the straw and lid that creates odor within 1–2 weeks.

**Daily cleaning minimum:** Rinse with warm water and dish soap, including running soapy water through the straw.

**Weekly deep clean:** Disassemble lid fully. Soak straw and gasket in white vinegar for 30 minutes. Run through dishwasher if parts are rated for it.

**Straw cleaning tools:**
- [OXO Small Bottle Brush Set](https://amazon.com/s?k=OXO+small+bottle+brush+straw+cleaner+set&tag=nickpicks-20) — fits most kids' straws
- [Full Circle Bottle Brush with Straw Cleaner](https://amazon.com/s?k=bottle+brush+with+straw+cleaner+kids&tag=nickpicks-20)

**Replace straws and gaskets annually** — they harbor bacteria regardless of cleaning once the surface begins breaking down.
