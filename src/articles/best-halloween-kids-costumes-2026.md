## Kids Costume Shopping Checklist

Before buying:
- [ ] Measure height, chest, and waist against size chart
- [ ] Check if the costume includes accessories or if they're sold separately
- [ ] Verify it's comfortable for wearing 2-3 hours of walking
- [ ] Look for machine-washable fabric (kids and candy are a messy combination)
- [ ] Check the return policy in case sizing is off
- [ ] Confirm delivery date before Halloween — don't rely on estimated delivery for character-specific costumes

- **Popular characters:** Order by September 30 — sizes sell out
