## Buying Guide

### Solving the Right Problem First

Before buying, identify which specific clutter problem you're solving:

- **Power strip and cable bundle** → CableBox-style enclosure
- **Cables running across the desk** → adhesive cable clips
- **Pens, scissors, and stationery** → mesh or compartmented desk organizer
- **Small accessories and loose items** → drawer unit
- **Papers and documents** → letter tray
- **Want warmth and natural aesthetics** → bamboo organizer with storage

Buying a general "desk organizer" without identifying the problem often results in a product that doesn't fit your specific chaos.

### Desk Aesthetics: Metal, Bamboo, or Acrylic?

| Material | Aesthetic | Best Pairing |
|---------|----------|-------------|
| Wire mesh | Industrial, minimal | Dark desk surfaces, metal accessories |
| Bamboo/wood | Warm, natural | Light wood desks, plant setups |
| Acrylic | Modern, transparent | Clean glass or white desk surfaces |
| Powder-coated metal | Clean, minimal | Black or white desk setups |

### Cable Management First

Before adding any desk organizer, tackle cables first. Visible cables undo every other organizational effort. Route cables before placing organizers so the cable clips and boxes don't conflict with organizer placement.

## FAQ

**What's the best cable management solution for a standing desk?**
Standing desks that move up and down need flexible cable management — rigid routes don't accommodate the height change. Use a cable sleeve (braided flexible tube that bundles cables together) from the desk surface down to the floor. Attach the sleeve to the desk leg with a velcro wrap. The sleeve extends and contracts with desk movement.

**How do I hide cables under a glass desk?**
Adhesive cable clips typically work on glass surfaces. Alternatively, a cable raceway (adhesive plastic channel) routes cables horizontally along the desk edge or underside. Cable boxes on the floor or on a shelf handle the power strip.

**Are bamboo desk organizers worth it?**
For people who care about desk aesthetics, yes — bamboo is more visually appealing than wire mesh or plastic at similar price points, and the material is durable and sustainable. The monitor stand versions add practical height-adjustment value.

**What size desk organizer do I need?**
Measure your available desk surface before buying. Most desk organizers are compact enough for a 24" wide workspace, but drawer units and multi-tier organizers can be surprisingly large. Always check dimensions before ordering.

**Can I use a desk organizer for a gaming setup?**
Absolutely. Cable management is especially important for gaming desks (mouse cable, headset cable, USB hub, charging cables). A CableBox for the power strip, adhesive clips for desk surface routing, and a minimalist pen holder for remotes and small accessories works well without cluttering the gaming aesthetic.
