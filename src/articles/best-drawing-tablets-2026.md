## How to Choose a Drawing Tablet

### Tablet-Only vs. Pen Display
- **Tablet only (no screen):** Lower cost, works with any monitor. Learning curve: your hand moves on the tablet while you look at the monitor. Most professionals adapt in 1–2 weeks.
- **Pen display (with screen):** More intuitive, higher cost. Draw directly where you see your artwork. Better for illustration and comics; not necessary for photo editing.

### Active Area Size
| Area Size | Best For |
|-----------|----------|
| Small (under 6") | Beginners, very limited desk space |
| Medium (6–9") | General illustration, photo editing, most uses |
| Large (10"+) | Large arm movement illustration, animation |

**Rule of thumb:** Match tablet active area to your monitor aspect ratio. A 16:9 1080p monitor pairs best with a 10" × 5.6" active area.

### Pressure Levels
8,192 levels is the current standard — any tablet at this spec is fine. 4,096 is acceptable for beginners. Below 4,096 starts to feel noticeably chunky in pressure transitions.

### Software Compatibility
- **Photoshop, Illustrator:** All tablets work; Wacom drivers are most stable
- **Clip Studio Paint:** Excellent with any brand
- **Procreate (iPad):** Use an iPad + Apple Pencil instead of a drawing tablet
- **Blender (3D sculpting):** Any pressure-sensitive tablet works

## FAQ

**Do I need a drawing tablet to draw digitally?**
No — you can use a mouse. But a tablet makes digital art dramatically more natural and enables pressure-sensitive brush strokes that mice can't replicate.

**Can I use a drawing tablet for photo editing?**
Yes. Photo editors (Lightroom, Photoshop) gain significantly from pen input — selecting, masking, and retouching are all more natural with a stylus than a mouse.

**Is an iPad + Apple Pencil a drawing tablet?**
Essentially yes — it's an integrated pen display that runs Procreate. For iPad users, this is often the best path; for desktop users, a separate drawing tablet is more flexible.

**Do Wacom tablets work on Mac?**
Yes. Wacom, Huion, and XP-Pen all support macOS. Driver quality is highest on Wacom across macOS versions.
