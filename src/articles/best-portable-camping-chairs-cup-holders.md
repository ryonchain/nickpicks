# Best Portable Camping Chairs with Cup Holders: Top Picks for Outdoor Comfort

A great camping chair is one of the most underrated pieces of outdoor gear. After miles of hiking or hours of fishing, sitting down in something comfortable — with your drink within arm's reach — makes all the difference. The cup holder is no small thing either: it keeps your coffee, beer, or water bottle from tipping over on uneven ground.

We've rounded up the five best portable camping chairs with cup holders available on Amazon, covering everything from ultralight backpacking options to full-featured base camp loungers.

---

## What to Look for in a Camping Chair

- **Weight and packed size** — If you're hiking in, ounces matter. Look for chairs under 3 lbs if portability is a priority.
- **Weight capacity** — Most camping chairs support 225–300 lbs. Heavy-duty options go up to 400–500 lbs.
- **Cup holder placement** — Armrest-mounted holders are the most convenient. Side pockets are a bonus.
- **Setup speed** — Snap-together poles versus fold-out designs differ significantly in ease of use.
- **Seat height** — Low-profile chairs are easier to pack; standard height is easier to get in and out of.
