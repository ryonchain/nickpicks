## What to Avoid in Budget Laptops

**4GB RAM:** In 2026, 4GB is not enough for comfortable use. Browser alone with 10 tabs exceeds available RAM. Minimum should be 8GB; 16GB is better.

**HDD storage:** If a laptop has a hard drive (HDD) instead of SSD, avoid it. Boot times of 2+ minutes and constant lag make the experience miserable regardless of processor.

**1366x768 display:** Low resolution makes text fuzzy and eye strain real for extended use. Minimum: 1920x1080 Full HD.

**eMMC storage:** Common on very cheap laptops — it's slow and non-upgradeable. Look for NVMe SSD.
