## What to Skip on Prime Day for Clothing

**Designer brands:** Nike, Under Armour, Lululemon, and similar performance brands rarely discount more than 20% — and often don't discount at all. Watch for third-party sellers marking up then discounting.

**Off-brand sizing gambles:** If you've never bought a brand before and sizing is unclear, Prime Day pressure creates bad impulse buys. Stick to brands with strong return policies.

**Formal occasion wear:** Wedding guest dresses, formal event clothing — these see modest discounts. Better saved for end-of-season clearance (August–September) for deeper cuts.
