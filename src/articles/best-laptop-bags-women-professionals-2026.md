## What to Look For

**Laptop size fit:** Most totes and backpacks are designed around 13" or 15.6" laptops. Measure your laptop (including case) before buying — a tight fit means the zipper strains on the compartment.

**Padded vs. unpadded sleeve:** Unpadded laptop "compartments" provide minimal drop protection. A padded sleeve with rigid back support is essential for protecting your machine.

**Weight:** An empty bag that weighs 2+ lbs adds significant daily carry load. Look for weights under 1.5 lbs empty when possible.

**TSA compliance:** If you travel for work, a lay-flat laptop compartment lets you pass security without removing your laptop at many TSA checkpoints. Worth having even for occasional travelers.
