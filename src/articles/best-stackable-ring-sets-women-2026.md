## What to Avoid

- **Zinc alloy as the base metal** — zinc causes green discoloration and skin reactions in many people. Look for brass or sterling silver bases.
- **Rings described only as "gold color" or "silver color"** — without specifying the plating type, you're getting the thinnest possible finish that will fade quickly.
- **One-size-fits-most ring designs** — adjustable rings that squeeze to size create pressure points and are uncomfortable for extended wear. Sized rings sit better.
- **Sets where all rings look identical** — the point of stacking is varied textures, thicknesses, and finishes. Sets with 5 of the same thin band give you no interesting stack.
