## TV Buying Checklist for Labor Day

Before you buy, verify:

- [ ] **HDMI 2.1 ports** — needed for PS5/Xbox Series X at 4K 120Hz
- [ ] **Refresh rate** — 60Hz for casual viewing; 120Hz for gaming
- [ ] **HDR support** — Dolby Vision is best; HDR10+ is Samsung-standard
- [ ] **Smart OS** — Google TV (TCL/Hisense), webOS (LG), Tizen (Samsung) are all solid
- [ ] **Warranty** — Many TVs come with 1 year; Best Buy Geek Squad adds 2–3 years

## Labor Day vs. Black Friday — When to Buy

| Timing | Typical Discount | Stock | Best For |

| Labor Day (Aug 30–Sept 1) | 20–40% off | Good | Current-year models |
| Black Friday (Nov) | 30–50% off | Varies | Prior-year closeouts |
| Prime Day (Oct) | 15–30% off | Good | Amazon-sold brands |

**Buy at Labor Day if:** You need it now, or you want 2026 models at a good price.  
**Wait for Black Friday if:** You want the deepest possible discount and don't mind older inventory.
