## What Apple Products to Skip This Prime Day

- **iPhone**: Apple does not discount current iPhone models. You will not find a real deal on an iPhone 16 or 16 Pro. Refurbished or previous-gen iPhones occasionally appear discounted from third-party sellers, but proceed with caution.
- **MacBook Pro**: The Pro line rarely drops more than $50–$100 during Prime Day. If you need a MacBook Pro, check Apple's own refurbished store for better value.
- **Apple Watch Ultra 2**: Drops exist, but at $649+ it's still a premium purchase. Unless you're a serious triathlete or backcountry hiker, the Series 10 is more than enough.
