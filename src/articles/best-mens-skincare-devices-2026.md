## What to Look For When Buying

**Cleansing first, then enhancement:** A sonic cleansing device (Foreo, PMD Clean) is the foundation for any skincare device stack. Add steaming or microdermabrasion after establishing a cleansing routine.

**Silicone vs. brush bristles:** Silicone (Foreo, PMD Clean) is more hygienic, more durable, and gentler. Nylon bristle brushes clean aggressively but harbor bacteria and wear out faster. For regular shavers, silicone is the safer choice.

**Post-shave timing:** Never use mechanical cleansing devices immediately after shaving. The 6–8 hour rule prevents irritation from applying mechanical friction to a compromised skin barrier.

**Consistency over intensity:** Any skincare device requires 4–6 weeks of consistent use to show results. One deep-cleansing session per week won't outperform a daily gentle cleanse.
