## Tips for Shipping a Care Package

**Ship directly from Amazon:** Save the box cost and assembly time by using Amazon's gift shipping directly to the campus address. Most items arrive in 2 days with Prime.

**Include a note:** Digital is fine, but a handwritten card hits differently.

**Check dorm restrictions:** Most dorms prohibit alcohol, aerosol sprays, and candles. Check the school's residence life website before sending anything that could be confiscated.

**Use their mailing address exactly:** Campus mail systems are specific about format. Get the full address including building name, room number, and student name as registered with the school.
