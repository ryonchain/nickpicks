## Battery Platform Guide: Pick an Ecosystem, Stay There

This is the most important decision you'll make in power tools. Batteries represent 25–40% of tool cost. Buying cross-brand means buying new batteries with every tool.

| Brand | Platform | Battery Volts | Compatibility |
|-------|----------|---------------|---------------|
| DeWalt | 20V MAX / FlexVolt | 20V, 60V | 20V MAX works in all 20V tools; FlexVolt works in 20V AND 60V |
| Milwaukee | M18 | 18V | Universal across entire M18 lineup |
| Makita | LXT | 18V | Largest LXT catalog; backward compatible to original LXT |
| Ridgid | 18V | 18V | Lifetime Service Agreement on batteries |
| Ryobi | ONE+ | 18V | Largest ecosystem for casual users; 175+ tools |

**Our recommendation:** If you're just starting, choose DeWalt (largest professional ecosystem), Milwaukee (best brushless motors), or Ridgid (best warranty). Avoid mixing brands.
