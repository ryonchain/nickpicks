## What to Look For

**Capacity:** 6-quart is standard for families of 4–5. 8-quart for batch cooking or large families.

**Modes:** Most households only use pressure cook, slow cook, and sauté regularly. More modes = higher price.

**Ease of sealing:** Poor sealing rings are the #1 failure point. Instant Pot's lids seal more reliably than competitors in our testing.

**Inner pot material:** Stainless steel is more durable and dishwasher-safe. Non-stick coats degrade over time.
