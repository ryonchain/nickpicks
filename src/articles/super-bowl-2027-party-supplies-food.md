## Super Bowl Party Checklist

**Food & Drink**
- [ ] Wings (2–3 per person, minimum)
- [ ] Dips: queso, guacamole, salsa, hummus
- [ ] Chips, crackers, pretzels
- [ ] Slider station or chili pot
- [ ] Vegetable tray
- [ ] Beer/wine + non-alcoholic options
- [ ] Ice (more than you think — 1 lb per person)

**Serving**
- [ ] Chafing dishes for hot food
- [ ] Serving bowls for dips
- [ ] Tongs, spoons, napkins
- [ ] Plates, cups, forks

**Entertainment**
- [ ] Super Bowl squares board set up before guests arrive
- [ ] Bingo cards printed
- [ ] Trivia cards for halftime
- [ ] TV tuned and tested an hour early

**Logistics**
- [ ] Extra trash bags visible (not just in the kitchen)
- [ ] Chairs and seating arranged before guests arrive
- [ ] Parking plan for 5+ cars
