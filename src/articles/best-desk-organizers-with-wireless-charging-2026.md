## Buying Guide: Wireless Charging Desk Organizers

### Qi vs MagSafe: Which Do You Need?

**Qi:** Universal standard. Works with all Qi-compatible phones (most iPhones, Android phones). Charges at 5W–15W depending on phone and charger support. Best for multi-device households or Android users.

**MagSafe:** iPhone 12+ exclusive. Charges at up to 15W and aligns magnetically so the phone snaps into position. Only relevant if you use an iPhone 12 or newer as your primary phone.

### Watts Matter: Charging Speed at Your Desk

| Charging Rate | Time to 50% (iPhone) | Time to 50% (Samsung) |
|--------------|---------------------|----------------------|
| 5W Qi | ~2 hours | ~1.5 hours |
| 10W Qi | ~1 hour | ~1 hour |
| 15W Qi/MagSafe | ~45 minutes | ~45 minutes |

At desk use, you're charging over 4–8 hours, so even 5W is usually sufficient for maintaining charge throughout the day. 15W is only meaningfully faster if you frequently need to go from 0% to 80% quickly.

### Storage Features to Look For

**Pen/pencil holder:** Keeps writing tools off the desk surface and in arm's reach.
**Drawer or hidden compartment:** Ideal for storing USB drives, small cables, business cards.
**Phone stand position:** Allows you to see notifications and use your phone during video calls without picking it up.
**Cable management clips:** Route desk cables to the organizer rather than crossing the desk surface.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Is wireless charging safe for my phone to do daily?**
Yes. Wireless charging doesn't damage phone batteries more than wired charging. The slight heat generated during Qi charging is within normal operating range for all major phone brands.

**Can I charge through a MagSafe case wirelessly?**
MagSafe cases pass through both MagSafe and Qi charging. Non-MagSafe cases work with Qi charging pads on all organizers. Metal cases or cases with magnetic closures may block charging.

**Do these organizers work internationally?**
Check the power adapter input voltage. Most include adapters rated 100–240V (worldwide compatible), but verify before traveling with the unit.
