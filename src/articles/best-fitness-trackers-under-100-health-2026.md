## What to Look For

**Heart rate accuracy during exercise** matters more than step count accuracy. Look for brands with published accuracy data. Fitbit and Garmin are the most rigorously validated. Amazfit and Xiaomi have improved but are still catching up.

**App quality** determines how useful your data is. Fitbit and Garmin apps are the best at turning raw data into actionable insights (Daily Readiness, Body Battery). Xiaomi's app is functional but less polished.

**Battery life vs. features** is the core trade-off under $100. Longer-battery devices (Amazfit, Xiaomi) sacrifice advanced health algorithms. Shorter-battery devices (Fitbit, Garmin) invest in better sensors and analysis.
