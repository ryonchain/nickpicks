## Buying Guide: What to Look For

**MagSafe vs. standard Qi.** MagSafe delivers 15W to iPhone 12+ vs. 7.5W on standard Qi pads. If you use an iPhone 12 or newer, MagSafe matters — you're leaving ~50% charging speed on the table without it. For Android phones, MagSafe is irrelevant; Samsung's 15W fast wireless requires a Samsung Fast Charge certified pad.

**Apple Watch fast charge.** Apple Watch Series 7+ supports fast charge (0–80% in 45 min instead of ~90 min). Only Apple-certified chargers support this. Belkin's stations support it; Anker and ESR do not currently.

**Number of simultaneous devices.** Confirm the station charges all three zones at the same time, not in a shared-power round-robin. All stations on this list support true simultaneous charging.

**Power adapter included vs. separate.** Most stations ship with a USB-C cable only — you need a 20W+ USB-C adapter to power them at full speed. The Belkin Pro ($100) includes its own adapter; others assume you have one. Factor this into the cost comparison.
