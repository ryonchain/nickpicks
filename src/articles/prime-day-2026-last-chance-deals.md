## What Happens After Prime Day Ends

Prices on most products reset to normal by midnight tonight. A small number of items maintain reduced pricing into the post-Prime Day "extended sale" window (48–72 hours), but those are unpredictable and the discounts are rarely as deep.

The next major discount window for most of these categories is **Labor Day (September 1)** for home and appliances, and **Black Friday (November 28)** for electronics.

If you see a deal that meets your budget tonight — buy it. The "I'll wait for Black Friday" strategy works sometimes, but Prime Day often matches or beats Black Friday on specific categories, especially Amazon devices, audio, and small appliances.
