## Application Tips

**Emulsify before applying:** Work product between palms for 5–10 seconds until it's warm and evenly spread. Never apply product directly from container to one section of hair.

**Work from the back:** Apply from the nape forward, distributing evenly through the back and sides before the top. Most men over-concentrate product on the visible top section and under-distribute in the back.

**Fine-tune with fingers:** After initial distribution, use fingertips to define texture, push hair into shape, and separate. A comb or brush adds polish; fingers add texture.

**Less product + build = more product at once:** If your first application isn't enough hold, rub a touch more between fingertips and add to specific areas. Applying too much at once is harder to recover from.
