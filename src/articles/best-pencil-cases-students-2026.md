## What to Look for by Grade Level

**K–2nd grade:** Hard shell case with simple interior. Holds 10–15 colored pencils and a few supplies. Doesn't matter if it gets dropped.

**3rd–5th grade:** Soft zip pouch with 2 compartments. Large enough for colored pencils, scissors, and a ruler. Machine washable.

**6th–8th grade:** Medium standing case or large zip pouch. Need to hold multiple highlighters, pens in different colors, correction tape, and a few pencils.

**9th–12th grade:** Large standing case or organized pouch. AP/advanced classes mean more supplies. Look for capacity over aesthetics, unless aesthetics matters for motivation.
