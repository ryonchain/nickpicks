## Puppy vs. Adult vs. Senior Food

- **Puppy:** Higher protein and calcium for growth. Look for "growth" or "all life stages" on the AAFCO statement.
- **Adult (1–7 years):** Maintenance formula. Balanced protein and fat.
- **Senior (7+ years):** Lower calories, joint support (glucosamine/chondroitin), easier-to-chew texture.

## Should You Go Grain-Free?

Only if your vet has identified a grain allergy (confirmed by elimination diet). Most dogs don't need grain-free food, and the FDA investigation into DCM links is ongoing. Stick with proven brands like Purina Pro Plan unless you have a specific medical reason.
