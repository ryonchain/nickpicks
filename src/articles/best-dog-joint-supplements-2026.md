## Additional Ingredients to Look For

**Omega-3 Fatty Acids (EPA/DHA):** Have strong evidence for reducing joint inflammation. Best provided by fish oil (separate supplement). Look for 500–2,000mg EPA+DHA per day depending on dog size.

**Boswellia Serrata:** Herbal anti-inflammatory with some clinical evidence in dogs. A good addition for dogs with moderate pain who aren't candidates for NSAIDs.

**Turmeric/Curcumin:** Growing evidence base for reducing inflammatory cytokines. Most effective in a bioenhanced form — raw turmeric has poor bioavailability.

**Hyaluronic Acid:** Joint lubricant that helps maintain synovial fluid quality. More relevant for dogs with significant joint fluid changes.

## Signs Your Dog Needs Joint Support

- Reluctance to jump, climb stairs, or get up from lying position
- Stiffness after rest that improves with movement
- Lagging behind on walks or tiring more quickly
- Licking, chewing, or favoring a specific limb
- Visible swelling or heat around a joint
- Behavior changes: irritability, reluctance to be touched
