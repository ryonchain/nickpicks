## What to Look for in Budget TWS Earbuds

**ANC effectiveness:** At sub-$100, ANC quality varies enormously. Test in your actual noise environment — office HVAC and transit noise respond differently than construction or crowds. Sony and Soundcore lead at this tier.

**Fit and seal:** The acoustic seal between the ear tip and your ear canal is the single most important factor for both sound quality and ANC effectiveness. Always try multiple tip sizes before judging sound or ANC performance.

**Battery life:** Look for 6+ hours per charge and 24+ hours total with case. Quick-charge (10 minutes = 1–2 hours) is valuable for daily commuters.

**IP rating:** IPX4 handles sweat and light rain. IP55 provides additional dust protection. For serious gym use, prioritize IPX5 or higher.
