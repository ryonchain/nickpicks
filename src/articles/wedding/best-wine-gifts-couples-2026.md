### 3. Coravin Model Two Wine System — Best Preservation Gift

**Price:** ~$199–$229 | [Check price on Amazon →](AMAZON_LINK_CORAVIN){rel="nofollow sponsored" target="_blank"}

The Coravin is the game-changing wine gift for wine enthusiasts. The system uses a surgical-grade needle to pierce the cork without removing it, draws out wine under argon gas pressure, and replaces the extracted wine with argon — an inert gas that doesn't react with wine. The cork naturally reseals when the needle is removed.

**The result:** A couple can pour one glass of a $60 bottle of wine and store the rest for 6 weeks, 6 months, or longer without the wine oxidizing or degrading.

**Who the Coravin is for:**
- Couples who buy better bottles but want to enjoy them over time
- One partner drinks red, the other white — no more wasting the bottle
- Building a wine collection and wanting to sample without committing to a full bottle

**Who it's NOT for:** Casual wine drinkers who finish a bottle in one sitting. The Coravin's value is in the selective preservation — if you always finish the bottle, you don't need it.

**Best for:** Enthusiast wine couples ($100+ bottles in their rotation), wine collectors, or anyone who winces at opening a good bottle for just one glass.
