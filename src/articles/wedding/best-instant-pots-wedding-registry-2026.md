### 3. Instant Pot Pro Crisp + Air Fryer (8 Qt) — Best Combo Unit

**Price:** ~$179–$199 | [Check price on Amazon →](AMAZON_LINK_INSTANT_POT_PRO_CRISP){rel="nofollow sponsored" target="_blank"}

The Pro Crisp combines the Pro's pressure cooking capabilities with a swappable air fryer lid. Pressure cook chicken thighs, swap lids, and air-fry to crispy in the same pot. For couples who want both functions without two separate appliances, it's compelling.

**The real-world tradeoff:**
- Air fryer basket holds about 4 servings — less than a dedicated air fryer

- Better value than buying Pro + separate air fryer

**Best for:** Couples with limited counter space who want pressure cook + air fry without two appliances. The compromise between functionality and footprint.
