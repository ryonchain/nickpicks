## What Makes a Great Wedding Gift

The best registry gifts share three traits: they get used regularly, they're better than what the couple already owns, and they're in a price range that doesn't require guilt about cost. Kitchen appliances, quality bedding, and cookware hit all three. Skip the decorative frames and novelty items — couples keep appliances for decades.

## What to Avoid

**Decorative items** — couples' tastes change, and decorative pieces often don't survive the first move. Kitchen appliances and bedding are safer universally.

**Off-registry gifts** — there's a reason they made a list. If you want to go off-registry, cash via a honeymoon fund is always welcome.

**Cheap duplicates of registry items** — if they've registered for a Le Creuset Dutch oven, a $30 version isn't a gift, it's a miss.
