**Bottom line:** The **Breville Barista Express** (~$700) is the best single-machine investment for espresso-drinking couples — built-in grinder, cafe-quality espresso, and eliminates the $6/day coffee shop habit. ## Coffee Maker Categories

**Drip machines:** Automatic, programmable, makes 8–12 cups. Best for couples who drink regular brewed coffee. $50–$500.

**Espresso machines (semi-automatic):** You grind, tamp, and pull shots; machine heats water and drives it through the puck. Best for couples who want barista-quality espresso. $300–$2,000.

**Fully automatic espresso machines:** Grind-to-cup in one machine at the push of a button. Easier to use than semi-automatic. $500–$1,500.

**Pour-over systems:** Manual brewing with kettle and dripper. Produces exceptional single-origin coffee. No moving parts, inexpensive, but requires technique. $50–$300 for full setup.
