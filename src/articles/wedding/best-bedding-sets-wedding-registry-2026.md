## Bedding Glossary

**Percale:** Crisp, cool, matte finish. Weave goes over/under one thread at a time. Best for warm sleepers or those who prefer hotel-crisp sheets.

**Sateen:** Silky, smooth, slight sheen. Weave goes over 4, under 1. Warmer, softer feel than percale. Best for cold sleepers or those who love silky texture.

**Linen:** Woven from flax fibers. Starts rough, softens dramatically over time. Breathable and temperature-regulating. Lasts 20–30 years vs. 5–10 for cotton.

**Thread count:** Less important than marketing suggests. 300–500 TC long-staple cotton > 1000 TC short-staple cotton.
