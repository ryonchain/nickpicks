# The Complete Wedding Registry Checklist 2026

The most strategic wedding registry isn't just a wishlist — it's a considered plan for building out a household. Here is the room-by-room, priority-ordered checklist that captures everything a couple needs in their first years together.

---

## Registry Strategy Before the List

**Register 2–3x guest count:** 100 guests = 200–300 registry items. Ensures every guest can find something at their budget.

**Spread across price tiers:**
- 20% items under $50
- 40% items $50–$150
- 25% items $150–$300
- 15% items $300+

**Use multiple registries:** Amazon + one specialty (Williams Sonoma, Crate & Barrel) + one department store (Target, Macy's, Pottery Barn) covers all shopping preferences.

**Mark group gift items:** Flag high-ticket items ($300+) as group gift suggestions so guests know they're intended for pooled contributions.

Typical honeymoon fund contributions: $50–$200 per couple, $25–$100 per individual.
