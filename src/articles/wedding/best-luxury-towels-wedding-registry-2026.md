## Towel Fabric Guide

**Turkish cotton (long-staple):** Dries faster than Egyptian cotton, becomes progressively softer with washing, excellent absorbency. Industry gold standard for luxury towels.

**Egyptian cotton:** Very soft from first use, excellent absorbency, dense pile. Dries slightly slower than Turkish. Premium positioning, slightly heavier feel.

**Organic cotton:** GOTS-certified cotton grown without pesticides. Same softness as conventional long-staple cotton with sustainability credentials.

**Waffle/honeycomb weave:** Textured, highly absorbent, and quick-drying. More casual aesthetic, less plush feel than traditional terry. Fast-drying for humid bathrooms.

**Linen:** The most breathable, fastest-drying option. Minimal lint. Develops texture over time. Best for warm climates.

## How to Build Your Towel Registry

**Recommended set (for two people):**
- 8 bath towels (2 per person + 4 spare/guest)
- 6 hand towels (3 per bathroom)
- 6 washcloths
- 2 bath sheets (oversized towels for those who prefer them)
- 2 bath mats (coordinating color)

**Color strategy:** Register in a single neutral color — white, cream, or light grey — that works across any future bathroom renovation. Bold colors lock you into a specific aesthetic.

**Monogramming:** Many registry platforms offer monogramming. Towels with the couple's monogram or last initial make gifts feel more personal and elevate the presentation.
