## Reishi Research: What the Science Shows

**Sleep studies:**
- A 2012 controlled trial showed reishi extract significantly improved total sleep time, sleep efficiency, and non-REM sleep compared to placebo in adults with neurasthenia
- Ganoderic acids interact with the GABAergic system, which helps explain the sleep and anti-anxiety effects

**Immune function:**
- Beta-glucans in reishi stimulate natural killer (NK) cell activity and macrophage function
- A 2006 Cochrane-reviewed meta-analysis found that cancer patients taking reishi alongside conventional treatment showed improved immune markers and quality of life

**Adaptogenic/stress:**
- Reishi reduces cortisol response in animal models and shows anti-anxiety effects in human studies
- The triterpene ganoderic acid A inhibits histamine release, providing mild anti-inflammatory effects

**Liver protection:**
- Studies show ganoderic acids protect against liver cell damage and may support detoxification processes — explaining traditional use for liver conditions

**Who benefits most from reishi:**
- People with chronic stress or sleep issues
- Those seeking general immune support during high-stress periods
- Anyone taking it as part of a functional mushroom rotation
