## Melatonin Do's and Don'ts

**Do:**
- Start with the lowest effective dose (0.3–1mg)
- Take it 30–60 minutes before bed
- Use in a dark room — light exposure undermines melatonin's effect
- Use for jet lag, shift work, or specific sleep scheduling issues

**Don't:**
- Take 5–10mg unless you have a specific reason
- Use nightly indefinitely without breaks
- Expect melatonin to sedate you like a prescription sleep aid — it signals sleep, it doesn't force it
- Give children adult doses (pediatric melatonin products are specifically dosed for children)

The best melatonin supplement is a low-dose, third-party tested product taken at the right time. For most people, 0.5–3mg taken 30 minutes before bed is sufficient — and switching from a 10mg product to a 1mg product often resolves the morning grogginess problem entirely.
