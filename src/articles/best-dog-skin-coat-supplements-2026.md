## Understanding Coat Problems by Symptom

**Excessive shedding:** Priority intervention — high-dose omega-3 (EPA+DHA). Rule out thyroid disease (bloodwork) if no response in 8 weeks.

**Dry, flaky skin:** Omega-3 first. Also check bathing frequency (over-bathing strips natural oils), shampoo type (use gentle oatmeal-based), and home humidity level.

**Dull, brittle coat:** Combination of omega-3 and biotin. Check protein quality of food (coat requires complete amino acids).

**Itching, hot spots, redness:** Could be allergic dermatitis — omega-3 helps, but identify and eliminate allergen (food or environmental). Vet evaluation important.

**Patchy hair loss:** Requires vet exam — could be mange, ringworm, or hormonal issues. Not supplement-addressable alone.

**Zinc-responsive dermatosis:** Thick, crusty skin on face and paws, often in northern breeds. Requires zinc supplementation specifically.
