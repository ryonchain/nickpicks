## Black Friday Kitchen Buying Tips

1. **KitchenAid stand mixers in popular colors sell out fast** — if you see your color at the sale price, buy immediately. Don't wait to compare.
2. **Vitamix Black Friday prices are rare — don't skip them** — Vitamix discounts are unusual. A $250 Vitamix E310 should be purchased on sight.
3. **Instant Pot deals often come early** — check for Instant Pot deals starting the first week of November, not just Black Friday.
4. **Read the return policy on large appliances** — KitchenAid and Breville have 30-day returns at major retailers; check before you buy.
5. **"Doorbuster" air fryers at Walmart may be downgraded models** — verify the exact model number matches the one you researched; Black Friday exclusive SKUs sometimes have reduced specs.
