## Coffee Maker Buying Guide

**Drip vs. single-serve:** Drip machines are better value for households drinking 2+ cups per day. Single-serve machines are best for households with mixed preferences (one person wants dark roast, another wants decaf).

**SCA certification:** The Specialty Coffee Association certifies machines that brew at the correct temperature and contact time. If coffee quality matters, look for this certification.

**Thermal vs. glass carafe:** Thermal carafes keep coffee hot without a hot plate that can burn coffee. Always choose thermal if you'll let coffee sit for more than 20 minutes.

**Grinder pairings:** Freshly ground beans dramatically improve any brewer. The Baratza Encore is the entry-level grinder most enthusiasts recommend.
