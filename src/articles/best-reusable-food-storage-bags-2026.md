## Reusable Food Storage Bag Buying Guide

### Silicone vs. Fabric vs. PEVA

**Platinum Silicone (Stasher, Zip Top, Lekue):**
- Best overall material — chemically inert, no leaching, no BPA or petrochemicals
- Safe for microwave, boiling, sous vide, freezer, and dishwasher
- Most durable — lasts 5+ years with proper care
- Highest upfront cost

**PEVA (Russbe, Bumkins lining):**
- Chlorine-free PVC alternative — safer than PVC but still plastic-derived
- Not microwave safe
- More affordable than silicone
- Good transition option — works like disposable zip-locks

**Nylon/Polyester Fabric (outer layer of Bumkins):**
- Best for dry snacks only
- Machine washable
- Wide variety of patterns and designs
- Not suitable for wet foods

### What to Store in Reusable Bags

| Food Type | Best Bag Type |
|---|---|
| Sandwiches | Stasher, Zip Top, or Russbe |
| Dry snacks (crackers, nuts) | Bumkins or Russbe |
| Frozen meat or vegetables | Stasher, Zip Top, or Lekue Freezer |
| Marinated meat | Stasher or Zip Top (silicone handles acids) |
| Soups and liquid foods | Stasher or Zip Top (airtight seal) |
| Sous vide | Stasher or Zip Top only |

### How Many to Buy

For an average household of 2:
- 4–6 sandwich/medium-size bags covers daily lunches
- 2–4 snack bags for smaller portions
- 2 freezer bags for batch cooking storage

Start with a mixed set and adjust based on what you actually reach for.

### Cleaning Reusable Bags

The main downside of silicone bags is cleaning. Tips:
- Turn inside out for thorough cleaning
- Dishwasher-safe silicone bags clean well on the top rack
- For garlic or onion odors, soak in a baking soda/water solution
- Air dry fully before closing to prevent moisture buildup

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Are reusable food bags safe for food storage?

Yes — platinum silicone (used in Stasher and Zip Top) is one of the safest food contact materials available, with no chemical leaching even at high temperatures. PEVA bags (Russbe, Bumkins) are free from chlorine and BPA. Avoid bags that don't specify their material or list PVC on the label.

### Can silicone bags go in the microwave?

Yes — 100% platinum silicone bags (Stasher, Zip Top) are microwave safe. Open the closure before microwaving to allow steam to vent. Fabric bags and PEVA bags are not microwave safe.

### How many times can a reusable bag be used before it wears out?

Quality silicone bags last hundreds to thousands of uses with proper care — effectively years of daily use. PEVA bags last less long (1–2 years with daily use) before the zip mechanism or material degrades. Fabric bags are machine-washable and can last 2–5 years.

### Do reusable bags seal as well as disposable zip-locks?

Silicone bags with pinch-loc or zip closures seal as airtight as disposable zip-locks or better. PEVA zip-style bags (Russbe) match zip-lock performance closely. The Stasher Pinch-Loc is particularly reliable for storing liquid-heavy foods.

### What's the most eco-friendly reusable bag material?

Platinum silicone is the most eco-friendly food storage bag material: made from silica (derived from sand), it's chemically inert, extremely long-lasting, and doesn't leach chemicals. At end of life, it's less recyclable than metal or glass but far better than single-use plastic. Fabric bags using organic cotton and natural dyes are the most biodegradable option for dry-snack use.
