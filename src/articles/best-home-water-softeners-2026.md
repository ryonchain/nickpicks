# Best Home Water Softeners in 2026

Hard water (high calcium and magnesium content) causes scale buildup in pipes, water heaters, and appliances — reducing their lifespan significantly. It also affects soap lathering and leaves spots on dishes and glass. Water softeners work via ion exchange: calcium/magnesium ions are replaced with sodium ions, which don't cause scaling.

## Before You Buy

**Test your water hardness:** Home test kits (~$10) measure GPG (grains per gallon). 
- Soft: 0–3 GPG
- Moderately hard: 3–7 GPG
- Hard: 7–10 GPG
- Very hard: 10–14 GPG
- Extremely hard: 14+ GPG

**Sizing guide:**
- Daily water use ≈ 75–100 gallons per person
- Tank capacity needed = (hardness in GPG) × (daily gallons) × (days between regeneration)
- For a 4-person family using 300 gallons/day at 15 GPG = 4,500 grains/day. A 32,000-grain tank regenerates every ~7 days.
