## Cyber Monday Laptop Buying Tips

### 1. Prioritize RAM and Storage

Don't buy a laptop with less than **16GB RAM** in 2026 — 8GB will bottleneck performance within 2 years. Storage minimum: **256GB SSD** (512GB preferred).

### 2. Verify the Exact Spec Configuration

Retailers sometimes list the same model at different prices because they're different configurations. Check the RAM/SSD/GPU specs carefully before comparing prices.

### 3. MacBook Refurbished = Best Value

Apple's certified refurbished store (apple.com/shop/refurbished) sells previous-gen MacBooks at 15–20% off year-round — often better value than Black Friday discounts on current models.

### 4. Check Return Policies

Most retailers extend return windows during the holiday season. Best Buy and Amazon typically offer 30-day returns on laptops bought during Cyber Monday.
