## Why ANC Headphones Matter

Active noise canceling works by using microphones to detect ambient sound and generating inverse sound waves that cancel noise before it reaches your ears. The difference between cheap and premium ANC:
- **Cheap ANC:** muffles low frequencies (AC hum), but voices and higher frequencies still bleed through
- **Premium ANC:** cancels across all frequencies; you hear almost nothing at moderate volumes

## What to Look For

**ANC strength:** Measured in decibels of noise reduction. Premium cans cut 20–30dB; budget cut 10–20dB.

**Battery life:** 20 hours minimum if you commute daily. Quick charge matters for forgetful users.

**Multipoint:** Lets you connect to phone + laptop simultaneously — worth it for remote workers.

**Codec support:** LDAC (Sony) and aptX (others) enable higher-quality Bluetooth audio from compatible devices.
