# Best Streaming Sticks 2026

*Disclosure: NickPicks earns from qualifying Amazon purchases at no extra cost to you.*

A streaming stick turns any TV with an HDMI port into a smart TV — and often outperforms built-in smart TV software that manufacturers stop updating after 2–3 years. The choice between Amazon Fire TV, Roku, and Google Chromecast comes down to your existing ecosystem, your smart home setup, and whether you want a remote or prefer phone-based control. Here's how they stack up in 2026.

**Quick picks:**
- **Best overall**: Amazon Fire TV Stick 4K Max — fastest Fire TV, Wi-Fi 6E, Alexa integration
- **Best Roku**: Roku Streaming Stick 4K+ — excellent UI, private listening via app, no subscription required
- **Best Google**: Google TV Streamer (4K) — replaced Chromecast, Google Assistant, Google TV interface
- **Best budget**: Amazon Fire TV Stick Lite — full Fire TV experience at $30
- **Best for neutral users**: Roku Express 4K+ — works equally well across all streaming services, no ecosystem lock-in

## HDR Format Guide

| Format | Supported By | What It Does |
|--------|-------------|-------------|
| HDR10 | All models | Base HDR — wider brightness range |
| HDR10+ | Fire TV 4K Max | Dynamic metadata adjusts per scene |
| Dolby Vision | Roku 4K+, Google TV | Higher quality dynamic HDR |
| HLG | Most 4K models | Live broadcast HDR |

Dolby Vision produces noticeably better results than HDR10 on compatible TVs. For the best HDR experience, prioritize Dolby Vision support.

## Do You Actually Need 4K?

Only if: (1) you have a 4K TV, (2) you watch content in 4K (Netflix, Disney+, Amazon Prime Video all offer it), and (3) you sit close enough to benefit. For TVs 40" and under at typical viewing distances, 4K vs 1080p is difficult to perceive. For 55" and larger TVs at normal viewing distance, 4K is noticeably sharper.

**If your TV is 1080p:** The Fire TV Stick Lite at $30 is the right call. Don't pay for 4K you can't display.

**If your TV is 4K:** Any of the 4K models here are worth the $10–20 premium.
