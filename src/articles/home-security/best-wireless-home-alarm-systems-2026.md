## Self-Monitoring vs. Professional Monitoring

**Self-monitoring:** You receive app alerts and decide whether to call 911 yourself. Free with most systems. Adequate if you're responsive to your phone and willing to triage alerts personally.

**Professional monitoring:** A 24/7 monitoring center receives your alarm, calls you to confirm, and dispatches police/fire if you don't respond. Typically $15–30/month. Recommended if you travel frequently, sleep heavily, or want guaranteed response if your phone is unreachable.

**Cellular backup:** Critical — if an intruder cuts your internet or power, a system without cellular backup goes silent. All top-tier systems (SimpliSafe, Ring Pro, Cove) include cellular backup in their monitoring plans.

## What Should Your System Include?

**Minimum kit:**
- Base station with siren
- Keypad for arming/disarming
- 2–3 entry sensors (front door, back door, garage entry)
- 1 motion detector (main living area)

**Recommended additions:**
- Additional entry sensors for all ground-floor windows and doors
- Smoke and CO detectors
- Flood/freeze sensors for basement or utility room
- Indoor camera for visual verification before dispatching police
