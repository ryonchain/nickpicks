## Key Buying Considerations

**Installation type:** Retrofit locks (August, Level) mount on your existing hardware. Full replacement locks (Schlage, Yale, Ultraloq) replace the entire deadbolt and are more secure.

**Hub requirement:** Schlage Encode, August Wi-Fi, and Ultraloq all have built-in Wi-Fi — no hub needed. Z-Wave and Zigbee locks require a compatible hub (SmartThings, Hubitat, etc.) but are more reliable on home networks.

**Access codes:** Look for 50+ code storage with scheduling. Time-limited guest codes for housekeepers, dog walkers, or short-term rental guests are a practical necessity.

**ANSI/BHMA grade:** Grade 1 (Schlage) is the highest residential rating. Grade 2 (most others) is solid but less tamper-resistant.
