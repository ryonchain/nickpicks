## Retrofit vs. Full Replacement: Which Do You Need?

**Choose a retrofit smart controller if:** Your existing garage door opener works fine and you just want to add remote control and alerts. Meross, Tailwind, and myQ Smart Garage Control all do this for under $30–50.

**Choose a full smart opener replacement if:** Your opener is over 10 years old, lacks safety auto-reverse, or you want built-in camera and battery backup. Chamberlain's B4643T and B550 are the top picks.

---

## Security Note

A smart garage door raises a security question: what happens if someone intercepts your app signal? Modern smart openers use 128-bit AES encryption for commands. The bigger risk is leaving the door open by accident — which smart controllers solve via auto-close timers and open alerts.
