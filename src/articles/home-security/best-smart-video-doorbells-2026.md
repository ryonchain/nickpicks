## What to Look For

**Power source:** Wired doorbells are more reliable (no charging), but require existing low-voltage wiring. Battery-powered models are easier to install but need recharging every 1–3 months.

**Subscription cost:** Ring Protect Basic runs $4.99/month per device. Google Nest Aware is $8/month. Eufy offers truly free local storage. Factor in the ongoing cost when comparing prices.

**Field of view:** A head-to-toe (1:1 or taller) field of view is critical for package detection at your doorstep. Standard widescreen FOV misses packages left on the ground.

**Integration:** Ring = Alexa/Amazon. Nest = Google Home. Arlo = cross-platform (HomeKit, Google, Alexa). Match your smart home ecosystem.
