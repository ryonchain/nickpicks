## Placement Tips for Maximum Deterrence

**Front entry and porch:** Mount 9–10 feet high, aimed at the path approaching your door. A 180° sensor coverage works best here.

**Garage and driveway:** A dual-head floodlight at 3,000+ lumens covers the full driveway width. Aim one head at the driveway, one at the garage door.

**Side yards and gates:** Solar or battery-powered lights work well here where running wire is difficult. Prioritize consistent motion coverage over maximum brightness.

**Back yard:** If you have outdoor cameras, pair them with a smart floodlight that triggers on the same motion event for color night vision activation.
