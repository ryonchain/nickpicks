## Security Camera vs. Baby Monitor: Which Do You Need?

**Choose an indoor security camera if:** You want to monitor multiple rooms, package deliveries, pets, or entry points while away from home. Bonus: they double as baby monitors in many cases.

**Choose a dedicated baby monitor if:** Sleep tracking, breathing motion detection, sleep coaching, or a specific crib-overhead view matters to you. Nanit and Owlet are purpose-built for this.

---

## Privacy Considerations

Indoor cameras carry higher privacy risk than outdoor. Look for:
- **Physical privacy shutter** (Ring Indoor Cam) — blocks the lens mechanically
- **Local storage** (Eufy) — footage stays on your network
- **Activity scheduling** — auto-disable camera when home via geofencing
