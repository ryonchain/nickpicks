## What to Look For

**Resolution:** 1080p is sufficient for most homes; 2K–4K is worthwhile if you need to identify faces or license plates at distance.

**Power source:** Battery cameras are flexible but need recharging. Wired cameras are always on but need installation. Solar cameras are hands-free in sunny locations.

**Night vision:** Color night vision (using a spotlight or ambient light) is significantly better for identification than standard infrared night vision, which produces a black-and-white image.

**Subscription:** Ring ($4.99/device/month or $10/unlimited), Arlo ($12.99+), Nest ($8+). Eufy and Reolink offer local-only no-subscription options.
