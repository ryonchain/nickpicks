## Door Thickness Warning

Most over-door organizers are designed for hollow-core interior doors (typically 1.375"–1.5" thick). Solid wood doors (exterior grade or older construction) may be thicker — check the hook clearance before ordering. Some organizers include spacers or longer hooks for thicker doors.

**Measure:** Open your door, measure the thickness at the top edge. If it's over 1.5 inches, look for organizers labeled "fits thick doors" or "door gap 2 inches."
