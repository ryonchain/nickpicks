## Why Gel Memory Foam Sometimes Disappoints

Gel infusion helps in the first 30–60 minutes but eventually equilibrates to body temperature. After that, it's just warm foam. This is why true ventilation (Purple's hex grid) or active moisture-wicking (copper, phase-change covers) outperforms simple gel-infusion in all-night testing.

**What actually works for all-night cooling:**
1. Open-cell structure (ventilation > gel)
2. Phase-change covers (absorb and release heat)
3. Copper infusion (conducts heat away)
4. Natural materials (latex, buckwheat) — don't trap heat like foam
