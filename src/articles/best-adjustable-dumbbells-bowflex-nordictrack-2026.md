## Frequently Asked Questions

**How long do adjustable dumbbells last?**
Quality sets from Bowflex and PowerBlock last 10+ years with normal home use. The plastic dial mechanisms are the weak point — avoid dropping the dumbbells, especially at max weight.

**Can I do every dumbbell exercise with adjustable dumbbells?**
Nearly all exercises work well. Some awkwardness with specific movements: Bowflex 552s are long at max weight (awkward for floor press), and PowerBlocks' square shape makes hammer curls less natural. Both are minor tradeoffs for the storage benefits.

**Is 52.5 lbs enough per dumbbell?**
For most people, yes. The average home gym user rarely needs more than 40–50 lbs per hand for dumbbell exercises. Exceptions: large men doing heavy Romanian deadlifts, rows, or chest-supported rows may eventually want more.
