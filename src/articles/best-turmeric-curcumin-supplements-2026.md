## Understanding Curcumin: What the Research Actually Shows

**Best-evidenced uses:**
- **Osteoarthritis:** Multiple RCTs show curcumin comparable to NSAIDs for pain and function in knee OA, with fewer GI side effects
- **Metabolic syndrome:** Reduces inflammatory markers (CRP, IL-6, TNF-α) in overweight adults
- **Depression adjunct:** A 2014 RCT showed curcumin significantly reduced depression symptoms vs placebo
- **Post-exercise recovery:** Reduces DOMS (delayed onset muscle soreness) in athletes

**Absorption forms ranked by bioavailability:**
1. Theracurmin (nano-colloidal suspension)
2. Meriva/Phytosome (phospholipid complex)
3. BCM-95 (curcumin + essential oils)
4. Longvida (lipid SLCP technology)
5. Curcumin + piperine (BioPerine)
6. Standard 95% curcuminoid extract (without enhancement)
7. Raw turmeric powder (~3% curcumin, no enhancement)

**Take with food:** All fat-soluble curcumin forms absorb best with a meal containing fat. Water-soluble forms (Theracurmin, Qunol) are the exception.
