Memorial Day weekend (May 23–26, 2026) is the first major shopping event of the summer. Certain categories have their single best prices of the year on Memorial Day — and knowing which ones saves you hundreds. This guide covers what's worth buying, what to skip, and the specific deals to look for.

## Why Memorial Day Sales Are Real

Unlike some retail "sales" that inflate prices before discounting, Memorial Day has genuine inventory pressure behind it:

- **Mattress companies** start new model years and must move existing inventory
- **Outdoor furniture** arrives in fall; retailers clear winter/spring stock
- **Appliances** get refreshed lineups in June/July, so May clearance is real
- **Clothing** transitions from spring to summer — last season's stock is marked down
