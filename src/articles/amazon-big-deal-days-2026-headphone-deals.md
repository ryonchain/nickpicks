## ANC Headphones vs. Earbuds — Which to Buy?

**Choose over-ear headphones (Sony XM6, Bose QC Ultra) if:**
- You wear them for 3+ hours continuously
- Long-haul travel (airplane, train)
- Deep focus work at home or office
- You want the best possible ANC

**Choose true wireless earbuds (AirPods Pro, Sony XM5) if:**
- You need portable / pocketable form factor
- Active lifestyle (gym, commute, running errands)
- Don't want to deal with carrying a case
- Calls are a major use case

**Choose open-ear headphones (Shokz, Bose Ultra Open) if:**
- You run or cycle and need road awareness
- You want to stay connected without isolation
- Long wear without fatigue

## Should You Wait for Black Friday?

Headphone deals on Big Deal Days often match or beat Black Friday prices. The difference: **stock**. Popular Sony and Bose models frequently sell out on Black Friday, while Big Deal Days has better availability.

**Buy on Big Deal Days** if a model drops 20%+.
**Wait for Black Friday** if the October price barely moves — some models see steeper November cuts.
