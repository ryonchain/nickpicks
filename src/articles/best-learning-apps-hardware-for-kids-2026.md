## Learning Apps & Hardware Buyer's Guide

### App-Only vs. Hardware-Paired Learning

| Approach | Investment | Best For |
|---------|-----------|---------|
| App-only (Khan Academy, Duolingo, Prodigy) | Free–$10/mo | Supplemental learning on existing devices |
| Dedicated learning tablet (LeapFrog Epic) | ~$100 | Focused learning with minimal distraction |
| Hardware + app hybrid (Osmo) | $80 + iPad | Most engaging early childhood experience |

### Free vs. Paid Education Apps

Most of the best educational apps have genuinely functional free tiers: Khan Academy Kids is completely free; Duolingo's free tier covers all 40+ languages; Prodigy's free tier is fully functional for children. Paid upgrades typically add parent reporting, offline access, or ad removal — worth considering but not required for the child's educational experience.

### The Importance of Adaptive Learning

The best learning apps in 2026 are adaptive — they adjust content difficulty to the individual child, ensuring the child is always working at the edge of their ability (the "zone of proximal development"). Non-adaptive apps deliver the same content regardless of mastery, which bores advanced learners and overwhelms struggling ones. Prioritize apps that specify adaptive algorithms (Khan Academy Kids, Prodigy, LeapFrog's Learning Path).
