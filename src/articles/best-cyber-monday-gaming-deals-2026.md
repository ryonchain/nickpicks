## When Do Cyber Monday Gaming Deals Start?

Cyber Monday gaming deals follow the Black Friday timeline — most start November 28 and run through December 5–7.

| Date | What to Expect |
|------|---------------|
| Nov 28 (Black Friday) | Console bundles, gaming laptop deals go live |
| Nov 29–30 | Accessories, headsets, controllers drop further |
| Dec 1 (Cyber Monday) | Online-exclusive deals; deepest discounts of the year |
| Dec 2–7 | Extended Cyber Week pricing |

**Strategy:** Don't wait for December 1 specifically. The best gaming deals often appear Black Friday weekend and sell out before Cyber Monday.

## Cyber Monday Gaming Buying Tips

1. **Console bundles beat console-only deals** — the savings are in the included games and accessories.
2. **GPU deals are modest** — 10–15% off typically. Only buy if you genuinely need an upgrade now.
3. **Headsets and controllers get the deepest cuts** — 30–50% off is common.
4. **Gaming laptops see the biggest percentage savings** — $400–$600 off on premium models.
5. **Check stock alerts** — PS5 bundles sell out within minutes on Cyber Monday.
