## Standing Desk Buying Guide

**Motor count:** Dual motors lift faster, hold more weight, and are quieter. Worth it if your setup has monitors, docks, or other heavy equipment.

**Height range:** Make sure the minimum height works when seated and maximum height works when standing. Standard sitting height is 28"–30"; standing height for a 6' person is around 42"–44".

**Stability:** Wobble at standing height is the #1 complaint. Read reviews specifically mentioning wobble. Heavier frames + wider legs = more stability.

**Warranty:** This is the most important factor. Standing desks with motors can fail. A 5+ year warranty signals manufacturer confidence.
