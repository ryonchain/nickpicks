## How to Choose a Men's Fragrance Gift Set

### Fragrance Families for Men
- **Fresh/Aquatic (Acqua di Gio, Cool Water):** Clean, office-appropriate, universally liked
- **Oriental/Warm (Eros, 1 Million):** Bold, romantic, evening-focused
- **Woody/Aromatic (Sauvage, Bleu de Chanel):** Versatile, masculine, all-occasion
- **Gourmand (Spicebomb, Black Afgano):** Sweet, intense, statement-making

### EDT vs EDP
- **EDT (Eau de Toilette):** 5–15% fragrance concentration, lighter, better for daytime
- **EDP (Eau de Parfum):** 15–20% concentration, stronger, longer-lasting, better for evenings

### Gift Set Red Flags to Avoid
- Sets with sizes under 50ml that feel like a trial
- No-name "inspired by" fragrances in gift packaging (not authentic)
- "Tester" bottles not in gift boxes — these are often sold as gifts improperly
