Good meal prep containers make the difference between a week of healthy eating and a week of takeout. ### What to Look for in Meal Prep Containers

- **Material**: Glass for microwave/freezer versatility; BPA-free plastic for lighter weight
- **Leak-proof lids**: Critical for sauces, soups, and dressings
- **Portion size**: 2–3 cup containers for lunches; 4–5 cup for full meals
- **Stackability**: Uniform sizes make refrigerator organization easier
- **Dishwasher safe**: Tops and bottoms should both be dishwasher safe
- **Oven safe**: Glass containers that go from fridge to oven save time
