# Best Squat Racks for Home Gym in 2026

A squat rack is the foundational piece of a serious home gym — it enables squats, bench press, overhead press, pull-ups, and barbell rows safely. The decision tree: power cage (four upright posts with safety bars) vs. half rack (two posts, open back) vs. wall-mounted folding rack (space saver). For home gyms with limited ceiling height, upright height is critical.

## Ceiling Height Requirements

- 90" racks: Need 8+ foot ceilings (most residential) — fine for standard basement or garage
- 83" racks: Low-ceiling version for 7.5-foot ceiling garages
- Wall-mounted: Typically 84–90" depending on model

**Before buying:** Measure ceiling height, measure your barbell length (7-foot standard bar = 7'4" overall), and account for overhead press range of motion.
