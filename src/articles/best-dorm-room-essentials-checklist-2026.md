## What NOT to Bring

- **Full-size iron** — use a wrinkle-release spray or travel steamer instead
- **Heavy decorations** — you'll carry them back in May
- **Your entire wardrobe** — you'll wear 30% of what you bring
- **Printer** — campus printers are free/cheap; a printer is dead weight
- **Desktop computer** — a laptop is more practical in every class situation
