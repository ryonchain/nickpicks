## Foil vs. Rotary: Which Is Right for You?

This is the most important decision in electric shaving. The two technologies suit different users.

**Foil shavers** use oscillating blades beneath a perforated metal foil. They shave in straight lines (up-down, left-right) and work best for:
- Fine to medium beard density
- Sensitive skin
- Precise edges and straight lines
- Users who shave frequently (daily or every other day)

**Rotary shavers** use three or four circular heads that pivot independently. They trace facial contours and work best for:
- Thick or coarse beards
- Multi-directional beard growth patterns
- Users who shave less frequently (every 3–5 days)
- Contoured faces (strong jaw angles, prominent cheekbones)

**Wet vs. dry:** Most premium shavers support both. Wet shaving (with foam, gel, or in the shower) adds lubrication and reduces irritation. Dry shaving is faster and portable. If you have sensitive skin, wet shaving typically delivers better results.
