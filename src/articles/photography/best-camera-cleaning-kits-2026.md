## Camera Cleaning Protocol

**Lens cleaning steps:**
1. Use the blower to remove loose dust — never wipe first
2. Use a lens brush to sweep remaining particles
3. Apply cleaning fluid to a lens tissue (not directly to the glass)
4. Wipe in circular motion from center outward
5. Follow with dry microfiber cloth

**Sensor cleaning steps (wet):**
1. Charge battery fully (mirror lock-up holds the shutter open)
2. Use Rocket blower at minimum 3 puffs — check again
3. If spots remain, attach swab and apply 1-2 drops of Eclipse
4. Single pass across sensor, even pressure
5. Check results at f/16 against bright sky or paper
