## What Makes a Great Cologne Gift Set

### Concentration Matters
- **Eau de Cologne (EDC):** 2–4% fragrance oil. Light, appropriate for gym or office in summer.
- **Eau de Toilette (EDT):** 5–15%. Most versatile; what most men wear daily.
- **Eau de Parfum (EDP):** 15–20%. Richer, longer-lasting. Best for fall/winter or evenings.
- **Parfum/Extrait:** 20–30%+. Most intense. A few sprays last all day.

Gift sets typically bundle an EDT or EDP with matching aftershave, deodorant, or body wash. The best sets share fragrance DNA across every product.

### Fragrance Families for Men
- **Woody/Earthy:** Cedarwood, sandalwood, vetiver. Sophisticated, classic, office-safe.
- **Fresh/Aquatic:** Citrus, sea notes, bergamot. Energetic, warm-weather favorites.
- **Spicy/Oriental:** Amber, vanilla, oud. Powerful evening presence.
- **Aromatic/Fougère:** Lavender, herbs, oakmoss. Most traditional men's cologne style.
