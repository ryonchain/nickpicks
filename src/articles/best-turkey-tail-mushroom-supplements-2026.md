## The Clinical Evidence Behind Turkey Tail

Turkey tail's evidence base is stronger than any other functional mushroom — PSK (Krestin) has been through rigorous clinical trials:

**Cancer research highlights:**
- **Gastric cancer:** Japanese trials (Nakazato et al.) showed 5-year survival rates of 73% vs 60% for PSK + chemotherapy vs chemotherapy alone
- **Breast cancer:** A 2021 trial showed turkey tail PSP improved immune function in breast cancer patients during conventional treatment
- **Colorectal cancer:** Meta-analysis of 8 trials showed significant survival benefit for PSK alongside conventional treatment

**Gut microbiome research:**
- A pilot human study by Pallav et al. (2014) showed turkey tail consumption selectively increased *Lactobacillus* and *Bifidobacterium* populations — beneficial gut bacteria associated with immune function

**Important context:**
- These trials used PSK at doses of 3,000mg/day — higher than most supplement products
- Turkey tail is adjunct therapy, not standalone treatment
- The effect size in cancer trials is meaningful but should not be interpreted as turkey tail "curing" cancer

For healthy adults using turkey tail for immune support and gut health, 1,000–2,000mg daily of quality fruiting body extract is a reasonable, evidence-informed approach with an excellent safety profile.
