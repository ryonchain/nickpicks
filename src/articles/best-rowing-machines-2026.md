# Best Rowing Machines 2026: Top 5 Rowers for Every Home Gym

Rowing is the rare cardio exercise that works your legs, core, and upper body simultaneously — making it one of the most efficient workouts you can do. The machines have gotten better too: connected rowers with live classes have made rowing genuinely enjoyable, while budget options have closed the gap on build quality. Whether you want the gold-standard machine used by Olympic athletes or an affordable entry point to rowing, 2026's lineup delivers. Here are the five best rowing machines on the market right now.

## Buying Guide: What to Look for in a Rowing Machine

**Resistance Type:** Air rowers (Concept2) are most responsive to effort. Magnetic rowers are quietest — ideal for apartments. Water rowers offer the most realistic feel. All can give an excellent workout.

**Monitors & Connectivity:** Basic rowers track stroke rate, time, and distance. Connected rowers add live classes, virtual routes, and coaching. Subscription costs add $30–$44/month to connected options.

**Space:** Most rowers need an 8-foot by 4-foot footprint when in use. Many fold or store vertically. Measure your space before buying.

**User Weight Limit:** Most quality rowers support 250–500 lbs. Verify capacity if you're above 250 lbs.

**Noise:** Air rowers make a fan-like whoosh. Magnetic and water rowers are dramatically quieter — important if you share walls or floors.
