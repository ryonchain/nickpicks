## What to Skip at Labor Day Furniture Sales

- **IKEA furniture** — IKEA doesn't do Labor Day sales. Prices stay flat.
- **Very cheap patio furniture (under $150/set)** — At this price, clearance quality is usually end-of-life inventory with broken or missing parts.
- **Floor model deals at furniture showrooms** — "Floor model only" items often have damage not disclosed online.
