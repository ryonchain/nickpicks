## What to Skip

**Mega-dose immune cocktails:** Products with 30+ ingredients at token doses. No evidence they outperform single-ingredient supplements.

**Colloidal silver:** No credible evidence. Potential toxicity.

**"Immune boost" proprietary blends:** Usually underdosed at key ingredients to hit a price point.

---

## Quality Markers When Buying

- **USP, NSF, or Informed-Sport certification:** Third-party tested
- **No proprietary blends:** You should see exact mg per ingredient
- **Form matters:** Zinc picolinate > zinc oxide. Vitamin D3 > D2. Active B vitamins > synthetic.
