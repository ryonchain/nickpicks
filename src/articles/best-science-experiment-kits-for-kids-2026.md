### One-Time Kits vs. Subscriptions

**One-time kits** (Thames & Kosmos, National Geographic) offer controlled spending and consistent content quality. **Subscriptions** (MEL Science) provide ongoing novelty and premium experience quality but cost more long-term. If your child loves chemistry and will use a subscription monthly, MEL Science provides the best experience quality. If you're not sure of the commitment level, start with a one-time kit.

### Safety Standards

Look for kits that are explicitly EN71 or ASTM-certified for toy safety. Reputable brands like Thames & Kosmos, National Geographic, and MEL Science include safety goggles and follow strict chemical safety standards. Always read the age recommendations — chemistry kits for under-8s use different (safer) chemical sets than those for older kids.
