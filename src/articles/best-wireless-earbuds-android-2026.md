## FAQs

**Do AirPods work with Android?**
Yes, but with limited functionality. You lose Siri, automatic ear detection, Spatial Audio, and iCloud seamless switching. Android users are better served by dedicated Android earbuds.

**What codec should I look for?**
For Android: LDAC (Sony), LHDC/LHDC 5.0 (Nothing), Samsung's seamless codec, or aptX. All deliver better quality than standard SBC/AAC.

**Are $30 earbuds worth it?**
For casual listening, yes. For consistent sound quality, ANC, and call clarity — spend at least $60–$80 on a tested brand.
