## Black Friday Camera Shopping Strategy

**1. Buy kit bundles over body-only.** Camera kits (body + lens + accessories) are better value than buying pieces separately. Black Friday kit pricing often gives you a lens at essentially no cost vs. body-only price.

**2. Gray market risks.** Some camera deals on Amazon come from third-party gray-market sellers — no US warranty. Buy from Amazon directly or authorized dealers (B&H Photo, Adorama) to ensure warranty coverage.

**3. Action cameras and drones sell out fastest.** GoPro and DJI Black Friday deals historically sell out within 12 hours. Set alerts and buy immediately when stock is available.

**4. Lenses are the best investment.** Bodies depreciate and get updated; quality glass holds value for decades. If you're choosing between upgrading the body or upgrading the lens — the lens is almost always the better investment at Black Friday prices.
