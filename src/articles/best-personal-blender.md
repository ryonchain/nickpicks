## FAQ

**What wattage do I need?** For soft fruits: 200–350W. For frozen fruit/ice: 700W+.

**Can I blend hot liquids?** Most personal blenders are not rated for hot liquids — check the manual.

**How do I clean it?** Add warm water + soap, blend 20 seconds, rinse. Most cups are dishwasher-safe on the top rack.

**Is USB charging worth it?** Yes if you travel or camp. For home use, a corded model offers better power-to-dollar value.
