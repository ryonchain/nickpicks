## What to Look for in a Mattress Under $1,000

### Firmness Level
- **Soft (1-4/10):** Side sleepers, lighter weight
- **Medium (5-6/10):** Combination sleepers, most couples
- **Firm (7-9/10):** Stomach and back sleepers, heavier weight

### Construction Types
- **All-foam:** Best motion isolation, pressure relief; can sleep hot
- **Hybrid (coils + foam):** Best of both worlds; better airflow, more responsive
- **Innerspring:** Most traditional; most airflow; least pressure relief

### Trial Periods
Never buy a mattress without a trial. Most reputable brands offer 100–365 nights. Skip any mattress without at least a 100-night trial.

### Certifications to Look For
- **CertiPUR-US:** Confirms foam free from harmful chemicals
- **OEKO-TEX:** Broader textile safety certification
- **GREENGUARD Gold:** Low chemical emissions (important for children's rooms)

## FAQ

**Q: Should I buy a mattress on Amazon?**
A: Amazon carries many reputable brands including Zinus, Linenspa, Casper, and others. The advantage is Prime shipping and easy returns. For premium brands like Saatva, buying direct often includes better service.

**Q: How long should a mattress last?**
A: Quality mattresses last 7–10 years. Signs it's time to replace: sagging, waking with pain, visible wear.

**Q: Is a mattress topper a good alternative?**
A: Toppers can extend mattress life and improve comfort but can't fix a structurally compromised mattress. Use them to add softness or cooling, not to repair a sagging mattress.

**Q: What's the best mattress for back pain?**
A: Medium-firm mattresses generally perform best for back pain. The Amerisleep AS3 and Saatva Luxury Firm are standout options. See our full guide to [best mattresses for back pain].
