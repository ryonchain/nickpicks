## Black Friday Vacuum Shopping Strategy

**1. Dyson is worth full attention.** Dyson vacuums almost never go on sale outside of Black Friday and Prime Day. If you've been eyeing a Dyson V15 or V12, November is your window.

**2. Robot vacuums see the biggest discounts.** The self-emptying dock models (Roomba j9+, Roborock Q5+) drop the most in percentage terms. If you've been on the fence about a robot vacuum, this is when to move.

**3. Stick vacuums sell out.** Dyson V15 and Shark Stratos often sell out on Black Friday — if you see the price you want, buy immediately.

**4. Don't buy cordless for carpet-only homes.** Corded upright vacuums (Shark Navigator, Dyson Ball Animal) maintain consistent suction — cordless battery degrades over time. For heavy carpet-only homes, corded is more reliable long-term.
