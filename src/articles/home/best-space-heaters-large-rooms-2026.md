## Safety Checklist for Space Heaters

All quality space heaters include basic safety features. Verify these before purchase:

- **Tip-over auto-shutoff** — Essential. If the heater falls, it turns off immediately.
- **Overheat protection** — Shuts down if internal temperature exceeds safe limits.
- **Cool-touch exterior** — Prevents burns if touched, important for homes with children or pets.
- **3-foot clearance rule** — Keep all heaters at least 3 feet from furniture, curtains, and bedding.
- **Never leave unattended** — Space heaters should be off when you leave the room or sleep (exception: oil-filled heaters designed for overnight use with proper safety ratings).

For bedroom use overnight, oil-filled radiators with precise thermostats are the safest option — they maintain temperature without continuous full-power operation.
