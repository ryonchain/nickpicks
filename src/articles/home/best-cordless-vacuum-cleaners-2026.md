## How to Choose a Cordless Vacuum

### Suction Power (Air Watts)

Air watts (AW) measure actual cleaning power more accurately than motor watts or "Pa" suction ratings. For reference:
- 100 AW: Hard floor only (not effective on carpet)
- 100–150 AW: Low-pile and medium carpet
- 150–230 AW: All carpet types including thick pile

### Battery Life vs. Home Size

A 2,000 sq ft home takes 20–30 minutes to vacuum depending on floor type. Boost mode on Dyson (max suction) cuts battery to 10 minutes — use it sparingly for carpets, switch to Eco for hardwood.

**Required runtime by home size:**
- Under 1,000 sq ft: 30 minutes sufficient
- 1,000–2,500 sq ft: 45–60 minutes recommended
- 2,500+ sq ft: 60+ minutes or swappable batteries

### Hard Floors vs. Carpet Bias

Cordless vacuums generally excel on hard floors. Carpet performance varies significantly with suction power. If your home is more than 50% carpet, prioritize higher AW ratings (150+) and motorized brush heads.
