## Installation Note for 240V Garage Heaters

Most high-output electric garage heaters require a 240V dedicated circuit (30A–40A). If your garage has standard 120V outlets only, budget for electrical work before purchasing a 240V heater. Typical electrician cost for running a new 240V circuit to a garage: $200–$600 depending on distance from the panel. This is a one-time cost that enables year-round garage use — worthwhile if you use your garage regularly.

For plug-in convenience without electrical work, the Mr. Heater Big Buddy (propane) or a 120V electric heater (1,500W max) are your alternatives — though 1,500W is rarely sufficient for garage spaces larger than 150 sq ft.
