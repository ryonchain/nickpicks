## Buying Guide

### Hardwood Floors: What Matters Most

For hardwood, the key factors are **suction consistency** (not raw maximum Pa), **soft rubber side brushes** that don't scatter debris, and a **slim profile** to get under furniture. Bristle brushes work fine on hardwood but can occasionally scuff older floors — rubber rollers are safer.

### Pet Hair: Tangle-Free Is Non-Negotiable

Pet hair wraps around brushrolls constantly. Look for **self-cleaning brushrolls** (Roomba j7+, Shark IQ) or **tangle-resistant rubber rollers**. Also prioritize **high dustbin capacity** or a self-emptying dock — a dustbin that fills in one run defeats the purpose.

### Self-Emptying Docks: What They Actually Do

Self-emptying docks pull debris from the dustbin into a sealed bag or bagless base. Most hold 30–60 days of waste. Higher-end docks (Roborock S8 Pro Ultra) also wash and dry the mop pads, refill water, and charge the unit. Budget docks (Shark IQ) just auto-empty — which is still a significant convenience upgrade.

### LiDAR vs. Camera Navigation

- **LiDAR:** Laser-based, room-precise mapping, works in the dark, best for complex floor plans.
- **Camera AI:** Excellent obstacle avoidance (Roomba j7+), learns your home over time, but requires decent lighting and can be less precise on large floor maps.

For most homes, LiDAR is the more reliable long-term investment above $250.

## FAQ

### Can robot vacuums scratch hardwood floors?

Quality robot vacuums use soft rubber side brushes and smooth plastic underbodies that don't scratch hardwood. Cheap models with stiff plastic parts can occasionally scuff older or softer wood floors. Look for models with rubber brushrolls specifically.

### How often should a robot vacuum run in a pet household?

Daily 20–30 minute runs in high-traffic areas (living room, kitchen) outperform a single long weekly run. Most LiDAR models support per-room daily schedules. With a self-emptying dock, daily runs are maintenance-free.

### Do robot vacuums replace manual vacuuming?

For hardwood floors: yes, for most households. For thick carpet or heavy shedding dogs: robot vacuums reduce frequency but rarely eliminate the need for a manual deep-clean every 2–3 weeks.

### What size dustbin do I need for pet hair?

A minimum of 0.5 L for single-pet households with daily runs. For multi-pet homes, go self-emptying — even a large dustbin (0.75 L) fills within one run during shedding season.
