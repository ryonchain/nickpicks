## How to Identify a Real Monitor Deal

Monitor pricing is complex. Key things to know:

- **Panel type matters more than resolution.** IPS panels offer the best color and viewing angles. VA panels have deeper blacks for movies. TN panels are fastest for gaming. OLED monitors are emerging and worth the premium if you do color work.
- **Refresh rate for gaming.** 165Hz is the sweet spot for PC gaming in 2026. 240Hz+ is for competitive players. 60Hz is fine for office work.
- **Size vs. resolution.** A 27-inch 1080p monitor looks noticeably soft. Target 1440p for 27-inch, or 4K for 32-inch and above.

## Buying Tips

- **Get USB-C when possible.** USB-C monitors charge your laptop and connect over a single cable. It's a meaningful quality-of-life upgrade if you use a MacBook or modern Windows laptop.
- **Avoid 1080p at 27 inches.** The pixel density is too low at this size. Spend slightly more to get 1440p.
- **G-Sync vs FreeSync.** Both eliminate screen tearing. G-Sync is for Nvidia GPUs; FreeSync is AMD. Check your GPU before buying a gaming monitor.
- **Returns are easy.** Amazon's monitor return window covers you if colors are off or there are dead pixels. Buy with confidence, return if needed.
