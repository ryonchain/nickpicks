## Wireless Kids Headphone Buyer's Guide

### Why Battery Life Matters More for Kids

Adults can reliably remember to charge headphones nightly. Children often forget — and dead headphones on a school bus or during a flight are a parenting emergency. Prioritize 20+ hour battery life. The Skullcandy Grom (40 hours) and Belkin SoundForm Mini (30 hours) essentially eliminate charging as a concern for weekly use.

### Bluetooth Range and School Compatibility

Most kids headphones provide 30 feet (10m) of Bluetooth range — sufficient for standard use. However, many schools prohibit Bluetooth devices in classrooms. Look for headphones with wired fallback (3.5mm) for school environments: Puro BT2200, JLab JBuddies, Onanoff BuddyPhones all include it. The Skullcandy Grom does not.

### Volume Limiting: Hardware vs. Software

**Hardware volume limiting** (physical driver limit) is safer than **software limiting** (OS parental controls). Software limits can be bypassed or reset; hardware limits cannot. All headphones on this list use hardware limiting. Puro's is the only audiologist-certified version.
