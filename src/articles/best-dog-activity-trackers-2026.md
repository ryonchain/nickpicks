## Choosing the Right Dog Tracker

**GPS vs radio-based:** LTE GPS trackers (Fi, Whistle, Tractive) need cellular coverage. Radio trackers (Garmin) work anywhere with line of sight.

**Health vs location priority:** Whistle and PetPace emphasize health anomaly detection. Fi and Tractive prioritize precise location and escape detection.

**Battery life tradeoff:** Frequent GPS pings drain batteries faster. Fi's 3-month life uses periodic GPS check-ins; Tractive's Live Mode uses the battery in 2–3 days.

**Subscription cost:** Factor in monthly subscription costs ($5–15/month) when comparing prices. A "cheap" tracker with a mandatory $15/mo subscription costs $180/year more than the device price suggests.
