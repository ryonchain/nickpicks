## College Meal Prep Tips

**Mini fridge space planning:** Rectangular containers stack better than round ones. Before buying, measure your mini fridge shelf height — a standard shelf is 8–10 inches tall. 1L rectangular containers typically stack 3 high in a standard mini fridge shelf.

**Microwave-safe = remove lid:** No container on this list is microwave-safe with the lid on. Always remove the lid before microwaving (or use a microwave cover). The steam from reheating can warp plastic lids.

**Communal kitchen reality:** If you use a communal dorm kitchen, label your containers with your name and room number. Use a Sharpie directly on a piece of painter's tape on the bottom — it peels off cleanly for resale.
