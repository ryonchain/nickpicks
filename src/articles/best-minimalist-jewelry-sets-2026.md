## What to Avoid

- **Sets where pieces share a theme but not a design language** — "pearl and gold" earrings with a completely different pearl necklace are not a set, they're three separate items. Look for sets designed together.
- **Studs with butterfly backs for all-day wear** — butterfly backs can cause pressure and irritation over extended wear. Screw backs or secure snap backs are more comfortable for long-term wearing.
- **Brass chains without anti-tarnish coating** — bare brass tarnishes quickly in humid environments and with skin contact. Plated or coated brass resists tarnish significantly longer.
- **Sets that only offer one size bracelet** — adjustable chain bracelets with extension links fit more wrists. Fixed-size bracelets run either too tight or too loose depending on wrist size.
