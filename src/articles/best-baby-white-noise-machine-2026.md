## White Noise Science: What the Research Says

A 1990 study in the journal *Archives of Disease in Childhood* found that 80% of infants fell asleep within 5 minutes when played white noise (vs. 25% in the control group). More recent research confirms that consistent white noise helps infants maintain sleep by masking environmental disturbances.

**Safety note:** The American Academy of Pediatrics recommends keeping white noise machines at a maximum of 50 dB at the recommended distance. Most machines have volume controls — start low and increase as needed.
