## How to Use NAC

**Standard doses by application:**

| Application | Typical Dose |
|-------------|-------------|
| General antioxidant/liver support | 600mg once daily |
| Immune support / respiratory | 600mg twice daily |
| Mental wellness (OCD, addiction support) | 1,200–2,400mg/day (divided) |
| Hangover prevention (alcohol detox) | 600mg before drinking + 600mg next morning |

**Timing:** Take with food to reduce nausea. Divided doses are better absorbed than single large doses.

**What to expect:**
- Liver enzyme improvements: 8–12 weeks of consistent use
- Respiratory mucus thinning: Within days to weeks
- Mental wellness applications: 4–8 weeks minimum

**Important interactions:**
- **Blood thinners:** NAC has mild antiplatelet properties — consult doctor if on warfarin or similar
- **Nitroglycerin:** NAC can enhance hypotensive effects
- **Activated charcoal:** Do not take concurrently — charcoal can adsorb NAC

NAC's evidence base is unusually strong for a dietary supplement, grounded in decades of medical use. For liver support, respiratory health, and raising glutathione levels, it remains one of the most evidence-backed options available without a prescription.
