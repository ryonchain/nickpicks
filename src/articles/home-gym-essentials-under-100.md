## How to Build a Home Gym Under $100 Total

You don't need all six items on day one. Here's a suggested build order:

**Week 1 ($25–$35):** Resistance band set + jump rope  
**Week 2 ($40–$60):** Add a pull-up bar + ab roller  
**Week 3–4 ($80–$100):** Add adjustable dumbbells or a foam roller

This progression lets you build training habits before spending on equipment — which is the most common home gym mistake (buying first, using never).
