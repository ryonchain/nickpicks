## Black Friday Gaming Buying Tips

1. **Console bundles beat bare consoles** — the game/subscription value is real
2. **Gaming laptops see the biggest absolute savings** — $400–$600 off on premium models
3. **GPU discounts are modest** — 10–15% off is typical, not 50% off
4. **Controllers and headsets get deepest cuts** — 40–50% off common
5. **Track prices at CamelCamelCamel** — many "Black Friday" prices are regular prices with a misleading "was" label
6. **Set stock alerts** — PS5 bundles and popular gaming laptops sell out fast
