## Inkjet vs. Laser: The Honest Guide

This is the most important decision, and retailers rarely explain it clearly:

**Choose inkjet if:**
- You print photos or colorful documents
- You print less than 200 pages/month
- You need versatility (photos, envelopes, thick paper)
- Budget is your priority upfront

**Choose laser (monochrome) if:**
- You print primarily text documents
- You print 200+ pages/month
- Fast print speeds matter to you
- You want lower cost-per-page and longer cartridge life
- You're tired of ink drying out between uses

**The cost-per-page reality:**
- Average inkjet: $0.05–0.15/page
- Budget inkjet (standard cartridges): $0.10–0.25/page
- EcoTank/MegaTank refillable: $0.01–0.02/page
- Monochrome laser: $0.01–0.03/page

For most home offices printing under 100 pages/month, inkjet total cost of ownership is fine. For heavy users, laser or EcoTank pays for itself quickly.

## Wireless Printing Troubleshooting: The Most Common Issues

**Problem: Printer keeps going offline**
- Solution: Assign a static IP to the printer in your router settings, or use USB printing as backup

**Problem: Cartridges dry out between uses**
- Solution: If you print less than weekly, use a laser printer; inkjet ink dries when unused for weeks

**Problem: Color printing looks wrong**
- Solution: Run the "Nozzle Check" and "Head Cleaning" utilities in printer software; may need 2–3 cycles

**Problem: Mobile printing doesn't work**
- Solution: Confirm both phone and printer are on the same Wi-Fi network (not 2.4GHz vs. 5GHz mismatch)
