## FAQ

**What is the difference between Pa and AW for suction power?**
Pascals (Pa) measure suction pressure; Air Watts (AW) measure suction force accounting for airflow. AW is the more reliable comparison metric — a high Pa with low airflow doesn't clean as effectively. Brands often quote whichever measurement makes their product look better.

**How long should a cordless vacuum battery last?**
Most quality cordless vacuums offer 30–50 minutes in standard mode. For a 1,500 sq ft home, 40 minutes is sufficient. For larger homes, look for swappable battery systems (Tineco A11 Hero includes two batteries) or opt for a model with a 60-minute runtime.

**Are cordless vacuums good for pet hair?**
Yes, if you choose correctly. Look for models with self-cleaning brush rolls (Shark IX141) or tangle-free brush technology. High suction in the 100+ AW range also helps lift embedded pet hair from carpet.

**Can cordless vacuums replace a traditional upright vacuum?**
For most households — yes, especially homes with primarily hard floors. Homes with lots of thick carpet or heavy debris may still benefit from a corded upright for deep cleaning once a week.
