## Platform Sneaker Buying Guide

### How Much Platform Do You Want?

**Subtle (1–1.25 inches):** Barely noticeable under pants, most versatile, easiest to walk in. Best for daily wear.

**Medium (1.5–1.75 inches):** Clearly visible platform, adds noticeable height, still very walkable. The sweet spot for most buyers.

**Bold (2+ inches):** Maximum height, statement-making look, requires more attention to stability on uneven ground.

### Style Tips

**With jeans:** Straight-leg or mom jeans show off the platform best — the contrast between the chunky sole and a clean leg line is the most popular styling.

**With skirts/dresses:** Platform sneakers ground flowing or feminine silhouettes with an edge that heels don't.

**With loungewear:** The "elevated casual" look that defined the platform trend — oversized hoodie, bike shorts, chunky sneakers.

### Sizing Notes

Platform sneakers from Steve Madden and Sam Edelman typically run true to size. Dr. Martens run large — most women size down 1 full size from their US size.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Are platform sneakers comfortable to walk in?**
Yes — when the platform is evenly stacked (not wedge-style), your foot sits level and walking mechanics are natural. Quality platforms from the brands on this list are comfortable for hours of wear.

**How do you style platform sneakers?**
The most common looks: with straight-leg jeans and an oversized tee, with a floral midi dress, or with cycling shorts and a crop top. Platform sneakers work best when the outfit isn't competing for attention.

**Do platform sneakers run true to size?**
Most run true to size. Dr. Martens is the exception — size down one full size from your usual US size.

**How do you clean platform sneakers?**
For synthetic uppers, a damp cloth and mild soap handle most dirt. For leather uppers (Dr. Martens), use a dedicated leather cleaner and conditioner. Always air-dry, never machine wash.
