## Buying Guide: Baby Shower Tips

**Check the registry first** — parents put things there for a reason. Buy registry items before buying something creative.

**If buying off-registry, stick to:**
- Consumables (diapers, wipes, formula)
- Items with universal use (white noise machine, carrier)
- Food/meal support

**Size up on clothing:** Buy 6 months ahead of the due date. Newborn clothes are outgrown in weeks. Buy 6-9 month sizes for a winter baby.

**Avoid:** Stuffed animals (unless you know they want them), themed nursery decor (taste-specific), anything requiring a specific model of product they already have.

**Group gifts work:** The Owlet Dream Sock ($180), Ergobaby carrier ($180), or Hatch Rest+ ($170) are all great group gifts when 3-4 friends split the cost.
