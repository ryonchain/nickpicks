## Standing Desk Buying Guide

**Height range matters:** Measure your elbow height when seated (arms at 90°) and standing. A desk height range that covers both positions is essential — not all desks go low enough for shorter users or high enough for taller users. Verify the minimum and maximum heights against your own measurements before purchasing.

**Dual motor vs. single motor:** Dual motors stabilize each leg independently, significantly reducing wobble at standing height under load. For heavy setups (dual monitors, large desktop towers), dual motor is worth the additional cost. For light laptop setups, single motor is adequate.

**Weight capacity:** A standard dual-monitor setup (two 27" monitors + stands + laptop + accessories) weighs approximately 60–90 lbs. Most desks in this category handle that comfortably. Problems arise with heavy desktop PCs, multiple large monitors, or significant desk accessories. Check the spec if your setup is unusually heavy.
