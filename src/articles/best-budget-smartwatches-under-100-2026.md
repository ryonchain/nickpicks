## How to Choose a Budget Smartwatch

### Android vs. iPhone: pick your ecosystem first

Android users get the most choice: Galaxy Watch FE (Wear OS), Amazfit (Zepp OS), Garmin (Connect), and Fitbit all work well. iPhone users should strongly consider the Apple Watch SE 2nd gen — the seamless iOS integration is hard to replicate on any other platform.

### Battery life or features: choose your priority

Short battery (1–2 days): Samsung Galaxy Watch FE, Apple Watch SE — packed with features but you charge nightly.
Long battery (7–15 days): Amazfit GTS 4 Mini, Garmin Vivoactive 5 — less daily maintenance, slightly fewer real-time features.

### GPS built-in vs. connected GPS

Built-in GPS is essential for solo runners, cyclists, and hikers who leave their phone behind. Connected GPS (phone required) is fine for gym-goers or casual walkers who carry their phone on workouts. The Amazfit Bip 5's main trade-off is connected GPS to achieve its $50 price.
