## Housewarming Gifts to Avoid

- **Candles:** Safe but generic. 90% of recipients already have too many.
- **Wine:** Fine unless you know their preferences. Most housewarming wine gets re-gifted.
- **Fancy kitchen gadgets they won't use:** Egg separators, avocado slicers, strawberry hullers. Skip.
- **Expensive decorative items:** Taste is personal. A $100 vase may clash with their entire design scheme.
