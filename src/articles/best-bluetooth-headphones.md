## Buying Guide: What Matters

**ANC quality.** True active noise cancellation — not just passive isolation from the ear cup seal — requires dedicated ANC processors. The Sony XM4 leads under $200. At the budget tier, the Anker Q45 and JBL Tune 770NC both offer functional ANC for transit and office use.

**Call quality vs. listening quality.** Most headphones under $150 optimize one or the other. The Jabra Evolve2 55 leads on mic quality; the Sennheiser HD 450BT leads on playback accuracy. The Sony XM4 is the best all-around balance.

**Battery life.** ANC reduces battery life significantly — always check the "with ANC active" number rather than the maximum spec. 25+ hours with ANC is excellent; 30+ hours (Sony XM4) is class-leading.

**Codec support.** aptX HD and LDAC deliver better audio quality than standard SBC over Bluetooth. The Sony XM4 supports LDAC for high-resolution audio streaming. For casual listening, the difference is subtle; for audiophiles, it matters.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Are the Sony WH-1000XM4 worth buying in 2026?**
Yes — the WH-1000XM4 is still one of the best ANC headphones available. When the XM5 was released, the XM4 dropped significantly in price. You get class-leading noise cancellation, excellent sound, and 30 hours of battery life for under $150 on sale.

**What is the difference between the Sony XM4 and XM5?**
The XM5 has slightly better ANC and a sleeker foldable design. The XM4 folds flat for storage, which many users prefer for travel. Sound quality and battery life are comparable. At the price difference, the XM4 is the better value.

**Do Bluetooth headphones sound as good as wired headphones?**
Modern aptX HD and LDAC Bluetooth codecs approach the quality of wired connections for most music. At the $100–$150 price range, Bluetooth headphones sound excellent for streaming. Audiophiles doing critical listening may prefer wired, but for everyday use the difference is minimal.

**How long should Bluetooth headphones last on a charge?**
Good Bluetooth headphones in 2026 last 30–40 hours per charge without ANC active, and 25–35 hours with ANC. The Sony XM4 achieves 30 hours with ANC on — industry-leading at this price. A 10-minute quick charge on the XM4 gives 5 hours of playback.

**Are over-ear headphones better than earbuds for noise cancellation?**
Over-ear headphones generally provide stronger ANC than earbuds because the larger drivers and sealed ear cups create a physical barrier before the ANC electronics engage. The Sony XM4 and Sennheiser HD 450BT outperform most true wireless earbuds on active noise cancellation.
