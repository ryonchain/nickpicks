## Modeling vs. Tube: What's Right for You?

| | Modeling Amp | Tube Amp |
|---|---|---|
| **Tone versatility** | Many amp sounds | Usually 1–2 characters |
| **Effects** | Built-in | External pedals only |
| **Maintenance** | None | Tube replacement every 2–5 years |
| **Low-volume tone** | Good at any volume | Best sound at medium-high volume |
| **Price** | $80–$300 | $200–$500+ for comparable quality |
| **Sound character** | Accurate, clinical | Organic, "alive" |

**Beginner recommendation:** Start with modeling (Boss Katana 50 or Fender Mustang Micro). The versatility teaches you what tone you're looking for. Buy a tube amp when you know exactly which character you want.
