## What to Look for in a Smart Water Bottle

**Tracking method:** Most use weight sensors or flow sensors in the cap. Weight sensors (Hidrate) are more accurate; flow sensors are simpler and use less battery.

**App ecosystem:** If you use Apple Health, Fitbit, or MyFitnessPal daily, make sure the bottle integrates natively — not just via IFTTT workarounds.

**Battery life:** Aim for at least 14 days. Charging a water bottle weekly is annoying. Thermos's replaceable battery is the most low-maintenance option.

**Reminder style:** LED glows (Hidrate) work at your desk without disrupting others. Push notifications work when your phone is nearby. Vibration reminders (some models) work anywhere.

**Insulation:** Smart features mean nothing if the water goes warm. Prioritize bottles with at least 24-hour cold retention.
