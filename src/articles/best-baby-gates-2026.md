# Best Baby Gates in 2026

Baby gates are one of the most critical baby safety purchases. The most important distinction: **pressure mount gates must never be used at the top of stairs** — a child pushing through a pressure mount gate at the top of a staircase can push it out and fall. Only hardware-mounted (screw-in) gates are approved for stair tops.

## Installation Notes

**Top of stairs — hardware mount required:**
1. Locate wall studs with a stud finder
2. Use the included mounting brackets
3. Confirm the gate opens away from the stairs (outward swing only)
4. Test gate before relying on it — apply body weight toward the stairs to confirm secure

**Pressure mount (room to room):**
1. Adjust width to doorway minus 1/4"
2. Insert spindle into mounting cups
3. Turn spindle to tighten against door frame
4. Apply pressure toward gate — should not move

**Never use pressure mount at top of stairs** — the same force a falling child would apply can dislodge a pressure mount gate from the frame.
