## How to Identify a Real Camera Deal

Camera deals require extra scrutiny. Watch out for:

- **Kit lens bundles.** A "great deal" that adds a mediocre kit lens is often worse value than a body-only at a slightly higher price.
- **Gray market vs. authorized seller.** Stick to Amazon-sold and fulfilled items or authorized dealers to protect warranty coverage.
- **Sensor generation.** Many Prime Day camera deals clear previous-generation stock. Older sensors are still excellent — just know what you're buying.
