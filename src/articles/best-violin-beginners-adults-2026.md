## What to Buy With Your Violin

**Shoulder rest ($15–30):** Connects between violin and collarbone to hold the instrument without hunching. Kun Collapsible or Everest are standard choices. Not included in most outfits.

**Rosin ($8–15):** Applied to bow hair to create friction on strings. All starter outfits include a block. Pirastro Obligato is an upgrade.

**Tuner app (free):** GuitarTuna and Fine Chromatic Tuner work perfectly for violin. No additional hardware needed.

**Metronome app (free):** Tempo for iPhone and Metronome Beats for Android.
