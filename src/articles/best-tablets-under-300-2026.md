## How to Choose the Right Tablet

### Apple vs Android vs Fire OS

- **Apple iPad:** Best app quality, best performance-per-dollar at $300, best for Apple households.
- **Android tablets (Samsung):** Best ecosystem flexibility, full Google Play Store, best for non-Apple users.
- **Amazon Fire:** Best media consumption for Amazon Prime subscribers, worst for non-Amazon apps.

### Storage: How Much Do You Need?

- **32GB:** Reading and streaming only.
- **64GB:** Standard usage — apps, photos, some offline content.
- **128GB+:** Serious productivity, local video, large app libraries.

Always check if the tablet has a microSD slot — Fire and Samsung tablets do, iPad does not.
