## What to Look for in a Labor Day Laptop

### RAM
- **8GB**: Acceptable for light use, web browsing, Office
- **16GB**: Recommended for students and professionals
- **32GB**: Needed for video editing, heavy multitasking

### Storage
- **256GB SSD**: Minimum — fill up fast
- **512GB SSD**: Sweet spot for most users
- **1TB SSD**: Ideal for large files, photos, videos

### Display
- **1080p (FHD)**: Fine for 13–15" laptops
- **1440p or 2K**: Noticeably sharper, worth paying for
- **OLED**: Best colors and contrast, worth the premium

### Battery Life Claims vs. Reality
Manufacturers test battery life in ideal conditions. Real-world numbers:

| Claimed | Real-World |
|---------|-----------|
| MacBook Air M4: 18 hrs | ~14–16 hrs |
| PC laptop: 12 hrs | ~7–9 hrs |
| Chromebook: 10 hrs | ~8–10 hrs |

MacBook's battery advantage is real and significant for students.
