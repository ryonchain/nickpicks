## What Portable Blenders Can and Can't Do

**Handle well:**
- Fresh fruit smoothies (banana, berries, mango)
- Protein powder + milk/water
- Green smoothies with spinach or kale
- Pre-frozen smoothie packs (soft-frozen fruit)

**Struggle with:**
- Rock-hard ice cubes (only the VOLTRX handles these decently)
- Nut butter in large quantities
- Frozen fibrous vegetables (beets, carrots)
- Anything requiring 90+ seconds of continuous blending

For complex recipes with multiple frozen ingredients, a countertop blender (Vitamix, Ninja Professional) is the better choice.

## USB-C Charging: What to Expect

Most portable blenders charge from 0–100% in 2–3 hours and deliver 12–20 blends per charge. The Ninja Blast's fast-charge feature is useful if you forget to charge overnight — 30 minutes gives you enough for your morning smoothie.

---

## Cleaning

All recommended blenders support self-cleaning: add warm water + a drop of dish soap, blend for 30 seconds, rinse. Don't put the motor base in water. Most cups and lids are top-rack dishwasher safe.
