## Which Electrolyte Is Right for You?

**Endurance athlete or heavy sweater:** LMNT or Redmond Re-Lyte — you need the high sodium.

**General hydration and taste-focused:** Liquid IV or Nuun Sport.

**Sick, hungover, or dehydrated:** DripDrop ORS — the medical formulation works fastest.

**Daily casual support on sodium-restricted diet:** Ultima Replenisher.

**Budget and portability:** Nuun Sport tablets.

The biggest mistake in electrolyte supplementation is choosing a product with inadequate sodium. Sweat is predominantly sodium — products with under 200mg per serving are mostly flavored water with minimal electrolyte replacement value for anyone exercising in heat.
