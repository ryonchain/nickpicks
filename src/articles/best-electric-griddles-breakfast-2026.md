## What to Look For

**Cooking surface size:** 200+ sq. in. for a family of 4. Count eggs or pancakes: a standard 4" pancake needs ~13 sq. in., so a 200 sq. in. surface fits ~15.

**Temperature range:** 400°F minimum for searing; 450°F+ for smash burgers. Lower max temps limit high-heat cooking.

**Surface material:** Non-stick is easiest to clean but degrades over time. Cold-rolled steel (Blackstone) develops natural non-stick properties with seasoning and lasts much longer.

**Grease drainage:** Tilt or channel drainage systems reduce splatter and make cleanup faster.

**Dishwasher safe:** Makes cleaning trivial. Non-removable surfaces must be wiped by hand.
