Labor Day is the #1 shopping holiday for major appliances — and it's not close. Retailers have been training consumers for decades: if you need a washer, dryer, refrigerator, or dishwasher, wait for Labor Day. ## Why Labor Day Is the Best Time to Buy Appliances

Appliance manufacturers push model refreshes to retailers in the fall. That means end-of-summer clearance on current-year units. Combine that with competing sale events at Home Depot, Lowe's, Best Buy, and Samsung's own store — and you get the deepest discounts of the year.

**Historical Labor Day appliance savings:**
- Washing machines: 15–35% off
- Dryers: 15–30% off
- Refrigerators: 20–40% off
- Dishwashers: 20–35% off
- Ranges/ovens: 20–30% off

## Tips for Getting the Best Price

1. **Price match guarantee** — Best Buy will match any competitor's price within 15 days of purchase. Buy early, claim a lower price if it drops.
2. **Credit card rewards** — Use a card that offers 5–6% cash back at Home Depot or Best Buy (Discover/Chase rotating categories).
3. **Delivery + haul-away** — Most retailers include free delivery and haul-away of old appliances on Labor Day sales. Confirm before checkout.
4. **Rebates** — Many energy-efficient appliances qualify for federal and local utility rebates. ENERGY STAR website has a database by ZIP code.
5. **Dented/scratch models** — Home Depot's "Overstock and Clearance" section often has $100–$400 off perfectly functional scratch-and-dent units.
