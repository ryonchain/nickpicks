## What to Know Before You Buy

**Start with bundling:** Before buying routing accessories, use VELCRO ties to bundle parallel cables together. Three bundles of 4 cables are easier to route and hide than 12 individual cables.

**Map your cable runs first:** Trace each cable from its device to its power source or hub. Group cables that travel the same path. A 20-minute planning exercise before buying anything saves wasted product.

**Adhesive vs. screw-mount:** Adhesive works on most hard surfaces but will eventually release on painted drywall or textured wood. For long-term permanent installations, use screw-mounted raceways or under-desk trays.

**Going wireless reduces the problem:** The fastest cable management upgrade is replacing wired peripherals with wireless equivalents. A wireless keyboard, mouse, and speakers eliminate 3–6 cables immediately — before you route a single clip.
