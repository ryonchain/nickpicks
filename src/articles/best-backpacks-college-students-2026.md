## What to Look For in a College Backpack

**Laptop sleeve:** Should be padded, snug (no movement), and fleece-lined to prevent screen scratches. Minimum: 1/2" foam. Best: cellular foam (Incase) or fleece-lined sleeve (North Face).

**Capacity:** 25–35L covers a standard campus day. Under 20L works for light commuters. 35L+ is for students who carry books, gym clothes, and multiple items daily.

**Back system:** Cheap bags use flat foam panels that dig into shoulders. Quality bags use ergonomic S-curved straps and ventilated panels that distribute weight across your frame.

**Warranty:** JanSport, North Face, Osprey, and Timbuk2 all offer lifetime or extensive warranties. Pay more once vs. replace annually.
