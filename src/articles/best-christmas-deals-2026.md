Christmas 2026 falls on **Friday, December 25** — a Friday Christmas means the full week before (Dec 19–24) is prime shopping time, and retailers traditionally front-load their biggest deals starting the weekend before. Here's what to expect, where to shop, and which categories offer the deepest discounts.

## When Do Christmas Deals Start in 2026?

| Date | What Happens |
|------|--------------|
| Black Friday (Nov 28) | Year's first major price drop across all categories |
| Cyber Monday (Dec 1) | Online-only extensions, some new deals |
| Cyber Week (Dec 2–7) | Many BF/CM prices hold |
| Weekend of Dec 12–14 | "Pre-Christmas" sale wave — retailers restock sold-out items |
| Dec 18–22 | Last-chance deals with guaranteed Christmas delivery |
| Dec 25 | Christmas Day — gift card redemptions, digital downloads |
| Dec 26 | After-Christmas clearance begins immediately |

**Key insight:** For most shoppers, the best Christmas price on physical goods is Black Friday or Cyber Monday — not the week before Christmas. If you're waiting until mid-December, you're trading selection for convenience.

## Christmas Deals Shopping Tips

1. **Buy TVs before Christmas, not the week before** — Black Friday and Cyber Monday have the actual lowest prices. The week of Christmas, selection shrinks and prices creep up.
2. **Set price alerts** — Amazon lets you track any product; CamelCamelCamel sends email alerts when prices drop.
3. **Check delivery windows first** — a deal is worthless if it won't arrive before Dec 25. Filter by Prime to confirm guaranteed delivery.
4. **Gift cards are always in stock** — if you're shopping Dec 23–24, digital gift cards and streaming subscriptions are safer than physical goods.
5. **After-Christmas is real** — if you can wait until Dec 26, clearance sales on leftover holiday inventory can be 40–70% off on toys and decorations.
