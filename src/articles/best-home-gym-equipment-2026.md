## Home Gym Equipment Not Worth Buying

- **Multi-station home gyms (cable machines with everything)** — Take up enormous space, difficult to add weight, usually poorly built. Better to buy each piece separately.
- **Smith machines** — Fixes barbell path unnaturally; builds strength patterns that don't transfer to free weights. Spend the money on a real rack.
- **Ab rollers and "ab machines"** — Abs are built in the kitchen and through compound movements. Save the money.
- **"Vibrational platforms"** — No evidence of fitness benefit; a rack or barbell will do more.
