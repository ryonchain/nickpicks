## What to Check Before Buying Any Black Friday Laptop

1. **RAM:** 8GB minimum, 16GB preferred for longevity. Verify if RAM is soldered (can't upgrade later).
2. **Storage:** 256GB minimum; 512GB preferred. SSD only — no HDD.
3. **Display resolution:** 1080p minimum; 1440p or 2K preferred for 14"+ screens.
4. **Ports:** Does it have the ports you need? HDMI, USB-A, SD card — or just USB-C with dongles?
5. **Battery claim vs. reality:** Budget Windows laptops often claim 10 hours; real-world is 5–6. MacBook Air's 18-hour claim is real.
6. **Warranty:** Extended warranty on Black Friday is usually reasonable — $79–$129 for 3 years with accidental damage.
