## Garage Shelving Tips

- **Measure your garage wall height** — standard units are 72–78" tall
- **Install on studs** for wall-mounted (use a stud finder — $20 investment)
- **Keep 18–24" away from sprinkler heads** if your garage has fire suppression
- **Store heavy items on lower shelves** — center of gravity matters
- **Leave 6" clearance from ceiling** for any shelving near garage door opener rail
