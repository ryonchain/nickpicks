## What to Look For in a Pet Vacuum

**Tangle-free brush roll:** Standard brush rolls collect pet hair into wrapped tangles you have to cut out. A tangle-free or hair-screw design eliminates this maintenance headache.

**HEPA filtration + sealed system:** Pet dander is a major allergen. True HEPA filtration only works if the entire vacuum is sealed — otherwise air (with dander) bypasses the filter.

**Battery life:** A 3-bed/2-bath home needs at least 40 minutes of real-world runtime. Manufacturers often quote max runtime at lowest suction — check reviews for real-world numbers.

**Dustbin size:** Daily pet shedding fills small dustbins fast. Look for at least 0.5L for pet-heavy homes.
