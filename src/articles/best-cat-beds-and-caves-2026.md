## What to Know Before You Buy

**Sizing:** Measure your cat's length from nose to base of tail. The cave interior should be at least 1.5x that length so your cat can circle and settle. Most standard caves (Medium) suit cats under 12 lbs; Large suits up to 18–20 lbs.

**Material:** Wool felt (Meowfia) is the most natural, temperature-regulating option. Sherpa and plush are softer and fully machine washable but less breathable. Choose based on your climate and laundry habits.

**Machine washability:** This is the single most practical feature to check. Cats shed heavily in caves, and a bed you can't wash easily will smell within weeks. Check whether the entire bed is washable or just the liner.

**Cave adoption:** Some cats need 2–3 weeks to adopt a new bed. Place it in a spot your cat already sleeps, put a worn shirt or their existing bedding inside, and be patient. Most cats come around.
