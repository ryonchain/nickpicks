## Garage Storage Planning Tips

**Before buying anything:**
1. Measure your available wall area (height × width, between doors and windows)
2. Locate studs — most storage requires stud mounting for weight capacity
3. Categorize what you're storing — tools (pegboard), seasonal (ceiling rack), daily (rail system)

**Weight rules:**
- Drywall anchors: 50 lbs max per anchor — never use for heavy storage
- Studs: 100–200 lbs per stud mount (depends on fastener)
- Ceiling joists: 600+ lbs when properly distributed
