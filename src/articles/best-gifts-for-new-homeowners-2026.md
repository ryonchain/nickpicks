## Gift Strategy: What to Avoid

**Avoid generic decor:** Art, throw pillows, and decorative items are highly personal. Very few new homeowners actually use generic housewarming decor gifts — they end up in donation boxes.

**Avoid perishables:** Flowers, plants, and food baskets are consumed quickly and forgotten. They don't scale with the milestone.

**Avoid candles:** Overcrowded category. New homeowners receive 5+ candles from well-intentioned friends and family.

**Best approach:** Ask where they registered (if applicable) or what they still need. New homeowners almost always have a mental list of "things I haven't bought yet." A gift card to Amazon or Home Depot removes the guessing entirely.
