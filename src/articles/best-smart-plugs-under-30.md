# Best Smart Plugs Under $30: Top Picks to Automate Your Home on a Budget

Smart plugs are the easiest entry point into home automation. For under $30, you can control any lamp, fan, or coffee maker from your phone — or program it to run on a schedule without ever touching a switch. Whether you're building a full smart home or just tired of getting up to turn off the lights, these devices deliver a massive return for a tiny investment.

After testing and researching the top-rated options on Amazon, here are the five best smart plugs under $30.

---

## What to Look for in a Smart Plug Under $30

Before diving into recommendations, here's what separates a great budget smart plug from a frustrating one:

- **Voice assistant compatibility** — Alexa and Google Home are the most common. Matter support is a newer bonus.
- **App quality** — A clunky app kills the experience. Look for apps with scheduling and energy monitoring.
- **Form factor** — Some smart plugs block neighboring outlets. Low-profile or side-outlet designs are better.
- **Energy monitoring** — Knowing what's drawing power is a bonus not all budget plugs offer.
