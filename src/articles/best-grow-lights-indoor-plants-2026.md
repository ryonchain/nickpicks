## Understanding Grow Light Specs

**PAR (Photosynthetically Active Radiation):** The light wavelengths plants actually use (400–700nm). More PAR = faster growth.

**PPFD (Photosynthetic Photon Flux Density):** PAR intensity at the plant surface. Seedlings need ~100–200 µmol/m²/s. Herbs: 200–400. Fruiting plants: 400–800+.

**Spectrum:** Full spectrum (white light) covers all plant needs. Red+blue is efficient but plants grow faster under full spectrum. Warm white (3000K) encourages flowering; cool white (5000K+) encourages vegetative growth.

**Distance:** Most grow lights need to be 6–24 inches from the plant canopy. Too close = light burn. Too far = stretchy, leggy plants.
