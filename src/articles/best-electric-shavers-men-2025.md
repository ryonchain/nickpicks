## Foil vs. Rotary: Which Is Right for You?

**Foil shavers** use a thin perforated metal screen over oscillating blades. They shave closer, work better on flat areas (neck, jaw), and are ideal for daily shavers with fine-to-medium stubble. Best brands: Braun, Panasonic.

**Rotary shavers** use 3 or 4 spinning disc heads mounted on flexible pivots. They contour to facial curves better and handle longer stubble (2–3 days of growth) more effectively. Better for irregular growth and rounded face shapes. Best brand: Philips Norelco.

**Bottom line**: If you shave daily with fine/medium hair — foil. If you shave every 2–3 days or have coarser, irregular growth — rotary.

## Maintenance: Making Your Shaver Last

**Daily**: Rinse the head under warm water for 10 seconds after each shave.

**Weekly**: Remove the head, tap out whiskers, rinse with water or shaver cleaner spray.

**Monthly**: Apply a drop of shaver oil to the blades to reduce friction.

**Every 12–18 months**: Replace foil and blade heads. Performance drops noticeably when blades dull — don't wait.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Is a foil or rotary electric shaver better?**
Foil shavers (like Braun) use oscillating blades under a thin metal screen and excel at close, clean shaves — ideal for daily use and straight grain growth. Rotary shavers (like Philips Norelco) use spinning heads that flex to contour the face — better for longer stubble and irregular growth patterns.

**How long do electric shavers last?**
A quality electric shaver lasts 5–7 years with proper maintenance. Replace the foil/blade heads every 12–18 months for best performance. Braun and Philips both offer replacement heads for their current models.

**Can electric shavers cause ingrown hairs?**
Electric shavers cause fewer ingrown hairs than razors because they don't cut below the skin surface. For men with sensitive skin or curly hair, a foil shaver used with the grain is the safest choice.

**Are cordless electric shavers as powerful as corded ones?**
Modern cordless shavers match or exceed corded performance. Braun's Series 9 and Philips' S9000 Prestige run on lithium-ion batteries delivering 60–90 minutes of shaving from a 1-hour charge — enough for weeks of daily use.

**What electric shaver is best for sensitive skin?**
The Braun Series 5 5018s with SensoSmart pressure sensor is the best for sensitive skin — it alerts you when you're pressing too hard, reducing irritation and redness. The Philips Norelco S9000 Prestige also uses gentle floating heads ideal for reactive skin.
