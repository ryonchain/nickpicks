## What to Look For

### Step Motion Type
- **Independent steps (scissor motion):** Most stair steppers. Each foot moves independently.
- **Linked steps (piston):** Both steps move in tandem — less natural, better for balance support.
- **Stair mill (rotating stairs):** The closest to real stair climbing — found on StairMaster SM5 and similar commercial machines.

### Resistance System
- **Hydraulic:** Budget models. Non-linear resistance, quieter.
- **Magnetic:** Mid-range. Smoother, more adjustable.
- **Electronic/motorized:** Premium. Precise, programmable.

### Handlebar Style
- **Support handles:** Essential for balance for most users
- **No handles (mini stepper):** Uses more core stabilization — harder, but less safe for beginners
