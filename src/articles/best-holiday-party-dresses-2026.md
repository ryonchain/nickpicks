## Holiday Dress Color Guide

**Classic choices:** Red, forest green, black, navy — holiday traditionalist

**Modern choices:** Emerald, deep burgundy, cobalt blue, burnt orange — elevated seasonal palette

**Statement choices:** Gold, silver, champagne — sequins and metallics for maximum impact

**Avoid:** Pastels and summer brights — they look out of season at holiday events
