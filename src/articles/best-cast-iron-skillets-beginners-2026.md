## Cast Iron Care for Beginners

**Seasoning:** A new bare cast iron skillet needs to be re-seasoned over time. After each use, dry completely (put on low heat for 2 minutes), then rub a thin layer of flaxseed or vegetable oil while hot. This builds the polymerized coating that makes cast iron semi-non-stick.

**Cleaning:** You CAN use soap (the old "never use soap" rule is a myth for modern detergents). What you cannot do is soak it in water or leave it wet — this causes rust.

**What to cook first:** Bacon, sautéed vegetables, or anything fatty. Avoid acidic foods (tomatoes, citrus) until the seasoning is well established.

**Rust:** If you see orange rust, it's not ruined. Scrub with steel wool, rinse, dry completely, and re-season. Cast iron is nearly indestructible.
