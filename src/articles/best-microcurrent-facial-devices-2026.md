## What Microcurrent Actually Does

Microcurrent therapy delivers sub-sensory electrical current (measured in microamperes) that mimics the body's own bioelectrical signals. These low-level currents re-educate facial muscles, stimulating ATP production and protein synthesis in muscle tissue — effectively "re-training" sagging muscles to their lifted, more youthful position.

**Clinical evidence:** Studies show microcurrent increases ATP production by up to 500% and boosts collagen and elastin synthesis by 45% with consistent use. Results are cumulative — most users see visible lifting and contouring after 60-day daily use, with maintenance sessions needed to sustain results.

**How it differs from LED:** LED works on skin cells. Microcurrent works on facial muscles below the skin. Most advanced users combine both modalities.

## Buying Guide: How to Choose a Microcurrent Device

### Output Level (µA)
Higher is not always better for beginners. Start at 200 µA (NuFace Mini), establish a protocol, then consider upgrading to 400+ µA once your muscles are acclimated. Jumping straight to high output can cause muscle fatigue or temporary puffiness.

### Waveform Type
Traditional microcurrent uses a constant sine wave. Advanced devices like ZIIP use alternating nanocurrent/microcurrent protocols that may produce faster results. The trade-off is price and complexity.

### Conductor Requirements
All microcurrent devices require a conductive medium between device and skin. NuFace's Primer Gel, ZIIP's proprietary gels, and water-based aloe alternatives all work — some devices require brand-specific formulas.

## FAQ

### How long does it take to see results from microcurrent?
Most users notice temporary lifting immediately after their first session. Cumulative, lasting results build over 60 days of daily use. After the initial 60-day protocol, maintenance sessions 2–3 times per week sustain results. Stopping use causes gradual reversal over weeks.

### Can microcurrent replace Botox or fillers?
No — but it occupies a different space. Microcurrent addresses muscle tone and surface texture without freezing muscle movement. Many users combine microcurrent with injectables for comprehensive anti-aging. Consider it a fitness routine for facial muscles rather than a medical treatment replacement.

### Is microcurrent safe during pregnancy?
Manufacturers universally recommend avoiding microcurrent devices during pregnancy, as the electrical current effects on fetal development are not studied. Consult your OB before using any electrical skin device during pregnancy or breastfeeding.
