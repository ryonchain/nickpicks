## Building a Fall Fragrance Strategy

**Layer by room:**
- Entryway: Bright, welcoming (apple cider, cinnamon)
- Living room: Warm, cozy (wood smoke, pumpkin spice, vanilla)
- Bedroom: Soft, relaxing (cashmere musk, vanilla amber)
- Kitchen: Clean, spiced (apple harvest, ginger)
- Bathroom: Fresh, clean (eucalyptus with a hint of wood)

**Avoid fragrance fatigue:** Rotate scents every few days. Your nose adapts to familiar scents within 20 minutes — you stop smelling what guests can still smell strongly.
