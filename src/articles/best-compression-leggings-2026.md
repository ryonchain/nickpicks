## Compression Leggings Buying Guide

### Understanding Compression Levels

In activewear, "compression" isn't clinically graded like medical compression stockings. It refers to the tightness and fabric density of the garment. For workout purposes, you want:

**Light compression:** Soft, buttery fabrics like CRZ YOGA Butterluxe. Good for yoga, pilates, and low-impact work. Prioritizes feel.

**Medium compression:** Denser fabrics like Gymshark Vital. Better for weighted training and high-impact movement. Prioritizes support.

**Recovery compression:** Graduated compression from ankle upward. Specifically designed for post-workout blood flow support.

### The Squat Test

Before committing to any light-colored legging, perform the squat test:
1. Put on the leggings
2. Stand in front of a mirror and drop into a deep squat
3. Check for sheering (see-through) at the seat

Any legging that fails this test will embarrass you in a yoga or gym class. Dark colors almost never fail; light gray and oatmeal are the highest risk. Both CRZ YOGA and Colorfulkoala pass in dark colors.

### Sizing Notes

Both CRZ YOGA and Colorfulkoala run slightly small — size up one size from your typical activewear size if you're between sizes. Gymshark UK sizing runs larger than US — check their size guide chart carefully.

### Care Instructions

Machine wash cold, inside out, on gentle cycle. Never use fabric softener — it degrades the spandex and reduces compression over time. Air dry or tumble dry low. Proper care extends the life of compression leggings by years.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Do compression leggings actually help with workouts?**
Yes — compression reduces muscle oscillation (the vibration muscles experience during impact), which reduces fatigue and soreness. Multiple studies support compression garments' effectiveness for performance and recovery.

**How long should compression leggings last?**
With proper care, 1–2 years of regular use. Loss of elasticity is the primary indicator it's time to replace — if the waistband rolls or the fabric no longer returns to shape after stretching, replace them.

**Can I wear compression leggings all day?**
Yes, provided the compression level is comfortable. Moderate compression leggings like CRZ YOGA Butterluxe are comfortable for extended wear. Avoid very high-compression leggings for more than 4–6 hours of continuous wear.

**Are CRZ YOGA leggings a Lululemon dupe?**
CRZ YOGA is often positioned that way, and the comparison is apt — the Butterluxe fabric closely mirrors Lululemon's Nulu fabric in feel and compression. CRZ YOGA is an independent brand, not an official Lululemon product.
