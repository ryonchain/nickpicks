## Additional Must-Have Grill Accessories

**Grill Brush:** A quality brass or stainless steel brush removes residue fast. Avoid wire brushes with loose bristles — they're a food safety hazard.

**Grill Basket:** A hinged or perforated basket keeps vegetables, shrimp, and fish from falling through grates. Get one with a long handle.

**Basting Brush/Mop:** Silicone basting brushes handle sauce better than natural-bristle brushes and are dishwasher safe.

**Drip Pan:** A disposable aluminum pan underneath your grill grates catches fat for easier cleanup and prevents flare-up grease from building up.

**Chimney Starter:** Essential for charcoal grilling — lights coals without lighter fluid in 15 minutes.
