## Temperature Cheat Sheet

| Meat | Pull Temperature | Notes |
|------|----------------|-------|
| Chicken breast | 160–165°F | Carryover takes it to 165°F |
| Chicken thighs | 175°F | Higher temp = better texture |
| Pork chops | 145°F | Pink is safe at this temp |
| Pork shoulder (pulled) | 195–205°F | Probe should slide in like butter |
| Beef steak (medium-rare) | 130–135°F | Pull at 127°F, rest 5 min |
| Brisket | 195–205°F | Test with probe for tenderness |
| Burger (ground beef) | 160°F | Food safety standard |
| Salmon | 125–130°F | Slightly translucent center |
