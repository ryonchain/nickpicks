## Essential Pizza Oven Accessories

**Pizza Stone:** Most ovens include a cordierite stone floor. Keep a backup — stones can crack.

**Pizza Peel:** Essential for launching and turning pizza. Get a 12" or 16" aluminum peel for launching and a smaller perforated peel for turning.

**Infrared Thermometer:** Point-and-shoot reads the stone surface temperature in 1 second. Know exactly when you're ready to cook.

**Pizza Dough Scraper:** A bench scraper speeds up dough shaping and transfers pizza to the peel without tearing.
