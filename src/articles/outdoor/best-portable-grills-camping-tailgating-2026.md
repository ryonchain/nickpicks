## What to Consider Before Buying a Portable Grill

**Weight and packability:** For backpacking, under 3 lbs is the target. For car camping and tailgating, 15–50 lbs is manageable if it folds to a compact shape.

**Fuel type:** Propane is the most widely available at gas stations and sporting goods stores. Charcoal requires sourcing briquettes and managing ash. Butane burns clean but canisters are harder to find in rural areas.

**Cooking surface:** 150 sq in handles 2 people. 250–320 sq in serves 4–6. 400+ sq in is for groups.

**Two-zone cooking:** Two independent burners (propane) or damper control (charcoal) let you zone cook — essential for thicker proteins that need direct sear then indirect finish.
