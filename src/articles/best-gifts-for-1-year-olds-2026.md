## What to Avoid When Buying for 1-Year-Olds

- **Small parts:** Anything with pieces smaller than a toilet paper roll is a choking hazard. Check the age label.
- **Screen-based toys:** Tablets and phone apps provide zero developmental benefit at this age. Skip them.
- **Complex toys:** 1-year-olds are overstimulated by too many sounds and colors. Simple is better.
- **Fast-fashion toys:** Plastic toys that break in a week are a waste. Invest in brands like Melissa & Doug, Fisher-Price, VTech, and Battat.
