## Women's Hoodie Buying Guide

**Silhouette options for 2026:**
- **Oversized:** The dominant trend. Boxy, relaxed, worn solo or as a layer over base layers.
- **Classic regular fit:** Versatile, works for gym and casual.
- **Cropped:** Better for high-waist pants; shows midriff with movement.
- **Fitted:** Best for gym use or structured looks.

**Fabric feel spectrum:**
- **Ultra-soft fleece (Lululemon, Aerie):** Cloud-like feel; best for lounging and casual wear.
- **Standard fleece (Amazon Essentials, Nike):** Reliable, breathable; best for active use.
- **Heavyweight cotton (Champion, Carhartt):** Durable, structured; best for outdoor and casual durability.

**Styling for fall:**
- **Oversized hoodie + biker shorts + platform sneakers** — athleisure street look
- **Cropped hoodie + high-waist jeans + white sneakers** — casual weekend
- **Pullover hoodie + midi skirt + boots** — elevated casual
- **Hoodie + joggers + puffer vest** — cold-weather layering
