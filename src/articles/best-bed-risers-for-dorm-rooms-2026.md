## What You Can Fit Under a Raised Dorm Bed

| Clearance | What Fits |
|-----------|----------|
| 10–14" (stock dorm bed) | Flat under-bed bins, rolled items, shoes |
| 14–17" (+ 3–5" risers) | Most under-bed storage containers, small suitcase |
| 17–22" (+ 6–8" risers) | Full-height storage drawers, carry-on luggage |

**Practical tip:** Buy your under-bed storage containers AFTER you know your total clearance (bed height + risers). Measure before ordering. A 12" storage container won't fit under a 10" clearance, but works fine with 3" risers added.

## Checklist: Before You Buy Bed Risers

- [ ] Measure your current dorm bed leg diameter (most are 1.5"–2.5")
- [ ] Check the riser's maximum leg diameter fits your bed
- [ ] Decide target total clearance (current + riser height)
- [ ] Check dorm policy — most allow risers, some prohibit them
- [ ] Order under-bed storage AFTER knowing final clearance
- [ ] Confirm riser weight capacity includes your weight + mattress + topper

Most dorm beds have legs in the 1.5"–2" diameter range. Standard risers fit this. The only exception: platform bed frames (no individual legs) — these don't work with cup-style risers at all.
