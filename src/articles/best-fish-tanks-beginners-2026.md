## Beginner's Guide: Setting Up Your First Tank

**Cycling your tank (critical):**
Never add fish immediately after setup. The nitrogen cycle needs 2–4 weeks to establish beneficial bacteria. Symptoms of uncycled tank: fish gasping at the surface, ammonia spikes, fish deaths.

Steps:
1. Set up tank, add dechlorinated water
2. Add a pinch of fish food daily (creates ammonia)
3. Test water weekly with a liquid test kit (API Master Test Kit, ~$25)
4. When ammonia and nitrite read 0 and nitrate reads 5–40 ppm — the tank is cycled
5. Add fish slowly (2–3 at a time, not all at once)

**Fish for beginners:**
- Betta (solo in 5–10 gallon)
- Guppies (community, pairs or groups)
- Neon tetras (school of 6+)
- Zebra danios (active, hardy)
- Corydoras catfish (bottom cleaners)
