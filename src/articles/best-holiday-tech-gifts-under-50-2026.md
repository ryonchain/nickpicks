### 2. Tile Mate (2023) — Best for the Person Who Loses Everything

https://www.amazon.com/s?k=Product&tag=nickpicks-20

The Tile Mate is a Bluetooth tracker that attaches to keys, wallets, luggage, or any bag. When something goes missing, the Tile app shows its last known location on a map. Anyone nearby running the Tile app (tens of millions of users) silently updates the item's location anonymously.

**Why it wins:** The problem it solves (lost keys/wallet) is universal and frustrating. Setup takes 2 minutes. Water-resistant. Loud ring when within Bluetooth range. Replaceable battery lasts ~3 years.

**Best for:** Forgetful family members, frequent travelers, anyone who's ever missed a flight looking for their keys.

**Cons:** Location relies on the Tile network — works best in cities and suburbs. Apple AirTags have wider network coverage if the recipient is deep Apple ecosystem.

**Bundle for impact.** A Tile Mate + Anker charger + quality USB-C cable is a $50 bundle that feels like a $100 gift and solves three real problems at once.
