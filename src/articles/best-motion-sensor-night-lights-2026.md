## Where to Place Motion Sensor Night Lights

**The essential locations:**

1. **Hallway outside bedroom door** — the first dark space you enter at night; a light here prevents the worst navigation hazards
2. **Bathroom entrance or inside bathroom** — the overnight destination; install at the light switch height or just inside the door
3. **Top and bottom of stairs** — falls on stairs are a serious injury risk in the dark; lights at both ends provide complete coverage
4. **Kitchen** — for late-night water or snack trips; under-cabinet lights work well here

**Secondary locations:**

5. **Children's rooms and hallways** — kids navigate at night too; a dim night light in their room and the hall outside prevents panic
6. **Garage** — motion sensor lights with real output (like the Mr. Beams MB720A) make the garage functional without hunting for a light switch
7. **Closets** — under-shelf or ceiling-mount motion lights eliminate the need for a dedicated closet light switch
8. **Basement stairway** — one of the higher-risk navigation points in a dark house

## Frequently Asked Questions

**What is the best motion sensor night light?**
The Govee Motion Sensor Night Light is the best overall for flexibility (rechargeable, magnetic mount, adjustable brightness). For plug-in permanence, the AUVON 6-pack is the best value. For outdoor/garage use, the Mr. Beams MB720A.

**Are motion sensor night lights safe to leave on all night?**
Yes. LED night lights draw less than 2W and produce minimal heat. They activate for 30-90 seconds per motion trigger and are otherwise off — total overnight power draw is negligible.

**How long do battery-powered motion sensor night lights last?**
Rechargeable models last 1-3 months between charges with typical overnight use. AA-battery models last 2-4 months. D-battery models (like the Mr. Beams MB720A) can last up to 1 year.

**Where should I put motion sensor night lights?**
The critical path: bedroom hallway → bathroom. Secondary: stairs (top and bottom), kitchen, closets. Cover the full overnight navigation route to eliminate every dark segment.

**Can motion sensor night lights be used outside?**
Some are rated for outdoor use — look for IP44 or IP65 weatherproofing. The Mr. Beams MB720A in this guide is outdoor-rated. Standard plug-in models are indoor only.
