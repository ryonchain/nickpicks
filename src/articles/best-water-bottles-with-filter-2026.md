## FAQs

**Can I use these bottles with salt water?**
No. None of these filters remove salt. For saltwater, you need a desalination device or reverse osmosis system.

**Are filtered water bottles allowed on planes?**
Yes — empty bottles are TSA-allowed. You can fill them at an airport fountain or post-security. The GRAYL's 16.9 oz capacity stays under 3.4 oz carry-on limit when empty.

**Do these bottles make water taste better?**
Carbon-filtered bottles (Brita, Epic) dramatically improve tap taste. Hollow fiber filters (LifeStraw, Sawyer) remove microbes but don't affect taste as much. GRAYL removes most chemicals including those that cause taste issues.
