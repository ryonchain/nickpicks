## Gaming Deals to Skip on Prime Day

- **Generic gaming chairs.** Sub-$100 gaming chairs with "racing" designs are uncomfortable and fall apart within 18 months. Either buy quality or use a proper office chair.
- **Last-gen console bundles at full price.** Verify that any bundle saves you at least $40–$60 over buying components separately. Some "bundles" are just two items stacked at list price.
- **No-name gaming mice and keyboards.** The sensor matters for mice. Major brands (Logitech, Razer, SteelSeries, Corsair) use sensors that perform consistently under load. No-name sensors spin out at high speed.
