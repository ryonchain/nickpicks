# Best Cornhole Boards and Sets in 2026

Cornhole is the most popular yard game in the US — playable at tailgates, backyards, beaches, and camping. A complete set includes two boards and eight bags (4 per team). The variables: board size (regulation vs. smaller), construction (MDF vs. hardwood), portability (folding legs vs. fixed), and extras (LED lights for night play).

## Official ACL Regulation Specs

- Board size: 48"x24"
- Hole: 6-inch diameter, 9 inches from top, centered horizontally
- Surface: Smooth (balls slide naturally — no wax needed on proper boards)
- Regulation height: Front 2.5"–4" from ground, back legs adjustable
- Playing distance: 27 feet (foul line to foul line)

## Bag Types Explained

**Duck cloth:** The standard bag material. Slightly rough texture; slides moderately well on smooth boards. Most versatile.

**Suede:** Slicker than duck cloth. Better for "airmail" shots (straight into hole). Less good for controlled sliding.

**Dual-sided (recommended):** Most quality sets include dual-sided bags — one duck cloth side, one suede side. Players pick which side to use based on strategy. Standard in competitive play.

## Scoring

- **Bag through hole:** 3 points
- **Bag on board:** 1 point
- **Cancellation scoring:** Teams' points cancel each other. Team A scores 5, Team B scores 3 → A earns 2 points this round.
- **Win:** First to exactly 21 (busting back to 15 if you exceed 21 by some rulesets; or "first to 21 or over" in casual play).
