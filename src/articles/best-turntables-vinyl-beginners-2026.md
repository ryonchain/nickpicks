## Beginner Setup Guide

**Minimum required components:**
1. **Turntable** (this guide)
2. **Phono preamp** (built into most beginner turntables)
3. **Powered/active speakers** (like Edifier R1280T) OR a **stereo receiver** with passive speakers

**What you don't need to buy immediately:**
- Fancy record cleaning equipment — a carbon fiber brush ($15) removes surface dust and is sufficient to start
- A separate DAC or preamp upgrade — use the built-in preamp while building your collection
- An expensive first record — start with new pressings of albums you know well, so you can hear what your setup sounds like on familiar music
