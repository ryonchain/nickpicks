## Fall 2026 Denim Trends

**What's trending:**
- Wide-leg and barrel leg in all washes
- Dark wash straight-leg (clean, work-appropriate)
- Light-wash vintage-inspired flares
- High-waisted in nearly every silhouette
- Barrel fit (relaxed through the hip, tapered at the ankle) — an emerging silhouette

**What's fading:**
- Skinny jeans (still available but losing fashion ground)
- Distressed/ripped at-the-knee (still sold but no longer feels fresh)

---

## Jeans Fit Guide

**Rise:** High-rise (9"+ inseam at front) is the dominant silhouette for 2026 across all fits.

**Width through leg:** Wide and straight are most current. Skinny remains functional but not fashion-forward.

**Inseam:** Standard women's jeans inseam is 30–32". For tall women (5'9"+), look specifically for "tall" or "long" options.

**Stretch vs. non-stretch:** Non-stretch (rigid) denim looks more polished, holds its shape better, but requires break-in. Stretch is more comfortable from day one.
