## What to Look For

- **Corded vs. cordless:** Corded 12V vacuums give unlimited runtime but keep you near the car; cordless give freedom but typically 10–20 minutes of use before needing a recharge.
- **Suction power:** For car use, look for 100W+ on corded models. Cordless power is harder to compare — AW (air watts) is more meaningful than volts alone.
- **Attachment quality:** A motorized brush head for upholstery is the biggest upgrade over a standard attachment set. Crevice tools are essential; brush tools help for vents and dashboards.
- **Cord length:** 12V cords under 15 feet won't comfortably reach rear seats from a front port in most SUVs. Prioritize 16 feet or longer for full coverage.
- **Filter type:** Washable filters save ongoing cost and maintain performance longer than disposable ones. HEPA or HEPA-type filtration matters for allergy sufferers.
- **Dirt cup size:** Small cups (under 0.5L) require frequent emptying during a full clean. Larger cups let you finish the job without stopping.
