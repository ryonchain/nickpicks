## Sample $500 Home Gym Workout Plan

**Monday (Push):** Dumbbell bench press, overhead press, tricep extension, push-ups (3x10–15)
**Tuesday (Pull):** Pull-ups/chin-ups, dumbbell rows, bicep curls, face pulls with band (3x10–15)
**Wednesday (Legs):** Goblet squats, Romanian deadlifts, Bulgarian split squats, banded lateral walks (3x12–15)
**Thursday (Rest or active recovery)**
**Friday (Full body + Cardio):** Kettlebell swings, dumbbell complexes, push-ups, plank circuits (20–30 minutes)
**Weekend:** Yoga/mobility, walks, recreational activity

This covers strength, hypertrophy, and cardiovascular fitness — all with equipment under $500.
