## Buying Tips for Prime Day Shoe Deals

**Check your size before assuming it's in stock.** Popular sizes (men's 10–11, women's 7–9) sell out fastest during Prime Day shoe deals. If your size is available at the deal price — buy it.

**Read the return policy.** Amazon has a generous return policy on most footwear, including free returns on Prime orders. You can order your normal size and a half-size up to try, then return the pair that doesn't fit. Just initiate both returns within the return window.

**Look for coupon stacking.** Some shoe listings include a "clip coupon" for an additional 5–20% off. Clip the coupon before adding to cart — Prime Day base price + clipped coupon is the true floor price.

**Buy at least a year's worth if you love a specific model.** Running shoes typically need replacement every 300–500 miles. If you've found a model you trust, Prime Day is the time to stock a backup pair.
