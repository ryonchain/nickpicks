## USB Mic Setup Tips

**Microphone distance:** Position 6–8 inches from mouth, slightly below mouth level aimed up. This minimizes plosives (harsh P and B sounds) and maximizes voice presence.

**Room treatment:** A closet with hanging clothes is the easiest DIY acoustic treatment. Blankets, pillows, and carpet all absorb high-frequency reflections. Even a bathroom towel draped behind the mic stand reduces echo significantly.

**Gain setting:** Set input gain so your voice peaks at -12 to -6 dBFS during normal speaking. Peaks that consistently hit 0 dBFS (red meter) will distort.
