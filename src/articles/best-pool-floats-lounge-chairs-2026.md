## Float Care and Storage

**Inflation:** Use an electric pump or hand pump — blow-up by mouth is unhygienic and leaves moisture inside (causing mildew). Most standard inflation needles fit pool floats.

**Deflation and storage:** Dry completely before rolling or folding. Store in a mesh bag or open storage bin that allows air circulation. Never store folded while damp — vinyl develops mold and odor within days.

**UV protection:** Apply 303 Aerospace Protectant to vinyl floats to extend color life. Brings vinyl back from chalky-dry to smooth and supple.

**Repair:** Small punctures are easy to fix with a vinyl patch kit ($5–$8). Large tears usually aren't worth patching — replacement is more cost-effective at these price points.
