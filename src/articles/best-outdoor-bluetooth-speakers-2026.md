## IP Rating Guide

| Rating | What It Means | Use Case |
|--------|--------------|----------|
| IPX4 | Splash-resistant (rain, spray) | Outdoor tables, covered patios |
| IPX5 | Jet-proof (shower, heavy rain) | Poolside, boating |
| IP65 | Dustproof + jet-proof | Beach, camping |
| IP67 | Dustproof + 1m submersion (30 min) | Pool, lake, rain |
| IP68 | Dustproof + deeper submersion | Kayaking, diving scenarios |

For outdoor use, IP67 minimum is the right target. IPX4 is acceptable for covered areas but won't survive being dropped in water.
