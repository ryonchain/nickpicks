## Back to School Shopping Strategy for Prime Day

**Buy now vs. wait:**
- **Buy on Prime Day:** Laptops, headphones, tablets, smart speakers, USB hubs, storage
- **Wait for Black Friday:** Gaming PCs, large monitors, TVs
- **Don't wait for anything on sale:** Textbooks, course materials — buy those when you have the syllabus

**For college freshmen:**
Start with the essentials (laptop, headphones, charger, portable battery) and buy everything else after move-in when you know what you actually need. It's easier to assess dorm storage, space, and priorities once you're there.
