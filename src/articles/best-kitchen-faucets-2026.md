## Pull-Down vs. Pull-Out

**Pull-down:** Spray head pulls straight down. Better for filling tall pots and rinsing the sink. The more popular choice for deep single-basin sinks.

**Pull-out:** Spray head pulls forward and out. Better for small sinks where downward swing is limited. More maneuverable around crowded sinks.
