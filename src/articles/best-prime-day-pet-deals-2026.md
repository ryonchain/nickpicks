## Prime Day Pet Buying Guide

**Factor subscription costs into GPS tracker decisions.** All GPS trackers require a recurring subscription. Calculate annual subscription cost (typically $60–$100/year) alongside the hardware price. An annual subscription paid upfront is usually 30–40% cheaper than monthly billing.

**Timing automatic feeder setup before Prime Day ends.** Automatic feeders take 30–60 minutes to configure via app — set them up promptly after delivery so your pet adjusts to the new routine during a lower-stress period.

**Water fountains need filter replacement.** Most pet fountains use carbon filters that need replacement every 4–8 weeks. Factor filter subscription costs into your ongoing budget — usually $10–$20 per 3-pack.

**Robot vacuums and pet hair:** The Roomba j7+ requires self-emptying station consumable bags (~$20 for 3). Running a cycle daily in a home with shedding pets will fill a bag roughly every 1–2 weeks.
