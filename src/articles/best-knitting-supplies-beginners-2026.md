## Accessories Worth Adding

**Stitch markers** (Knitter's Pride split-ring style, ~$6): clip-on or slip-on rings that mark pattern repeats and row beginnings. Essential for any pattern with increases, decreases, or stitch counts.

**Tapestry needles** (~$5): blunt-tipped large-eye needles for weaving in yarn ends. Every project ends with this step.

**Row counter** (KnitPro mechanical, ~$5): click-to-count rows so you don't lose count on long pieces.

**Project bag** (canvas drawstring tote, ~$10): keeps yarn clean and contained, and projects portable.
