Peloton's Bike+ is the bike that started the connected fitness revolution, and despite years of competition and company turbulence, it remains the class leader in one critical area: the quality and quantity of live and on-demand cycling classes.

## How to Choose an Indoor Exercise Bike

### The subscription question
Every connected bike comes with a subscription cost. Over 3 years:

| Platform | Monthly Cost | 3-Year Total |
|----------|-------------|--------------|
| Peloton All-Access | $44/mo | $1,584 |
| NordicTrack iFit | $39/mo | $1,404 |
| Echelon | $34.99/mo | $1,260 |
| Bowflex JRNY | $19.99/mo | $720 |
| Schwinn IC4 (no sub) | $0 | $0 |

Factor this into your total cost of ownership calculation. The Schwinn IC4 + Peloton App subscription ($12.99/mo) = $768 over 3 years — cheaper than any proprietary subscription.

### Resistance types

**Magnetic resistance (most common at $500+):**
- Silent — important if you live in an apartment or share space with family
- Consistent — resistance doesn't drift
- Low maintenance — no contact, no friction, no wear

**Friction resistance (common under $400):**
- Uses felt pads against the flywheel
- Some noise and requires pad replacement over time
- Still effective, just less premium

### Flywheel weight
- Under 20 lbs: light, choppy feel — acceptable for casual riding
- 20–40 lbs: smooth for most users
- 40+ lbs: road-like inertia, best for serious cyclists

### SPD vs. standard pedals
Cycling cleats (SPD compatible pedals) dramatically improve power transfer and reduce foot movement during intense sessions. If you already own cycling cleats or plan to get them, look for dual-link pedals that support both options.

## Peloton App Without Peloton Hardware

One underappreciated option: the Peloton App ($12.99/mo) works on any bike via Bluetooth — including the Schwinn IC4, Echelon, and many other connected bikes. You get access to Peloton's full class library (minus auto-following resistance) without paying for Peloton hardware.

This is often the best strategy: buy quality hardware like the Schwinn IC4 ($799), subscribe to Peloton App ($12.99/mo), and enjoy 50,000+ classes at a fraction of the hardware cost.
