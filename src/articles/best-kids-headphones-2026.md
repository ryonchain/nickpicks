## What the Research Says About Children's Hearing

**WHO Safe Listening Guidelines:**
- 85 dB maximum for extended daily listening
- At 85 dB: safe for up to 8 hours
- At 94 dB: safe for only 1 hour
- At 100 dB: safe for less than 15 minutes

Children's eardrums and hair cells are more sensitive than adults'. Hearing damage from noise exposure is cumulative and permanent — there's no treatment for noise-induced hearing loss.

**Software volume limits aren't enough:** iOS and Android parental controls can limit software volume, but kids can reset these. Hardware-limited headphones (like Puro) enforce the limit at the driver level.
