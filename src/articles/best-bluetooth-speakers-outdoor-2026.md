## What IP Rating Means for Water

- **IPX4:** Splash-resistant from any direction. Not for pool use.
- **IPX6:** Water jets resistant. Good for rain; still not submersible.
- **IPX7:** Submersible to 1 meter for 30 minutes. Pool-safe.
- **IP67:** Dust-tight + submersible to 1 meter for 30 minutes. Best for outdoor use.
