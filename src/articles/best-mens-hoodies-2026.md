## Men's Hoodie Buying Guide

**Weight categories:**
- **Midweight (6–8 oz):** Good for mild weather, layering under jackets. More breathable for gym use.
- **Heavyweight (10–12 oz):** Warmer, more durable, holds structure better. Best for outdoors and cold weather.

**Pullover vs. zip-up:**
- **Pullover:** Warmer (no zipper draft), cleaner silhouette, most classic look.
- **Zip-up:** More versatile layering piece, easier on/off, works as a light jacket.

**Fit guide:**
- **Regular/relaxed:** Best for outdoor work and layering over base layers.
- **Slim/athletic:** Better silhouette for lifestyle and streetwear.
- **Oversized:** Fashion-forward, works best in neutral colors.

**Fabric care:**
- Wash inside-out on cold to prevent pilling
- Tumble dry low — high heat accelerates wear on fleece
- Don't overload the dryer — friction causes pilling
