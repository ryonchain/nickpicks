## What Is Amazon Prime Student?

Amazon Prime Student is a discounted membership for college and university students. It includes everything regular Prime offers, at half the price — after a 6-month free trial.

**What's Included:**
- Free 2-day shipping on millions of items (no minimum order)
- Prime Video (streaming movies and TV)
- Prime Music (90 million songs ad-free)
- Prime Reading (free Kindle books and magazines)
- Prime Gaming (free games and in-game content each month)
- Amazon Photos (unlimited photo storage)
- Same-day delivery in eligible zip codes
- Exclusive student-only deals during Prime Day and back-to-school season

**Cost:**
| Period | Price |
|--------|-------|
| First 6 months | **FREE** |
| After trial | $7.49/month or $69/year |
| Regular Prime price | $14.99/month or $139/year |

You save $50 if you pay annually after the trial.

## How to Sign Up for Prime Student

**Requirements:**
- Must be enrolled at a 2-year or 4-year accredited college or university
- Must have a valid `.edu` email address (or provide enrollment documentation)
- Must be 18 or older

**Steps:**
1. Go to [Amazon Prime Student](https://www.amazon.com/prime?tag=nickpicks-20)
2. Click "Try Prime Student"
3. Verify your enrollment with your `.edu` email
4. Enter payment info (you won't be charged for 6 months)
5. Cancel anytime before the trial ends if you don't want to continue

**Important:** Set a calendar reminder for 5 months out so you can decide before being charged.

## Is the 6-Month Trial Really Free?

Yes — Amazon does not charge you during the 6-month trial period. You need to enter a credit card to sign up, but it won't be billed until after the trial ends.

The trial resets every year you're enrolled — but in practice, Amazon allows one trial per student. You cannot create a new account just to restart the trial.

## Amazon Prime Student FAQ

**Can I share my Prime Student account?**
Yes — you can add one household member (like a sibling or parent at the same address) to share Prime shipping benefits. They won't get student-specific deals.

**Does Prime Student work for community college?**
Yes, if your school has a .edu email or accreditation.

**Can I use Prime Student internationally?**
Prime Student is US-only. International students at US schools qualify if they have a US .edu email.

**What happens after I graduate?**
Amazon will switch you to regular Prime pricing after your graduation date. You can cancel or switch to a monthly plan. You won't lose your account or purchase history.

**Can grad students get Prime Student?**
Yes — Prime Student is available for graduate and professional students with valid .edu emails.
