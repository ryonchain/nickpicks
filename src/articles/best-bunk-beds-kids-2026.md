## Safety Rules for Bunk Beds

1. **No child under 6 on the top bunk** — CPSC recommendation; guardrails are sized for older children's center of gravity.
2. **No jumping on the top bunk** — repeated impact load can fatigue joints.
3. **Check all bolts every 6 months** — wood expands and contracts seasonally, loosening joints.
4. **Top bunk guardrail on both sides** — most beds only include one; add a second rail on the wall side for active sleepers.
5. **Night lights** — kids climbing down at night without light is a fall risk; a plug-in night light at floor level helps.
