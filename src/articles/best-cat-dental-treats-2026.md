5. **Anesthetic professional cleaning** annually — addresses what home care cannot

## The Professional Cleaning Conversation

No amount of home dental care eliminates the need for professional veterinary dental cleanings. Subgingival plaque (below the gumline) cannot be reached by any home care product and requires anesthetic cleaning. For cats with moderate to severe dental disease, professional cleaning is often the most impactful single intervention — and home care afterward dramatically extends the interval before the next cleaning is needed.

The goal of daily home dental care isn't to eliminate professional cleanings — it's to extend them from every 12 months to every 2–3 years.

## Frequency Recommendations

- **Dental treats:** Daily (once or twice — not ad libitum)
- **Water additives:** Every time water is changed (at least daily)
- **Brushing:** Daily ideal; minimum 3x weekly for meaningful benefit
- **Professional cleaning:** Based on vet assessment; typically 1–3 years
