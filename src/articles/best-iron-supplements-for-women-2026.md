## When to See a Doctor

Iron supplementation is appropriate for confirmed or suspected deficiency, but the dose and duration should ideally be guided by a clinician who's reviewed your labs. Signs that warrant a doctor visit:

- Extreme fatigue not resolved by rest
- Pale skin, gums, or inner eyelids
- Shortness of breath on minimal exertion
- Pica (cravings for non-food items like ice, clay, dirt)
- Heart palpitations

A complete blood count (CBC) and ferritin level are the key tests. Ferritin is the most sensitive marker — you can have "normal" hemoglobin but depleted stores, which still causes symptoms.
