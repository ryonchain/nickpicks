# Best Bathroom Vanities in 2026

A bathroom vanity is a major renovation investment — it sets the design tone of the bathroom and affects daily functionality. The key specs: cabinet material (solid wood and plywood outlast MDF, which warps in humidity), countertop material (quartz is the most durable; cultured marble is mid-tier; ceramic is entry-level), sink type (undermount is easier to clean), and soft-close hardware.

---

## Sizing Guide

| Vanity Width | Bathroom Type |
|-------------|---------------|
| 18–24 inches | Small powder room / half bath |
| 30–36 inches | Standard single-sink bathroom |
| 42–48 inches | Large single-sink or compact double |
| 60–72 inches | Double-sink primary bathroom |

**Measuring tip:** Allow at least 15 inches of clear space from the edge of the vanity to any wall or fixture for comfortable use.

## Cabinet Material Ranking

1. **Solid wood** — Best humidity resistance, longest lifespan, most expensive
2. **Plywood** — Good humidity resistance, strong, less expensive than solid wood
3. **MDF** — Most common, lowest cost, susceptible to swelling in prolonged moisture exposure
4. **Particleboard** — Cheapest, avoid for bathrooms with poor ventilation
