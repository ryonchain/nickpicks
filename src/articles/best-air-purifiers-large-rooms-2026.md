## Air Purifier Buying Guide

**What to look for:**

- **True HEPA:** Don't buy "HEPA-type" or "HEPA-style" — these are not tested to the 99.97% at 0.3 micron standard. Look for "True HEPA" or "H13 HEPA."
- **CADR rating:** Clean Air Delivery Rate measures cubic feet of purified air per minute. For a room, you want CADR ≥ (room sq ft × 0.6) for 4-5 air changes per hour.
- **Coverage claims vs. reality:** Manufacturers often cite their largest coverage number at 2 air changes per hour. For meaningful air quality improvement, aim for 4–5 air changes per hour — which means buying a purifier rated for 1.5–2× your actual room size.

**Wildfire smoke:** Fine particulate matter from wildfire smoke (PM2.5) is effectively captured by True HEPA. During smoke events, run the purifier on high continuously and keep windows closed. Replace filters more frequently during active wildfire seasons.
