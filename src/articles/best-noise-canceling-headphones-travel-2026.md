## Travel Headphone Checklist

**Before every flight:**
- Charge to full (connect night before)
- Pack 3.5mm cable for in-seat entertainment
- Pack USB-C cable for mid-trip charging at airports
- Confirm airline adapter is in the case if your airline uses the two-prong system

**In-flight tips:**
- ANC is most effective on consistent sounds (engine drone) — not intermittent sounds
- For sleeping: Aware/Transparency mode off, music or white noise at low volume helps with the "pressure" feeling some users report from ANC alone
- Wired mode preserves battery during very long flights — most airports have USB-A charging at gates
