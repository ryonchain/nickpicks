## What to Look For in a Travel Pillow

**Support type:** Upright sleepers need lateral support (Trtl-style). Reclined sleepers do fine with classic U-shape foam.

**Pack size:** Inflatable pillows are smallest but least comfortable. Memory foam packs larger but sleeps better. The Trtl is the best of both worlds.

**Cover material:** Fleece is soft but warm. Microfiber breathes better for long flights. Always choose a removable, washable cover.

**Chin support:** Standard pillows don't prevent chin drop. If that's your pattern, go BCOZZY or Trtl.
