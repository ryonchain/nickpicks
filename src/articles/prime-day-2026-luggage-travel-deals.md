## What to Skip

**Away luggage** — Away doesn't sell on Amazon and doesn't participate in Prime Day. Their occasional direct sales are the only way to get a discount.

**Tumi** — Tumi rarely discounts meaningfully on Prime Day. A 20% off "deal" on a $600 bag is not a deal worth rushing for.

**Luggage sets with 5+ pieces** — The extra bags (garment bags, underseat bags, etc.) are usually low quality fillers. Spend the same money on a solid 2–3 piece set from Samsonite or Travelpro.

**Vintage/retro styled hardside bags** — These look great but the construction is typically inferior. Spinner wheels are critical; anything with static bottom wheels is not worth buying regardless of price.
