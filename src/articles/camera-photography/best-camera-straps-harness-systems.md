## Buying Guide

### Strap Style vs. Shooting Style

| Shooting Style | Best Strap Type |
|---------------|-----------------|
| Street photography | Wrist strap or slim sling |
| Travel | Convertible sling (Peak Design Slide) |
| Events/weddings | Cross-body sling (BlackRapid) |
| Wildlife/hiking | Harness (Cotton Carrier G3) |
| Studio/casual | Comfort neck strap (Op/Tech) |

### Tripod Socket vs. Strap Lug Attachment

Strap lug attachment (traditional) keeps your tripod socket free for tripod use. Tripod socket attachment (BlackRapid, Peak Design Capture) allows more ergonomic positioning but requires disconnecting before using a tripod. Choose based on how frequently you transition between handheld and tripod use.

### Security Considerations

In crowded environments, neck and cross-body straps provide more theft resistance than wrist straps. Camera bags with hidden attachment points add another layer of security. Always consider your shooting environment when choosing strap security level.
