## Buying Guide

### Tripod Stability vs. Travel Weight

Most photographers need one tripod for all purposes. The practical compromise:
- **Hiking or travel shooting:** Carbon fiber under 3.5 lbs, 60" height
- **Studio and landscape (car access):** Aluminum, 67"+ height, 5+ lbs
- **Travel minimalist:** Joby GorillaPod as supplement to phone/camera

### Center Column vs. No Center Column

Center columns allow extra height but add vibration and instability. For long exposures, lower the center column all the way and use leg height instead. Tripods without center columns (three-leg designs) are more stable but less versatile.

### Head Types for Different Photography

- **Ball head:** Best for general photography — fast, flexible
- **Pan-tilt head:** Best for video panning and studio precision
- **Fluid head:** Best for video — smooth movement, no locks
