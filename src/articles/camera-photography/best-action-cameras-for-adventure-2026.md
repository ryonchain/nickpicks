## Buying Guide: What to Prioritize

### Stabilization First

If your footage looks shaky, no one will watch it. Prioritize stabilization above resolution, battery, or price. GoPro HyperSmooth and DJI RockSteady are in a class above everything else.

### Waterproof Rating vs. Activity

- **Snorkeling/surface water sports:** 10m is sufficient (GoPro, DJI stock)
- **Freediving:** 18m+ required (DJI) or a waterproof housing
- **Scuba:** 40m+ housing required for any camera

### Battery Planning for Adventures

For any day-long activity, pack:
- 3+ charged batteries for GoPro (90-min each)
- 2+ batteries for DJI Osmo Action 4 (160-min each)
- Portable charging bank if no vehicle access
