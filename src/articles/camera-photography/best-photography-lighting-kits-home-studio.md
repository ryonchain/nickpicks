## Buying Guide

### Choosing Light Modifiers

| Modifier | Quality of Light | Best Use |
|----------|-----------------|----------|
| Softbox | Soft, directional | Portraits, beauty |
| Umbrella | Broad, scattered | Fill light, general |
| Beauty dish | Semi-hard, centered | Beauty/fashion |
| Octabox | Soft, wraparound | Portraits, product |

### Color Temperature for Photography

**5500–5600K** (daylight) is the standard for photography — matches window light and simplifies mixed-light situations. **3200K** (tungsten) is traditional video standard but requires correction for photography.
