## Buying Guide

### Choosing Between Inkjet and Dye-Sub

| Consideration | Inkjet | Dye-Sub |
|--------------|--------|---------|
| Paper size flexibility | High (4x6 to 13x19) | Low (4x6 only) |
| Print quality (4x6) | Excellent | Excellent-lab grade |
| Print speed | Moderate | Fast and consistent |
| Cost per print | $0.20–$0.50 | $0.30–$0.40 |
| Document printing | Yes | No |

### Ink System: Cartridge vs. Refillable

Standard cartridges are convenient but expensive per milliliter. EcoTank and similar refillable systems cost more upfront but reduce ongoing costs by 80%+ for photographers who print frequently.
