## Buying Guide

### Matching Bag Style to Shooting Style

| Shooting Style | Best Bag Type |
|---------------|---------------|
| Events/weddings | Backpack (large) |
| Street photography | Shoulder bag or sling |
| Travel/hiking | Backpack |
| Day trips | Sling or shoulder bag |

### Padding: What to Look For

Divider padding should be firm enough to prevent lens-to-lens contact during transport. Look for at least 1 inch of padding in divider walls. Bottom padding should be at least 0.5 inch on any bag with meaningful carry distance.

### Rain Protection

Budget category: bring your own rain cover or use a plastic bag.
Mid-range: look for included rain covers or DWR-coated exteriors.
Premium (Peak Design): integrated weatherproofing without a separate cover.
