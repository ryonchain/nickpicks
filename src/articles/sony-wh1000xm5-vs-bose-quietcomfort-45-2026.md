## Noise Cancellation

**Sony WH-1000XM5:** Eight microphones and two processors power Sony's HD Noise Canceling Processor QN1. It's widely considered the best ANC available in any over-ear headphone. Background hum, airplane engines, HVAC noise, and office chatter are reduced dramatically. In loud environments, it isolates you more effectively than Bose.

**Bose QC45:** The QC45's ANC has been refined over 20+ years of Bose headphone engineering. It handles low-frequency rumble and ambient noise exceptionally well — it just doesn't fully match Sony's XM5 in very loud environments. For most daily use (office, public transit, coffee shops), the difference is noticeable but not dramatic.

**Winner:** Sony WH-1000XM5 — by a clear margin in objective testing.

---

## Sound Quality

**Sony WH-1000XM5:** Sony's 30mm carbon-fiber-composite drivers produce a wide soundstage with prominent bass. The Sony Headphones Connect app lets you apply the DSEE Extreme AI upscaler and customize EQ across 5 bands. LDAC support enables high-resolution wireless audio for compatible devices.

**Bose QC45:** Bose tunes for clarity and balance — vocal and acoustic performance is exceptional. The QC45 doesn't support Hi-Res audio or LDAC, but its tuning is excellent for podcasts, spoken word, and classical music. Bass is present but not overpowering.

**Winner:** Sony for audiophiles and bass heads; Bose for neutral/natural sound preference.

## Comfort

**Sony WH-1000XM5:** The XM5 uses a redesigned headband with soft synthetic leather and memory foam ear cushions. It's comfortable for 3–4 hour sessions. The fixed headband (no folding) means it's less packable.

**Bose QC45:** Bose is synonymous with comfort. The QC45 weighs only 240g (lighter than Sony's 250g), uses plush around-ear cushions, and the headband applies even, gentle clamping force. Wearers with glasses or large heads consistently rate Bose higher for all-day wear.

**Winner:** Bose QC45 — widely regarded as the most comfortable over-ear headphone you can buy.

## Call Quality

**Sony WH-1000XM5:** Four microphones handle calls using Precise Voice Pickup Technology. Performance is good in quiet environments, but callers sometimes report a slightly processed sound in very noisy environments.

**Bose QC45:** Three-mic array with wind noise reduction. Call quality is consistently rated higher — voices come through clearly, and Bose's microphone implementation is better at isolating your voice from background noise.

**Winner:** Bose QC45 for professional calls and phone-heavy users.

## Battery Life

Sony delivers 30 hours on a single charge (40 hours with ANC off). Quick charge provides 3 hours of listening from 3 minutes of charging. Bose gives 24 hours with ANC on and supports quick charge (15 minutes for 3 hours of playback).

**Winner:** Sony WH-1000XM5 by 6 hours.
