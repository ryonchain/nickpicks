## How to Evaluate Premium Sunglasses

### Lens Quality: The Real Premium
Most consumers focus on frames; the lens is where optical quality is determined:

- **UV400:** Blocks 99–100% of UV-A and UV-B radiation. Every reputable sunglass brand meets this standard; avoid any pair that doesn't specify UV400 protection.
- **Polarization:** Eliminates glare from horizontal reflective surfaces (water, roads, snow). Essential for driving, fishing, beach, and skiing. Not necessary for urban daily wear.
- **Lens material:**
  - *Polycarbonate:* Lightweight, impact-resistant, adequate optical clarity
  - *Glass:* Heavier, superior optical clarity, scratch-resistant
  - *CR-39 plastic:* Industry standard, good optical properties, affordable
  - *Proprietary (Maui Jim SuperThin, Oakley Plutonite):* Engineered for specific performance

### Frame Materials
- **Acetate:** Made from cotton and wood pulp, not cheap plastic. Italian acetate (Mazzucchelli) is the luxury standard — richer color depth, better hold, luxurious feel.
- **Titanium:** Lightest metal frame option, hypoallergenic, durable. Used in premium Japanese and European eyewear.
- **Injected nylon/Grilamid:** Lightweight, flexible, highly durable. Used in performance frames (Oakley).

### Hinge Quality
Spring hinges ($15 addition to manufacturing cost) extend the life of a frame significantly. Barrel hinges without springs are more aesthetically clean but will loosen with use. Premium frames use either quality barrel hinges with tight tolerances or well-engineered spring hinges.

## Sunglass Care for Longevity

**Cleaning:** Rinse with water first to remove particles, then clean with lens cloth. Never wipe dry — particles on a dry lens scratch coatings.

**Storage:** Hard case always. Soft pouches allow frames to be crushed and lenses to contact other objects.

**Hinges:** Never force temples wider to put on with one hand. Open and remove with both hands to maintain hinge tension.

**Polarization test:** Look at an LCD phone screen at 45°. Polarized lenses will darken completely — non-polarized won't change.
