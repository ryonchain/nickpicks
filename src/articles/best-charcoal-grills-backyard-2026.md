## What to Look For

**Cooking area:** 300–400 sq in serves 4–6 people. Add 100 sq in per additional 2 people.

**Vent control:** More vents = more airflow control = better temperature management. Look for both top and bottom dampers.

**Grate material:** Cast iron retains heat and creates better sear marks but requires seasoning. Stainless steel is easier to maintain.

**Ash management:** One-Touch or equivalent ash removal systems make cleanup dramatically faster.

**Lid thermometer:** Built-in thermometers are typically inaccurate. Use a separate probe thermometer for precise readings.
