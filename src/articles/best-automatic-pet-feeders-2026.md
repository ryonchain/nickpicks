## Feed Your Pet on Schedule — Even When You Can't Be There

Whether you're working late, traveling, or just tired of the 5 AM breakfast demand, an automatic pet feeder solves the problem. The best models in 2026 do more than drop kibble on a timer — they track meals, send phone notifications, and some even let you watch your pet eat via built-in cameras.

This guide covers the best automatic feeders for cats and dogs across every budget and use case in 2026.

---

## What to Look for in an Automatic Pet Feeder

**Portion accuracy:** Small dogs and cats need precise portions. Look for feeders that dispense in 1/10 to 1/4 cup increments. Larger dogs are more forgiving of minor variation.

**Backup battery:** Critical for homes with occasional power outages and pets with timed medications or sensitive digestion. AA battery backup is the most common format.

**Jam resistance:** Kibble can clump and jam the dispenser. Quality feeders have jam-detection features or twist-dispensing mechanisms that break up clumps before they block the tube.

**App connectivity:** Nice to have for monitoring meals and adjusting schedules remotely. Not necessary for basic scheduled feeding.

**Camera:** Built-in cameras let you watch your pet eat and confirm the feeder dispensed correctly — useful for peace of mind during travel.
