## What to Look For in a Gaming Laptop

### GPU Tier Guide

- **RTX 4060 (laptop):** 1080p High/Ultra. Can handle 1440p Medium–High with DLSS. Best value for under $1,000.
- **RTX 4070 (laptop):** 1440p High/Ultra. 1080p maxed. Worth spending $200 more if 1440p is your target.
- **RTX 4050 (laptop):** 1080p Medium–High. Fine for esports and older titles. Not recommended in 2026 when 4060 is available at similar prices.

### Display Resolution

- **1080p 144Hz:** The sweet spot under $900. The RTX 4060 handles this comfortably.
- **1440p 165Hz:** Noticeable improvement in image quality. Requires DLSS to maintain 60+ FPS in demanding titles.

### RAM and Storage

16GB DDR5 is the minimum for 2026 gaming. 512GB SSD fills up fast — prioritize laptops with an easy second M.2 slot upgrade.
