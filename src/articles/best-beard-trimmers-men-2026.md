## What to Look For in a Beard Trimmer

**Blade material:** Steel is standard. Titanium-coated steel lasts longer. Ceramic runs cooler (less irritation on sensitive skin). Self-sharpening blades maintain edge without maintenance.

**Length increments:** Budget trimmers offer 5–6 settings. Good trimmers offer 12+. Professional/premium trimmers offer 0.5mm precision.

**Battery:** 60 minutes is the minimum for real-world use. 90–120 minutes is comfortable. Fast-charge is useful if you forget to charge overnight.

**Wet/dry:** Important if you trim in the shower or want to rinse under the tap easily. Look for IPX5 or higher waterproof rating.
