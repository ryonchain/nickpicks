## Buying Guide

### Desk Clamp vs. Grommet Mount

**Desk clamp** attaches to the edge of the desk — no holes required. Works on desks up to ~3.5 inches thick. Easy to reposition or remove.

**Grommet mount** passes through a pre-drilled or existing hole in the desk surface. More permanent, but more stable and aesthetically cleaner. Required if your desk has no edge to clamp to.

Most arms include both mounting options.

### How to Check Compatibility

Before buying, confirm:
1. **VESA pattern** — back of your monitor. Most use 75×75mm or 100×100mm.
2. **Monitor weight** — check your monitor's spec sheet, not the screen size alone. 27" monitors range from 8 lbs to 18 lbs.
3. **Desk thickness** — desk clamps typically work up to 3.5". Verify for standing desks or thick wooden surfaces.

### Single vs. Dual Arms

A single-arm post can support two arms (like the Ergotron LX Dual). Dedicated dual-arm products are usually more cost-effective and stable than bolting two single arms together.

### Gas Spring vs. Spring-Tension Mechanism

Gas spring arms (North Bayou, some Ergotrons) adjust smoothly with a push — ideal for users who reposition frequently. Spring-tension arms (standard Ergotron LX) require an Allen key to adjust resistance — set once, rarely touch. Both hold position reliably when correctly adjusted.

## FAQ

**What monitor arm works with an IKEA desk?**
Most desk-clamp arms work with standard IKEA desks (Linnmon, Bekant, Karlby). Verify desk edge thickness is under 3.5" for clamp mounting. IKEA Idåsen desks have edge thickness that accepts most standard clamps.

**Can monitor arms support ultrawide monitors?**
Yes, but check weight capacity. Most 34" ultrawide monitors weigh 15–22 lbs. The North Bayou gas spring arm (up to 33 lbs) and Ergotron LX Dual handle these comfortably. Standard arms are often undersized.

**Do monitor arms reduce neck strain?**
Yes. The primary benefit is placing the screen at exact eye height — most monitor stands don't go high enough. Proper screen height (top of screen at or just below eye level) significantly reduces neck and shoulder tension over long sessions.

**What's the difference between the Amazon Basics and Ergotron LX?**
Functionally minimal — Amazon Basics is manufactured by Ergotron and uses the same mechanism. The LX has better cable management, a more premium finish, and a 10-year warranty vs. 1 year. Both hold position well.

**Can I attach a monitor arm to a glass desk?**
Desk clamps need a solid edge to grip — glass desks typically have no usable edge. A grommet mount through a hole in the glass works but requires careful installation. Some glass desks are not strong enough to support the torque of a fully-extended arm. Verify before purchasing.
