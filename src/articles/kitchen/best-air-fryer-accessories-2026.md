## Universal Fit vs. Model-Specific

Most air fryer accessories are listed by basket size (typically in quarts). The most common sizes:
- **5.3 qt** (Philips, older Ninja): round basket, ~8" diameter
- **5.8 qt** (Cosori, newer Ninja): round basket, ~8.5" diameter
- **6 qt** (Instant Vortex, some Ninja): square or rectangular basket

Measure your basket's inner diameter before ordering any round accessory.
