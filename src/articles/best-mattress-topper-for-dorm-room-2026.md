## Important Notes for Dorm Mattress Toppers

### Size: Always Twin XL
39" × 80". Not Twin (39" × 75"). Standard Twin toppers will leave a 5-inch gap at the foot. This is the most common ordering mistake — check the product dimensions before purchasing.

### Thickness + Sheet Compatibility
A 2" topper added to a 6" dorm mattress = 8" total. Standard sheets have 9" pockets — they'll fit, but barely. A 3" topper brings you to 9" — at the limit of standard sheets. Use **deep pocket sheets (14–16")** when using a 3" topper to prevent fitted sheet pop-off.

### Off-Gassing
Memory foam toppers typically have a temporary chemical smell when first unboxed. Unbox 24–48 hours before sleeping on it. The smell dissipates completely.

### Transport
Toppers are compressed and rolled for shipping. They expand to full size over 24–72 hours. Don't expect to use it immediately upon unboxing.
