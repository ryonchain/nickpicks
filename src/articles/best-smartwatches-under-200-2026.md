## What to Look For

**Ecosystem:** Apple Watch only works with iPhone; Wear OS works best with Android. Garmin and Fitbit work with both.

**Battery life:** Apple Watch needs daily charging. Garmin and Amazfit last 1–2 weeks. Choose based on your charging tolerance.

**GPS:** Built-in GPS is essential for runners. "Connected GPS" (uses phone's GPS) is fine for casual use.

**Health sensors:** ECG is genuinely useful for detecting atrial fibrillation. Blood oxygen (SpO2) and temperature sensors are less clinically validated but useful for tracking trends.
