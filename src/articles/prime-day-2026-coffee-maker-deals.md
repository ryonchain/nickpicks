## What to Skip

**Keurig K-Classic / K-Compact** — These machines go on sale frequently throughout the year. The Prime Day "deal" is often identical to what they run in October and February. Don't rush.

**Single-serve pod machines under $40** — Quality control is poor and replacement pod costs add up. Spend more on a Nespresso or get a proper drip maker.

**Jura super-automatics** — Beautiful machines, but Jura's Prime Day discounts are usually $100–$150 off $1,500+ machines. The relative value isn't there compared to De'Longhi at the same discount depth.
