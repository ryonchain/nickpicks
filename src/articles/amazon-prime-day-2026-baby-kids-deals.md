## Baby and Kids Prime Day Tips

1. **Don't buy car seats, strollers, or cribs second-hand.** Safety recall risk makes buying used risky. Prime Day new deals are the best time to buy these at a fair price.
2. **Age-up one level on educational toys.** Kids grow fast — buying toys that are slightly more advanced than current age is often the right call.
3. **Subscribe & Save on consumables.** Diapers, wipes, formula, and baby food are already cheaper on Subscribe & Save than Prime Day prices in many cases. Stack Subscribe & Save with Prime Day to save the most.
4. **Check the age/weight minimums on car seats before buying.** A convertible seat is only good for infants over a certain weight threshold — verify the minimum before purchasing.
