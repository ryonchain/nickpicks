## Tips for Gift Baskets on Amazon

1. **Check Prime availability** — Many gift baskets ship with Prime 1–2 day delivery, but some third-party sellers take 3–7 days. Filter by Prime if timing matters.
2. **Check included items carefully** — Some "baskets" in listings are mostly filler/packaging. Read bullet points for actual item counts.
3. **Order by December 18** — For guaranteed Christmas delivery via standard shipping. Prime members have more flexibility.
4. **Gift message is free** — Add a personal note at checkout; it prints on a card included with the shipment.
5. **Gift wrap** — Most food gift baskets come wrapped; check the listing if presentation matters.
