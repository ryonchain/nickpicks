## Activewear Sets Buying Guide

### Choosing the Right Support Level

| Activity | Support Needed | Best Pick |
|----------|---------------|-----------|
| Yoga / Pilates | Low-Medium | Colorfulkoala, IUGA |
| HIIT / Gym | Medium-High | CRZ YOGA, Alo Yoga |
| Running | High | Dedicated running bra + separate leggings |
| Casual/Lifestyle | Low | Sunzel, Amazon Essentials |

### Fabric Guide

- **Nylon-Spandex (82/18 or 80/20):** Most popular, smooth, squat-proof, good recovery
- **Polyester-Spandex:** More affordable, dries fast, slight texture
- "Buttery soft" / "naked feel": Marketing terms for brushed nylon with a smooth interior finish

### Avoiding Common Fit Problems

**Waistband rolling:** Look for sets with 6"+ waistbands labeled "high-rise" or "high-waist" (IUGA, CRZ YOGA, Alo)
**Sheerness:** Check verified buyer reviews and look for "squat-proof" in product descriptions; test in natural light when possible
**Size consistency:** Amazon Essentials and CRZ YOGA have reliable size charts; third-party brands vary

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Are Amazon activewear sets as good as Lululemon?**
CRZ YOGA Butterluxe is genuinely comparable for yoga and pilates. For high-impact cardio, Lululemon's construction is more durable long-term. For casual and studio use, the quality gap has narrowed significantly.

**How do I wash activewear sets to maintain quality?**
Cold machine wash, gentle cycle, no fabric softener (softener degrades spandex elasticity), hang to dry. Avoid dryer heat.

**What size should I order for leggings vs. sports bra?**
Size guides for leggings should be followed by waist and hip measurement. Sports bras often size more loosely — many buyers size down one from their legging size for better support.

**Are these sets suitable for pregnancy?**
High waist stretchy sets (Colorfulkoala, IUGA) can be worn in early pregnancy but aren't designed for maternity use. For dedicated maternity activewear, look for sets with fold-over waistbands designed for belly growth.
