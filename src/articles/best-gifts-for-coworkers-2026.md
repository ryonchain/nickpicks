## What to Avoid for Coworkers

**Skip these:**
- Anything too personal (perfume, clothing, fashion accessories)
- Anything that implies personal observations ("I noticed you're always stressed...")
- Alcohol unless you know the person and their preferences
- Food items with common allergens (nuts, gluten) without labeling
- Gag gifts unless you have a genuinely jokey relationship

**Best rule:** If you'd hesitate giving it to your boss, don't give it to a coworker you don't know well.
