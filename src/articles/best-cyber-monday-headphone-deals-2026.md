## Cyber Monday Headphone Buying Tips

### ANC vs. Transparency Mode — Which Matters More?

- **Commuters and office workers:** Prioritize ANC depth (Sony XM6, Bose QC Ultra)
- **Runners and outdoor users:** Prioritize Transparency Mode (AirPods Pro 2 is the best here)
- **Work-from-home:** Balance both — any flagship ANC headphone works well

### Earbuds vs. Over-Ear for Cyber Monday

| Feature | Earbuds | Over-Ear |
|---------|---------|----------|
| Portability | Better | Bulkier |
| ANC effectiveness | Good | Excellent |
| Battery life | 6–8 hours per charge | 20–40 hours |
| Sound quality ceiling | Good | Better |
| Price at Cyber Monday | $150–$250 | $220–$300 |

### Check the Return Policy

Most retailers (Amazon, Best Buy) extend return windows to January for Cyber Monday purchases — important for gifts.
