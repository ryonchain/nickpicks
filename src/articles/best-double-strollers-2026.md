## Side-by-Side vs. Tandem: How to Choose

**Side-by-side:**
- Both kids see forward equally
- Easier to check on each child
- Width may not fit narrow doorways or elevators (measure first)
- Better for twins of the same age

**Tandem (in-line):**
- Fits through standard doorways
- Rear child has limited view
- Easier to push through crowds
- Better for infant + toddler combinations
