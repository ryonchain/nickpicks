## What to Consider Before Buying

**Closed-back vs open-back:** Closed-back = noise isolation, good for commuting and office use. Open-back = better soundstage and imaging, home listening only.

**Wired vs wireless:** Wired maintains signal quality and never needs charging. Wireless is convenient and modern wireless codecs (LDAC, aptX Adaptive) are excellent.

**ANC strength:** Sony and Bose lead. Apple AirPods Max is competitive but costs $550.

**Multipoint connection:** Connects to two devices at once — essential if you switch between phone and laptop frequently.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Are over-ear headphones better than earbuds?**
For audio quality and ANC, yes — larger drivers and better seal win. For portability and gym use, earbuds win.

**Does expensive mean better sound?**
Not always. The Anker Q45 sounds better than many $150 headphones. Above $200, you're paying for better ANC, premium build quality, and comfort — all of which matter for daily use.

**What codec do I need?**
If you use Android with a Sony or high-end device: LDAC gives the best wireless quality. iPhone: AAC is standard. Most people won't hear the difference in casual listening.
