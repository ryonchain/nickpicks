## How to Choose the Right Air Purifier

### Room Size is Everything

Match the purifier's rated coverage to your room size. A purifier rated for 200 sq ft won't effectively clean a 400 sq ft room. For best results, pick a unit rated 20–30% higher than your actual room size to get 2+ air changes per hour (ACH).

### CADR: The Number That Matters

Clean Air Delivery Rate (CADR) measures cubic feet of air cleaned per minute. Higher is better. For a 200 sq ft room, you want a CADR of at least 130. Most quality purifiers publish CADR ratings from AHAM testing.

### Filter Types

| Filter | What It Removes |
|--------|----------------|
| True HEPA | Particles ≥0.3 microns: dust, pollen, pet dander, mold spores |
| Activated Carbon | Odors, VOCs, smoke, chemicals |
| Pre-filter | Large particles (hair, lint) — extends HEPA life |
| UV-C | Bacteria and viruses (effectiveness varies) |

### Smart Features: Worth It?

Smart purifiers with air quality sensors let the unit automatically ramp up when air quality degrades — even while you sleep. If you have allergies, pets, or live near traffic/wildfire smoke, auto mode is a genuine quality-of-life upgrade.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**How often should I replace HEPA filters?**
Most HEPA filters last 6–12 months depending on usage and air quality. Pre-filters are washable and should be cleaned monthly. Run your purifier on auto mode and watch the filter indicator light.

**Do air purifiers help with COVID and viruses?**
True HEPA filters capture virus-carrying particles (aerosols). Higher-end models with UV-C can deactivate pathogens. Air purifiers are one layer of defense but shouldn't replace ventilation and other precautions.

**Should I run my air purifier 24/7?**
Yes — air purifiers work best when running continuously. Modern units on auto mode are energy-efficient (as low as 5–10W on low settings) and cost $2–5/month in electricity.

**Can an air purifier eliminate pet odors?**
True HEPA + activated carbon combination units significantly reduce pet odors and dander. For heavy odor loads (multiple pets, small spaces), choose units with thicker carbon filters like the Winix 5500-2 or Coway Mighty.

**Where should I place my air purifier?**
Place it in the room where you spend the most time, ideally near the center or main source of pollutants (litter box, cooking area). Keep it away from walls and furniture to allow 360° airflow.
