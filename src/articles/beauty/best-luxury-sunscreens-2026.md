## How to Apply Sunscreen for Maximum Effectiveness

1. **Apply as the last step in your AM skincare routine** — after moisturizer, before makeup
2. **Use 1/4 teaspoon for the face** — most people underapply and get less than half the labeled SPF
3. **Apply 15 minutes before sun exposure** — chemical sunscreens need time to activate
4. **Reapply every 2 hours outdoors** — no SPF is sweat-proof or waterproof indefinitely
5. **Don't skip the neck and hands** — these show age faster than the face from cumulative sun exposure

The best sunscreen is the one you'll actually apply every morning. If texture, scent, or finish is what's stopping you, spend more on a formula you enjoy wearing.
