## The Case for Powered Cleansing

Manual face washing typically removes surface debris but struggles with the pores and the oil trapped in skin texture. Electric cleansing devices — whether sonic vibration or oscillating bristles — break that oil-debris bond more effectively through physical agitation, increasing cleansing efficacy and preparing skin to better absorb the serums and treatments that follow.

The secondary benefit is consistency: it's harder to rush a 60-second cleansing cycle when the device is timing it for you.
