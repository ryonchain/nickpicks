## What At-Home IPL Can (Realistically) Achieve

IPL hair removal is permanent hair *reduction*, not guaranteed permanent hair *removal*. The distinction matters:

- **Realistic expectation:** 70–90% reduction in hair growth after 3 months of consistent use
- **Maintenance sessions:** Required every 1–2 months for most people indefinitely
- **Results variability:** Works fastest on coarse, dark hair on light skin; slower on fine or light hair; unsafe for very dark skin

Compared to professional laser treatments (~$200–400 per session), at-home IPL devices pay for themselves within a few months and allow treatment on your own schedule. The trade-off is that professional equipment is more powerful and often faster to results.

## IPL Treatment Tips for Best Results

1. **Shave 24 hours before treatment.** Don't wax or pluck — the follicle must be intact. IPL targets the root via the hair shaft; removing the shaft removes the light path.
2. **Avoid sun exposure for 48 hours before and after.** Tanned skin absorbs more light energy and increases burn risk dramatically.
3. **Start at the lowest intensity setting.** Work your way up over 2–3 sessions as you learn your skin's response.
4. **Be consistent with the 2-week interval.** Missing sessions between active hair growth cycles significantly slows progress.
5. **Apply SPF 30+ to treated areas in the days after treatment.** Treated skin is temporarily photosensitive.
