## What Makes a Good Eye Cream

The eye area demands formulation care that most regular moisturizers skip:

- **No fragrance or essential oils** — these are the #1 cause of allergic contact dermatitis around the eyes
- **Target-specific actives** — caffeine for puffiness, vitamin K/C for dark circles, peptides for firmness
- **Appropriate texture** — heavy formulas can cause milia (small white bumps) under the eyes; lightweight is usually better
- **Stable packaging** — eye creams should be in tubes or airless pumps, not open jars exposed to fingers

A luxury eye cream earns its price by delivering meaningful concentrations of actives, exceptional texture, and fragrance-free formulation at higher quality than mass-market alternatives.
