## Why Vitamin C Is Worth Paying For

Vitamin C (L-ascorbic acid) is one of the few skincare ingredients with robust clinical evidence behind it. In a morning routine it does three things no other ingredient does:

1. **Neutralizes free radicals** from UV exposure — protecting against photoaging before it starts
2. **Inhibits melanin production** — preventing and fading discoloration and dark spots
3. **Stimulates collagen synthesis** — supporting skin structure and reducing fine lines

The challenge: L-ascorbic acid is notoriously unstable. It oxidizes on contact with air and light, rendering cheap or poorly packaged serums ineffective within weeks of opening. Luxury serums justify their price partly through the research investment required to stabilize the formula.
