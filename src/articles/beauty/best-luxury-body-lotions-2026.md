## Why Body Care Deserves More Attention

Body skin ages similarly to facial skin — it loses collagen, elasticity, and moisture-retention capacity over time. Sun-exposed areas (hands, décolletage, arms) show UV damage nearly as quickly as the face. Yet most people invest significant effort in facial skincare while applying whatever body lotion is on sale to the rest.

Premium body care closes that gap: better hydration, improved texture, and the sensorial experience that makes skincare a ritual rather than a chore.

## Body Care Application Guide

1. **Apply within 3 minutes of bathing** on damp (not soaking) skin
2. **Focus on high-dryness zones first**: shins, elbows, heels, knees
3. **Don't neglect the décolletage** — UV damage accumulates there as fast as the face
4. **Apply with upward strokes** for better circulation and potential lymphatic benefit
5. **Use SPF on exposed body areas** during summer — body skin ages from UV just like facial skin

The best luxury body lotion is the one you'll use consistently. A $35 product applied daily beats a $200 cream applied twice a month.
