## How to Choose a Yoga Mat for Home Practice

**Thickness by practice type:**
- **Restorative / yin yoga:** 6–10mm — you're spending time on the floor, cushion matters
- **Vinyasa / flow yoga:** 4–6mm — balance of comfort and ground connection
- **Power yoga / advanced:** 3–5mm — stability in balancing and arm balance poses

**Material guide:**
- **PVC:** Most durable, easy to clean, best grip when dry. Not eco-friendly.
- **Natural rubber:** Excellent grip (wet and dry), biodegradable, heavier. Avoid if latex allergic.
- **TPE:** Eco-friendlier than PVC, lighter, decent grip. Less durable than rubber.
- **NBR foam:** Very cushioned, lightweight, affordable. Less grip at higher thickness.

**Sizing:**
Standard mats are 68" × 24". If you're taller than 5'10", look for 71–85" long versions. Wider mats (26") give more room for wide-leg poses and provide a more comfortable experience overall.
