## Building Your First Smart Home: A Sensible Order

**Step 1: Voice Hub.** Pick your ecosystem (Amazon, Google, or Apple HomeKit) and buy the matching smart speaker. Everything else should be compatible with it.

**Step 2: Smart Plugs.** Add 4–6 Kasa smart plugs to lamps and small appliances. This gives you immediate automations without touching wiring.

**Step 3: Lighting.** Add smart bulbs (Hue) or in-wall switches (Lutron) to rooms where you control lights most frequently — bedroom and living room first.

**Step 4: Automations.** Create schedules and routines via the hub's app. "Turn off all lights at midnight" and "dim bedroom lights at 10pm" are simple wins that save daily friction.
