## Dyson vs. Shark vs. Eufy — Which Brand Is Right for You?

**Choose Dyson if:** You want the most advanced technology (laser detection, piezo sensors, precise HEPA filtration) and have a larger budget. Dyson leads on suction science and build quality, but you pay for it.

**Choose Shark if:** You have pets or thick carpets and want the best hair-handling performance. The Stratos line's DuoClean PowerFins and tangle-free brush rolls are genuinely superior for pet owners. The swappable battery on the IZ862H adds long-term value.

**Choose Eufy if:** Budget is the priority, or you need a lightweight, easy-to-use vacuum for a smaller space. Eufy punches above its price range on hard floors and delivers honest value without false promises about carpet performance.
