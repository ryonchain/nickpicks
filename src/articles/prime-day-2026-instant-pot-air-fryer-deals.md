## Instant Pot vs. Air Fryer: Which Should You Buy?

**If you cook protein, grains, and one-pot meals:** Buy an Instant Pot. It's unrivaled for making tender proteins, legumes, and broth fast. It doesn't do what an air fryer does (no browning or crisping).

**If you reheat leftovers, cook frozen food, and want crispy textures:** Buy an air fryer. It's the best reheating appliance ever made — it restores pizza, fries, and roast chicken to near-original quality. It doesn't do what an Instant Pot does (no pressure cooking).

**If you want both:** Buy the Instant Pot Duo Crisp + Air Fryer, or pair a basic Instant Pot Duo with a compact air fryer. Both fit on a medium-sized counter.
