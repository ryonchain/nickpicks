## Best Buy vs Amazon Prime Day — When Each Wins

**Best Buy wins on:**

## Best Buy's Price Match Guarantee — Your Best Weapon

This is the most underused feature during Prime Day week:

**How it works:**
1. Find a lower price at Amazon, Walmart, Costco, or Target
2. Show the price to a Best Buy associate (or use chat online)
3. Best Buy matches it — including Amazon Prime Day prices

This means you can buy something at Best Buy for in-store same-day pickup, then price-match it to Amazon's Prime Day deal, then return to Amazon's deal if Best Buy can't match.

**What Best Buy matches:** Amazon.com prices (including Prime Day deals), Walmart, Target, Costco, Crutchfield, and other major retailers.

**What they don't match:** Amazon Warehouse (used/open-box), third-party Amazon sellers, marketplace prices.
