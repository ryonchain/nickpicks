## Youth Football Safety: What Parents Need to Know

Before buying any gear, understand these safety basics:

- **Helmet ratings matter**: Look for NOCSAE certification (required) and Virginia Tech STAR rating (4–5 stars recommended)
- **Sizing is critical**: A helmet that fits poorly is worse than no helmet
- **Recertification**: Helmets must be recertified every 2 years by a NOCSAE-certified reconditioner
- **Return policies**: Buy from retailers that allow fit-testing returns

## Youth Football Sizing Guide

### Helmet Sizing

Measure the circumference of the head just above the eyebrows.

| Head Circumference | Youth Size |
|-------------------|-----------|
| Under 20.5" | XS (Peewee) |
| 20.5"–21.5" | S |
| 21.5"–22.5" | M |
| 22.5"–23.5" | L |

### Shoulder Pad Sizing

Measure across the shoulders at the widest point.

| Shoulder Width | Pad Size |
|--------------|---------|
| Under 12" | XS |
| 12"–13" | S |
| 13"–14" | M |
| 14"–15" | L |
