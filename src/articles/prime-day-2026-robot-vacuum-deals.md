## Robot Vacuum Buying Guide: Quick Specs That Matter

| Feature | Budget Picks | Mid-Range | Premium |
|--------|-------------|-----------|---------|
| Suction | 1,500–2,000Pa | 3,000–5,500Pa | 8,000–10,000Pa |
| Navigation | Gyroscope | LiDAR | LiDAR + Camera AI |
| Obstacle Avoidance | None | Basic | 3D Structured Light |
| Self-Empty Dock | Rare | Common | Standard |
| Mop + Vacuum | Rare | Yes (basic) | Yes (auto-lift) |
| Price Range | $100–$200 | $250–$500 | $600–$1,600 |

**Suction:** 2,500Pa handles bare floors and low-pile carpet. 5,000Pa+ is needed for thick carpet and serious pet hair.

**Navigation:** Gyroscope models clean randomly. LiDAR models create an accurate map and clean systematically — a meaningful difference in floor coverage and efficiency.

**Self-empty dock:** Adds $100–$150 to the price but means you empty the dock every 30–90 days instead of the robot bin after every run. Worth it for most households.
