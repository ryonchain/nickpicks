## What to Look For

- **Size for your lifestyle:** Small crossbodies (under 7" wide) work for evenings and light days; medium (7"–10") handles everyday carry; large (10"+) doubles as a work or travel bag.
- **Strap adjustability and comfort:** Look for straps that adjust at least 5–6 inches and are at least 1" wide. Padded straps are a significant comfort upgrade for heavier bags.
- **Closure type:** Zip closures offer the best security, especially for travel; magnetic snap closures are quicker to access but less secure in crowded places.
- **Interior organization:** Card slots, key hooks, and zip pockets keep small items from disappearing into the main compartment — a huge quality-of-life factor for daily use.
- **Material durability:** Full-grain leather ages best; Saffiano and pebbled leather resist scratches well; coated canvas is the most water-resistant and easiest to clean.
- **RFID blocking:** If you carry contactless bank cards or a passport, look for a bag with RFID-blocking lining or pockets for added security in crowded environments.
