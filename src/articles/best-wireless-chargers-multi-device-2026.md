## Wireless Charging Guide

**MagSafe vs. Qi:** MagSafe (iPhone 12+) delivers 15W through Apple-certified magnets that align perfectly. Qi is the universal standard (all Qi-compatible phones) at 5–15W depending on the charger. MagSafe chargers can also charge Qi phones, just at Qi speeds.

**Multi-device charging tip:** Most multi-device chargers draw from a single USB-C cable. Make sure your wall adapter provides enough watts to cover all devices simultaneously (typically 30W+).

**Why certifications matter:** Uncertified MagSafe chargers may not deliver full 15W and can potentially damage battery management over time. Anker, Belkin, and ESR are generally safe; random no-brand chargers are riskier.
