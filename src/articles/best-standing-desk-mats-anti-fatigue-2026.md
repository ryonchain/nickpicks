## Flat vs. Terrain Mats: Which Is Right for You?

**Flat mats are better if:**
- You use your mat with bare feet and want predictable surface feel
- You spend less than 2 hours standing per day
- You have balance concerns or mobility issues

**Terrain mats are better if:**
- You stand 3+ hours per day
- You have chronic lower back or leg fatigue from standing
- You're willing to invest 1–2 weeks of adjustment for long-term gain

**Thickness:** 3/4" is the minimum for meaningful anti-fatigue effect. 1" is ideal for all-day use.
