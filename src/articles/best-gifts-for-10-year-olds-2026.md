## Buying Guide: 10-Year-Old Gift Considerations

**Social proof matters at 10:** Ask what their friends play, watch, or wear. A gift that connects to their social world (game others play, brand others use) will be received far better than something excellent but unfamiliar.

**Gaming notes:**
- Roblox and Minecraft are still dominant at 10
- Nintendo Switch Lite games (Pokémon, Mario) are appropriate for most
- Avoid M-rated games or online FPS unless parents have approved

**Phone readiness:** Many 10-year-olds have or are about to get their first phone. Phone accessories (cases, ring lights, chargers) can be great gifts if you know they have one.

**Reading:** Chapter books transition to middle-grade at this age. Percy Jackson, Harry Potter, Wimpy Kid, Big Nate, Wings of Fire, Boxcar Children.

**Avoid:** Baby-skewing toys (ages 5-8 packaging), board games they've outgrown, or tech that requires a monthly subscription without parent approval.
