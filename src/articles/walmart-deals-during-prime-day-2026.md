Every Amazon Prime Day, Walmart runs competing sales to capture deal-hungry shoppers who don't have Prime memberships — or who want to price-check Amazon against the competition. **Walmart's counter-event for 2026 is active July 16–17**, overlapping directly with Amazon Prime Day.

## Is Walmart's Sale Better Than Prime Day?

**Short answer: depends on the category.**

- **Amazon wins on:** Echo/Alexa devices, Kindle, Ring cameras, premium headphones (Sony, Bose), laptop deals from major brands, robot vacuums
- **Walmart wins on:** groceries and household essentials, store-brand electronics, same-day pickup deals, Onn TVs (Walmart's house brand at surprisingly good prices), and anything you need in-store today

For pure electronics and Amazon-brand deals, Prime Day is usually better. For everyday goods and big-box staples, Walmart often matches or beats Amazon's sale pricing.
