## Fall Activewear Buying Guide

**For cold-weather outdoor running (below 50°F):**
- Fleece-lined leggings + moisture-wicking base layer + packable jacket
- Warm socks, light gloves, and ear warmer complete the kit
- Avoid 100% cotton next to skin — it absorbs sweat and chills you

**For the gym in fall:**
- Standard leggings work if the gym is temperature-controlled
- A zip-up midlayer for warm-up and cool-down
- Any moisture-wicking sports bra with your typical support level

**For outdoor yoga or Pilates:**
- Lululemon Align leggings or thermal-weight alternatives
- A looser long-sleeve layer that doesn't restrict movement

**What "fleece-lined" actually means:**
Fleece-lined activewear has a brushed interior (usually polyester microfiber). It adds 5–8°F of warmth compared to standard athletic fabric. Best for temps 35–55°F. Not windproof — add a shell layer on windy days.
