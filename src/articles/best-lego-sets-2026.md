## LEGO Buying Tips

**What's worth the price:**
- Any Classic box (value per piece is excellent)
- Technic sets for 8+ (real mechanical learning)
- Icons/Botanical (beautiful display pieces that hold value)

**What to skip:**
- Licensed sets with fewer than 200 pieces (overpriced for the build time)
- Sets at full MSRP that go on sale — check Amazon for Lightning Deals during Prime Day and Black Friday
- Bootleg "Lepin" LEGO alternatives — incompatible pieces damage real LEGO sets

**When to buy:**
- Prime Day (July): 20–30% off major sets
- Black Friday: 30–40% off, best annual sale
- Amazon Lightning Deals: random discounts throughout the year
