## Why Video Doorbells Are Worth It

Package theft costs Americans $19 billion annually. A visible doorbell camera:
- Deters 60% of package thieves (University of North Carolina study)
- Provides evidence for police if theft occurs
- Lets you answer the door remotely while traveling
- Monitors visitor patterns even when you're home

## What to Look For

**Resolution:** 1080p minimum; 1536p+ if you want readable package labels.

**Field of view:** Wide (160°+) captures both face and packages. "Head-to-toe" or dual-camera models eliminate blind spots.

**Motion detection:** AI person/package detection dramatically reduces false alerts. Basic motion sensors trigger on every passing car.

**Subscription cost:** Ring Protect: $4.99/month. Google Home: $8/month. Eufy: $0. Factor this into total cost of ownership.

**Wired vs. battery:** Wired = no charging; battery = flexible placement.
