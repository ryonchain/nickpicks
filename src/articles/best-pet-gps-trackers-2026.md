## GPS vs. Bluetooth Trackers

### GPS Trackers
- Track anywhere with cellular coverage
- Update location every 2–10 seconds (real-time models) or every few minutes
- Require monthly subscription ($5–$15/month)
- Battery: 1–7 days per charge
- **Best for:** Dogs that escape, outdoor cats, rural properties

### Bluetooth Trackers
- Short range only (Bluetooth range: ~400 feet)
- No subscription required
- 1-year coin battery
- Use crowdsourced "find" networks (Tile network, Apple Find My)
- **Best for:** Finding a lost item in your home; not reliable for escaped pets

**Recommendation:** For pets that could actually escape your property, invest in a GPS tracker with a subscription. Bluetooth trackers are genuinely inadequate for pet safety.
