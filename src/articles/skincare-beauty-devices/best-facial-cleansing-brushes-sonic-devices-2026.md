## Why Use a Powered Cleansing Device?

Manual face washing removes surface debris but often misses oil trapped in pores and skin texture. Sonic and oscillating devices break that oil-debris bond through consistent physical agitation — making your cleanser work harder and prepping skin to absorb serums more effectively. The secondary benefit is routine: a 60-second mode keeps you cleansing long enough to actually matter.
