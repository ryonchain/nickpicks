## Rolling vs. Gua Sha: Which Do You Need?

Face rollers are the gentler option — they stimulate lymphatic drainage, help products absorb deeper, and provide a calming massage effect. Use them morning and evening on a clean, serum-prepped face.

Gua sha is more therapeutic. The flat-edge scraping technique (done with intention — not aggressively) works on fascia, muscle, and circulation more directly than rolling. Many skincare enthusiasts use both: roller for daily morning de-puffing, gua sha for deeper evening facial massage.
