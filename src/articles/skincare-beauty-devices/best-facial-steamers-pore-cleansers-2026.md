## Why Facial Steaming Works

Steam softens the bonds that hold debris in your pores, making manual extraction (comedone extractor, gua sha, pore strips) or chemical exfoliation far more effective. The warmth increases local circulation, temporarily brightening skin tone. Hydration from nano ionic steam goes beyond what topical moisturizers can deliver to the deeper skin layers. Used as a pre-treatment before masks, serums, or extraction, steaming multiplies the effectiveness of the products that follow.

The key variable is steam particle size — nano ionic steamers deliver smaller water molecules that penetrate more deeply than traditional hot steam.
