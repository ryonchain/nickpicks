## What Makes Ultrasonic Scrubbers Effective

Unlike traditional exfoliation, ultrasonic spatulas use cavitation — the rapid formation and collapse of micro-bubbles in liquid — to penetrate pores and dislodge debris mechanically. The process is gentler on the skin surface than physical scrubs while being more effective at clearing what's inside pores. When you see debris accumulating on the spatula blade, that's content that was previously trapped in your skin.

The dual functionality (extraction + infusion) is what makes these tools genuinely useful: you clean the pores out, then push your actives in.
