## What Dermaplaning Does

Dermaplaning uses a sharp blade (manual scalpel or motorized device) to shave the surface of the face, removing dead skin cells and vellus hair (peach fuzz) in one pass. The result: instantly smoother skin with improved texture, better makeup application, and enhanced product absorption. Unlike most exfoliation methods, results are visible after a single session.

Professional dermaplaning (with a surgical scalpel) is more aggressive and better for deep scarring work. At-home tools are safer but deliver meaningful results for texture refinement and peach fuzz removal.
