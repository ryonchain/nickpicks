## The Science Behind LED Therapy

LED (Light Emitting Diode) therapy works through photobiomodulation — specific wavelengths of light absorbed by skin cells trigger biological responses including collagen synthesis, cellular repair, and bacterial kill. The key variables are wavelength precision, power output (irradiance), and treatment duration. Cheap LED masks often fail on all three — wavelengths are approximate, power output is low, and dosing is inconsistent.

The masks below all have at least some clinical evidence behind their wavelength choices.
