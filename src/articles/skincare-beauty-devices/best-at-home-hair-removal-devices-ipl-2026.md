## How IPL Works

IPL (Intense Pulsed Light) emits a broad-spectrum light that targets melanin in the hair shaft. The light converts to heat, which damages the follicle and inhibits future growth. Because the technology targets melanin contrast between hair and skin, it performs best on light-to-medium skin tones with dark hair. It works less effectively on blonde, red, gray, or white hair — which lack sufficient melanin.

At-home devices are lower intensity than professional laser, making them safe for home use but requiring more sessions to achieve comparable results.
