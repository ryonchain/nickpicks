## What Home Microdermabrasion Actually Does

Professional microdermabrasion uses abrasive particles or a diamond-tipped wand under vacuum suction to remove the outer layer of dead skin cells, unclog pores, and stimulate collagen production. Home devices work the same way at reduced intensity — enough for meaningful improvement without the recovery time (or price tag) of a professional treatment. Expect smoother texture, refined pore appearance, and improved product absorption with consistent use.
