## Microcurrent: The Facial Fitness Method

Microcurrent therapy delivers low-level electrical current (measured in microamperes — millionths of an ampere) that mimics the body's own bioelectric signals. This stimulates the ATP (adenosine triphosphate) production in facial muscles, re-educating muscle memory and improving the structural tone that holds facial contours in place. Think of it as passive facial exercise — the device does the work while you glide it across your skin.

Unlike lasers and light therapy, microcurrent works on facial muscles and connective tissue, not skin surface. Results compound with consistent use over weeks.
