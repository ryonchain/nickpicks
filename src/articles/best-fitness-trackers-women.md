## Buying Guide

### Menstrual Cycle Tracking: What to Look For

The most accurate cycle tracking uses temperature sensing (Oura Ring, Fitbit Sense 2 with skin temperature). Symptom-only logging (most trackers) can predict cycles based on historical data but can't detect real-time hormonal changes.

For fertility tracking, look for FDA-cleared cycle tracking features. The Fitbit Sense 2 with Premium and Oura Ring provide the most evidence-backed cycle predictions in wearables currently.

### Form Factor Options

**Traditional watch:** Most feature-complete. Samsung Galaxy Watch 7, Fitbit Sense 2.
**Slim bracelet:** Most wearable for everyday. Fitbit Luxe, Amazfit Band 7.
**Fashion watch:** Style-first with health features. Garmin Lily 2.
**Ring:** Most comfortable 24/7, most accurate for temperature/sleep. Oura Ring.

### Skin Sensitivity

Many women with sensitive skin find that stainless steel cases and silicone bands cause reactions with extended wear. Look for trackers with multiple band options including soft woven bands or leather alternatives. Garmin and Fitbit both offer wide third-party band ecosystems.
