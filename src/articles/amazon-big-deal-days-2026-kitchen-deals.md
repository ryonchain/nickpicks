## Should You Buy Kitchen Gear Now or Wait for Black Friday?

**Buy on Big Deal Days** for kitchen essentials you need right now. Kitchen appliances don't usually see dramatically deeper Black Friday cuts — the October deals are just as good.

**Wait for Black Friday** on big-ticket items (KitchenAid, Vitamix, Breville espresso) if you can tolerate the risk of stock running out. The absolute lowest annual prices often hit on Black Friday Saturday for those categories.

## Tips for Kitchen Appliance Shopping in October

1. **Check Amazon Warehouse Deals** — Open-box returns on air fryers and Instant Pots often save an additional 20%
2. **Read return windows** — Amazon's holiday return window extends from October through January; buy confidently
3. **Bundle deals** — Amazon often bundles accessories (extra pods, replacement filters) into appliance purchases
4. **Brand comparison** — Ninja and Instant Pot dominate Amazon deals; Breville and KitchenAid hold value better and last longer
5. **Wattage for blenders** — 900W+ for serious blending; 500–700W fine for occasional smoothies
