## Women's Cardigan Buying Guide

**Silhouette options:**
- **Fitted/classic:** Buttoned, fitted through torso. Best for office and smart casual.
- **Open-front/wrap:** No buttons, drapes freely. Most versatile for layering.
- **Oversized/longline:** Relaxed, cozy. Best for casual and WFH wear.
- **Chunky knit:** Thick, warm, statement-making. Best for weekend casual.
- **Duster/maxi:** Hip to below-knee length. Current trend.

**Fabric guide:**
- **Cashmere:** Softest, priciest. Premium everyday or investment piece.
- **Fine-gauge wool/merino:** Warm, slightly less soft than cashmere. Excellent for office.
- **Cotton or cotton blend:** Lighter, best for transitional weather.
- **Acrylic/polyester blend:** Machine washable, affordable, less breathable.

**Fall styling:**
- **Classic cardigan + white tee + straight-leg jeans + loafers** — effortless casual
- **Open-front cardigan + midi skirt + ankle boots** — feminine fall look
- **Chunky cardigan + fitted turtleneck + wide-leg trousers** — elevated cozy
- **Duster cardigan + basic leggings + sneakers** — casual comfort
