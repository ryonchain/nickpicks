## Bath Bomb Buying Guide

### What Makes a Good Bath Bomb?

**Fizz duration:** A good bomb should fizz for 3–5 minutes at full intensity. Bombs that dissolve instantly are too citric-acid-heavy with insufficient baking soda balance.

**Ingredients:** Look for shea butter, coconut oil, or argan oil for skin benefit. Essential oils for genuine scent. Avoid "fragrance oil" in premium products — it's synthetic and can irritate sensitive skin.

**Colorants:** Skin-safe bath colorants (FDA-approved cosmetic dyes or natural plant-based colors) are fine. Glitter or mica should only be used in bath-safe formulations — cosmetic glitter, not craft glitter.

### Bath Gift Set Checklist

A good gift set should include:
- 2+ products (bath + body pairing works well)
- Coherent scent across products in the set
- Attractive packaging that doesn't need to be rewrapped
- Products the recipient will genuinely use (not just display)

### Epsom Salt vs. Bath Bombs: Which to Choose?

**Bath bombs** are the experience choice — fizz, color, and scent make the bath feel like an event. Best for gifting and occasional indulgence.

**Epsom salt soaks** are the therapeutic choice — magnesium absorption, muscle recovery, and stress reduction are the goals. Best for athletic recovery or chronic stress.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Do bath bombs expire?**
Yes — the citric acid and baking soda that create the fizz react over time with humidity. Properly stored bombs last 6–12 months. Look for individually sealed bombs in premium sets.

**Are bath bombs safe for sensitive skin?**
Look for bombs that are free of synthetic fragrances, glitter, and artificial dyes. Brands like Dr Teal's and Sky Organics offer options specifically formulated for sensitive skin.

**Can I use a bath bomb in a jetted tub?**
Proceed with caution — oils in bath bombs can clog jet nozzles. If you have a jetted tub, choose oil-free bath salts instead.

**How do I clean the tub after a bath bomb?**
Rinse immediately after draining — oil residue wipes away easily when fresh. Shimmer-heavy bombs may require a quick scrub. All major bath bomb brands recommend rinsing after use.
