## Key Features to Evaluate

### Video Resolution
- **1080p:** Baseline — faces recognizable at moderate distance
- **2K (4MP):** Better detail — license plates readable at 20–30 feet
- **4K (8MP):** Maximum detail — license plates at 40+ feet

### Night Vision Types
- **Infrared (IR):** Grayscale, works in complete darkness, range up to 30+ feet
- **Color night vision:** Color video in low light using ambient light amplification
- **Spotlight/floodlight:** Activates to illuminate in color when motion detected

### Motion Detection Intelligence
- **Basic motion:** Any movement triggers alert. High false positive rate (trees, cars)
- **AI person/vehicle/animal:** Filters for relevant triggers. Dramatically reduces false alerts
- **Familiar face recognition:** Premium feature (Nest, Arlo) — identifies known people

### Power Options
- **Wired:** No battery concerns; always on
- **Battery:** Flexible placement, periodic recharge required
- **Solar + battery:** Near-maintenance-free in sunny climates
- **PoE (Power over Ethernet):** Best for professionals; no separate power cable

## Subscription vs. No-Subscription Reality

Many "free" cameras limit free cloud storage to 24–72 hours. Evaluate:
- **Free storage window:** How many days of events are stored?
- **Local storage option:** SD card or NAS support?
- **AI features gated behind paywall?**
