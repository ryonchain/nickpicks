## Mattress Buying Guide: What Actually Matters

### Sleep Position
- **Side sleepers:** Soft to medium (Purple, Casper) — shoulder and hip pressure relief
- **Back sleepers:** Medium to firm (Saatva, Leesa Hybrid) — lumbar support
- **Stomach sleepers:** Firm (Purple firm option, Saatva firm) — spine alignment
- **Combination sleepers:** Medium hybrid (Leesa Sapira, Bear Hybrid)

### Foam vs. Hybrid vs. Innerspring
- **Foam:** Best motion isolation, conforms to body, can sleep warm
- **Hybrid:** Coils + foam layers — best of both, better edge support, cooler
- **Innerspring:** Most breathable, traditional bounce, less conforming

### Trial Periods
All reputable online mattress brands offer at least 100 nights. Nectar offers 365 nights. Don't buy a mattress without a return policy — your body needs 30–60 nights to adjust.
