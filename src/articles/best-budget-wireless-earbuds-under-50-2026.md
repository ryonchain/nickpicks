## What to Look for in Budget Wireless Earbuds

**Bluetooth chip:** Qualcomm and Realtek chips outperform generic alternatives on stability and latency. Look for Bluetooth 5.0 or higher.

**ANC reality check:** True ANC costs money. Under $50, ANC reduces noise meaningfully but won't match Sony or Apple's flagship cancellation. Expect 15–25 dB reduction, not 40 dB.

**Battery life:** The 8–10 hour per-bud range is standard. Prioritize case battery life if you frequently forget to charge.

**Water rating:** IPX4 handles sweat and light rain. IPX7/8 is for swimming and heavy precipitation.

The budget earbud space rewards research. Spend $45 on the right pair and you'll forget you didn't spend $150.
