## The Two-Ingredient Anti-Aging Strategy

Everything else is incremental. If you only do two things for anti-aging:

1. **Sunscreen or SPF moisturizer daily** — UV exposure causes 80% of visible skin aging. Fine lines, wrinkles, dark spots, and loss of firmness are primarily UV-driven.

Everything else — peptides, vitamin C, hyaluronic acid, growth factors — supports but doesn't replace these two.

## What to Look For When Buying

**SPF 30 minimum for daily outdoor exposure:** SPF 15 in morning moisturizers blocks roughly 93% of UVB rays; SPF 30 blocks 97%. The difference compounds over years of daily use.

**Retinol requires patience and SPF:** Don't start retinol without committing to consistent sunscreen — retinol increases sun sensitivity and your gains will be offset by UV damage.

**Texture matters for compliance:** The best moisturizer is the one you actually use. If a formula is too heavy, you'll skip it. Choose texture first, then actives.

**Night moisturizers can be richer:** At night there's no UV to address and your skin is in repair mode. Richer, active-ingredient formulas (retinol, glycolic acid) work best applied before bed.
