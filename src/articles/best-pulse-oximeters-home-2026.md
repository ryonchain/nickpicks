## What SpO2 Numbers Mean

| SpO2 | Interpretation |
|------|---------------|
| 95–100% | Normal |
| 91–94% | Mild hypoxemia — notify your doctor |
| 86–90% | Moderate hypoxemia — seek care |
| <86% | Severe — emergency |

*At altitude (8,000+ feet), 90–95% can be normal for acclimatizing individuals.*

## Fingertip vs Wrist vs Ring: Which is Right?

**Fingertip (spot-check):** Most accurate for one-time readings. Best during illness, before/after exercise, travel to altitude. The Masimo MightySat or Zacurate 500DL.

**Wrist ring (continuous overnight):** Best for sleep monitoring, COPD management, or anyone needing all-night trend data. The Wellue O2Ring is the clear winner here.

**Smartwatch (lifestyle integration):** Convenient but less clinically reliable. Best for wellness trends, not diagnostic use.
