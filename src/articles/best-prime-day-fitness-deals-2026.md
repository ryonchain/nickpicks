## Prime Day Fitness Buying Guide

**Prioritize equipment you'll actually use.** Prime Day pressure can lead to impulse purchases on expensive treadmills or rowing machines that end up as coat racks. Only buy what fits your existing workout habits or a realistic new routine you'll commit to.

**Shipping timing matters for large items.** Treadmills and large gym equipment may take 1–2 weeks to deliver and require setup. Factor that into your timeline if you're eager to start.

**Wearables and Prime Day:** Smartwatch deals during Prime Day are often the steepest discounts of the year — particularly on outgoing model years. The Series 9 at ~$249 is a substantial discount that rarely appears at other times.

**Compare with your gym membership cost.** A $299 dumbbell set pays for itself in under a year if it replaces a $30/month gym membership — and remains useful for years after.
