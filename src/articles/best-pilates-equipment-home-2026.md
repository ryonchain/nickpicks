## Building Your Home Pilates Setup

**Stage 1 — Mat pilates (no equipment needed):** Start with just a quality yoga or pilates mat. Classical mat pilates is highly effective on its own.

**Stage 2 — Add accessories ($30–$100):** A pilates ring and a set of mini resistance bands multiply your exercise options and add measurable resistance to mat work.

**Stage 3 — Add a reformer ($300–$1,500):** A home reformer enables the full pilates exercise catalog and provides spring-loaded resistance not achievable with bands alone.

**Stage 4 — Full home studio ($1,500+):** Add a pilates chair, ladder barrel, or cadillac for advanced work. Most home practitioners never need this level.
