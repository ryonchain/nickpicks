## iPhone 17 Pro vs. 17 Pro Max: Different Cases?

Yes — cases are different between models due to the larger size (17 Pro Max is 6.9 inches vs. 6.3 for 17 Pro). All the brands above make versions for both. When buying, confirm you're ordering the correct model. Pro and Pro Max cases are not interchangeable.

## What to Look for in an iPhone 17 Pro Case

**MagSafe compatibility:** If you use a MagSafe wallet, charger, or mount, look for "MagSafe compatible" (has a built-in magnet ring). Cases without this magnet ring won't hold MagSafe accessories reliably.

**Camera bump coverage:** The iPhone 17 Pro has a significant camera system. A raised lip around the camera protects the lens when you set the phone face-up on a table.

**Military Grade certification:** Means the case was tested to MIL-STD-810G — a drop test standard. Not all drops, but meaningful protection for everyday accidents.

**Grip texture:** Titanium is slippery. A case with textured sides dramatically reduces how often you drop it in the first place.
