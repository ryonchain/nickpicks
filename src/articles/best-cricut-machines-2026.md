## Our Top Picks

## Cricut Design Space — The Software

All Cricut machines use Design Space, a cloud-based design app (free to use). It runs on desktop, iOS, and Android. The free tier lets you access a limited library; Cricut Access ($9.99/mo) unlocks 100,000+ images, fonts, and full project access.

**Is Cricut Access worth it?** For regular crafters: yes. For occasional use: save money and use free SVG files from sites like Creative Fabrica or Freepik instead.

## What Can You Make with a Cricut?

- **Vinyl decals** — car windows, walls, mugs, laptops
- **Iron-on/HTV** — custom T-shirts, tote bags, hats
- **Paper crafts** — cards, invitations, gift boxes
- **Stickers** — printable sticker sheets (requires Explore 3+)
- **Leather** — earrings, bookmarks (requires Maker 3)
- **Fabric** — quilting, appliqué (requires Maker 3 with rotary blade)
