## Gifts to Skip for 3-Year-Olds

- **Tablets:** The American Academy of Pediatrics recommends limiting screen time at this age. Most screen-based gifts don't deliver developmental value.
- **Toys that "do everything":** 3-year-olds learn through open-ended play, not passive watching. Avoid toys that perform for the child instead of responding to them.
- **Toys with 100+ small pieces:** Fun in theory, impossible to manage in practice.
