## How to Build a Fall Jacket Wardrobe

**The 3-jacket strategy:**

1. **Lightweight layer** (puffer vest or denim jacket) — for 55–65°F
2. **Mid-weight jacket** (quilted, leather, or fleece) — for 40–55°F
3. **Winter coat** (wool or down) — for below 40°F

Most women get the most wear from the mid-weight jacket because it covers the longest part of the season.

**What to look for:**
- **Warmth rating:** Fleece and quilted = moderate. Down/puffer = very warm. Wool = versatile.
- **Water resistance:** For fall rain, look for DWR (durable water repellent) coating or a dedicated rain shell.
- **Pockets:** Deep, functional pockets are non-negotiable in outerwear.
- **Length:** Hip-length for active/casual, knee-length for professional/formal.
