## Setting Up Your Streaming Webcam

**Lighting beats camera hardware.** Even the best webcam looks bad in a dark room. Add a key light (like the Elgato Key Light Air) or position a desk lamp slightly off to your side at face level. Backlit setups (window behind you) create silhouettes — move the light source in front of you.

**Eye-level positioning.** Mount your webcam at eye level rather than at desk level looking up. A monitor arm or clip-to-monitor mount at eye height is ideal. Looking down at a webcam creates unflattering angles and makes you appear disengaged.

**Frame yourself intentionally.** In OBS, crop and position your webcam overlay so it shows face and shoulders. A common mistake is showing too much of the room background — tight framing keeps focus on your reactions.
