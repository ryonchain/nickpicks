## Buying Guide: What Matters

**Insulation technology:** True vacuum-insulated double-wall is the only way to achieve 24-hour cold retention. "Double-wall" without "vacuum" just means two walls with air — far less effective.

**Lid design:** The lid is where most bottles fail. Look for reviews on leak-proofing, ease of cleaning, and whether replacement lids are available.

**Mouth size:** Wide mouth (fits ice cubes; easier to clean), standard mouth (fits most straws; better for backpacks). Match to your use case.

**Warranty:** Top brands (Stanley, Hydro Flask, YETI) offer lifetime warranties. This signals confidence in the product and is worth factoring into cost-per-use calculations.
