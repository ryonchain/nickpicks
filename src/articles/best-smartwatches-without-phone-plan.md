## Buying Guide

### What Works Without a Phone Plan

Everything you'd normally use a smartwatch for works on the Bluetooth/GPS model:

- **Notifications:** All notifications mirrored from your phone via Bluetooth
- **Fitness tracking:** Steps, heart rate, sleep, workouts — all local to the watch
- **GPS:** Built-in GPS for outdoor activity tracking
- **Music:** Stored music playback; streaming via Bluetooth from phone
- **Payments:** NFC contactless payments (Apple Pay, Samsung Pay, Garmin Pay) work without cellular
- **Health monitoring:** ECG, SpO2, stress, sleep — no carrier plan needed

### What Requires a Phone Plan on the Watch

- **Calls without phone nearby:** Sending/receiving calls over LTE when your phone is at home
- **Texts without phone nearby:** Sending messages when your iPhone is out of Bluetooth range
- **Emergency SOS without phone:** Some cellular watches can contact 911 without a phone nearby

### LTE vs. GPS: When is LTE Worth It?

LTE is worth the extra monthly fee (~$5-15/month) when you regularly leave your phone at home — specifically for runners, cyclists, and outdoor athletes who leave their phone in the car or at home during workouts. If your phone is almost always with you, the GPS model saves money without sacrificing features.

### Battery Life Comparison

Removing the LTE radio dramatically improves battery life. The Samsung Galaxy Watch 7 GPS gets 40 hours vs. 30 hours for the LTE model. Garmin watches (all GPS-only) last 14 days. Budget watches like Amazfit last 14+ days. If battery anxiety is a concern, the no-phone-plan category wins by default.

## FAQ

**Can a smartwatch work without a phone plan?**
Yes. GPS, health tracking, notifications, and payments all work via Bluetooth connection to your phone — no carrier plan needed on the watch.

**What is the difference between Bluetooth and LTE smartwatch?**
Bluetooth connects to your phone; LTE has its own cellular radio for calls/texts without the phone nearby. LTE requires a monthly carrier plan ($5-15/month).

**Do Bluetooth smartwatches have GPS?**
Many do — Apple Watch SE 2, Samsung Galaxy Watch 7 Bluetooth, Garmin Venu 3, and Amazfit GTR 4 all have built-in GPS that tracks workouts independently.

**Which smartwatch has the best features without a phone plan?**
Apple Watch SE 2 (GPS) for iPhone users; Samsung Galaxy Watch 7 Bluetooth for Android users. Both deliver full smartwatch features without carrier fees.
