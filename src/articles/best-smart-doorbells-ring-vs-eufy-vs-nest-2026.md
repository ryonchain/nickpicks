Three brands dominate the smart doorbell market in 2026: Ring (Amazon-owned), Eufy (Anker), and Google Nest. Each takes a fundamentally different approach to cloud storage, subscriptions, and privacy. Picking the wrong ecosystem can cost you $100+ per year in subscription fees you didn't budget for. Here's how to choose.

---

## The Core Difference: Subscription Philosophy

**Ring:** Almost all premium features require a Ring Protect plan ($4.99/mo per device or $10/mo for unlimited devices). Without a subscription: no cloud video history, no video sharing, no advanced motion zones. Live view and basic alerts are free.

**Eufy:** Local storage via HomeBase hub — no subscription required for 90 days of encrypted video history stored on your local device. Cloud backup available as an optional add-on. This is Eufy's core selling point.

**Google Nest:** 3 hours of event history is free (no subscription). Extended 30-day cloud history requires Google Home Familiar Faces + Nest Aware ($8/mo or $40/yr). Offers the cleanest free tier of the three.

## Privacy Comparison

**Ring:** Amazon retains cloud access to video. Amazon has a history of sharing footage with law enforcement without user consent (though this policy was updated in 2023). For privacy-conscious users, Ring is the worst choice.

**Eufy:** Local storage with end-to-end encryption is genuinely private. No cloud dependency means no third-party access. Past controversy (2022 Eufy cloud storage exposure) prompted major security overhauls — current HomeBase 3 architecture is fundamentally local-first.

**Nest:** Google stores video on its servers with standard Google privacy policies. Better than Ring's past record but still cloud-dependent. For Google ecosystem users already comfortable with Google's data practices, this is a non-issue.

## Which Should You Buy?

**Buy Ring if:** You're deep in the Amazon Alexa ecosystem, you have multiple Ring devices (alarm, cameras), and you want the simplest "it just works with Alexa" experience. Budget for the Protect subscription.

**Buy Eufy if:** You want local storage without monthly fees, you care about privacy, or you want the dual-camera package coverage. Get the HomeBase hub.

**Buy Nest if:** You have Google Home devices, Chromecast TVs, or Nest thermostats. The AI detection (people/packages/animals) free tier is the best in the category. Get Nest Aware only if you need 30-day history.
