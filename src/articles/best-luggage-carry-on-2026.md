## Airline Size Limits (What You Need to Know)

Most major US airlines allow carry-on bags up to **22" × 14" × 9"** (56 × 35.5 × 23 cm). International airlines (especially budget carriers) are often stricter: **20" × 15" × 10"** or smaller.

The Away and Monos bags are sized to the US standard. If you fly international budget airlines frequently (Ryanair, EasyJet, Spirit), verify your specific airline's requirements before buying.

**Weight limits:** Most US airlines don't weigh carry-ons. Overhead space is the constraint. International carriers sometimes enforce 10–15 lb limits strictly.
