## Women's Blazer Buying Guide

**Silhouette styles for 2026:**
- **Oversized:** The dominant trend. Wide shoulders, relaxed through body. Worn as an outer layer.
- **Fitted/classic:** Professional standard. Tailored through the torso.
- **Relaxed structure:** In between — not tight, not baggy. Most versatile.
- **Cropped:** Best for high-waist pants. More casual than fitted.

**Best fall outfit formulas:**
- **Oversized blazer + straight-leg jeans + loafers + turtleneck** — the fall casual uniform
- **Fitted blazer + trousers + pointed-toe heels** — polished professional
- **Plaid blazer + leather pants + ankle boots** — fall fashion statement
- **Oversized blazer + mini dress + knee-high boots** — elevated casual
- **Blazer + joggers or biker shorts** — athleisure elevated

**Fabric options:**
- **Structured wool/suiting:** Formal, holds shape well. Best for office.
- **Ponte/jersey:** Stretch fabric, comfortable, less formal. Best for casual blazing.
- **Linen:** Breathable but wrinkles easily. Best for spring/summer.
- **Velvet:** Statement piece. Best for evenings and fall parties.

**Care tips:**
- Most blazers should be dry cleaned or hand washed
- Use a fabric shaver to remove pilling on suiting fabric
- Hang on proper shoulder-width hangers to maintain shape
- Steam rather than iron when possible
