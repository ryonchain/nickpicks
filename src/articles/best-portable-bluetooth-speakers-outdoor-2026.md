## IPX Rating Guide for Outdoor Speakers

| Rating | Protection Level |
|--------|-----------------|
| IPX4 | Splash-resistant (rain OK, no submerging) |
| IPX7 | 1m submersion for 30 minutes |
| IP67 | Dust-proof + IPX7 water protection |
| IP68 | Dust-proof + deeper/longer submersion |

For beach and pool use, **IPX7 minimum**. IPX4 speakers are not suitable for pool use.
