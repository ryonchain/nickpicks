## Frequently Asked Questions

**Which smart home devices actually improve productivity?**
Smart plugs and smart bulbs have the highest productivity ROI. Smart plugs automate desk peripheral power, reducing friction from workday start and end rituals. Smart bulbs set focus-optimized lighting (cool 5000K) and wind-down lighting (warm 2700K) automatically.

**What color temperature is best for work productivity?**
Cool white light at 5000–6500K supports alertness and focus during work hours. Warm light at 2700–3000K is better for evening wind-down. Smart bulbs like Philips Hue let you schedule this transition automatically.

**Do I need a smart home hub for smart bulbs?**
No. Most modern smart bulbs (Govee, LIFX, Wyze, and Kasa) connect directly to your home Wi-Fi without a separate hub. Philips Hue requires a Bridge hub but offers deeper integration with other smart home systems.

**What is a TimeFlip2 and how does it help productivity?**
TimeFlip2 is a physical 12-sided dice tracker. Flip it to a labeled side to start tracking that task category. It syncs to an app and generates time-use reports. The physical act of flipping creates a context-switch ritual that improves task focus.

**Can smart plugs save energy in a home office?**
Yes. Scheduling monitors, desk lamps, and chargers to power off during non-work hours eliminates phantom load. A smart plug on a 27-inch monitor saves roughly 2–4 kWh per month in standby power.
