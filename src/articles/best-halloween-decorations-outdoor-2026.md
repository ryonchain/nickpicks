## Best Halloween Fog Machines

Fog machines ($30-80) transform any yard into a proper haunted scene. Ground-hugging fog creates the classic graveyard atmosphere when used near dry ice or a "low-lying fog" chiller attachment.

**What to know before buying:**
- 400W fog machines cover a small porch/entrance area
- 700-1000W machines fill a full yard
- Fog fluid is sold separately — buy a gallon for a full Halloween night
- Position upwind of your entrance so fog rolls toward visitors
