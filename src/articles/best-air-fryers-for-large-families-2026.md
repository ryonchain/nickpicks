## Tips for Large-Family Air Frying

**Don't overcrowd the basket.** Even in a 10 qt air fryer, a single layer is important for even crisping. Cook in two batches if needed rather than stacking.

**Dual basket = parallel cooking, not doubled single-item capacity.** A 10 qt dual-zone air fryer doesn't mean 10 qt for one item — each 5 qt basket is independent.

**Preheat for 3–5 minutes.** Large air fryers benefit from preheating (especially Philips and Ninja models) — it significantly improves crispiness on chicken skin, fries, and frozen foods.

**Use the shake reminder.** Most models have a shake alert — use it for fries and wings to ensure even browning.
