## How to Wear Fall Scarves

**The Parisian loop:** Fold the scarf in half lengthwise, loop around the neck, and pull the ends through the fold. Classic and keeps the scarf in place.

**The oversized wrap:** Use a blanket scarf as a shawl over a denim jacket or blazer. Belt at the waist for structure.

**The triangle drape:** Fold a square scarf diagonally into a triangle, drape the point in front, and tie the ends at the back of the neck.

**The loose drape:** Simply drape a long rectangle scarf over the shoulders with both ends hanging equally in front. Works with everything.
