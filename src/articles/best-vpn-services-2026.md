## How to Choose a VPN

### For Streaming
Priority: server variety, consistent unblocking, fast speeds.
**Best choice:** ExpressVPN or NordVPN.

### For Privacy
Priority: jurisdiction, verified no-logs, open-source code.
**Best choice:** ProtonVPN (Switzerland) or Mullvad (Sweden, anonymous signup).

### For Multiple Devices / Families
Priority: simultaneous connections, price.
**Best choice:** Surfshark (unlimited devices, lowest annual price).

### For Traveling
Priority: server variety, reliability, customer support.
**Best choice:** NordVPN (most servers, strong support).

### On a Budget
Priority: price, adequate speeds, basic privacy.
**Best choice:** Surfshark ($2.49/mo annual) or ProtonVPN free tier.
