## Women's Pajama Buying Guide

**Material comparison:**
- **Cotton jersey:** Breathable, soft, year-round option. Best for normal sleepers.
- **Flannel:** Warmest option. Best for cold bedrooms or women who run cold.
- **Modal/bamboo:** Moisture-wicking, temperature-regulating. Best for hot sleepers.
- **Satin:** Silky feel, slides easily in bed. Polyester version is affordable; doesn't regulate temperature.
- **Real silk:** Best temperature regulation, natural, hypoallergenic. Expensive but worth it.
- **CozyChic/microfiber knit:** Ultra-soft, medium warmth. Best sensory experience.

**For gifting:**
Pajama sets are among the most appreciated gifts women receive. Flannel sets for cold winters, satin for a luxurious touch, CozyChic for sensory comfort. Consider matching sets that come in gift-ready packaging.

**Seasonal guide:**
- **Summer:** Lightweight cotton or bamboo shorts sets
- **Fall/Spring:** Jersey cotton long-sleeve sets, lightweight flannel
- **Winter:** Heavyweight flannel, fleece, or thermal sets
- **Year-round:** Silk and modal regulate temperature across seasons
