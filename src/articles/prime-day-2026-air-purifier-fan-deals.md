## Buying Guide: What to Know Before You Purchase

### Air Purifier Sizing

Match the purifier's CADR (Clean Air Delivery Rate) to your room size. A general rule: your purifier's CADR should be at least 2/3 of your room's square footage.

- **Small bedroom (150 sq ft):** Coway Mighty or Levoit Core 300
- **Master bedroom or living room (350–500 sq ft):** Levoit Core 400S, Winix 5500-2, or Blueair 311i
- **Open-plan living/kitchen (600+ sq ft):** Coway AP-1619A or Levoit Core 600S
- **Whole-home or large space:** Dyson TP07 (multiple units) or commercial-grade options

### Filter Replacement Cost

Calculate the total cost of ownership, not just the upfront price. A $99 purifier with $49 annual filters is more expensive after 3 years than a $149 purifier with $29 annual filters.

- **Levoit:** Filters typically $25–$35/year
- **Coway:** Filters typically $20–$30/year
- **Winix:** Has washable pre-filter that reduces ongoing costs
- **Dyson:** Filters run $50–$70/year but last 12 months

### Fan Type Guide

- **Tower fans:** Good looking, oscillate, good for bedrooms
- **Bladeless (Dyson):** Premium, silent, safe, easy to clean
- **Whole-room circulators (Vornado):** Best for actually cooling a whole room
- **Box/window fans:** Powerful, cheap, ugly — good for garages and workshops
