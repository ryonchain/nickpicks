Cobbler-style stainless steel with a built-in strainer lid and measuring cap. The OXO shaker is leak-proof, easy to open after shaking (cobbler shakers can vacuum-seal — this one doesn't), and dishwasher safe. Includes a shaker, strainer cap, and lid. The best beginner shaker available.

**Price:** ~$35  
**Best for:** Home bartenders, beginners, anyone making shaken cocktails

### 2. Viski Professional Hawthorne Strainer — Best Cocktail Strainer

Fine spring coil strained over a Boston tin, stainless steel, and properly sized for all standard shaking tins. The Hawthorne strainer is the industry standard — holds back ice and fruit pulp while pouring cleanly. A cobbler shaker starter can upgrade to a Hawthorne/Boston setup with this.

**Price:** ~$15  
**Best for:** Upgrading from cobbler shaker, cocktail enthusiasts

### 3. OXO Good Grips Double Jigger — Best Jigger

Stainless steel with 1oz/2oz markings on the outside and additional measurement lines inside at ½oz and 1½oz increments. Etched (not printed) measurements that don't wear off. Consistent pours are the difference between a recipe and a cocktail — a quality jigger is the most underrated bar tool.

**Price:** ~$12  
**Best for:** Everyone who makes cocktails — non-negotiable for accuracy

### 4. Belmont Wooden Muddler — Best Muddler

**Price:** ~$15  
**Best for:** Mojito and Old Fashioned makers, home bartenders who muddle regularly
