## How to Choose: Laser vs Inkjet for College

**Choose laser if:**
- You mostly print text (papers, reading, notes)
- You'll use the printer sporadically (toner doesn't dry out)
- You want the lowest cost per page over 4 years
- Black and white is sufficient for 90% of your printing

**Choose inkjet if:**
- You need color printing (presentations, photos)
- You have a low budget upfront ($60–$70 range)
- You print infrequently and prefer lower upfront cost
- You also need scanning/copying (look at all-in-ones)

**The math:** A typical college student prints ~500 pages per semester, or ~4,000 pages over 4 years. At 15¢/page (inkjet) that's $600 in ink alone. At 2¢/page (laser), it's $80. The Brother HL-L2370DW pays for itself versus an inkjet in the first year.
