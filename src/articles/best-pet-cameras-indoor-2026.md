## Pet Camera Buying Guide

**Field of view:** 120°–160° wide-angle lenses cover more room with fewer cameras. Aim for 130° or wider for living rooms.

**Night vision:** Color night vision (Wyze, Arlo) is noticeably better than infrared black-and-white at identifying what your pet is doing.

**Treat dispenser:** Verify compatibility with your treat size. Furbo and Petcube are tuned for standard kibble-sized treats — larger treats may jam.

**Subscriptions:** Most cameras push toward cloud subscriptions for video history. Eufy stands out for generous local-first storage.

**Placement:** For cats, aim for a high shelf or table surface. For dogs, countertop height captures the whole dog vs. just a snout.
