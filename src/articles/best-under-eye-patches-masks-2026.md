## Buying Guide: Under-Eye Patches

### Why Patches Work Better Than Creams for Some Concerns

Patches create an occlusive microenvironment that:
1. Drives active ingredients deeper into skin than open-air application
2. Prevents product evaporation (maximizing ingredient time on skin)
3. Physically de-puffs through cooling and gentle pressure
4. Creates a hydration reservoir that keeps skin moist for hours after removal

For acute concerns (pre-event de-puffing, tired eyes after travel), patches deliver faster and more visible results than creams.

### What Patches Can and Can't Do

**Can do:** Temporarily reduce puffiness (especially caffeine-formula options), deeply hydrate, temporarily reduce fine line appearance through plumping, mildly brighten with niacinamide over time.

**Can't do:** Permanently eliminate dark circles caused by genetics or blood vessel visibility, remove structural hollows (tear troughs), or provide lasting results beyond 6–12 hours for acute effects.

### How to Use Under-Eye Patches

1. Cleanse and tone face before application
2. Apply patches to clean, dry under-eye area
3. Leave for indicated time (5–30 minutes depending on product)
4. Remove and gently pat residue into skin — don't rinse
5. Apply eye cream on top to lock in moisture

Refrigerate patches before use for enhanced cooling and de-puffing effect.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**How often should I use under-eye patches?**
Depends on the formula. Caffeine/hydration patches can be used daily; retinol formulas should be limited to 3–4× per week. Luxury patches ($7+ per pair) are typically used 2–3× per week as a treatment.

**Do eye patches help with dark circles?**
Temporarily yes — caffeine vasoconstriction and physical de-puffing reduce the shadow effect. Niacinamide improves brightening over 6–8 weeks. Structural dark circles (genetics, tear troughs) are not addressed by topical patches.

**Can I use eye patches with other products?**
Yes. Apply patches before any other eye cream or serum. After removal, apply your regular eye routine on top.

**Are Korean eye patches better than Western brands?**
Korean brands (Mediheal, TONYMOLY) pioneered the hydrogel patch format and generally have excellent formulas. Western luxury brands (Peter Thomas Roth, Charlotte Tilbury) compete effectively in this space. Choose based on formula ingredients rather than country of origin.
