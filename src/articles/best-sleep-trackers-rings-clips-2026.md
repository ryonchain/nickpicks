## How to Choose Your Sleep Tracker Form Factor

**Ring:** Best overall — most comfortable, most accurate, discreet. Best option if you're serious about sleep data.

**Wristband:** Best if you already wear a fitness tracker and don't want two devices. Slightly less comfortable than a ring but more convenient if you prefer wrist devices.

**Under-mattress:** Best for anyone who cannot wear a device (skin sensitivity, claustrophobia, preference). Also useful for tracking two people independently. Less accurate for sleep staging.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Which sleep tracker is best without a subscription?**
The Ultrahuman Ring Air provides ring-level sleep tracking accuracy with no monthly fee. The Garmin Vivosmart 5 is the best subscription-free wristband option — Garmin Connect is completely free.

**How do I improve my sleep using tracker data?**
Look for consistent patterns over 2–4 weeks: bedtime, wake time, deep sleep %, and REM %. The most impactful changes are consistent wake time (the strongest circadian anchor), reducing alcohol (it suppresses REM dramatically), and avoiding screens within 1 hour of sleep.
