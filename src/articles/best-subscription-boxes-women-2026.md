## Questions to Ask Before Subscribing

1. **Can I skip months?** Important for travel or budget months.
2. **Is there a cancellation fee?** Most boxes are month-to-month.
3. **Can I customize?** Better boxes let you choose preferences or swap items.
4. **What's the first-box offer?** Many boxes offer 40–60% off the first box.
