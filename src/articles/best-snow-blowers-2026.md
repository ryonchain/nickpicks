# Best Snow Blowers in 2026

Snow blowers split into three categories: single-stage (lightweight, best for up to 8" of light snow on paved driveways), two-stage (heavy-duty, handles deep snow and gravel driveways), and electric/battery (quiet, low-maintenance, best for light snow areas). Engine displacement (cc) and clearing width determine clearing speed.
