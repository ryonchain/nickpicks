## What to Know Before Buying

**Battery access:** All holders must allow battery replacement. AirTag batteries last about a year. Check that your holder allows the back to be twisted off.

**Signal passthrough:** Metallic enclosures can reduce UWB precision slightly — stick to plastic or silicone for highest accuracy tracking indoors.

**Waterproofing:** The AirTag itself is IP67 waterproof. Holders vary — if you're attaching to outdoor gear or pet collars, verify the holder is water-resistant as well.

**Speaker access:** AirTags emit a sound when pinged via Find My. Good holders have open sides or perforations that let the sound through clearly — important for locating a lost bag.
