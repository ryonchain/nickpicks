## How to Choose Wireless Earbuds

### ANC vs. Passive Isolation

Passive isolation (a good physical seal) reduces high-frequency noise. Active noise cancellation adds electronic countering of low-frequency noise (engines, HVAC, crowds). For maximum noise blocking, you need both — earbuds with good ear tips AND strong ANC. The Sony XM5 and AirPods Pro 2 lead in both categories.

### Codec Matters on Android

On Android with LDAC (Sony WF-1000XM5, Soundcore Liberty 4 NC), music sounds noticeably better than AAC or SBC — approaching hi-res audio quality wirelessly. On iPhone, the AAC codec is the ceiling for all earbuds regardless of marketing claims. If audio quality matters to you, use LDAC-supporting earbuds on Android.

### Battery Life in Practice

Manufacturer battery claims assume 50% volume with ANC off. Real-world ANC-on battery is typically 20–30% less. Budget 60–70% of claimed battery for realistic planning with ANC enabled.

### Call Quality

For video calls and phone calls in professional settings, call quality matters more than music quality. Apple AirPods Pro 2 and Sony XM5 lead this category. Budget earbuds generally produce adequate call quality for casual calls but struggle in noisy environments.
