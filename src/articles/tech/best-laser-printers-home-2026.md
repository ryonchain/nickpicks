## Laser vs. Inkjet: When to Choose Laser

**Choose laser if you:**
- Print more than 20 pages per month
- Mostly print text documents, forms, school work
- Want the lowest cost per page
- Hate dried ink cartridges from infrequent use
- Need fast printing (32–36 ppm vs. 8–15 ppm for inkjet)

**Choose inkjet if you:**
- Print photos regularly (inkjet produces better color gradients)
- Print very infrequently (under 5 pages/month) and want lower upfront cost
- Need to print on specialty media like glossy photo paper

For most home users, laser is the smarter long-term choice. The Brother HL-L2350DW has paid for itself in toner savings within 6 months for anyone who previously owned a standard inkjet printer.
