## How to Choose a Smartwatch

### iPhone vs. Android

This is the primary decision. Apple Watch Series 10 works only with iPhone (Bluetooth and LTE require iOS pairing). Samsung Galaxy Watch works with all Android phones but integrates more deeply with Samsung Galaxy. Garmin and Fitbit work with both ecosystems but sacrifice deep integration for cross-platform compatibility.

### Battery Life vs. Feature Density

High-feature smartwatches (Apple Watch, Samsung) sacrifice battery life for sensors and display quality. Athletic-focused watches (Garmin) prioritize battery for extended tracking. Fitness bands (Fitbit) balance both by doing less.

### Fitness Depth vs. Smartwatch Breadth

Apple Watch and Samsung Galaxy Watch are excellent general-purpose devices that happen to track fitness. Garmin is the opposite — purpose-built for athletic training that happens to show notifications. If running races or doing triathlons is a primary use case, Garmin is the right choice.
