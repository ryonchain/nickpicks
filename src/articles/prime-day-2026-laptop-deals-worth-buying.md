## How to Spot a Real Laptop Deal on Prime Day

Not every "Prime Day deal" is actually a deal. Watch for:

- **Price history matters.** A laptop "50% off" that was inflated for 3 weeks before Prime Day isn't a deal. We track baseline prices year-round.
- **Model year.** Prime Day often clears last year's stock. That's fine — just know what you're buying. A 2025 model at 25% off is often smarter than a 2026 model at 10% off.
- **Chip generation.** Intel Core Ultra 7 and AMD Ryzen AI 9 are the current-gen chips for 2026. Anything on 12th-gen Intel or Ryzen 5000 series is last-gen.

## What to Check Before Buying

1. **RAM**: 8GB is minimum for 2026; 16GB is the target. 32GB for power users.
2. **Storage**: 256GB SSD is workable; 512GB is comfortable; 1TB is future-proof.
3. **Display**: IPS is baseline; OLED is worth paying up for if price gap is under $100.
4. **Battery life**: Advertised hours are always optimistic — subtract 25–30% for real-world.
5. **Return window**: Amazon extends Prime Day return windows. Buy with confidence.
