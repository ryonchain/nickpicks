## Buying Guide: 11-Year-Old Gift Considerations

**What changed at 11:** Social status within peer group matters enormously. Gifts that connect to what friends are doing (same games, same brands, same trends) will land better than objectively superior alternatives.

**Trends in 2026 for 11-year-olds:**
- Instant cameras (Instax) — still very popular
- Friendship bracelets — ongoing
- Stanley cups / Hydro Flask
- Roblox
- Cozy, Pinterest-core bedroom aesthetics

**Avoid:** Toys targeted at 5-8 year olds, anything with childish branding, games they've clearly outgrown, and anything that assumes gender preferences you haven't confirmed.
