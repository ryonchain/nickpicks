## How to Choose a Quality Luxury Handbag Under $500

**Material:** Genuine leather ages beautifully. Saffiano leather (pebbled, cross-hatch texture) resists scratches and water. Coated canvas (LV's signature) is also durable and easy to clean.

**Hardware quality:** Check that zippers glide smoothly, clasps click firmly, and metal hardware is substantial. Cheap hardware is the telltale sign of a low-quality bag regardless of brand.

**Construction:** Look at the stitching — it should be even, tight, and matching thread. Check interior seams for raw edges versus bound edges.

**Resale value:** Coach, Kate Spade, and Longchamp hold their value reasonably well in the pre-owned market.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Is Coach a true luxury brand?**
Coach is considered "accessible luxury" or "bridge luxury" — below Chanel/LV but meaningfully above fast fashion. Quality and craftsmanship are genuine.

**What's the difference between luxury and designer?**
"Designer" means it bears a designer name. "Luxury" implies exceptional craftsmanship, materials, and exclusivity. Some designer bags qualify as true luxury; others are aspirational mass-market.
