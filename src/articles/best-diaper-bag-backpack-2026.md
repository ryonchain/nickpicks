## What to Look For in a Diaper Bag Backpack

**Size:** Aim for 20–30 liters for day trips. Larger for travel or twins.

**Organization:** Dedicated pockets for diapers, wipes, bottles, and a changing pad slot are non-negotiable.

**Durability:** Water-resistant exterior and wipeable interior save sanity.

**Comfort:** Padded straps and back panels matter when you're carrying 10+ pounds of baby gear.

**Style:** You'll carry this bag everywhere for 2+ years. Pick one you actually like looking at.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**How long do I need a diaper bag?**
Most parents use a diaper bag from birth through potty training — typically 2–3 years.

**Can dads use a diaper bag?**
Absolutely. Many parents choose gender-neutral designs like the Skip Hop Forma or JuJuBe precisely because both parents carry it.

**Do I need a changing pad?**
Most diaper bag backpacks include one. Having your own is more hygienic than public changing tables.
