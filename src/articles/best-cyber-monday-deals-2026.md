## What NOT to Buy on Cyber Monday

- **Furniture and mattresses** — Black Friday and Memorial Day are better
- **Jewelry** — Year-round promotions are usually as good
- **Gift cards** — Not typically discounted
- **Budget electronics from unknown brands** — Quality risk is high
- **Seasonal perishables** — No reason to buy online vs. locally
