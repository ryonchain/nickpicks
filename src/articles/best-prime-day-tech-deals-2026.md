## Prime Day Tech Buying Guide

**Amazon's own devices hit the deepest discounts.** Echo Dots, Ring doorbells, and Fire tablets see the steepest Prime Day cuts of any product category on Amazon — sometimes 50–70% off. If you're in the Amazon ecosystem, this is the time to expand.

**Check for bundle deals.** Amazon frequently bundles Echo Dots with smart plugs or Ring doorbells with Ring Protect subscriptions at additional Prime Day discounts. Search for bundles before buying components separately.

**Refurbished options.** Amazon Renewed (certified refurbished) devices often have their own Prime Day deals — particularly on headphones and tablets. Renewed products carry a 90-day Amazon Renewed Guarantee.

**Compatibility before you buy.** Smart home devices have ecosystem requirements. Ring works best with Alexa; Google Nest works best with Google Home and Google Assistant. Check that your existing ecosystem aligns before purchasing.
