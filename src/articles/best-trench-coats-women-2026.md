## Trench Coat Fit Guide

**Length:** Knee-length is most classic. Midi (below knee) is more modern. Mini trench is a very different garment — don't confuse them.

**Shoulders:** Should lie flat and smooth. A dropped shoulder or shoulder that falls off looks sloppy regardless of price.

**Belt:** Always belt the trench — it transforms the silhouette. A loose, unbuckled trench just looks like a coat. Belted = intentional.

**Collar:** Should lay flat when turned down or fold neatly when turned up. Poor-quality coats have floppy collars that won't hold shape.
