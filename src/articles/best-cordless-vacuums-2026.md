## Buying Guide

### Key Metrics Explained

**Suction (Air Watts / Watts):**
- Air Watts (AW) measures actual suction at the cleaning head: the most meaningful measure
- "Watts" or "W" describes motor input power — not directly comparable to AW
- 150+ AW: Strong, handles carpet and embedded debris
- 200+ AW: Exceptional, handles even medium-pile and high-pile carpet

**Runtime:**
- Manufacturers cite runtime at minimum suction settings
- Realistic runtime at medium settings: 50–70% of maximum rating
- Large homes (2,000+ sq ft) need 45+ minutes at medium settings

**Weight:**
- Under 6 lbs: Easy overhead cleaning and extended use
- 6–9 lbs: Manageable for most users
- Over 9 lbs: Noticeable during extended use; fine for quick cleans

### Corded vs. Cordless in 2026

For most homes, cordless is now the right primary vacuum. The only scenarios where corded maintains an advantage:
1. Deep carpet cleaning (daily heavy use) where runtime is always a constraint
2. Very large homes over 4,000 sq ft where battery anxiety is real
3. Budget below $200 — corded quality at that price point is still superior

### Battery and Charging

Most premium cordless vacuums use removable batteries — you can buy a spare and swap mid-cleaning. Budget models typically have built-in batteries requiring the whole unit to charge.

Charging time: 2.5–4 hours for a full charge from empty on most models. Fast-charge accessories exist for some brands (Dyson, Samsung).

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Can a cordless vacuum replace a full-size upright?**
For most modern homes, yes. The Dyson V15 Detect and Samsung Bespoke Jet AI outperform most corded uprights under $500. The remaining advantage of corded vacuums is unlimited runtime — relevant only for very large homes or commercial use.

**How long do cordless vacuum batteries last?**
Battery cells typically last 3–5 years (200–300 full charge cycles) before noticeable capacity degradation. Replacement batteries cost $60–$120 for most models. Factor battery replacement cost into the long-term cost calculation.

**Are cordless vacuums good for thick carpet?**
With high suction (200+ AW): yes. The Dyson V15 Detect handles medium and high-pile carpet effectively. Budget cordless vacuums (under $200) struggle with thick carpet — their suction isn't sufficient to lift embedded debris.

**What's the best cordless vacuum for pet hair?**
The Dyson V15 Detect handles pet hair better than any competitor. For a dedicated pet-hair-focused option at lower cost, the Shark Stratos IX141 with its anti-tangle brush roll is excellent.
