## FAQs

**How long do cordless vacuum batteries last?**
Modern lithium-ion batteries last 3–5 years with regular use. Both Dyson and Shark offer replacement batteries for $50–80.

**Is Dyson worth the premium over Shark?**
On deep-pile carpet and fine dust detection, yes. For most homes with mixed flooring and pet hair, the Shark Stratos closes the gap significantly.

**Can cordless vacuums replace uprights?**
For homes under 2,500 sq ft, yes — especially with self-emptying bases becoming available for cordless models. For larger homes, a corded upright as a backup is worth having.

**What's the best cordless vacuum for pet hair?**
The Dyson V15 Detect and Shark Stratos IZ862H are tied for pet hair — both use anti-wrap technology that prevents tangled bristles. The Dyson V15's laser also reveals pet dander on hard floors.
