## What to Know Before You Buy

**Confirm your Instant Pot size:** Most accessories are sized for 6-qt (the most common) but many are also compatible with 8-qt. The 3-qt Mini uses different sizes — check the product listing for your specific model.

**Official vs. third-party:** For inner pots and sealing rings, official Instant Pot brand accessories guarantee fit and pressure ratings. For trivets, steamers, and baking accessories, third-party options often perform equally well at lower prices.

**Sealing ring odors:** The silicone sealing ring that came with your Instant Pot absorbs cooking odors over time. Buying a two-pack and dedicating one ring to savory cooking and one to sweet/dessert cooking is the best solution. Official Instant Pot replacement rings are sold in 3-packs.

**Pot-in-pot cooking:** A trivet + springform pan or a trivet + a heat-safe bowl allows you to cook two items simultaneously in one Instant Pot — one in the main pot, one elevated above it. A silicone trivet with handles is essential for this technique.
