## Portable Speaker Buying Guide

### Waterproof Rating Explained

- **IPX4:** Splash-resistant — survives sweat, light rain, and accidental splashes. Not pool-safe.
- **IPX5:** Water jet resistant — survives heavy rain and direct water streams.
- **IP67:** Submersion-proof — can be dunked in 1 meter of water for 30 minutes. Pool and beach safe.
- **IP68:** Deep submersion — can survive deeper dives (varies by manufacturer spec).

For beach, pool, and kayaking use: get IP67 minimum. For hiking and casual outdoor use, IPX5 is adequate.

### Battery Life in Practice

Manufacturer battery claims are typically measured at 50–60% volume. Outdoor use at higher volumes will reduce battery life by 30–50%. A "20-hour" speaker at full outdoor volume may run 10–12 hours.

### Stereo Pairing

Many speakers support stereo pairing — buy two identical speakers and pair them for true left/right separation. This dramatically improves the music experience in larger spaces. JBL's PartyBoost and Bose's SimpleSync are the most seamless implementations.

### What About Sound Quality at the Beach?

Low-frequency sound (bass) dissipates quickly in open outdoor environments. At the beach or in a park, you need a speaker with strong bass output — passive radiators (JBL Charge 5, Bose SoundLink Flex) help a lot. A speaker that sounds good indoors can sound thin outdoors.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Which portable Bluetooth speaker has the best battery life?**
UE Hyperboom (24 hours) and Anker Soundcore 2 (24 hours) lead in battery life. JBL Charge 5 (20 hours) is the best balance of battery and overall quality.

**Is IP67 necessary for a beach speaker?**
Yes, if your speaker will be near water. IP67 allows up to 30 minutes of submersion in 1 meter of water — essential for pool decks and beaches where splashing is inevitable. IPX4 or IPX5 speakers can be damaged by a direct dunking.

**Can I pair two different Bluetooth speakers?**
Only if they support the same pairing protocol. JBL's PartyBoost works with other JBL PartyBoost speakers. Bose SimpleSync works with Bose speakers only. You cannot mix brands for stereo pairing.

**How far can I be from my speaker via Bluetooth?**
Most modern speakers use Bluetooth 5.0 or 5.1 with a rated range of 33–100 ft. In practice, expect 30–50 ft through walls and 100 ft in open space. Walls, electronics, and other interference reduce range.

**Do portable speakers work with both Android and iPhone?**
Yes — all Bluetooth speakers are device-agnostic. They pair with any Bluetooth-capable device regardless of operating system.
