The best webcams for work from home under $100 are genuinely excellent, and a few are under $50. ## What to Look For in a Home Office Webcam Under $100

**Resolution:** 1080p is the target. At this price, 720p is outdated and 4K isn't available — but 1080p at 30fps is the sweet spot for video calls and looks genuinely sharp.

**Low-light performance:** This matters more than resolution. Most home offices aren't perfectly lit, and a webcam that produces muddy, dark video in average indoor light is useless regardless of its spec sheet. Look for faster apertures (f/2.0 or better) and larger sensors.

**Autofocus:** Continuous autofocus keeps you sharp when you lean forward or shift position. Fixed-focus webcams are cheaper but softer at close distances.

**Privacy shutter:** A physical lens cover you can close between calls. Increasingly standard even at budget prices.
