## Buying Guide: Tips for Gifting Under $50

**The $50 rule:** At this budget, practical beats decorative. Things people use daily (tumbler, sleep mask, silk pillowcase) outperform things people display occasionally.

**Consumable vs. durable:**
- **Consumable** (candles, food, soaps): Feel generous, get used up, need replacing — great if you'll see the person regularly
- **Durable** (tumbler, speaker, blanket): Last for years — better for distant friends or acquaintances you won't see often

**Personalization wins at $50:** A $30 item monogrammed adds $5-10 in cost and doubles the perceived thoughtfulness. Many items offer laser engraving through Amazon.

**Best categories at under $50:**
1. Insulated drinkware
2. Sleep accessories (mask, silk pillowcase)
3. Portable audio
4. Skincare (serums, masks, tools)
5. Kitchen essentials they'd never upgrade themselves
6. Card and board games
