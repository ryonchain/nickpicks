## What NOT to Gift College Students

- **Decorative items:** Students have limited space and specific taste
- **Clothing in specific sizes:** Impossible to get right without knowing their exact preferences
- **Food items that need refrigeration:** Unless they have a full-size fridge (most dorm fridges are tiny)
- **Electronics that don't fit their ecosystem:** An Apple accessory for an Android user (or vice versa) will frustrate them
