## The Caffeine + L-Theanine Stack: Why It Works

The caffeine + L-theanine combination is one of the most well-studied cognitive enhancement pairings in nutritional research. Here's why the combination outperforms caffeine alone:

**Caffeine alone:**
- Increases alertness and reaction time
- Can cause jitteriness, anxiety, elevated heart rate
- Often followed by a crash as it wears off

**L-theanine alone:**
- Promotes alpha brain waves (relaxed alertness)
- Reduces anxiety without sedation
- Mild, subtle effect on its own

**Combined:**
- Enhanced focus and attention compared to caffeine alone
- Significantly reduced caffeine-associated anxiety and jitteriness
- No crash — the theanine buffers the cortisol spike that causes post-caffeine fatigue
- Multiple studies show improved accuracy on attention tasks versus caffeine-only

**The protocol:**
- 100–200mg caffeine (1 cup of coffee = ~95mg)
- 200–400mg L-theanine (2:1 ratio to caffeine)
- Take together before cognitively demanding work

## L-Theanine for Sleep

For sleep support, take 200–400mg L-theanine 30–60 minutes before bed:
- Does not cause next-day grogginess (unlike melatonin at high doses)
- Reduces racing thoughts and anxiety-driven sleep onset difficulty
- Improves sleep quality (measured by polysomnography in studies)
- Not sedating — it doesn't force sleep, it reduces the mental activation that prevents sleep

L-theanine for sleep is complementary to (not a replacement for) sleep hygiene basics: consistent sleep timing, dark and cool room, no screens before bed. But as a supplement, it's one of the safest and most evidence-backed options for people who lie in bed with active minds.
