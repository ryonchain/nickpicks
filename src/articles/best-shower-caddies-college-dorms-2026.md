## What to Look For in a Dorm Shower Caddy

**Drainability:** A caddy that holds water grows mold fast. Look for perforated bottoms, open mesh construction, or drilled holes. Never buy a solid-bottom caddy.

**Handle design:** You'll carry this every day with a towel and flip-flops in hand. A wide, ergonomic handle matters more than it sounds. Test whether you can carry it single-handed without gripping the handle tightly.

**Rust resistance:** Steel mesh caddies rust when the coating chips. Plastic and bamboo don't. For 4-year durability, plastic or bamboo beats cheap chrome steel.

**Size:** Match your routine. Bring only what you actually use — oversized caddies with half-empty pockets are annoying to carry and hard to keep organized.
