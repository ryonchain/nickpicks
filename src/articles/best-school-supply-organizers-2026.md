## Homework Station Location Tips

- **Dedicated space:** A consistent homework spot trains the brain that this location = study time
- **Face away from TV:** Visual distractions cut focus significantly for elementary students
- **Natural light or quality lamp:** Insufficient light causes eyestrain and shortened study sessions
- **Snack access nearby:** Short snack breaks outperform powering through hunger for most kids
