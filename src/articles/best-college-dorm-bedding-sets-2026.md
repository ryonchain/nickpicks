## What to Know Before Buying Dorm Bedding

### Why Twin XL Matters
Twin XL: 39" × 80". Standard Twin: 39" × 75". Five inches longer. Fitted sheets must match. Do not guess — check your school's housing FAQ or call housing services if unsure.

### Deep Pocket Sheets Are Essential
Dorm mattresses with a mattress topper can be 10–15" thick. Sheets with 9" standard pockets will pop off the corners every night. Buy sheets specifically labeled **14" deep pocket** or **16" deep pocket**.

### Warmth Rating Matters
Dorm room temperatures vary significantly — some schools heat aggressively, others under-heat. Check reviews from students at your specific school if possible. When unsure, choose medium warmth and add a lightweight throw blanket for cold nights.

### How Many Sets to Buy
**Two complete sets minimum.** College laundry cadence means your sheets may sit in a laundry bag for 3–5 days before you get to them. Having a backup set prevents sleeping on a bare mattress.

### Machine Washable Is Non-Negotiable
Do not buy dry-clean-only bedding for a college dorm. It will get dirty, and sending it out for cleaning is not sustainable for 4 years. Verify machine washable before purchasing.
