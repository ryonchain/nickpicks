Black Friday 2026 (November 28) is the single biggest TV sales event of the year. Retailers sell more TVs between Black Friday and Cyber Monday than in any other 5-day stretch — and they price accordingly. If a new TV is on your list, this is the guide to bookmark.

## When Do Black Friday TV Deals Start in 2026?

**Black Friday is November 28, 2026**, but TV deals start much earlier:

| Date | What Happens |
|------|--------------|
| Early November (Nov 1–14) | Amazon, Best Buy post "early Black Friday" deals |
| Mid-November (Nov 15–21) | Major drops; most "Black Friday" prices live |
| Thanksgiving Day (Nov 26) | Full deal activation; first wave of doorbusters |
| Black Friday (Nov 28) | Peak deals; doorbuster quantities, in-store exclusives |
| Weekend (Nov 29–30) | Prices hold; restocking of popular models |
| Cyber Monday (Dec 1) | Final wave; online-exclusive deals, some prices extend |

**The best strategy:** Don't wait until November 28. Mid-November prices are often identical to Black Friday prices — without the inventory pressure.

## Black Friday TV Buying Tips

### 1. Know What to Actually Compare

Don't compare TVs by specs alone. The only metrics that matter:
- **Panel type:** OLED vs. mini-LED vs. standard LED/LCD
- **Brightness:** Higher is better for bright rooms (500+ nits for living room; 200+ for bedrooms)
- **Refresh rate:** 60Hz fine for TV/movies; 120Hz+ if you game
- **HDMI version:** HDMI 2.1 required for PS5/Xbox 4K 120Hz

### 2. Avoid the Black Friday TV Traps

- **Doorbuster models** — Retailers sometimes create "BF-exclusive" model numbers with downgraded specs. Verify the exact model number against reviews.
- **Inflated "was" prices** — Check CamelCamelCamel for actual Amazon price history before trusting any "X% off" label.
- **Last year's models** — A "50% off" last year's TV may be same price as current model at regular price.

### 3. Set a Target Price

Set up price tracking at:
- **CamelCamelCamel.com** for Amazon TV price history
- **Google Shopping** for cross-retailer alerts
- **Honey** browser extension for automatic deal alerts

### 4. Buy During "Pre-Black Friday" If Possible

Prices in mid-November (Nov 14–21) are usually identical to Black Friday prices. Shopping early means better stock and guaranteed delivery before the holiday.

## OLED vs. Mini-LED for Black Friday 2026

**Buy OLED if:**
- Budget: $799+ (55") or $999+ (65")
- Watching in a dark or dim room
- Watching a lot of movies and TV
- You want the best picture quality money can buy

**Buy Mini-LED if:**
- Budget: $399–$799
- Watching in a bright room (sunlight, overhead lighting)
- Serious gaming (mini-LED can sustain brightness better)
- You don't want to worry about burn-in
