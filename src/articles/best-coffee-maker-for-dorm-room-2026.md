## How to Check Your Dorm's Coffee Policy

Before you buy anything with a heating element, check your school's housing policy. Search for "[Your School] housing appliance policy" or ask your RA. Most schools categorize appliances as:

- **Allowed:** Coffee makers with auto-shutoff and no exposed heating elements (Keurig K-Mini, Nespresso)
- **Check:** Drip coffee makers with hot plates
- **Prohibited:** Open-coil hot plates, some older-style coffee makers

The Keurig K-Mini and AeroPress are universally safe bets. When in doubt, go with a pod machine or manual method.
