## Buying Guide: How to Choose a Gaming Keyboard

### Switch Type Matters More Than Anything

**Linear switches** (Red, Speed Silver) — smooth, consistent, quiet. Best for fast gaming.
**Tactile switches** (Brown, Tactile) — bump without click. Good middle ground for gaming + typing.
**Clicky switches** (Blue, Green) — audible click. Best for typing, annoying in multiplayer.

### Form Factor Decisions

- **Full-size:** Every key available. Best for MMO, office crossover.
- **TKL (Tenkeyless):** Removes numpad. More mouse room for FPS players.
- **75%:** Keeps arrows and navigation cluster in a compact layout.
- **60%:** Minimum viable gaming keyboard. Maximum mouse space.

### Wired vs. Wireless

Competitive players prefer wired to eliminate any latency risk. For casual gaming and desktop use, modern Bluetooth 5.1 boards like the Keychron K2 V2 introduce no perceptible delay.
