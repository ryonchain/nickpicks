## Key Anti-Aging Ingredients to Look For

**Retinol/Retinoids:** Most proven ingredient for reducing fine lines. Prescription retinoids are most potent; OTC retinol is gentler.

**Peptides:** Signal proteins that stimulate collagen production. Look for copper peptides and palmitoyl pentapeptide-4.

**Hyaluronic Acid:** Humectant that pulls moisture into skin. Best applied to damp skin.

**Vitamin C:** Antioxidant that brightens and protects against UV damage. L-ascorbic acid is most effective.

**Ceramides:** Lipids that rebuild and maintain the skin's moisture barrier. Critical for dry/mature skin.

**Niacinamide:** Reduces redness, minimizes pores, and brightens skin tone. Highly compatible with most other ingredients.
