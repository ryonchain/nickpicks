## Gaming Chair Buying Guide

**The most important features, ranked:**
1. Adjustable lumbar support (integrated > pillow)
2. 4D armrests (height, angle, depth, pivot)
3. Seat depth adjustment (supports neutral knee position)
4. Cold-foam seat core (maintains shape over years)
5. Recline with lockable positions

**Height and weight guide:**
- Under 5'7" / 180 lbs: Any regular-size chair; consider smaller seat depth
- 5'7"–6'2" / up to 250 lbs: Standard size gaming chair (most options)
- 6'2"–6'7" / up to 390 lbs: Secretlab TITAN XL or similar XL options
- Over 6'7" or 390 lbs: Contact manufacturers directly for specialty options
