## How to Gift Tech Wisely

**Consider ecosystem compatibility:** AirTag works best with iPhone. Echo integrates with Alexa households. Check before buying.

**Check return windows:** Amazon's holiday return window extends into January for most gifts purchased after November. Buy with confidence.

**Pair with gift receipts:** Tech recipients often want the exact right color or spec. Always include a gift receipt.

**Bundle for impact:** A power bank + cable management kit + LED strip lights looks more impressive together than any single item. Keep an eye on total cost.
