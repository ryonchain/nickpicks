## New Year Sale Tips

1. **Don't buy fitness equipment on January 1** — demand is at its peak and some items actually increase in price as inventory tightens. Watch for January 7–15 when the first wave of New Year's resolutioners has already ordered and stock normalizes.
2. **Gym membership first month is negotiable** — walk into almost any gym in January and ask for a better deal. They're hungry for signups.
3. **Book travel early in January, not late** — the best January flight deals appear in the first two weeks; mid-January onward reverts to normal pricing as demand firms up for spring.
4. **CES week (Jan 5–10) is the best time to buy last-year's TV** — retailers know the new models are coming and start clearing inventory even before the new models arrive.
5. **The Container Store Elfa sale is a real deal** — the only sale they run all year. If you want closet systems, this is the window.
