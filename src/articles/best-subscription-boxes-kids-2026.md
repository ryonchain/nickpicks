## How to Choose a Kids' Subscription Box

**Consider the child's interests:**
- STEM/science-focused kid → KiwiCo or MEL Science
- Book lover → Bookroo or Literati
- Creative/artistic → Wonder Crate
- Curious about the world → Little Passports

**Age matters:** Most boxes specify age ranges. Going younger skews frustrating; going older skews overwhelming. Stick close to the recommended age.

**Engagement longevity:** Projects that produce a keepable result (a marble run, a painting, a book) tend to generate more lasting excitement than consumable activities.

**Budget note:** $20–$30/month is the going rate for quality kids' subscription boxes. That's $240–$360/year — comparable to a video game console game. Compare to screen time alternatives.
