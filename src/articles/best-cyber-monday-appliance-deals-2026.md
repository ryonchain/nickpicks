## Cyber Monday Appliance Buying Tips

### 1. Buy the Matching Set

Matching washer + dryer sets often come with bundle discounts ($100–$300 off) that aren't available buying separately. Ask about bundle pricing at any retailer.

### 2. Confirm Haul-Away

Most retailers include **free haul-away of your old appliance** with new appliance delivery. Confirm this before ordering — it saves a separate disposal fee.

### 3. Check Delivery Timelines

Major appliances require scheduled delivery (not same-day). Expect 7–21 day wait times during Cyber Week. If you need installation done by the holidays, order during the first week of December.

### 4. Verify Utility Compatibility

- **Washer:** Most modern washers require 240V electric outlet. Confirm your laundry room has the right hookup.
- **Dryer:** Gas vs. electric is a permanent choice based on your home's connections.
- **Dishwasher:** Standard 24" fits most cabinets; confirm measurement before buying.
- **Range:** Gas vs. electric vs. induction depends on your kitchen hookups.
