## Black Friday Beauty Shopping Strategy

**1. Build your wishlist in October.** Note current prices before deals start. Amazon's "tracked price history" browser extensions (Camelcamelcamel, Keepa) will tell you if a "Black Friday deal" is actually just MSRP relabeled.

**2. Act on gift sets fast.** Beauty gift sets sell out within hours of going live — add to cart immediately and check out before inventory is gone.

**3. Don't wait for Cyber Monday.** In the beauty category, most of the best deals are on Black Friday itself. Cyber Monday restocks are rare for gift sets and holiday bundles.

**4. Amazon Prime members get early access.** Prime subscribers often get a 30-minute early window on Lightning Deals. If beauty is a priority, Prime membership pays for itself on a single Dyson purchase.
