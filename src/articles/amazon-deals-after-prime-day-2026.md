# Amazon Deals After Prime Day 2026 — What's Still Worth Buying

Prime Day is over. But not all the deals are.

Amazon consistently runs a "soft tail" of discounts for 1–2 weeks after Prime Day ends. Sellers who didn't move inventory during the 48-hour window often keep prices reduced rather than pay restocking fees. And some categories — especially tech and home goods — see their lowest prices of the year linger through late July.

Here's what's still worth buying after Prime Day 2026, and what to hold until Black Friday.

---

## Why Deals Persist After Prime Day

Three reasons prices stay low after the event:

1. **Seller clearance**: Third-party sellers who didn't sell through their Prime Day inventory keep prices at deal-level to avoid returning stock to Amazon warehouses (FBA storage fees rise in Q4).

2. **Amazon's own follow-through**: Amazon often extends Amazon device discounts for 1–2 weeks post-event. Echo, Kindle, and Fire TV deals frequently persist.

3. **Back-to-school overlap**: Retailers know parents and students are price-sensitive in late July and August. Laptop, tablet, and school-supply deals often extend into the back-to-school push.

## What Reverts Immediately After Prime Day

Not everything lingers. These categories typically snap back to normal pricing within 24–48 hours of the event ending:

- **Apple products** (MacBook, AirPods, iPad): Apple enforces MAP pricing aggressively. Deals disappear fast.
- **Nintendo products**: Nintendo products rarely discount; Prime Day listings are from third-party sellers who snap back.
- **Major TV brands at sale-price tiers**: 75-inch+ Samsung and LG TVs at big discounts typically sell through and revert.
- **Fashion and clothing**: Cleared immediately.

## Should You Wait for Black Friday Instead?

**Black Friday wins** on:
- Toys and games (deepest discounts of the year in November)
- Major TVs (65-inch+ OLED, QLED)
- Gaming consoles and accessories
- Clothing, shoes, and fashion

**Prime Day wins** (or ties) on:
- Amazon devices (Amazon's own hardware)
- Robot vacuums
- Headphones and earbuds
- Air purifiers and home comfort

If you missed a Prime Day deal on Amazon devices or headphones, don't wait until November — the deals won't be meaningfully better and you'll have missed 4 months of use.
