Electronics are the top Christmas gift category by dollar volume — and December is when retailers run their deepest cuts on laptops, tablets, headphones, and smartwatches. Here's a complete breakdown of expected Christmas electronics deals for 2026.

## Christmas Electronics Deal Timeline

| Window | What to Expect |
|--------|---------------|
| Black Friday (Nov 28) | Deepest cuts on laptops, TVs, tablets |
| Cyber Monday (Dec 1) | Extended BF pricing; online-exclusive bundles |
| Dec 2–14 | Second wave — refurb prices drop; open-box deals appear |
| Dec 15–18 | "Last-chance" deals with guaranteed Prime delivery |
| Dec 19–24 | Flash deals; gift cards and digital products spike |
| Dec 26+ | After-Christmas clearance — open-box and refurb surge |

**Bottom line:** The best Christmas electronics prices happen at Black Friday and Cyber Monday, not the week before Christmas. Late shoppers pay a premium for remaining stock.

**Buy these before December 15:**
- MacBook Air / iPad — sells out in the discount configuration
