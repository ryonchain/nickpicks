## What to Skip

**$50–$80 no-name soundbars** — These appear every Prime Day with huge "discount" percentages. Sound quality is typically worse than a $150 Vizio. The Vizio V-series is the absolute minimum worth buying.

**Soundbars without eARC** — If your TV has eARC and your soundbar doesn't, you can't pass lossless Dolby Atmos. Any soundbar purchase for a 2022+ TV should have HDMI eARC.

**Single Amazon Echo devices for TV use** — Echo devices aren't designed for TV audio and lack HDMI connectivity. Use an Echo Studio paired as a Bluetooth device at most.

**Bose SoundLink Flex** — Good speaker, but the JBL Charge 5 outperforms it at a lower Prime Day price. The Flex's main advantage (360-degree sound at the beach) rarely justifies the price difference.
