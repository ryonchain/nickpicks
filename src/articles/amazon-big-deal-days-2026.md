## What Are Amazon Big Deal Days?

Amazon Big Deal Days is the company's fall shopping event — a two-day Prime member sale in October, roughly equivalent to a second Prime Day. Amazon launched the event in 2022 and has run it every year since, growing it into one of the largest fall shopping events alongside Target Circle Week and Walmart Deals.

**2025 Big Deal Days dates:** October 8–9, 2025
**Expected 2026 dates:** Mid-October 2026 (Amazon typically announces 2–3 weeks in advance)

The event is exclusive to Amazon Prime members. Non-members can sign up for a free 30-day trial before the event to access deals.

---

## Big Deal Days vs. Prime Day: What's the Difference?

Prime Day (July) and Big Deal Days (October) are both 48-hour Prime exclusive events, but they serve different shopping intents:

| | Prime Day (July) | Big Deal Days (October) |
|--|-----------------|------------------------|
| **Timing** | Summer (July) | Fall (October) |
| **Best for** | Tech, home appliances, summer gear | Holiday pre-shopping, seasonal home, fall fashion |
| **Typical discounts** | 20–50% off | 20–45% off |
| **Deal volume** | Largest Amazon sale event | Comparable but slightly smaller |
| **Holiday prep** | Early bird buyers | Primary holiday pre-shoppers |

The October timing makes Big Deal Days the prime window for early holiday shopping — especially for electronics, toys, and home items you'd otherwise buy in November at similar or worse prices.

## Strategy: How to Get the Best Deals

### 1. Make Your List Now (Before October)
Amazon uses deal anticipation to drive wish-listing behavior. Add items to your Wish List or cart now — Amazon notifies you when prices drop. The Best Time to Buy principle: know what you want before the event, don't impulse buy.

### 2. Track Prices with CamelCamelCamel
Many "Big Deal Days" discounts show prices that weren't actually lower than average Amazon pricing. CamelCamelCamel.com tracks price history for any Amazon product. Before buying anything in October, check if the deal is real.

### 3. Compare with Target and Walmart
Target Circle Week and Walmart Deals typically run simultaneously with Amazon Big Deal Days. Use Google Shopping to quickly compare prices across retailers before buying.

### 4. Check Lightning Deals Early
Lightning Deals are time-limited with limited quantities. They sell out. Amazon's app sends push notifications for upcoming Lightning Deals — turn these on 24 hours before the event.

### 5. Don't Skip the "Deals of the Day"
Deals of the Day run for 24 hours and often offer deeper discounts than the featured Lightning Deals. These are less publicized but worth checking.
