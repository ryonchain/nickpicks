## Buying Guide: How to Choose the Right Portable Projector

### Brightness: ANSI Lumens vs Lux vs LED Lumens

Many cheap projectors advertise in "LED lumens" or "display lumens" — numbers 5–10× higher than ANSI lumens (the standardized measurement). Always compare by ANSI lumens:

- **200–400 ANSI:** Requires dark room (ideal for bedrooms, camping, travel)
- **400–700 ANSI:** Works in controlled ambient light (curtains drawn)
- **700–2,000 ANSI:** Handles living rooms with lights on

### Resolution Reality

True 1080p (1920×1080 native) is the minimum for content that originated as HD video. Budget projectors claim "1080p support" while using a 854×480 native panel — always check native resolution, not "supported" resolution.

### Battery vs Plug-In

Battery projectors top out around 600–700 ANSI lumens and 4 hours per charge. For permanent or semi-permanent installations, AC-powered projectors offer 2–3× more brightness at the same price point.

### Throw Ratio

Throw ratio (TR) determines how far the projector sits from the screen to produce a given image size: image width = throw distance ÷ TR. A TR of 1.2:1 produces a 100" image from 10 feet away. Short-throw (0.5:1 or less) works in small rooms; standard throw (1.2–2.0:1) requires adequate room depth.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Can I use a portable projector in daylight?**
Only with 700+ ANSI lumen models, and only if you have shade control. Full direct sunlight washes out even 2,000 ANSI lumen projectors. The BenQ GP20 (600 ANSI) handles twilight outdoor conditions well.

**What surface can I project on?**
White or light gray walls work well. Textured surfaces (brick, stucco) reduce sharpness. Dedicated projection screens improve contrast by 2–3× vs painted walls. The best portable projectors include auto-keystone that works on angled surfaces.

**How long do LED projector bulbs last?**
Most LED light sources are rated 20,000–30,000 hours. At 2 hours/day, that's 27–41 years of use. Lamp projectors are rated 2,000–5,000 hours. Laser projectors (like the LG CineBeam) are rated 25,000+ hours. For a portable used occasionally, LED lifespan is essentially unlimited.

**Do portable projectors work with Netflix?**
Projectors with Android TV or Tizen OS can install Netflix directly. The Anker Nebula Capsule 3, XGIMI MoGo 3 Pro, and BenQ GP20 all support Netflix via Android TV. Some Android TV implementations require sideloading Netflix due to device certification requirements.
