# Best Walkie Talkies in 2026

Walkie talkies split into two license categories: FRS (Family Radio Service — no license required) and GMRS (General Mobile Radio Service — requires a $35 FCC license, covers your whole family). FRS is limited to 2W power; GMRS allows up to 50W. For casual family use, FRS is sufficient. For backcountry hiking or long-range communication, GMRS delivers better range and clarity.
