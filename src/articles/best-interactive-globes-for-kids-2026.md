### App-Required vs. Self-Contained Globes

**App-required** (Orboot): Provides the most engaging AR experience but needs a compatible smartphone or tablet. Technology evolves, so app support over 5+ years isn't guaranteed.

**Self-contained** (LeapFrog, National Geographic, GeoSafari): Works immediately with no device required, more reliable long-term, but less visually impressive. Best for young children who shouldn't use smartphones independently.

### What to Look For in a Kids Globe

**Content breadth:** Countries, capitals, populations, cultures, animals, and landmarks — look for a globe that covers multiple dimensions beyond just country names and capitals.

**Replay value:** 20+ activity modes (LeapFrog) or 400+ AR illustrations (Orboot) beat single-function globes for long-term engagement.

**Age-appropriate interaction:** Young children (under 6) need large targets, simple probes, and audio confirmation. Older kids can manage AR apps and touchscreens.
