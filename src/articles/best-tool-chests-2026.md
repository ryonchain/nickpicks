## What to Look for in a Tool Chest

**Drawer slides:** Ball-bearing (smooth, durable) vs. stamped metal slides (binds under load, fails early). This is the single most important quality indicator.

**Steel gauge:** 18–21 gauge is standard for consumer chests. 16 gauge is better. Thinner steel = more dent prone.

**Powder coat:** Protects against rust in humid garages. Look for "powder-coated" not just "painted."

**Caster size:** 4"–5" casters with locking mechanisms. Small casters get stuck on garage threshold transitions.

**Drawer organization:** Removable felt/rubber liners, or buy after-market drawer organizers (magnetic bit holders, socket organizers).
