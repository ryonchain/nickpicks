## What NOT to Buy on Black Friday

Some categories have fake "discounts" that are really just inflated original prices:

- **Jewelry** — Markups are enormous; the "70% off" is from a fictional MSRP
- **Kitchen sets** — Multi-piece knife block sets are often low quality
- **Clothing** — Sizes are picked over; most fashion sales are year-round
- **Generic/off-brand electronics** — Unknown brands inflating prices pre-Black Friday
- **Mattresses** — Major mattress sales actually happen on Memorial Day and Labor Day
