## Body Composition Tracking Tips

**Measure consistently:** Step on the scale at the same time each morning, before eating or drinking, after using the bathroom. Consistency matters more than perfection.

**Track weekly averages:** Daily weight fluctuates by 2–5 lbs from water retention, food volume, and hormonal cycles. Weekly averages cut through the noise.

**Body fat % trends matter more than the number:** Smart scale body fat estimates vary by ±3–5%. Focus on whether the trend is moving in the right direction over 4–8 weeks, not whether the absolute number matches a DEXA scan.

**Muscle and fat can shift without weight change:** One of the most valuable things smart scales reveal is body recomposition — when you're simultaneously losing fat and gaining muscle, the scale weight may stay flat while your composition improves dramatically.
