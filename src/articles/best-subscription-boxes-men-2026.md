## How to Gift a Men's Subscription Box

**Best approach:** Buy a 3-month gift subscription, not just 1 month. One box is a nice surprise. Three months turns it into an anticipation-building habit — and if he loves it, he'll keep it going.

**Tips:**
- Most boxes let you preview what's in next month's box before it ships — useful for setting expectations
- Look for first-box discounts (usually 20–50% off the first shipment)
- Many boxes allow skipping months — better for guys who travel
- Double-check that physical products can be sent to the recipient's address before gifting

**Best gifts by interest:**
- **Foodie:** Atlas Coffee or SnackCrate
- **Outdoorsy/adventurous:** Bespoke Post
- **Grooming-focused:** Dollar Shave Club or Manscaped
- **Music lover:** Vinyl Me, Please
- **Gamer:** Loot Crate
