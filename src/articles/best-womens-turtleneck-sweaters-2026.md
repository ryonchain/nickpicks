## Women's Turtleneck Sweater Buying Guide

**Full turtleneck vs. mock neck:**
- **Full turtleneck:** Classic, high collar that folds over. Maximum neck warmth. More formal.
- **Mock neck/turtleneck:** Shorter fold, doesn't double over. More casual, easier to wear with layering.
- **Cowl neck:** Draped, loose collar. More fashion-forward.

**Fiber guide for turtlenecks:**
- **Cashmere:** Softest, warmest for weight, most luxurious. Requires careful washing.
- **Merino wool:** Warm, breathable, odor-resistant. Excellent for office and travel.
- **Cotton-wool blend:** Slightly scratchier but durable. Machine washable in most cases.
- **Acrylic blend:** Machine washable, affordable, less breathable. Budget option.

**Best outfit formulas with a turtleneck:**
- **Black turtleneck + wide-leg trousers + loafers** — Parisian chic
- **Camel turtleneck + midi skirt + ankle boots** — classic fall
- **Cream turtleneck + blazer + straight-leg jeans** — polished casual
- **Chunky turtleneck + leather pants + knee boots** — editorial fall
- **Fitted turtleneck + high-waist skirt + tights** — retro feminine

**Care for turtlenecks:**
- Cashmere: hand wash or machine delicate in cold, lay flat to dry
- Merino: machine on wool cycle, reshape and lay flat
- Store folded, not on a hanger (knit shoulders stretch)
- Use a cashmere comb to remove pilling
