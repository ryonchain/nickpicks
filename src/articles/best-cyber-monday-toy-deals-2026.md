Cyber Monday 2026 (**December 1**) is the last major toy sale before Christmas. With only 24 shopping days left until December 25, parents use Cyber Monday to lock in deals on hot toys before they sell out — and retailers know it. Expect 20–40% off popular LEGO sets, Hasbro action figures, learning toys, board games, and kids' tech.

## When to Shop for Cyber Monday Toy Deals

| Date | What to Expect |
|------|---------------|
| Nov 28 (Black Friday) | LEGO, board games, learning toys go on sale |
| Nov 29–30 | Toy restocking; some prices drop further |
| Dec 1 (Cyber Monday) | Online exclusives; final push for pre-Christmas stock |
| Dec 2–7 (Cyber Week) | Deals extend; urgency increases as stock depletes |
