### How to Hang a Tapestry Without Damaging Walls

**Command strips (recommended):** 3M Command strips hold most lightweight tapestries without damaging paint. Use large strips rated for 4+ lbs at each corner.

**Tension rods:** For tapestries with pocket edges (check the listing), a tension rod in the corner of a room works with no wall contact.

**Curtain rod + brackets with Command strips:** For heavier tapestries, mount a curtain rod with adhesive brackets. Allows the tapestry to hang flat without bunching.

Avoid: thumbtacks (hole damage), tape (paint damage), or binder clips directly into plaster.
