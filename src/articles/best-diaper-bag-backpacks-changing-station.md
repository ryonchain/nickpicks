# Best Diaper Bag Backpacks with Changing Station: Top Picks for New Parents

The diaper bag backpack has replaced the traditional tote for most new parents — and for good reason. Wearing it on your back keeps both hands free for carrying a baby, pushing a stroller, or managing life in general. Add a built-in changing station and you've eliminated the need to carry a separate changing pad on every outing.

We tested and researched the top-rated options on Amazon to bring you the five best diaper bag backpacks with a changing station, covering style, storage, waterproofing, and comfort.

---

## What to Look for in a Diaper Bag Backpack with Changing Station

- **Changing pad** — Padded, wipeable, and at least 24" long to fit newborns. Should fold away cleanly when not in use.
- **Organization** — Multiple pockets for bottles, wipes, diapers, pacifiers, and your own belongings.
- **Waterproofing** — You will get things wet. Look for water-resistant exterior fabric.
- **Stroller straps** — Clips onto your stroller when you don't want to wear it.
- **Comfort** — Padded shoulder straps and a back panel make a difference when the bag is fully loaded (it will be).
- **Capacity** — At least 30L to comfortably fit newborn-to-toddler supplies.
