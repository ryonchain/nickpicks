## Fall Dress Styling Guide

**Sweater dress + knee-high boots + belt:** The classic fall uniform. Add a long cardigan for extra warmth.

**Midi floral + ankle boots + denim jacket:** Transitional shoulder-season combination — best from September through October.

**Wrap dress + tights + loafers:** Office-ready without trying too hard. Dark tights (70 denier) extend the season deep into November.

**Shirt dress + chunky knit + OTK boots:** Layering a long cardigan or oversized knit over a fitted shirt dress adds warmth and dimension.

---

## What Makes a Good Fall Dress

**Fabric:** Knit, ponte, velvet, or heavier woven fabrics. Avoid lightweight chiffon unless you're adding substantial layers.

**Color:** Burgundy, rust, olive, camel, plum, deep teal, and chocolate brown dominate fall. Black is always appropriate. Avoid bright pastels.

**Length:** Midi (below knee, above ankle) is the most versatile fall length — works with ankle boots, knee-high boots, and can be tights-free in September.

**Sleeves:** Long sleeves extend wearability. Three-quarter sleeves pair well with layering pieces.
