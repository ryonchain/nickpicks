## What Tech NOT to Buy on Prime Day

- **Recent iPhone and iPad Pro releases:** Apple's flagship products rarely see meaningful Prime Day discounts.
- **Gaming consoles (PS5, Xbox Series X):** Console prices are set by manufacturers and don't budge much.
- **Brand-new product launches:** If something was announced in the last 4–6 weeks, expect little to no discount.
