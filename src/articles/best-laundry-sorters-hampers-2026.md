## What Size Do You Need?

**Single person:** A single hamper, 50–80L. Do laundry every 1–2 weeks.

**Couple:** 2-section sorter, 60–100L total. One section darks, one lights.

**Family (4):** 3-section sorter, minimum 72L total (24L per section). Replace more frequently than a single hamper — if clothes overflow, add a second hamper.

---

## Mesh vs. Solid Fabric

**Mesh:** Best for damp workout clothes, damp towels, or gym bags placed inside. Air circulation prevents mildew smell from developing in 2–3 days before laundry day.

**Solid fabric:** Keeps the contents visually hidden (tidier appearance). Better for rooms where you don't want to see the laundry pile. Less ventilation — fine for mostly dry clothes.

**Recommendation for families:** Mesh bags inside a fabric-covered frame gives the best of both — hidden contents, ventilated bags.
