## How to Choose a Luxury Perfume

**Try before you buy:** Whenever possible, test on skin (not paper) and wait 30 minutes for the dry-down before deciding.

**Concentration matters:**
- Parfum (30%+ fragrance): most potent, longest lasting
- EDP (15–20%): standard for luxury, lasts 6–10 hours
- EDT (5–15%): lighter, usually cheaper
- EDC (2–4%): very light, shortest lasting

**Season and occasion:**
- Warm weather: citrus, aquatic, light florals
- Cold weather: orientals, woody, spicy, gourmand
- Office: lighter EDT or clean florals
- Evening: richer EDPs or orientals

**Skin chemistry:** The same perfume smells different on every person. Never buy based on someone else's recommendation without testing on your own skin.
