# Best Kids Bikes in 2026

Kids' bike sizing is determined by wheel size, not the child's age (children vary significantly by height). The key specs: weight (lighter = easier to learn on and more fun to ride), geometry (upright is more stable than aggressive), and brakes (hand brakes develop real bike skills vs. coaster brakes).

**Measure inseam, not just age** — a tall 5-year-old fits a 16" while a small 7-year-old may still be on a 14".

## Training Wheels vs. Balance Bikes

Research and experience consistently show that balance bikes teach cycling faster and better than training wheels. Training wheels prevent children from learning balance — the actual skill. Balance bike users typically transition to pedal bikes (no training wheels needed) in 30 minutes to 1 session.

If your child already has training wheels, the fastest path forward is to remove both training wheels and the pedals, lower the seat so feet touch the ground, and let them scoot until they're comfortable balancing — then reattach pedals.
