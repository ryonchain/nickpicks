## Doorway Pull-Up Bars: Best for Renters

Doorway bars work by bracing against the door frame — no screws, no permanent modification. They install in 30 seconds. The trade-off: they require a standard door frame (24–36" wide), and improper use can damage painted frames or cause wobble with heavy lifters.

## Wall-Mounted Pull-Up Bars: Best Permanent Stations

Wall-mounted bars require drilling into studs but provide rock-solid stability at any weight, for any movement style (kipping, weighted, muscle-up progressions). They don't damage door frames and work for ceiling-height movements impossible in standard doorways.

**[Check price on Amazon](https://www.amazon.com/s?k=rogue+matador+pull+up+system&tag=nickniche-20)**

**Best for:** Homeowners with a garage or basement gym who want permanent, commercial-quality pull-up and dip access.
