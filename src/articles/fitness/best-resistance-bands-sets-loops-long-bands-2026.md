## FAQ

### How many resistance band levels do I need?

A five-band loop set is the standard. Three levels (light/medium/heavy) is sufficient if you have specific goals. For tube bands, stackable systems give you more combinations from fewer physical bands.

### How long do resistance bands last?

Quality latex bands: 1–2 years of daily use. Store in a cool, dark location — UV and heat degrade latex. Fabric bands: 2–4 years. Tube bands with anti-snap cores: 3–5 years.

### Can resistance bands replace dumbbells for a full workout?

Yes for most home workout purposes. Tube bands with handles replicate most dumbbell exercises. Short loops cover lower body activation. Long loops handle rows and pull-ups. The main limitation is progressive overload at very high resistance levels where dumbbells provide cleaner load increases.
