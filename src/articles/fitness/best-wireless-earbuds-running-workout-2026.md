## What to Look for in Running Earbuds

**Secure Fit** — The most important factor. Ear hooks (JLab), ear wings (Beats), grip tips (Jabra), or bone conduction (Shokz) all solve this differently. Try the earbuds' specific fit system before committing if possible.

**IP Rating** — Minimum IPX4 for gym use. IPX5+ for outdoor running in rain. IPX7/IPX8 if you run in all weather or near water.

**Battery Life** — 6 hours minimum. 8+ hours for marathon or long-distance training.

**ANC vs. Open** — ANC + transparency mode for gym (indoor). Open/bone conduction for road/trail safety.

**Connectivity** — Most now offer Bluetooth 5.2+ with stable multipoint connection for switching between phone and music player.
