Pet hair is one of the hardest cleaning challenges — it embeds into carpet fibers, wraps around brush rolls, and floats onto upholstered furniture. Generic vacuums fail fast. This guide focuses on vacuums specifically designed (or proven) to handle pet hair without clogging.

---

## What Makes a Vacuum Good for Pet Hair?

1. **Tangle-free brush roll** — standard bristles wrap with hair; tangle-free designs cut or avoid it
2. **Strong suction** — 20+ AW for carpet pet hair pickup
3. **HEPA filtration** — captures dander (not just hair) down to 0.3 microns
4. **Pet tool attachments** — motorized mini-brush for furniture and stairs
5. **Large bin** — 0.5L minimum; 1L+ preferred for heavy shedders

## Tips for Vacuuming Pet Hair Effectively

1. **Vacuum high-traffic areas 3x per week** — hair accumulates fast
2. **Use a rubber squeegee on carpet first** — lifts embedded hair before vacuuming
3. **Empty the bin every use** — full bins lose 30–50% of suction
4. **Replace filters every 6–12 months** — even "washable" filters degrade
5. **Lint roll furniture before sitting** — prevents hair transferring to clothes
