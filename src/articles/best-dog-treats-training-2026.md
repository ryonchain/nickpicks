## Training Treat Principles

**Calorie budget:** Most treats should be ≤10% of daily caloric intake. A 30 lb dog needs ~700 calories/day — that's 70 calories max in treats. At 3.5 cal each, that's 20 Zuke's per session.

**Hierarchy of value:** For easy known behaviors (sit, down), use lower-value treats. For new behaviors, recall, and high-distraction work, use freeze-dried or highest-value options. Save the best for hardest situations.

**Size:** Pea-sized or smaller. Training rewards are markers, not meals — the dog should eat it in half a second and be ready for the next repetition.
