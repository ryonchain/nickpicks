## What to Look for in an Outdoor Bluetooth Speaker

**Waterproofing:** IP67 is the minimum for serious outdoor use — it guarantees submersion protection, not just splash resistance. Avoid products marketed as "water resistant" without an IP rating.

**Volume vs. quality at high volume:** Many budget speakers distort significantly at 80%+ volume. The JBL and Bose picks on this list maintain clean audio at outdoor volumes. Test at maximum volume before judging indoor sound.

**Battery life math:** Manufacturers rate battery at 50% volume. At outdoor volumes (70–80%), expect 60–70% of rated battery life. A speaker rated at 12 hours will deliver roughly 7–8 hours at true outdoor listening volumes.
