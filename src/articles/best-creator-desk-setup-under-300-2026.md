## What to Skip (and Why)

**External microphone (for now):** The Logitech C920x built-in mics are adequate for TikTok videos under 60 seconds. Add a Blue Snowball iCE (~$50) when you move to YouTube or long-form video.

**4K webcam:** TikTok downsamples video for delivery. A 4K webcam at 4K resolution is not meaningfully better than 1080p in the TikTok player. Save the money.

**RGB everything:** RGB keyboard, RGB mouse, RGB strip lights — each one individually is low-cost. Together they add up fast and often look better in a photo than in actual video content. Light your face first, then light your setup.
