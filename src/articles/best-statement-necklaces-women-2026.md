## What to Avoid

- **Zinc alloy bases** — zinc causes green skin discoloration within a few wears and signals the cheapest possible construction. Look for brass or sterling silver as the base metal.
- **Ultra-thin plating** — under 1 micron of gold plating chips off in weeks with regular wear. Look for "thick plating," "14K gold plated," or "gold filled" in product descriptions.
- **Glued embellishments** — stones and charms that are glued rather than set will fall off with wear or when the necklace snags on clothing.
- **No clasp specification** — quality necklaces specify lobster clasp or toggle clasp. Ambiguous "easy clasp" descriptions often mean spring ring clasps that pop open under tension.
