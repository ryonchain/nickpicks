## Home Gym Dumbbell Guide

**Adjustable vs. fixed:** Fixed dumbbells last forever and can be dropped without damage. Adjustable systems save space and money but have mechanical components that can fail. For most home gym users, adjustable wins.

**Weight progression:** Beginners start at 5–20 lbs for most exercises. Intermediate lifters need 15–50 lbs. Advanced lifters need 50+ lbs for compound movements (rows, deadlifts).

**Flooring:** Rubber dumbbells protect hardwood and laminate floors. If you're buying fixed weights, invest in horse stall mats or rubber gym flooring to protect your floor.

**Grip:** Knurled or contoured handles provide better grip than smooth chrome, especially for sweaty workouts. Hex handles (flat sides) are comfortable for pushups.
