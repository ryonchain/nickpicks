## What to Skip

**Athletic Greens / AG1** — not sold on Amazon at a discount. Their subscription model means no Prime Day pricing.

**Random "fat burner" supplements** — avoid anything with proprietary blends or thermogenic stimulants. The majority are ineffective at best and carry cardiovascular risk at worst.

**Extremely cheap protein powder from unknown brands** — protein spiking (using cheap amino acids to inflate the stated protein content) is a real issue in unregulated supplement manufacturing. Stick to brands that do third-party testing (NSF Certified for Sport, Informed Sport, or Informed Choice logos).
