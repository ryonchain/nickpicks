## Beard Trimmer Buying Guide

### What to Look for in a Cordless Trimmer

**Motor quality:** More important than most specs. A strong motor maintains speed through thick hair. Linear motors (Panasonic) provide the most consistent torque; standard rotary motors are fine for medium hair density.

**Blade material:** Stainless steel is the minimum for lasting sharpness. Titanium-coated blades (Philips) are harder and stay sharper longer. Japanese steel (Panasonic) is particularly sharp.

**Length precision:** If you maintain a specific beard length, 0.5mm increments matter. Most budget trimmers have 1mm or fewer settings, which means you can't reliably repeat an exact short-stubble length.

**Battery life:** For casual users (once a week), 60 minutes is plenty. If you travel frequently and forget chargers, 240 minutes (Wahl) is a genuine advantage.

**Waterproofing:** Fully waterproof (IPX5–IPX7) means you can use in the shower and rinse under running water. The Panasonic and Braun are both fully washable; the Norelco is attachments-only.

### Stubble vs. Short Beard vs. Full Beard: What Length?

| Style | Length Range | Guard Size |
|-------|-------------|-----------|
| Stubble | 0.5–3mm | No guard or 1–2mm guard |
| Designer stubble | 3–5mm | 3–5mm guard |
| Short beard | 5–10mm | 5–10mm guard |
| Medium beard | 10–20mm | 10–20mm guard |
| Full beard | 20mm+ | 20mm+ guard or scissors |

### How Often Should You Trim?

Stubble/short beard (under 5mm): every 2–4 days to maintain the look.
Medium beard (10–20mm): once per week for maintenance.
Full beard: trim monthly for shape, line up edges weekly.

### Do Blade Oils Matter?

Yes for longevity. A drop of clipper oil on the blade before or after use extends blade life and prevents rusting. Most trimmers that don't include oil still benefit from it. Wahl's blade oil is the standard recommendation — cheap and effective.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**What's the best beard trimmer under $50?**
The Wahl Stainless Steel Lithium Ion+ at ~$40 is the best value under $50. Professional-grade blades, 4-hour battery, and durability that outlasts most competitors.

**Can I use a beard trimmer for body hair?**
Yes, with the right guards. The Philips Norelco 7000 is specifically designed as a multi-purpose body and beard trimmer. For body use, use a longer guard (6mm+) and never go against the grain on sensitive areas.

**How do I trim a beard neckline?**
Use a bare blade (no guard) or 0.5–1mm guard. Place two fingers above your Adam's apple — that's roughly where the neckline should sit. Trim everything below that line. Use the trimmer's edge (or a separate precision trimmer) for clean lines.

**Are cordless trimmers as powerful as corded?**
Modern lithium batteries deliver consistent power throughout their charge. The Panasonic and Braun both match or exceed the cutting power of corded models during the first 80% of their battery life.

**How long do trimmer blades last?**
With regular oiling and proper cleaning, quality blades last 2–5 years. Budget trimmer blades dull within months. Wahl and Panasonic both sell replacement blades when needed.
