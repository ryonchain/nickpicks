## Best Valentine's Day Experience Gifts

Experience gifts beat physical products for men who "have everything":

- **Cooking Class** — A couples cooking class at a local culinary school. $80–$150/couple.
- **Whiskey or Craft Beer Tasting** — Many distilleries and breweries do private tours. $40–$100.
- **Spa Day or Massage** — More popular with men than you'd think. A 60-minute deep-tissue massage: $80–$120.
- **Concert or Event Tickets** — For his favorite band, sports team, or comedian. Check StubHub and Ticketmaster.
- **Golf Lesson** — A 1-hour private lesson with a pro: $60–$100. More appreciated than a new golf gadget.
- **Escape Room for Two** — Novelty, challenge, teamwork. $25–$35/person.

## What to Avoid

- **Generic cologne samplers** — He buys his own scent. Pick something specific or skip it.
- **"Funny" novelty gifts** — Shelf-life of one laugh.
- **Clothing without knowing his exact size** — Return friction kills the gesture.
- **Digital gift cards without context** — Feels lazy unless framed as "pick your own adventure."
