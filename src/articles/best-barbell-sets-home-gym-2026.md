## Weight Plate Types

**Cast iron:** Cheapest, but sizes vary by manufacturer (a 45-lb plate from one brand may be a different diameter than another). Standard for home gyms.

**Rubber bumper plates:** More expensive but bounce — essential for Olympic lifting (power cleans, snatches) and protect flooring. Highly recommended if you have a garage gym or concrete floor.

**Steel calibrated plates:** Competition standard, precisely weighted to ±10g. No benefit for home gyms; expensive.
