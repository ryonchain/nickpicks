## How to Choose a Home Security System

### Renter vs. Homeowner

**Renters:** Need wireless, no-drilling installation. Best options: Abode, SimpliSafe, Ring Alarm. Avoid: any system requiring in-wall wiring.

**Homeowners:** More installation options. Can consider hardwired components for reliability. Vivint's free professional install becomes attractive.

### Monitoring Preference

**Professional monitoring:** SimpliSafe ($9.99/mo) or Ring Protect Plus ($10/mo) are best values.

**Self-monitoring only:** Abode free tier or Ring without subscription.

**24/7 with video verification:** SimpliSafe Interactive ($19.99/mo) or Vivint.

### Contract Tolerance

**No contract:** SimpliSafe, Ring, Abode — all cancel anytime.

**Contract OK:** Vivint (in exchange for free professional equipment installation).
