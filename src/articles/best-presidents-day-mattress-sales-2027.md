Presidents' Day 2027 falls on **Monday, February 15**. Mattress retailers run some of the deepest discounts of the year during this weekend — often matching or beating Black Friday. If you've been putting off a mattress upgrade, this is the window.

---

## Why Presidents' Day Is the Best Time to Buy a Mattress

- **Retailers depend on it.** February is slow for mattresses (post-holiday, pre-spring). Presidents' Day is a deliberate traffic driver.
- **Discounts are genuine.** Unlike some "sale" events, Presidents' Day mattress deals typically offer 20–40% off with free accessories included.
- **Competition is fierce.** Every major brand runs simultaneous promotions, so they have to compete on value.
- **White glove delivery is often free.** Many brands waive the $100–$200 delivery and setup fee during Presidents' Day.

## Best Mattress by Sleep Position — Presidents' Day Picks

### Best for Back Sleepers: Saatva Classic (Luxury Firm)

Zoned support, lumbar enhancement, and the traditional innerspring feel that back sleepers prefer. The white-glove delivery and setup makes it worth the higher price during Presidents' Day.

### Best for Side Sleepers: Nectar or Helix Midnight

Side sleepers need pressure relief at the shoulder and hip. Both Nectar (foam) and Helix Midnight (hybrid) excel here. Nectar is the better value; Helix Midnight is the better overall mattress.

### Best for Stomach Sleepers: Casper Firm or Saatva Classic (Firm)

Stomach sleepers need firmness to prevent lumbar sag. Don't buy soft foam if you sleep on your stomach.

### Best for Hot Sleepers: Purple Restore or Tempur-Breeze

Purple's open polymer grid allows air circulation no foam mattress can match. Tempur-Breeze is the most advanced cooling mattress but at premium cost.

### Best for Couples (Different Preferences): Helix or Sleep Number

Helix builds custom models based on two people's sleep preferences. Sleep Number lets partners set independent firmness via air pressure (though it rarely discounts as deeply during Presidents' Day).

## What to Look For in Presidents' Day Mattress Deals

| What's Included | What It Means |
|-----------------|---------------|
| 365-night trial | Try it for a year, return if it doesn't work |
| White glove delivery | Delivery + setup + old mattress removal included |
| 10-year warranty | Minimum acceptable for a mattress purchase |
| Free pillows | Usually $60–$120 value per pillow |
| Free sheet set | $80–$150 value — legit bundle benefit |

**Avoid mattresses that don't offer at least a 100-night trial.** Mattresses feel different after 30 days of sleeping than they do in a showroom or on day one. A 100-night minimum gives you time to adapt and a real decision window.
