## What to Know Before Buying

**Height range:** For most adults 5'4"–6'4", a desk that goes from 24"–49" covers all seated and standing positions comfortably. Very tall users (6'3"+) should verify the max height matches their elbow height when standing.

**Single vs. dual motor:** Dual-motor desks are notably more stable at full height. For a primary work desk, spend the extra $80–$100 for dual-motor.

**Load capacity:** Add up your monitor(s), computer, accessories. A 34" ultrawide monitor alone weighs 18–22 lbs. Budget 60–80 lbs for a typical dual-monitor desktop setup; make sure the desk exceeds that with margin.

**Assembly time:** Most electric standing desks take 45–90 minutes to assemble. Read the instructions before starting — the most common assembly mistake is tightening the legs too early before the frame is aligned.
