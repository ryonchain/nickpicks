## Stocking Guide by Tank Size

| Tank Size | Fish Options |
|-----------|-------------|
| 5 gallon | 1 Betta OR 5 Neon Tetras |
| 10 gallon | 10 small fish (mix tetras + corydoras) |
| 20 gallon | 20 small fish OR community with angelfish/gourami |
| 29–55 gallon | Full community, cichlids, goldfish |

Rule of thumb: 1 inch of fish per gallon of water (though this is a rough estimate, not gospel).
