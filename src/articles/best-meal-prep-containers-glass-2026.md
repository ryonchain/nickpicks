## Glass vs. Plastic Meal Prep Containers

**Why switch to glass:**
- No BPA, BPS, or phthalate leaching — especially under microwave heat
- Stains don't stick (tomato sauce is the ultimate plastic-stainer)
- Odors don't absorb — no lingering fish smell
- More durable over time — no warping or scratching
- Feels more "premium" — motivates meal prep behavior

**Tradeoffs:**
- Heavier to carry to work
- Breakable (though borosilicate is very impact-resistant)
- More expensive upfront than plastic sets

**Recommendation:** Glass for home refrigerator storage; a good quality plastic container (like Rubbermaid Brilliance BPA-free) for packed lunches you're carrying around all day.
