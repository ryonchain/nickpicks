## iPhone 16 Cases: What's Changed in 2026

iPhone 16 and 16 Pro ship with MagSafe built in — the magnetic ring that enables wireless charging and a growing ecosystem of snap-on accessories. In 2026, the case market has consolidated around MagSafe-compatible designs. A case without MagSafe compatibility blocks the magnetic ring and renders your accessories useless.

**Rule #1:** Every case on this list is MagSafe compatible.

iPhone 16 and 16 Pro also feature the Camera Control button — a new physical button on the right edge for camera controls. The best cases have a precise cutout that doesn't interfere with it.

## What to Look For in an iPhone 16 Case

**MagSafe compatibility:** Essential. Look for "MagSafe compatible" or a built-in MagSafe ring. Cases that say "wireless charging compatible" may not have the magnetic ring.

**Camera Control cutout:** New to iPhone 16 — a precise cutout on the right edge is required for this button to function.

**Drop rating:** Most quality cases list MIL-STD-810 compliance. Look for the drop height tested (6 ft, 8 ft, 10 ft).

**Material:** TPU (flexible, shock-absorbing), polycarbonate (rigid, clear), or dual-layer hybrid (both) are the main options.
