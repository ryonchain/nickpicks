## Fall Comforter Warmth Guide

| Bedroom Temperature | Recommended Weight |
|--------------------|-------------------|
| 65–72°F (warm fall nights) | Light or "summer" weight |
| 58–65°F (typical fall) | Medium weight |
| 50–58°F (cool fall, old house) | Medium-warm or extra warm |
| Under 50°F | Heavy weight + heated mattress pad |

**Fill power explained (down):** Higher fill power = lighter, loftier, warmer for its weight.
- 400–500 FP: Budget down, adequate
- 550–650 FP: Mid-range, good quality
- 700+ FP: Premium, noticeably superior

## Duvet vs. Comforter: Quick Guide

**Comforter:** All-in-one. Has a shell and fill combined. Use as-is with a decorative coverlet or top sheet.

**Duvet insert:** Goes inside a duvet cover (sold separately). The cover is removable and washable — better for hygiene. European standard.

For fall, either works. The duvet + cover system is easier to wash (wash the cover, not the whole duvet), but adds purchase complexity.
