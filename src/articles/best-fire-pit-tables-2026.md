## Safety and Practical Notes

**Clearance:** Keep fire pit tables at least 10 feet from structures, overhangs, or furniture. Never use under covered patios or pergolas without specific ventilation.

**Lid on when not in use:** Covers prevent water pooling in the burner (common failure point) and debris accumulation in the fire media.

**Connect to propane properly:** Use a CSA-approved hose and regulator. Never check for leaks with an open flame — use soapy water on connections.

**Wind guards:** Glass wind guards (some included, some sold separately) extend comfortable use on windy evenings significantly.
