## What to Look For in 4-Year-Old Gifts

- **Open-ended play potential:** 4-year-olds thrive with toys that don't have a "right answer"
- **Cooperative play:** Look for games and toys that can involve 2+ kids
- **Motor challenge:** Fine motor (puzzles, art) and gross motor (scooter, ball games) are both important
- **Reading readiness:** Games that involve letters and words prep for kindergarten without feeling like homework
