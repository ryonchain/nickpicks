## Coat Care

**Down coats:** Machine wash on gentle with sport/down cycle. Dry thoroughly with tennis balls to restore loft. Never hang damp.

**Wool coats:** Dry clean or hand wash in cold with wool detergent. Never machine wash. Store with cedar blocks to prevent moth damage.

**Storage:** Store coats loosely on wide hangers (not folded). Folding creates permanent creases.
