# Best Standing Desk Converters 2026: Sit-Stand Solutions for Any Desk

Full standing desks are the gold standard — but they cost $350–1,500 and require replacing your entire workspace. Standing desk converters give you the same sit-stand capability for $80–400 by sitting on top of your existing desk. If you rent, work from a hotel frequently, or just don't want to commit to a full desk replacement, converters are the smarter path.

This guide covers the best converters by type, with honest assessments of which situations each is right for.

---

## Types of Standing Desk Converters

**Z-lift (scissor) converters:** The most common type. Raise and lower using a dual-arm scissor mechanism. Move keyboard and monitor together as one unit. Fast to adjust.

**Fixed-rise platforms:** A riser that sits at one height. No adjustability — you either stand or sit at your regular desk height. Better for users who will almost always stand.

**Full-surface converters:** Cover the entire desk surface, essentially turning it into a standing desk without replacing it. Heaviest and most expensive type.
