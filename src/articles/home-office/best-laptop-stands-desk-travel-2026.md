# Best Laptop Stands 2026: Desk and Travel Picks for Ergonomic Work

Using a laptop directly on a desk puts your neck at a 40–50° downward angle for hours at a time. That's the mechanical root cause of most remote workers' neck pain. A laptop stand elevates the screen to eye level — and combined with an external keyboard and mouse, turns your laptop into a proper ergonomic workstation.

This guide covers the best laptop stands for desk use, portability, and travel, across every budget.

---

## Why Laptop Height Matters

The ideal screen position places the top of the display at or slightly below eye level. Most people using a laptop flat on a desk are looking 30–40° downward — the equivalent of holding your head at a 10-pound forward lean for hours. Even a $20 laptop stand eliminates most of this strain if you use an external keyboard and mouse.
