# Best Desk Organizers & Cable Management Systems 2026

Cable clutter is the fastest way to make an otherwise clean desk look chaotic — and it's also a functional problem. Tangled cables snag, get accidentally unplugged, and make cleaning a desk a 20-minute untangling exercise. Good cable management takes under an hour to implement and the results last years.

This guide covers cable management systems by type, plus the best desk organizers for accessories, stationery, and small devices.

---

## Cable Management: The Four Approaches

| Approach | Best For | Cost |
|----------|----------|------|
| Cable trays/raceways | Hiding cables under desk surface | $20–60 |
| Cable clips/holders | Routing cables at desk edge | $8–15 |
| Cable sleeves | Bundling multiple cables together | $10–20 |
| Desk grommets | Clean pass-throughs in the desk | $10–25 |

Most setups benefit from combining a cable tray (for the power strip and transformer bricks) with cable clips (for the runs along the desk surface).
