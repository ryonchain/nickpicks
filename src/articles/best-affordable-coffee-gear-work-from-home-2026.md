## Why Grind Fresh Changes Everything

Pre-ground coffee stales within 15–30 minutes of grinding — volatile aromatic compounds oxidize rapidly once the bean surface is exposed. Whole bean coffee sealed in a proper bag stays fresh for 2–4 weeks. A burr grinder is the single highest-impact upgrade you can make to home coffee quality.

**Burr vs. blade grinders:** Blade grinders chop beans unevenly — different particle sizes extract at different rates, producing a muddled cup. Burr grinders crush beans to a uniform size, enabling consistent extraction. Any burr grinder beats the best blade grinder.

## Coffee Variables That Matter Most

| Variable | Impact | Easy Fix |
|----------|--------|----------|
| Bean freshness | Very high | Buy whole bean, roasted within 3–4 weeks |
| Grind size | Very high | Burr grinder, dial in for your brew method |
| Water temperature | High | 195–205°F (just off boil), not boiling |
| Water quality | Medium | Use filtered water if tap tastes off |
| Coffee-to-water ratio | High | Weigh your coffee (15–17g per 250ml for drip) |
