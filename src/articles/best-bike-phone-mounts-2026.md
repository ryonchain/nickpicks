## Installation Tips

1. **Center the phone on the stem** — weight distribution prevents wobble
2. **Run the cable through the clamp** — if your phone needs charging, position the port toward the stem
3. **Test at low speed first** — shake the handlebars and check for movement before a real ride
4. **Remove in wet weather** — no mount is fully weatherproof; heavy rain can fog cameras and damage ports
