## Buying Guide: Avoiding Common Mistakes

**Don't:** Buy appliances that frame her as the household worker (vacuum cleaners, dishwasher soap subscriptions).

**Do:** Buy things she'd use *for herself* — not things for the house she already manages.

**The best tells:**
- Has she mentioned anything in the last 6 months?
- Is there a product she researched, added to a wishlist, but didn't buy?
- What's a category she always opts for the cheap version in?

**Safe backup:** A spa gift card. It's not lazy when it's intentional — and it gives her something she almost never does for herself.
