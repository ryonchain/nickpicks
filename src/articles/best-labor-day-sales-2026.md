## What NOT to Buy on Labor Day

- **TVs and electronics** — Better deals at Black Friday (2 months away)
- **Laptops** — Back-to-school sales just ended; Black Friday is better
- **Clothing** — Labor Day clothing sales are mostly on items with bad inventory, not true deals
