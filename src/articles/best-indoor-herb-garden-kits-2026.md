### Tips for continuous harvest

- **Harvest regularly:** Don't let herbs flower (bolt). Pinch off flower buds immediately. Frequent harvesting encourages bushier growth.
- **Trim from the top:** Cut stems from the top 1/3, never the bottom third.
- **Stagger planting:** Replace one pod every 3–4 weeks instead of all at once to maintain continuous yield.
