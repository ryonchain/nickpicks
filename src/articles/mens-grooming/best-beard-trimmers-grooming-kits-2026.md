## How to Choose

**Stubble only (1–5mm):** Any quality trimmer handles this. Focus on skin comfort and 0.5mm precision steps.

**Medium beard (5–15mm):** Look for at least 10–20 length settings and a powerful motor that doesn't pull.

**Long beard (15mm+):** You need a trimmer with attachment combs up to 20–25mm. Wahl and Braun handle long length best.

**All-in-one convenience:** The Philips MG9750 or similar multigroom kits if you also need body, nose, and ear coverage.
