## What to Look For

**Skin-safety:** Ceramic or SkinSafe blades reduce the risk of nicks on sensitive skin vs. standard stainless blades.

**Waterproofing:** IPX7 (submersible to 1m for 30 minutes) is the benchmark for shower-safe use. Look for this rating if you want to trim in the shower.

**Guard lengths:** A range of 0–10mm covers most use cases. Below 3mm handles close trims; above 5mm maintains a more natural look.

**Replaceable heads:** Blade degradation is real. A tool with readily available replacement heads on Amazon extends useful life significantly.
