## Gifting Tips

- **When in doubt, go fresh or citrus.** These are the most broadly liked fragrance families.
- **Include a gift receipt.** Fragrance is personal. Even the best pick doesn't land for everyone.
- **Size matters.** A 100ml bottle lasts 1–2 years of daily use. Travel sizes (30ml) are perfect for someone with multiple fragrances they rotate.
