## What to Look For

**Foil vs. Rotary:** Foil is better for sensitive skin and closer shaves; rotary handles curved contours better. When in doubt, start with foil.

**Wet/Dry Rating:** Look for IPX7 if you want to shave in the shower with gel or foam.

**Cleaning Station:** Convenient but adds ongoing cost for replacement cartridges. Worth it for daily shavers.

**Battery Life:** 45–60 minutes of run time is plenty for daily use; most modern shavers hit this benchmark.
