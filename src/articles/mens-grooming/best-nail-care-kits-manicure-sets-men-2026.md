## How to Use a Nail Care Kit: The Basics

**Step 1 — Soften.** Trim nails after a shower while the nail plate is soft. Dry nails split when cut.

**Step 2 — Clip.** Cut straight across with slight curves at the corners. Don't cut below the natural tip of the finger.

**Step 3 — File.** Smooth rough edges with a nail file, moving in one direction (not back-and-forth which weakens the nail).

**Step 4 — Cuticles.** Push cuticles back gently with a cuticle pusher after shower. Don't cut live cuticle (the tissue, not the overgrown dead skin).

**Step 5 — Clean.** Use the V-groove tool or a nail brush to clean under nails.
