# Best Smart Plugs & Outlets for Home Automation (2026)

Smart plugs are the fastest, cheapest way to start automating your home. Plug one in, connect it to your Wi-Fi or smart home hub, and any dumb appliance — a lamp, coffee maker, fan, or space heater — becomes voice-controlled and schedule-able. No wiring, no electrician.

This guide covers the best smart plugs across Wi-Fi, Zigbee, and Matter protocols for different use cases and budgets.

---

## Smart Plug Protocols Explained

**Wi-Fi smart plugs** connect directly to your router — no hub needed, easy setup, but can clog a crowded 2.4GHz network and are slower to respond than mesh protocols.

**Zigbee smart plugs** require a Zigbee hub (like Amazon Echo 4th Gen or SmartThings) but deliver faster local control, run automations offline, and extend your Zigbee mesh.

**Matter smart plugs** are the newest standard — work with Alexa, Google, Apple HomeKit, and SmartThings from one app and are interoperable across ecosystems.

## Smart Plug Buying Guide

**Do you need energy monitoring?** If yes: Kasa EP25, SONOFF S31, or Eve Energy.

**What ecosystem are you in?**
- Alexa-only: Amazon Smart Plug for easiest setup
- Google-only: Kasa EP25 or TP-Link
- Apple HomeKit: Kasa EP25 or Eve Energy
- All three: Eve Energy (Matter)

**Do you have a Zigbee hub?** IKEA TRADFRI or SONOFF ZBMINIL2 for local control without Wi-Fi load.

**Do you want outdoor use?** Look specifically for outdoor-rated plugs with IP44 or higher weather resistance.
