# Best Smart Smoke & Carbon Monoxide Detectors (2026)

A smoke detector is one of the most important safety devices in your home — and also one of the most overlooked when it comes to smart home upgrades. Smart smoke and CO detectors do more than just beep: they send push notifications to your phone, speak the location and nature of the alarm, silence false alarms from the app, and interconnect wirelessly so every unit in your home alerts together.

This guide covers the best smart smoke and CO detectors by features, price, and ecosystem.

---

## Why Upgrade to a Smart Smoke Detector?

**Smart features that matter:**
- **Phone notifications:** Get alerted at work or while sleeping before damage spreads
- **Spoken alerts:** "Smoke detected in the kitchen" tells you where to look
- **Remote silence:** Stop false alarms from the app without climbing a ladder
- **Self-testing:** Automatic monthly checks confirm the sensor is working
- **Interconnection:** All units alert when one triggers — works without Wi-Fi bridge in some models
- **End-of-life reminders:** Know when it's time to replace (every 10 years)

## Replacing Your Existing Detectors: What to Know

**Replacement schedule:** Smoke detectors should be replaced every 10 years. CO detectors every 5–7 years. Check the manufacture date on the back of your current detectors.

**UL certification:** All smoke detectors sold in the US must be UL-certified. Every product on this list meets UL 217 (smoke) and UL 2034 (CO) standards.

**Interconnected alerts:** For the safest setup, every detector in your home should interconnect — when one triggers, all alarm. Both hardwired interconnect and wireless interconnect via Wi-Fi are available in the products above.

**Installation:** Battery-only models mount in minutes with one screw. Wired models require turning off power at the breaker and connecting to the existing wiring — straightforward for DIYers with basic electrical knowledge.
