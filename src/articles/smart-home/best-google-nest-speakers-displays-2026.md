# Best Google Nest Speakers & Displays (2026)

Google Nest devices run Google Assistant — the smartest voice assistant on the market for answering complex questions, managing Google Calendar, and integrating with Android phones. If you're in the Google ecosystem, Nest devices are the natural choice.

Google's lineup is smaller than Amazon's, which makes picking easier. This guide covers every current Nest speaker and display with honest assessments of where each one wins.

---

## Why Choose Google Nest Over Amazon Echo?

**Google Assistant advantages:**
- Superior at complex web queries and conversational context
- Best-in-class Google Calendar, Gmail, and Android integration
- Faster response times on knowledge queries
- Better multilingual support in multilingual households

**Where Alexa has the edge:**
- Wider smart home device compatibility (especially older Zigbee/Z-Wave devices)
- More third-party skills and integrations
- Better shopping integration (Amazon orders)

## What About Chromecast and Google TV?

Google's streaming lineup (Chromecast with Google TV) is separate from the Nest speaker line but integrates naturally. If you want voice control for your TV without a separate speaker, the Google TV remote includes a dedicated Google Assistant button.

## Setting Up Multi-Room Audio

Google Home supports multi-room audio groups across Nest devices. Add Nest speakers to a room group and say "Hey Google, play jazz in the kitchen and living room" — all speakers sync and play simultaneously. Pair two Nest Audios or Nest Hub Max speakers for stereo pairing in a single room.
