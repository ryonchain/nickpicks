# Best Smart Garage Door Openers & Controllers (2026)

Your garage door is one of the last things in your home that's still dumb — but it doesn't have to be. Smart garage controllers let you check if the door is open, close it remotely, get alerts when it's been open too long, and integrate it with your smart home automations. The best part: most work with your existing garage door opener without replacement.

This guide covers retrofit smart controllers (add to existing opener) and full smart opener replacements, with honest notes on the myQ subscription controversy.

---

## Retrofit vs. Replacement

**Retrofit smart controllers** ($25–$100) add Wi-Fi and app control to your existing garage door opener. They attach to your current motor, detect door state via a sensor, and add remote operation. No electrician needed.

**Full smart openers** ($200–$400) replace your entire garage door motor with a new Wi-Fi-enabled unit. Required if your opener is 15+ years old, failing, or if you want built-in battery backup.

---

## The myQ Controversy

Chamberlain/myQ is the market leader in smart garage control — their hardware is excellent. But in 2023 they blocked third-party integrations (removing Alexa, Google, and SmartThings support) unless you pay for their $5/month Amazon Key subscription for remote delivery. Direct Alexa/Google voice control no longer works without the subscription.

If you want free Alexa/Google integration, look at Meross, Ratgdo, or Tailwind instead.
