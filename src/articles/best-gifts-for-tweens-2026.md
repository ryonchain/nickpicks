## Buying Guide: Tween Gift Tips

**What tweens want:** To feel older, to express themselves, to be taken seriously.

**What to avoid:** Toys that feel young (Fisher-Price, baby-adjacent branding, anything with "for ages 3-8" on it).

**Best signals from the tween themselves:**
- What YouTube channels do they watch?
- What games do their friends play?
- What brands do they mention?
- What do they spend babysitting money on?

**Safe categories:** Experiences (concerts, sports events), gift cards to brands they actually like, and tech that matches their phase — cameras if they make content, speakers if they love music.
