## Crossbody Bag Buying Guide

### What Size Do You Need?

**Mini crossbody (under 7 inches):** Phone, cards, and lip balm only. Best for events, nights out, or light travel.

**Small-medium crossbody (7–10 inches):** Phone, wallet, keys, sunglasses. The everyday sweet spot for most women.

**Large crossbody (10–12 inches):** Adds room for a small water bottle, tablet, or full makeup kit. Approaches a shoulder-bag silhouette.

### Strap Considerations

Look for an adjustable strap that can be shortened (for a higher, crossbody-style fit) or extended (for a lower, hip-level fit). Straps between 22 and 48 inches give the most flexibility. Chain straps are fashionable but can cause shoulder fatigue on longer outings — for all-day use, padded nylon or leather straps are more comfortable.

### Security Features to Look For

- **Zipper closure:** More secure than magnetic snap
- **RFID-blocking lining:** Protects credit cards and travel documents
- **Slash-resistant strap:** For high-theft environments
- **Interior key hook:** Prevents digging for keys at the bottom

### Faux Leather vs. Genuine Leather at This Price Point

In the under-$100 range, most bags are faux leather (PU or vegan leather). Quality faux leather (like Saffiano PU) is durable and aesthetically convincing. For genuine leather under $100, look specifically to outlet prices from Coach or Kate Spade — it requires timing but is achievable.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**How do I know if a Coach or Kate Spade Outlet bag is authentic?**
Purchase from official outlet stores, the brand's own outlet website, or Amazon — not third-party resellers. Authentic bags have consistent stitching, hardware weight, and interior lining quality.

**Can crossbody bags go over a shoulder too?**
Most crossbody bags can also be worn as a shoulder bag when the strap is at its shortest. Check the shortest strap length before buying if shoulder-bag wear matters to you.

**How do I clean a faux leather crossbody?**
A damp cloth with mild soap handles most marks. Avoid saturation — faux leather doesn't dry well when soaked. Condition annually with a faux leather protector to extend life.

**Are crossbody bags good for travel?**
Excellent for travel, especially bags with anti-theft features. The hands-free design is ideal in airports and on walking tours.
