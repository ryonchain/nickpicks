## FAQ

**What's the best streaming device for 4K on a budget?**
The Roku Streaming Stick 4K+ (~$50) or Fire TV Stick 4K Max (~$60) are both excellent 4K options without breaking $60.

**Do I need a streaming device if my TV is already smart?**
Smart TV software is often slow and gets outdated. A streaming stick delivers a faster, more up-to-date experience and is worth it on any TV older than 2–3 years.

**What streaming device has the most apps?**
Roku has the broadest app catalog. Fire TV and Google TV are close behind. Apple TV lacks some niche apps but covers all major services.
