## Are Smart Scales Accurate?

The bioelectrical impedance analysis (BIA) technology used by all smart scales is a *trend indicator*, not a lab-grade measurement. Individual body fat readings can vary ±3–5% from DEXA scans. What it does well:

- **Week-over-week trends** (losing or gaining body fat over time)
- **Relative comparisons** (between two people on the same scale)
- **Motivation** (seeing muscle increase while fat decreases, even at the same weight)

Best practice: weigh at the same time each day (morning, after bathroom, before eating/drinking) for the most consistent readings.

## Family Use: Multi-Profile Support

All recommended scales support multiple profiles — typically 8–16 users. The scale automatically detects who stepped on based on recent weight patterns. For new users or weight-range overlaps, you may need to manually select your profile from the app.
