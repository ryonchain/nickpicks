## What to Skip: Overrated School Supplies

**Branded "school supply kits"** — You pay 40% more for the packaging. Buy individual items in bulk.

**Rolling backpacks** — Banned or impractical in most schools with lockers.

**Laminating pouches** (unless your student is in art) — Teachers rarely ask students to laminate.

**Expensive colored pens** — Pilot G2s and standard ballpoints work fine. Don't overbuy.

**Trapper Keepers and oversized organizers** — Don't fit modern school lockers.
