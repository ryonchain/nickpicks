## Buying Guide: Open-Back vs Closed-Back vs Wireless

**Open-back** (HD 650, DT 990 Pro): Best soundstage and air around instruments. Leak sound in both directions — not for offices or commuting. Require quiet environments. Best for serious home listening.

**Closed-back wired** (ATH-M50x): Passive isolation without electronics. Better for recording or noisy environments. Slightly smaller soundstage than open-back.

**Wireless ANC** (Sony WH-1000XM5, Bose QC45): Best versatility. ANC handles low-frequency drone (planes, trains, AC units) better than passive isolation. Trade-off: compression of Bluetooth audio vs wired lossless.
