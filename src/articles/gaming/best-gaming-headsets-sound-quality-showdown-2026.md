## How We Evaluate Gaming Headsets

Gaming audio has two jobs: **hearing enemies before they hear you** and **communicating clearly with your team**. Neither requires $200 headphones — but both require the right balance of driver quality, impedance matching, and microphone pickup pattern.

Key metrics:
- **Frequency response:** Flat or V-shaped? Gaming headsets often boost bass and treble to sound impressive at the expense of midrange clarity.
- **Soundstage:** Width of the perceived audio space. Critical for 3D positional audio in FPS games.
- **Microphone:** Directional (cardioid) vs. omnidirectional. Noise rejection, frequency capture, Discord compression artifacts.
- **Wireless latency:** Under 20ms is imperceptible. Over 40ms causes echo and desync on fast audio cues.
- **Comfort at 4+ hours:** Weight, clamping force, ear pad material, and heat buildup.
