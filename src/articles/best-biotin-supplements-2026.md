# Best Biotin Supplements of 2026: Hair Growth, Nails, and Skin Picks

That said, biotin is safe, inexpensive, and worth trying if you're experiencing unexplained hair loss or brittle nails — but not at the megadoses (10,000 mcg) commonly marketed, which exceed clinical use doses by 1,000-fold.

## What Biotin Won't Do (Important)

**Hair loss causes biotin won't fix:**
- Androgenetic alopecia (genetic male/female pattern baldness) — requires minoxidil, finasteride, or PRP
- Alopecia areata (autoimmune) — requires immunosuppressive treatment
- Hormonal hair loss (thyroid, PCOS, postpartum) — requires treating the underlying condition
- Iron deficiency anemia — requires iron supplementation and dietary correction
- Protein deficiency — requires dietary protein adequacy

If hair loss is significant, get tested for: ferritin (iron stores), TSH (thyroid), complete blood count, and vitamin D. Addressing a true nutritional deficiency almost always produces better results than high-dose biotin in non-deficient individuals.
