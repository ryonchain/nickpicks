## Why Black Friday Is the Best Time to Buy a Robot Vacuum

Robot vacuums have two reliable sales events per year: Prime Day (July) and Black Friday (November). Both offer comparable discounts — but Black Friday tends to include:

1. **Deeper absolute discounts** on flagship self-emptying models
2. **Bundle deals** (extra accessories, extended warranty offers)
3. **Cross-retailer competition** — Amazon, Best Buy, Walmart, and Target all compete, which drives prices down

If you missed Prime Day 2026, Black Friday 2026 (November 27) is your next best opportunity.
