## Most Overlooked Safety Items

**Anti-tip furniture straps (highest priority):** Tip-overs from dressers and bookshelves kill approximately 30 children annually in the US. IKEA's free Secure It kits (available by request) anchor dressers to walls. Any purchased dresser should be anchored before the child is mobile.

**Toilet locks:** Toddlers can drown in toilet bowls. A toilet lock keeps the seat down when not in use.

**Stove knob covers:** Toddlers can turn on gas burners. Covers slip over knobs and require adult-size hand pressure to operate.

**Baby gate for stairs:** The most important single purchase after an infant is mobile. Pressure-mounted gates work for room separation; hardware-mounted (screw-in) gates are required for the top of stairs.
