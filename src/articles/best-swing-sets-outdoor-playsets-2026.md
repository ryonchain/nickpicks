## Yard Planning

- **Space required:** Add 6 ft on all sides of the playset footprint for fall zone clearance.
- **Ground surface:** Use wood chips, mulch, rubber playground mulch, or pea gravel under the playset. Grass alone fails ASTM fall protection standards.
- **Anchoring:** All full-size playsets should be anchored to the ground (stakes or concrete footings depending on soil type).
