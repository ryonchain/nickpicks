## Essential Oil Diffuser Buying Guide

### Ultrasonic vs Nebulizing vs Evaporative vs Heat

| Type | Method | Aroma Strength | Oil Use | Noise |
|------|--------|---------------|---------|-------|
| Ultrasonic | Vibration + water | Moderate | Low | Low |
| Nebulizing | Pressurized air + oil | High | High | Moderate |
| Evaporative | Fan + heat pad | Low | Low | Fan noise |
| Heat/candle | Heat evaporation | Low-moderate | Low | Silent |

For most users, ultrasonic is the right choice. For intensive aromatherapy or large rooms, nebulizing.

### Coverage Area Reality
Manufacturer claims assume optimal conditions (no ventilation, small sealed room). Practical coverage:
- 200 sq ft claimed → 100–150 sq ft actual
- 400 sq ft claimed → 200–300 sq ft actual
- Account for air circulation and open floor plans

### Essential Oil Compatibility
Most oils work with ultrasonic diffusers, but very thick, resinous oils (vetiver, benzoin, myrrh) can clog ultrasonic atomizers and gum up the vibrating plate. For these oils, use a nebulizing diffuser or warm them slightly first.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**How many drops of essential oil should I use?**
Start with 3–5 drops per 100ml of water. Dense or potent oils (peppermint, eucalyptus, clove) use 2–3 drops. Lighter florals (lavender, ylang-ylang) use 5–8 drops. Don't overfill with oil — it can film over the ultrasonic plate.

**Do essential oil diffusers humidify the air?**
Yes, mildly. A 500ml ultrasonic diffuser adds some moisture to the air, which can be helpful in dry climates or during winter. It's not a substitute for a dedicated humidifier if humidity is the primary goal, but the combined effect (aromatherapy + mild humidification) is a real benefit.

**Are essential oil diffusers safe around pets?**
Many essential oils are toxic to cats and dogs. Eucalyptus, tea tree, clove, and cinnamon are particularly dangerous to cats. Consult a vet before diffusing around pets. Use diffusers in well-ventilated areas and never diffuse continuously in rooms where pets spend extended time.
