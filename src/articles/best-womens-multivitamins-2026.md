## Life-Stage Guide

**20s–30s (menstruating):** Prioritize iron (15–22 mg), folate as methylfolate, vitamin D3. New Chapter Every Woman's One Daily or Garden of Life Vitamin Code Women.

**30s–40s (considering pregnancy):** Higher folate critical. Look for 400+ mcg methylfolate. Ritual Essential or Thorne Basic Nutrients Women.

**40s–50s (perimenopause):** Magnesium becomes key for mood and sleep, plus increased vitamin D. Thorne Basic Nutrients Women or MegaFood Women's One Daily.

**50+ (post-menopause):** Remove iron, add more D3 and K2, consider calcium. Garden of Life Vitamin Code Women 50+ is purpose-built for this stage.

## Ingredients to Look For (and Avoid)

**Look for:**
- Folate as methylfolate (not folic acid)
- B12 as methylcobalamin
- Iron as bisglycinate (gentle on the stomach)
- D3 (not D2)
- K2 as MK-7 (bone-directed calcium)

**Avoid:**
- Folic acid (suboptimal for MTHFR variants)
- Magnesium oxide (poor absorption)
- Artificial colors and sweeteners
- Iron post-menopause unless prescribed
- Megadose formulas (more isn't always better)

A multivitamin is one piece of a larger wellness picture. Pair it with a diet rich in leafy greens, protein, and healthy fats, and you've covered your nutritional bases.
