## Buying Guide: Secret Santa Tips

**For coworkers you barely know:**
- Stick to consumables (chocolates, coffee, candles) — nothing personal
- Avoid: alcohol, perfume, anything political or religious
- Safe universals: socks, insulated tumbler, portable charger, chocolates

**For friends or family:**
- You can go more personal (their interests, hobbies, favorite brands)
- Funnier or more specific gifts land better when you know the person

**Budget strategy:**
- Under $20: chocolates, socks, hand cream, playing cards
- Under $30: candle, tumbler, speaker, coffee sampler
- Under $50: portable charger, cocktail set, game expansion

**One rule:** Wrapping quality matters. A nice gift in terrible wrapping lands worse than a mediocre gift wrapped beautifully. Spend $5 on quality wrapping paper.
