## What to Prioritize as a Student

**Call quality** comes first. You'll use these for Zoom lectures, group project calls, office hours, and phone calls with family. Earbud microphones vary wildly — look for reviews that specifically test call clarity rather than just music listening.

**Battery life** matters when you're going 10 hours without returning to your dorm. Look for 6+ hours per charge on the buds themselves, plus additional charge in the case. Most earbuds in this price range offer 20–30 hours total (buds + case).

**Fit for active wear.** If you're using these at the gym or walking across campus in wind and rain, look for an ear hook or wing-tip design. Rounded earbud tips without hooks can pop out during exercise.

**ANC (active noise cancellation)** at under $50 is often mediocre — present but not transformative. Passive noise isolation (seal + tip fit) usually matters more at this price. Don't pay a premium for ANC unless you've verified the specific model delivers it.
