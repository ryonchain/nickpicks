## What Safety Certifications to Look For

**JPMA (Juvenile Products Manufacturers Association)** certification is the gold standard for US high chairs. It means the product has been independently tested to exceed ASTM F404 safety standards — covering stability, restraint systems, tray security, and folding mechanisms.

**5-point harness:** Essential for babies who haven't yet developed the core strength to sit without toppling. A 5-point harness (two shoulder, two waist, one crotch strap) is the safest restraint. Avoid chairs with 3-point harnesses only.

**Anti-tip legs / wide base:** Stability on uneven kitchen flooring matters. Look for chairs with a wide, weighted base and rubber-tipped legs.

**GREENGUARD Gold:** For parents concerned about chemical exposure, GREENGUARD Gold certifies that the materials meet stringent VOC limits — important for a product your baby's face will be close to for years.
