## Types of Beard Trimmers

**Standard trimmers** use a straight blade (like a wider razor) that you guide along your beard. They set a specific length via a comb attachment or adjustable guard.

**Hybrid shavers/trimmers** (like the OneBlade) use a blade that both trims to length and shaves close to the skin, giving you more versatility in a single tool.

**Detail trimmers** are narrow and precise — designed for lining up edges, cleaning up sideburns, or trimming a mustache. Often come as an attachment on full-size trimmers.

**Body trimmers** use skin-safe guards that prevent nicks on larger surface areas. Some trimmers include both a beard head and a body head.

## What to Look for in Battery Life

- **Under 60 min:** Fine for once-weekly trims, inconvenient for longer sessions
- **60–90 min:** Adequate for most users
- **90+ min:** Best for heavy use, households with multiple users, frequent travelers
- **Quick charge:** Look for "5 min = 1 use" feature for emergency top-ups
