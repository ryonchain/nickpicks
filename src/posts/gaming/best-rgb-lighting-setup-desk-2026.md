## Why RGB Lighting Actually Matters (Beyond Aesthetics)

RGB lighting isn't purely cosmetic — there are real functional benefits:

- **Bias lighting behind your monitor:** Reduces eye strain in dark rooms by increasing ambient luminance around the screen. Studies show it reduces eye fatigue during long viewing sessions.
- **Ambient lighting for streaming/content creation:** A well-lit background dramatically improves webcam image quality. RGB setups that match your brand colors create a professional streaming aesthetic.
- **Keyboard and peripheral RGB:** Marginal practical value, but customizable per-key lighting can help with keybindings in the dark.

## RGB Ecosystem Compatibility Note

If you have RGB peripherals, sync them to lighting:
- **Razer Synapse:** Syncs Razer peripherals with Govee and Philips Hue (via Razer Chroma Connect).
- **Corsair iCUE:** Syncs Corsair peripherals; limited third-party lighting integration.
- **SteelSeries GG:** Syncs SteelSeries products; integrates with Nanoleaf and Govee via Moments.
- **Open-source alternative:** Openrgb is a free tool that syncs many brands that don't have native cross-ecosystem support.
