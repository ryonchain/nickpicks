## Dorm Shopping Tips

**Buy multipacks:** Buying supplies in bulk (hangers, storage bins, cleaning supplies) usually costs less per unit. Split packs with your roommate if you're coordinating before move-in.

**Check your school's move-in list:** Most schools publish a recommended supply list and note what's prohibited (candles, certain appliances, specific mounting hardware). Read it before ordering.

**Wait to buy some things:** You won't know your exact needs until you've lived in the room for a week. Don't over-order before move-in. Buy the basics, then supplement once you know what you're missing.

**Coordinate with your roommate:** You don't need two microwaves, two mini fridges, or two sets of cleaning supplies. Split the big shared items before move-in day.
