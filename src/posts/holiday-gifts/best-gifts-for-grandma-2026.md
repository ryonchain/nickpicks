## Gifts to Avoid

**Complicated tech** that requires setup assistance every few months. Choose tech with voice control and simple interfaces, or skip it.

**Generic spa sets** from drug stores — the fragrance may not suit her, and the packaging often looks cheap.

**Items that address aging in a patronizing way** — "elderly" mobility aids framed as gifts rather than health tools.

The best gift for grandma is one that makes her feel seen — something that reflects who she actually is, not a generic "old person" checklist.
