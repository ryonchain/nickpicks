## Buying Advice

Before buying a laptop accessory as a gift, confirm what laptop the recipient owns:

- **Laptop stands** are mostly universal (specify max laptop width)
- **Sleeves and bags** are size-specific (13", 14", 15", or 16" — get the exact size)
- **USB hubs** need to match the laptop's port type (USB-C hub for new MacBooks and Windows ultrabooks; USB-A for older machines)
- **Keyboard covers** are model-specific — check the exact year and model

When in doubt, a laptop stand + USB-C hub combination covers the most use cases across the most machines.
