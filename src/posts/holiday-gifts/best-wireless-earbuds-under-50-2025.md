## What to Prioritize When Buying Budget Earbuds

**Codec support matters.** If you have an Android phone, look for aptX support — it delivers better wireless audio quality than SBC. iPhone users benefit from AAC. Cheaper earbuds without these codecs have noticeably worse audio.

**Driver size isn't everything.** A 6mm driver with quality materials can outperform a 12mm driver with poor tuning. Trust reviews over spec sheets.

**Battery life math.** Earbud-only hours matter as much as total hours. A 5-hour earbud battery with a 35-hour case is more useful than a 7-hour earbud with an 18-hour case if you forget to charge the case.

**IP rating.** IPX4 means sweat and light splash resistant. IPX7 means submersion proof. If they're for gym use, make sure the rating is at least IPX4.
