## Stocking Stuffer Strategy

**Fill from the front.** Small items fill the toe and sides; a medium item can sit in the center; one "star" item sits at the top visible above the stocking.

**Price distribution:** 3–4 small items ($3–6 each) + 1 featured item ($10–15) + candy = full stocking at $30 total.

**Avoid gas station tier.** Nail files from dollar bins, mystery chocolates, and random toys signal no thought. The items above signal thought without exceeding budget.
