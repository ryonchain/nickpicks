## Buying Advice

**For headset gifts:** always confirm whether they game on PC, PlayStation, or Xbox — compatibility varies. USB receivers work on PC and PlayStation, but not on Xbox (which uses its own wireless protocol). Wireless is universally preferred if the budget allows.

**For chair gifts:** don't buy the cheapest gaming chair you can find. The foam structure and lumbar support are what make or break comfort after 2 hours. Set a minimum of $100 and wait for a sale rather than buying a $60 chair that'll need replacing in 6 months.

**Safe gift option:** A gift card for the platform they actually game on (Xbox, PlayStation, Steam) is the one gift that's never wrong.
