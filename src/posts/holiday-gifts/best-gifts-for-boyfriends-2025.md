## Gift Calibration by Relationship Stage

**New relationship (3–8 months):** Anker power bank, Moleskine notebook, Carhartt beanie, quality pocketknife. Keep it practical and under $40.

**Mid-relationship (1–2 years):** Keychron keyboard, Theragun Relief, Osprey backpack, Patagonia Better Sweater. The $80–$130 range for something specific to him.

**Serious/long-term:** Bose QC Earbuds II, Sony XM5 headphones, Ember Mug, experience-based gift (Ooni pizza oven, brewery tour, weekend trip). This is where personal knowledge of his taste should drive the pick.

Always choose based on something you've actually heard him mention — "I wish I had better headphones" or "my charger is always dead" is the signal. The best boyfriend gift is the one that proves you were listening.
