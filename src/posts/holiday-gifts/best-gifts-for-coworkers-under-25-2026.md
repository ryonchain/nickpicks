## What Makes a Great Coworker Gift

**Universal > Personalized at this price point:** You often don't know enough about a coworker to personalize meaningfully. Choose universally appropriate gifts rather than guessing about their taste.

**Consumable = safe:** Food, drinks, and skincare items get used and don't create obligation to keep something you don't need.

**Professional context matters:** Gifts with inside jokes, alcohol, or strong political/cultural references don't fit every work environment. When in doubt, choose office-neutral.

**Presentation is free:** A ribbon, a gift bag, or a handwritten sticky note on a Ghirardelli box transforms the experience. Never hand over a gift in its Amazon shipping packaging.
