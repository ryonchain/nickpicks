## What Dog Lovers Actually Want

The best gifts for dog lovers are about **the dog's experience** and **the owner's life with that dog**. Quality gear, enrichment toys, and veterinary-adjacent health items score higher than novelty items. Personalized items with the dog's actual name or image are reliably moving.
