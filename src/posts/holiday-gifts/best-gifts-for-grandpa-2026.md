## What to Avoid

**Health-focused gifts framed as "gifts"** — blood pressure monitors, pill organizers, or mobility aids can feel clinical rather than celebratory unless he's specifically asked for them.

**Gift cards without thought** — a generic Amazon gift card signals you didn't know what to buy. A gift card to his favorite hardware store, restaurant, or sporting goods retailer shows you paid attention.

**Clothing without knowing his preferences** — unless you know his size, brand loyalty, and style, clothing gifts often get returned.

The best gift for grandpa shows you thought about who he actually is — his hobbies, his daily frustrations, and what makes him feel useful and valued.
