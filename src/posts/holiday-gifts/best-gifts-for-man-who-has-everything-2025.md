## Buying Advice

The man who has everything has prioritized his own preferences over years. Beat that by either going more premium than he'd justify (Laguiole knives, Theragun, Breville) or introducing a category he hasn't explored (Balmuda toaster, Rocketbook, Ooni pizza oven). Avoid duplicating things he clearly already has — check his kitchen, garage, and desk setup before buying. The strongest gifts here are ones that solve a problem he didn't know had a solution, or elevate something he does every day but has never fully optimized.
