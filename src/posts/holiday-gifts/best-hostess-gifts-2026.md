## What Makes a Great Hostess Gift

**Consumable > Decorative:** Items that get used (food, cocktail ingredients, candles, kitchen tools) are universally appreciated. Decorative items risk style mismatch — what you love may clash with their home.

**Practical > Novel:** The best kitchen tools get used repeatedly and remind the host of you every time. A fun novelty item is used once.

**The right price window:** $25–$75 is the sweet spot for most dinner party hosting situations. Under $20 can read as afterthought; over $150 can make the host feel obligated unless it's a significant occasion.

**Avoid:** Wine from a grocery store (unless you know their taste), scented candles from chain stores (too generic), items that require storage.
