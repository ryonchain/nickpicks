**No hub required for these picks.** Everything on this list works via Wi-Fi without a separate smart home hub. This is the most important factor for first-time smart home recipients — they shouldn't have to buy extra hardware to use the gift.

- Smart speaker + smart plug = $65 (adds voice control to any lamp)
