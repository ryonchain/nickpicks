## Buying Advice

Split your approach by what kind of gift resonates with him. **Practical husbands** want the YETI tumbler, Leatherman, or DEWALT — things that solve daily problems they haven't prioritized. **Gear-focused husbands** want the Bose earbuds or Patagonia jacket — quality upgrades to things they use regularly. **Self-care-resistant husbands** (most of them) benefit from the Theragun — the physical-recovery gift that becomes a habit. If in doubt: drinkware and multi-tools have the highest husband gift satisfaction scores because they're visible daily. Avoid gifts he can buy at a grocery store, gifts that require ongoing subscriptions without a clear daily benefit, and clothing you're not confident about the fit.
