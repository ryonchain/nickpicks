## Gifting Rules for Neighbors

**Price point:** $15–$40 is appropriate for holiday neighbor gifting. $25–$60 for someone who has been a particularly good neighbor or helped you significantly. $50+ for a genuine close friendship that happens to involve living next door.

**Avoid:** Gifts that require their home to accommodate (decorative items that may not match their style), gifts that presuppose their dietary restrictions (wine to someone who may not drink, food baskets without checking for allergies).

**Presentation matters:** A nice ribbon, a handwritten card, or a simple bow transforms a $15 candle into a considered gift. The card says more than the gift itself in most neighbor interactions.
