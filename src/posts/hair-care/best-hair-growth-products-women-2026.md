## Ingredients with Actual Evidence

**Minoxidil (2% for women):** The only FDA-approved over-the-counter topical for female hair loss. Proven in multiple placebo-controlled trials to increase hair count and thickness with consistent use. Takes 3–6 months to show results; must be used indefinitely or shedding resumes.

**Caffeine:** Laboratory evidence shows caffeine blocks DHT (a hormone that shrinks hair follicles) and stimulates follicle cell proliferation. Topical application has shown modest improvement in hair thickness in clinical studies.

**Biotin:** Only effective for hair growth if you have a biotin deficiency. Most people eating a varied diet are not deficient. Worth supplementing if you have brittle nails alongside hair concerns.

**Niacinamide:** Improves scalp circulation and strengthens the hair follicle environment. Strong supporting evidence for scalp health as a foundation for growth.

**Saw Palmetto:** Plant-based DHT blocker. Some evidence for slowing hair loss in androgenetic alopecia; less robust than minoxidil but worth considering as a supplement.
