## FAQ

**Q: What LEGO set is best for a child who has never built before?**
A: LEGO City Starter Sets (for ages 5–7) or LEGO Creator 3-in-1 sets (for ages 7–10) are the best entry points. Both have manageable piece counts and clear instructions that build confidence.

**Q: Are Technic sets compatible with regular LEGO bricks?**
A: Technic uses a different primary connection system (holes and axles rather than studs), but the two systems can be combined. Most older kids' collections include a mix of both.

**Q: Which LEGO theme has the best replay value?**
A: LEGO Creator 3-in-1 gives the most per-piece replay value since each set contains three complete builds. City sets offer the most open-ended play once built.
