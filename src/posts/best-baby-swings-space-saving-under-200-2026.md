## Space-Saving Design: What to Look For

**Compact footprint:** Traditional full-size swings can take up 4+ square feet of floor space. Compact alternatives like the rockaRoo and Ingenuity SmartSize cut that in half or more.

**Easy fold or collapse:** A swing that folds for storage lets you reclaim the space when it's nap time or bedtime without keeping the unit out permanently.

**Plug-in power:** Swings that run on AC power (vs. batteries only) are better value — swings burn through D batteries at a surprising rate. Look for AC adapter included, or a rechargeable model.

**Multiple motion directions:** Babies have preferences — some are soothed by front-to-back motion, others prefer side-to-side. If you're buying blind (before you know your baby's preference), choose a multi-motion model.
