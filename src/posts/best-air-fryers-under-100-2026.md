## Buying Guide: What to Look For

### Capacity
Choose your air fryer size based on how many people you typically cook for:
- **2.6–3 qt:** 1–2 people
- **4–5 qt:** 2–3 people (most popular)
- **5.5–6 qt:** 3–5 people
- **6+ qt:** Families of 5+, meal preppers

### Wattage
More watts = faster preheating. Most air fryers in this range run 1500–1700W. The higher the wattage, the faster your food cooks — but the difference is usually 1–2 minutes, not dramatic.

### Preset Functions
Nice to have, but don't over-index on these. More presets doesn't mean better food. Focus on build quality and heat distribution first.

### Ease of Cleaning
Look for dishwasher-safe baskets and non-stick crisper plates. A model that's annoying to clean gets used less.

### Noise Level
Air fryers are louder than microwaves — expect fan noise at 55–65 dB. The Ninja AF101 is one of the quieter models in this range.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Can an air fryer replace my regular oven?**
For smaller portions, yes. Air fryers preheat in 3–5 minutes vs 10–15 for an oven, use less energy, and produce crispier results for fried-style foods. However, they can't handle large roasts, sheet pan dinners for 6+, or baking jobs that need radiant heat.

**Do air fryers actually taste better than ovens?**
For foods where crispiness matters — fries, wings, breaded items, vegetables — air fryers win because hot air circulates from all sides. For slower-cooked meats or baked goods, a regular oven is often better.

**Is preheating an air fryer necessary?**
Most manufacturers recommend 2–3 minutes of preheat for best results. Some models have a preheat button; others you simply run at temperature for a few minutes before adding food.

**How do I keep my air fryer from getting smoky?**
High-fat foods (bacon, fatty burgers) can cause smoke. Add a tablespoon of water to the bottom of the basket to reduce smoke from dripping fat. Clean the heating element regularly.

**Are air fryer plastics safe?**
All FDA-approved models are BPA-free. The plastic housing gets warm during use but the cooking basket itself is metal. Look for PFOA-free non-stick coatings if you have concerns about cookware coatings.
