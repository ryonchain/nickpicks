## What to Skip

**Egg slicers:** Use a sharp knife. A dedicated egg slicer takes up space and has only one use.

**Apple corers/slicers:** A knife does this in 20 seconds with zero cleanup.

**Strawberry hullers:** A paring knife or a regular straw (push up from the bottom) works perfectly.

**Electric can openers:** Slower to set up than a manual opener for most cans.

The best kitchen tools are the ones that get used every week, not once a year. Focus on daily-use items — spatulas, scales, garlic press — before buying specialty tools.
