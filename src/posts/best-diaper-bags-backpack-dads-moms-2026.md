## What to Look For in a Diaper Bag Backpack

**Storage capacity:** You need room for diapers, wipes, a change of clothes, snacks, bottles, pacifiers, burp cloths, and your own phone, wallet, and keys. Look for at least 20–25L capacity and 10+ pockets for organization.

**Insulated pockets:** At least one insulated bottle pocket to keep formula or breast milk at temperature. Two is better for longer outings.

**Changing pad:** Most decent diaper bags include a fold-out changing pad. Padded ones are more comfortable; wipe-clean surfaces are more practical.

**Gender-neutral design:** If both parents will use the bag, choose a neutral color (black, gray, tan, olive) and a design that works with casual clothing.

**Stroller straps:** A stroller attachment clip keeps the bag accessible without wearing it. Nearly standard on bags above $80.
