## Foil vs Rotary: Which Shaver Type Is Right for You?

**Foil shavers** use oscillating blades under a thin perforated metal screen. The foil lifts hairs and the inner blade cuts them as they pass through the holes. They shave in straight strokes and tend to give a closer cut.

**Best for:** Men who shave daily or every other day, men with sensitive skin, men with straight fine-to-medium hair, men who like a close shave similar to a razor.

**Rotary shavers** use three spinning circular heads arranged in a triangle. Each head pivots independently to follow the contours of your jaw, neck, and chin.

**Best for:** Men who shave every 2–4 days, men with thicker or coarser hair, men with angular jawlines, men who want minimal pressure and movement.

**Wet vs. Dry:** Most modern electric shavers work both wet (with gel or foam in the shower) and dry. Wet shaving with an electric shaver improves comfort but makes cleanup slightly more involved.
