## What to Look For in Work-From-Home Headphones

**ANC Effectiveness:** Not all noise cancellation is equal. Budget ANC often handles low-frequency sounds well (AC hum, engine noise) but struggles with voices and higher-frequency sounds. Read reviews for real-world ANC performance, not just whether the feature exists.

**Microphone Quality:** For video calls, your headphone mic matters. Look for boom microphones (extend toward your mouth) or models specifically rated for call clarity — many over-ear headphones with good ANC have poor mics.

**Battery Life:** For all-day work, 20–30+ hours of battery with ANC on is important. Nobody wants headphones dying during an afternoon call.

**Comfort for Long Sessions:** Ear pad material (leather vs. fabric), headband padding, and clamping force all determine whether headphones feel fine after 30 minutes and unbearable after 3 hours.

**Wireless vs. Wired:** Wireless (Bluetooth) is more convenient but adds latency (usually unnoticeable for calls, sometimes noticeable for video). Wired delivers no latency but limits movement.

## ANC Headphones vs. Earbuds for Work

**Over-ear headphones:**
- Better passive isolation (cups cover the ear)
- More comfortable for 6-8 hour workdays
- Better ANC on the ear (larger hardware for noise processing)
- Warmer in hot weather

**True wireless earbuds with ANC (like Anker Soundcore Liberty 4, Sony WF-1000XM5):**
- More portable, no headband fatigue
- Better in video calls (less of your head movement is amplified by the headphone structure)
- Shorter battery life per charge
- ANC often comparable to headphones at similar price points

For all-day work sessions, most people prefer over-ear headphones for comfort. For hybrid workers who also commute, earbuds may be more practical.

**Q: Do cheap ANC headphones really work?**
A: Budget ANC (under $50) works well on constant low-frequency noise (airplane engines, AC hum). It struggles with voices and variable sounds. In the $70–100 range, ANC quality is noticeably better and can genuinely help in noisy home environments.

**Q: Will noise-canceling headphones help me focus at work?**
A: Yes. ANC reduces cognitive load by removing the ambient sounds your brain is constantly processing in the background. Most users report meaningful focus improvement.

**Q: Can I use ANC headphones for exercise?**
A: Most over-ear ANC headphones aren't sweat-resistant. For exercise, use earbuds rated IPX4 or higher.
