## Do You Actually Need Third-Party Accessories?

The controllers and headsets bundled with consoles are functional, but third-party accessories solve real problems:

- **Controllers** — stock thumbstick placement causes wrist angles that tire out during long sessions; premium controllers have better ergonomics, trigger stops, and paddle buttons
- **Headsets** — built-in TV audio can't match the spatial audio and voice chat isolation of a good headset; the PS5 Pulse headset is decent but doesn't work on Xbox
- **Charging docks** — USB cables left on the floor are tripping hazards and wear ports; a dock makes battery management invisible
