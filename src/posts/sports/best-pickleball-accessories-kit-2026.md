## Court Shoes for Pickleball

Don't play pickleball in running shoes. Running shoes are designed for forward motion — pickleball requires constant lateral movement and quick stops. Court shoes provide:

- Wider outsoles for lateral stability
- Herringbone tread for quick stops and pivots
- Reinforced toe cap for drag shots
- Lower heel drop for court feel
