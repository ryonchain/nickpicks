## What to Skip

**Wipe warmer:** Adds one more thing to maintain and fill. Cold wipes wake babies up — which is actually useful during diaper changes.

**Bottle sterilizer:** A pot of boiling water or a dishwasher hot cycle does the same thing for free.

**Baby shoes (newborn–6 months):** Babies can't walk. Shoes are cute props, not necessities.

**Nursing cover:** A receiving blanket works equally well for a fraction of the cost.
