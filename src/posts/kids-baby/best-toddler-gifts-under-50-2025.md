## What Makes a Great Toddler Gift

**Age appropriateness:** Toddler development varies significantly — a 13-month-old and a 3-year-old have very different abilities. Age ranges on packaging are guides, not rules, but choosing within the right window matters.

**Durability:** Toddlers throw, bang, and mouth things. Wood, thick plastic, and fabric toys hold up; thin plastic and tiny parts don't.

**Open-ended play value:** The best toddler toys don't have a single correct use. DUPLO bricks, play kitchens, and sensory bins adapt to the child's current interest and skill level.

**Parent-friendly:** No tiny pieces that scatter everywhere. Manageable noise levels. Easy to clean. Storage that makes sense.
