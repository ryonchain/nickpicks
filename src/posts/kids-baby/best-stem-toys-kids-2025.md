## Why STEM Toys Matter

Children who engage with STEM concepts through play develop stronger problem-solving skills, higher comfort with math and science in school, and early interest in technical careers. The key is finding toys that feel like play first — the learning is a byproduct of genuine engagement, not forced instruction.

The best STEM toys share a few traits: they require active problem-solving, they have a clear feedback loop (you know when you've succeeded), and they grow in complexity as skills develop.
