## Why Educational Games Work Better Than Worksheets

Educational games develop skills through play because they:

- **Create intrinsic motivation** — kids want to win, so they engage with the underlying skill
- **Provide immediate feedback** — bad moves fail immediately; good moves feel rewarding
- **Build iterative thinking** — multiple rounds teach through pattern recognition
- **Develop social skills** — turn-taking, negotiation, and sportsmanship alongside academic content

The best educational games don't feel like school. Kids shouldn't know they're doing math — they should think they're playing.
