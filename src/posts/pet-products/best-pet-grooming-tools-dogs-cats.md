## Why Home Grooming Matters

Regular grooming isn't just about appearance:

- **Coat health:** Regular brushing distributes natural oils, prevents matting (which can cause skin infections), and removes dead undercoat that traps heat
- **Skin inspection:** Regular handling and brushing gives you the opportunity to find lumps, irritations, parasites, and wounds early
- **Nail health:** Overgrown nails change a dog's gait and can cause joint pain over time, or curl back into the paw pad if neglected
- **Bonding:** Regular, calm grooming sessions build trust and reduce anxiety for veterinary visits
