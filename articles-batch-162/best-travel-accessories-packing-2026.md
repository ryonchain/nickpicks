## What's Actually Overrated

**Travel-sized toiletry sets:** Just decant your own products into reusable travel bottles. The pre-packaged sets contain products you don't use and cost 3x what decanting costs.

**Hard-shell toiletry cases:** Ziplock bags pass TSA screening identically. A quality soft toiletry bag is more useful than hard shell.

**Elaborate cable organizers:** For most travelers, one medium-sized zipper pouch handles all cables without a specialized organizer.

**Neck fans:** Novelty item. Battery dies in 3-4 hours; awkward to wear through airports or on planes.
