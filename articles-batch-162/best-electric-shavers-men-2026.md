## Foil vs. Rotary: How to Choose

**Choose foil if you:**
- Shave every day or every other day
- Have sensitive skin prone to razor burn
- Prefer straight, clean shave lines
- Shave against the grain

**Choose rotary if you:**
- Let stubble grow 3+ days between shaves
- Have thick, coarse facial hair
- Have a complex facial contour (strong jawline, prominent chin cleft)
- Have used rotary shavers before and liked them
