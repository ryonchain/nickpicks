## Mounting Position: What to Know

**Dashboard:** Most stable option. Clear view of the road. Legal everywhere. Use suction cup with a smooth, clean dashboard surface, or adhesive for permanent mounting.

**Windshield:** Excellent view, very stable with suction cup. **Illegal in California, Texas, Minnesota** and other states that prohibit windshield obstruction. Check your state before using.

**Air vent:** Easy to install and remove. Less stable — vibrates with the vent and the vent itself vibrates at highway speeds. Best for light phones in lighter cases.

**CD slot:** Highly stable, great for frequent phone-takers. Requires a CD slot, which is becoming less common in newer vehicles.

**Console:** ProClip-style console mounts are the most seamless but require a vehicle-specific part.
