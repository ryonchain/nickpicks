## Installation: What to Know Before You Buy

**SD card matters:** Buy the right card. Class 10 / U3 or higher is required for 4K recording without dropping frames. Use dashcam-specific cards (Kingston Canvas Go Plus, Samsung PRO Endurance) — regular SD cards fail faster from constant write cycles. 128GB is the minimum useful size.

**Hardwire vs. cigarette lighter:** Cigarette lighter power is fine for basic recording. Hardwire kits ($15-30) connect to your fuse box for always-on parking mode and eliminate cable clutter. If parking mode is important to you, budget for the hardwire kit.

**Placement:** Upper center of windshield (behind rearview mirror) is both optimal for coverage and cleanest installation. Avoid mounting in the driver's primary sightline — check your state's windshield obstruction rules.

**SD card cycling:** Most dashcams recommend formatting the SD card every 3-6 months to prevent file system corruption. Some cards last 2-3 years in dashcam duty; cheap cards fail in 6-12 months.
