## Closet Organization Strategy

**Start with a purge:** No organizational system fixes a space filled with things you don't need. Before buying any organizer, remove everything and sort into keep, donate, and discard piles. Most people find they have 20-30% less to organize than they thought.

**Group by category, not by color:** Color-coding looks great in photos but is impractical. Group clothes by type (work clothes together, casual together, exercise together) — you dress by occasion, not by color.

**Measure twice, buy once:** Closet systems fail when they don't fit the space. Measure your closet height, width, and depth before purchasing any system. Note the location of outlets, light fixtures, and door swings.

**Seasonal rotation:** Move out-of-season clothes to under-bed storage or a second closet. This alone creates 30-40% more usable primary closet space.
