## Inkjet vs Laser: Which Is Right for You?

| | Inkjet | Laser |
|---|---|---|
| Photo quality | Excellent | Poor |
| Document sharpness | Good | Excellent |
| Per-page cost (black) | ~3–8 cents | ~1–2 cents |
| Speed | 10–15 ppm | 20–30 ppm |
| Warm-up time | Instant | 10–30 seconds |
| Reliability after months idle | Often clogs | No issues |
| Initial cost | Lower | Higher |

**Choose inkjet if:** You print photos, occasional documents, or small volumes. 

**Choose laser if:** You print primarily text documents, print frequently, or want the lowest per-page cost.

## Wireless Connectivity Options Explained

**Wi-Fi (2.4 GHz / 5 GHz):** Connects to your home router. Print from any device on the same network.

**Wi-Fi Direct:** Connects devices directly to the printer without a router — useful when your router is in another room or you want to bypass the network.

**Bluetooth:** Short-range, used for initial setup or mobile printing without Wi-Fi.

**Apple AirPrint:** iPhone/iPad print directly without installing apps. Most printers support it.

**Google Cloud Print (replaced by Android Print):** Android devices print via Google's services. Look for "Android Print" or Mopria Print support.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Why does my inkjet clog?**
Inkjet heads dry out when unused. Print at least one page per week to keep heads wet, or run the cleaning cycle when clogs appear. If you print infrequently, a laser printer avoids this entirely.

**Is the HP Smart Tank worth the higher upfront cost?**
Yes, if you print more than 200 pages per month. The ink economics are compelling for moderate-to-high volume. For occasional printing (under 50 pages/month), a standard inkjet is a better value.

**How do I print from my phone?**
iOS: Apple AirPrint (built-in) or the printer's app (Canon PRINT, HP Smart). Android: Mopria Print Service (built-in) or manufacturer apps. Most printers support both without extra configuration.

**What's the best paper for photo printing?**
Canon's Photo Paper Pro Platinum or HP Premium Plus Photo Paper for glossy prints. Epson Ultra Premium Presentation Paper for matte. Paper quality affects output more than many people expect.
