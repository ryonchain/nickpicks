## Buying Guide

### GPS Accuracy for Kids Watches

All LTE-enabled kids watches combine GPS satellite positioning with cell tower triangulation. In open areas, GPS accuracy is within 10-20 meters. In dense urban areas or indoors, cell tower triangulation takes over at ~50-100 meter accuracy. For knowing which block or house your child is at, this is more than sufficient.

### Carrier Lock-in vs. Flexibility

**Carrier-locked (Verizon GizmoWatch, T-Mobile SyncUP):** Cheapest hardware, easiest setup, but no carrier flexibility. Change carriers and the watch becomes unusable.

**Carrier-flexible (TickTalk 4, Cosmo JrTrack):** Works with multiple carriers. More expensive hardware. Best if you might switch carriers or travel internationally.

### Age-Appropriate Features

**Ages 5-7:** Simple interface, large buttons, minimal contacts. Verizon GizmoWatch or Garmin Bounce.
**Ages 8-10:** More features, video calling, school mode. TickTalk 4.
**Ages 11-12:** Smartwatch-adjacent features. TickTalk 4 or step up to an entry-level smartwatch.

### Durability Considerations

Kids watches take abuse. Look for:
- IP67 or better water resistance (submersion, not just splashes)
- Silicone or rubber band (not leather or metal links that can scratch or break)
- Reinforced case corners
- Tempered glass or plastic display (glass looks better but cracks more easily)

## FAQ

**Do kids smartwatches require a phone plan?**
Most GPS kids watches require a cellular plan ($5-15/month) for real-time GPS and calling. Some use Wi-Fi-only location when a plan isn't active.

**At what age should a child get a GPS smartwatch?**
Ages 5-12 is the typical range. The watch becomes valuable when kids have some independence but aren't ready for a smartphone.

**How accurate is GPS tracking on kids watches?**
Accurate to 10-20 meters in open areas — enough to identify which house or block your child is at. LTE positioning supplements GPS indoors.

**Can kids make calls without WiFi on a GPS watch?**
Yes, over LTE cellular. Kids can only call and receive from an approved contact list managed by parents.
