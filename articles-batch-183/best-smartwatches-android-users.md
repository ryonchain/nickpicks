## Buying Guide

### Wear OS vs. Samsung One UI Watch vs. Amazfit Zepp OS

**Wear OS (Google Pixel Watch, TicWatch, Fossil):** Largest app ecosystem via Play Store. Best Google integration. Generally shorter battery life. Ideal for Google power users.

**Samsung One UI Watch:** Based on Wear OS but heavily customized. Best integration with Samsung phones. Works with any Android phone for core features. Best health sensors in category.

**Amazfit Zepp OS:** Not Wear OS. Fewer third-party apps. Exceptional battery life. Best for fitness-focused users who don't need a full smartwatch app ecosystem.

### Key Considerations

**Battery life:** Wear OS watches average 24-40 hours. Samsung Galaxy Watch gets 40 hours. Amazfit can last 14+ days. Decide how often you want to charge.

**GPS accuracy:** Look for multi-band GPS (L1+L5) for the most accurate outdoor activity tracking. Galaxy Watch 7 and Pixel Watch 3 45mm both have multi-band GPS.

**Health features:** ECG requires FDA clearance — Samsung and Google Pixel Watch both have it. Body composition analysis is Samsung-only in this category.

**App ecosystem:** Only Wear OS watches can run third-party apps from the Play Store. Amazfit supports select third-party apps but is primarily a fitness device.

## FAQ

**Does Apple Watch work with Android?**
No. Apple Watch requires an iPhone and does not work with Android. The best Android equivalents are Samsung Galaxy Watch 7 and Google Pixel Watch 3.

**What is the best smartwatch for Samsung Galaxy phones?**
The Samsung Galaxy Watch 7 integrates most deeply with Samsung Galaxy phones via Galaxy AI and Samsung Health, but it works well with any Android phone.

**Is Wear OS better than Samsung's One UI Watch?**
Wear OS has a larger app ecosystem; Samsung One UI Watch has better Samsung phone integration. For non-Samsung Android users, Wear OS or Samsung Watch both work well.

**Can I use a smartwatch without my phone nearby?**
LTE models can make calls without your phone. Basic tracking and stored music work offline. Notifications require Bluetooth connection to your phone.
